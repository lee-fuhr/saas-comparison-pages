If your remote team relies on Livestorm for live streaming and interactive webinars, you may be looking for alternatives that also work without extra plugins. Whether you need tighter integration with existing tools, different pricing, or a simpler interface, several platforms offer similar built-in interactivity. Below we compare three strong options: Zoom, Demio, and StreamYard.

alternative

Zoom

. Zoom provides live streaming to platforms like YouTube and Facebook along with robust webinar features such as Q&A, polling, and breakout rooms. No plugins are required for attendees, making it easy for any remote team to join from a browser or the app. It scales well from small meetings to large events, though its webinar add-on comes at an extra cost.

alternative

Demio

. Demio is built specifically for marketing webinars and live streaming, with a clean interface that lets you run polls, handouts, and CTAs without plugins. Attendees simply click a link to join in their browser, and you can stream to Facebook Live or YouTube simultaneously. It focuses on engagement and easy replays, making it a strong choice for teams that prioritize conversion.

Try Demio →
alternative

StreamYard

. StreamYard is a browser-based live streaming studio that integrates with Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Twitch. It includes built-in comments, banners, and guest invitations, and you can overlay polls and calls to action without any extra downloads. While it excels at streaming to social platforms, its webinar features are less advanced than Livestorm’s, so it is best for teams that want to broadcast rather than host highly interactive sessions.

Try StreamYard →

How they compare

All three alternatives let your remote team host live events without requiring plugins. Zoom offers the most comprehensive webinar toolkit but often needs a paid add-on. Demio is simpler and focused on marketing webinars with smooth interactivity. StreamYard is ideal for streaming to social channels but lacks some in-webinar engagement tools. Choose based on whether you prioritize social reach, easy replays, or deep webinar controls.

Frequently asked questions

Can these tools stream to multiple platforms at the same time?
Yes. Zoom, Demio, and StreamYard all support simultaneous live streaming to destinations like YouTube and Facebook. Some plans may restrict the number of destinations, so check each tool’s pricing page for details.
Do any of these alternatives require attendees to download software?
No. All three tools allow attendees to join from a web browser without installing extra plugins or desktop apps. This removes a common barrier for remote teams and external participants.
