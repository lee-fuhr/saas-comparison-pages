If your SaaS company is evaluating compliance automation tools, you have likely come across Vanta. It is a popular platform for achieving and maintaining SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certifications. However, Vanta may not fit every team’s workflow, budget, or feature requirements. Some users find its pricing steep for early stage startups, while others want deeper integration options or a different approach to evidence collection. This article reviews the main Vanta alternatives so you can compare them for automated SOC 2 and ISO compliance monitoring.

alternative

Drata

. Drata offers a wide network of native integrations with common SaaS providers like AWS, GCP, and Slack. Its continuous monitoring and automated evidence collection help reduce manual effort, and the platform includes a built in policy generator. Drata is often chosen by companies that want a fast setup with strong integration coverage.

alternative

SecureFrame

. SecureFrame focuses on making compliance straightforward for small to midsize businesses. It provides customizable risk assessments, automated evidence gathering, and a clear dashboard for tracking control status. SecureFrame is a good fit if you prefer a simple interface and need support for multiple frameworks including SOC 2 and ISO 27001.

alternative

Tugboat Logic

. Tugboat Logic uses a guided approach that walks you through compliance step by step. Its “Cannonball” onboarding feature helps new users get started quickly, and the platform includes automated evidence collection and vendor risk management. Tugboat Logic is recommended for teams that value a structured workflow and pre built control templates.

alternative

TrustArc

. TrustArc is a broader privacy and compliance platform that goes beyond SOC 2 and ISO monitoring. It offers tools for data mapping, privacy impact assessments, and consent management alongside compliance automation. TrustArc is best for organizations with complex privacy needs or those that must adhere to regulations like GDPR and CCPA in addition to SOC 2 and ISO.

How they compare

All four alternatives provide automated evidence collection, continuous monitoring, and support for SOC 2 and ISO 27001. Drata stands out for its large integration library, while SecureFrame appeals to smaller teams wanting simplicity. Tugboat Logic offers strong onboarding guidance, and TrustArc adds comprehensive privacy and risk management features. Pricing varies widely, so check current plans on each vendor’s website. Your choice should depend on your team size, existing tech stack, and whether you need privacy compliance beyond SOC 2 and ISO.

Frequently asked questions

How do these alternatives compare to Vanta in terms of pricing?
Vanta’s pricing often starts higher than some competitors, especially for early stage companies. Drata and SecureFrame typically offer more tiered plans for smaller teams, while Tugboat Logic and TrustArc may be priced based on the number of users or frameworks selected. It is best to request a custom quote from each vendor to compare costs for your specific scenario.
Can I use these tools to maintain ISO 27001 if I already have SOC 2?
Yes. Each of the alternatives supports both SOC 2 and ISO 27001 frameworks. Most allow you to map controls across multiple standards simultaneously, so you can maintain both certifications without duplicating work. The platform will track evidence and automate monitoring for each framework’s requirements.
