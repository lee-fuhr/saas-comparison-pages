If your enterprise sales and marketing team relies on ZoomInfo for B2B contact and company data, you may be exploring alternatives due to pricing, data freshness, or specific feature needs. ZoomInfo is a powerful platform, but it’s not the only option. There are several credible competitors that offer similar functionality, often with different strengths in areas like intent data, direct dial accuracy, or email verification. This article reviews five alternative tools to help you compare their value for your buying committee.

alternative

Apollo.io

combines a B2B database with a built-in engagement platform. It offers affordable pricing tiers, a large contact database, and advanced filtering options for prospecting. Apollo also includes email sequencing and CRM integration, making it a strong all-in-one choice for teams that want to move from data to outreach without switching tools.

alternative

Clearbit

focuses on real-time enrichment and intent signals rather than a static database. It integrates directly with your CRM to append firmographic and technographic data on incoming leads. Enterprise teams value Clearbit for its API-first approach and data append accuracy, though it is not a standalone prospecting database like ZoomInfo.

Try Clearbit →
alternative

Lusha

specializes in providing verified direct dial phone numbers and personal email addresses at scale. Its self-serve interface is simple and fast, suitable for individual reps who need quick contact lookups. Lusha offers a pay-as-you-go model, which can be more flexible for smaller teams, but its company-level data is less comprehensive than ZoomInfo’s.

alternative

Cognism

targets European and global compliance needs with GDPR-safe data. It matches B2B contacts to verified mobile numbers using a proprietary technology stack. Cognism also provides intent data and sales triggers, making it a solid alternative for organizations that prioritize data privacy and EU coverage.

Try Cognism →
alternative

Hunter.io

is purpose-built for finding and verifying professional email addresses. It offers a clean, intuitive search interface and domain-based email discovery. Hunter works best as a supplemental tool for outbound email campaigns, but it lacks the company firmographics, intent data, and CRM automation that enterprise teams often require from a primary data provider.

Frequently asked questions

Can any of these alternatives match ZoomInfo’s data volume for enterprise sales?
Apollo.io has a comparable database size for contacts and companies, while Clearbit and Cognism offer different strengths in enrichment and compliance. For sheer volume, Apollo comes closest, but each tool serves distinct use cases.
Are these alternatives more affordable than ZoomInfo?
Yes, most alternatives have lower entry prices or usage-based pricing. Apollo.io and Lusha offer plans starting well below ZoomInfo’s enterprise contracts. However, pricing scales with team size, so evaluate total cost for your seat count and data needs.
