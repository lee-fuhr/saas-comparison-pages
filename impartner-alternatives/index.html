If your organization is evaluating partner relationship management (PRM) software, you have likely come across Impartner. It is a mature platform with strong automation and partner portal features. However, it may not fit every budget or workflow. This article explores four credible Impartner alternatives: PartnerStack, Alliances.io, Channeltivity, and Partnerize. Each offers different strengths in onboarding, revenue sharing, or ecosystem management. We compare them to help you decide which PRM suite best aligns with your partner program goals.

alternative

PartnerStack

. This platform is built for SaaS companies that run referral and channel partner programs. It focuses on automated partner recruiting, onboarding, and commission tracking. PartnerStack also provides a partner directory and performance dashboards, making it a strong choice if your priority is scaling partner-led revenue quickly.

alternative

Alliances.io

. Designed for technology ecosystem partnerships, Alliances.io helps manage co-selling and co-marketing with alliance partners. It offers joint pipeline tracking, deal registration, and MDF (market development fund) management. This tool is ideal for organizations that rely on strategic tech partnerships rather than traditional reseller channels.

alternative

Channeltivity

. Channeltivity is a full-featured PRM that emphasizes partner onboarding, training, and deal registration. It includes a partner portal, automated workflows, and integration with CRM systems like Salesforce. It suits mid-market and enterprise companies that need a customizable, self-service partner experience.

alternative

Partnerize

. Partnerize is a performance-based partnership platform that goes beyond traditional PRM. It handles affiliate marketing, influencer partnerships, and revenue sharing with advanced attribution and fraud detection. This alternative works best for businesses that want to track and optimize a wide range of partner types under one roof.

How they compare

All four alternatives serve different partner strategies. PartnerStack excels for SaaS referral programs. Alliances.io is best for tech ecosystem alliances. Channeltivity offers a traditional, customizable PRM for reseller networks. Partnerize focuses on performance marketing and attribution. Consider your primary partner type: direct channel partners, affiliate marketers, or strategic alliances. Also evaluate ease of CRM integration, pricing model, and whether you need built-in fraud detection or MDF management. Impartner remains a strong contender, but these alternatives may offer a closer fit for specific program structures.

Frequently asked questions

Is PartnerStack a direct replacement for Impartner for a reseller channel?
Not . PartnerStack is optimized for referral and affiliate programs, not for managing complex reseller deals with tiered discounts, deal registration, or compliance checks. If your program relies on traditional reseller partners, consider Channeltivity or Alliances.io instead.
Can I migrate my partner data from Impartner to one of these alternatives?
Yes, most PRM tools support data import from CSV or API integrations. However, the migration process requires mapping fields like partner profiles, commission rules, and deal registration history. It is best to contact each vendor’s onboarding team to confirm specific Impartner export capabilities and expected timeline.
