Best Phrase alternatives

Phrase is a translation management system (TMS) that helps global marketing teams manage multilingual content. It offers features like translation memories, machine translation integration, and collaboration tools. However, you might explore Phrase alternatives for reasons such as pricing, specific workflow needs, or a preference for a different user interface. This article covers four popular alternatives: DeepL, Smartling, Lokalise, and Wordfast.

alternative

DeepL

. DeepL is best known for its high quality neural machine translation engine. It also offers a writing assistant and a glossary feature to maintain consistent brand terminology. DeepL can be used as a standalone translation tool or integrated into other platforms via API, making it a good choice for marketers who rely mostly on machine translation.

alternative

Smartling

. Smartling is a full featured enterprise translation management platform with strong automation and analytics. It helps marketing teams manage large scale projects through connectors for content management systems and continuous localization workflows. Smartling also offers a mobile app for on the go translation review.

alternative

Lokalise

. Lokalise is a cloud based localization platform built for agile development teams. It supports real time collaboration, automated screenshot generation, and integration with many developer tools. Marketers often choose Lokalise when they need to scale localization quickly without a steep learning curve.

alternative

Wordfast

. Wordfast is a desktop based translation memory tool popular among freelance translators and small teams. It offers versions compatible with Microsoft Word and a standalone platform, along with a cloud based option. Wordfast is a cost effective alternative for marketers who need basic translation memory features without a monthly subscription.

How they compare

Choose DeepL if your priority is raw machine translation quality and you need a simple integrated solution. Select Smartling when you require a full enterprise TMS with advanced workflow automation and analytics. Pick Lokalise if you want a modern, developer friendly platform that supports rapid scaling. Go with Wordfast when budget is tight and your team needs a reliable offline translation memory tool. Phrase itself remains a strong middle ground with a balance of features, integrations, and pricing.

Frequently asked questions

Is DeepL a complete replacement for Phrase?
No. DeepL primarily provides machine translation and a writing assistant, not a full translation management system. You would need to combine DeepL with other tools to manage translation projects, glossaries, and collaboration.
Which alternative is best for a small marketing team with a low budget?
Wordfast is often the most affordable option, especially if you choose the desktop version. For a cloud based solution with a free tier, Lokalise also offers a free plan for small teams with limited translation volume.
