Best UserVoice alternatives

If your support team relies on UserVoice to collect and manage customer feedback, you might be exploring other options. UserVoice is a solid tool for feedback and ticketing, but it may not fit every team’s budget, feature set, or workflow. This article covers three popular UserVoice alternatives – Zendesk, Canny, and Freshdesk – so you can decide which one best supports your feedback and ticketing needs.

alternative

Zendesk

. Zendesk is a comprehensive customer service platform that includes a built-in feedback feature through tickets and satisfaction surveys. It excels at routing support requests and tracking customer sentiment, but its feedback management is less specialized than UserVoice’s dedicated idea boards. For teams that want a single system for both support tickets and feedback, Zendesk offers strong integrations with third-party feedback tools as well.

alternative

Canny

. Canny is a focused feedback management tool designed to collect, prioritize, and share product ideas with your users. It is not a full ticketing system, but it integrates with support platforms like Zendesk and Intercom. If your team primarily needs a clean, standalone board for customer feedback and roadmaps, Canny is a lightweight and affordable alternative to UserVoice.

alternative

Freshdesk

. Freshdesk is an all-in-one helpdesk that includes feedback collection through tickets, surveys, and a suggestion portal. Its feedback features are built into the support workflow, making it easy to link customer issues directly to feature requests. Freshdesk is a good choice for teams that want a simpler, more affordable option with both ticketing and basic feedback management in one platform.

How they compare

UserVoice is a dedicated feedback and feature request platform, while Zendesk and Freshdesk are full helpdesk systems with feedback capabilities. Canny is purely a feedback board, not a ticketing tool. If you need deep feedback prioritization and roadmapping, Canny or UserVoice itself may be best. If you want to unify support tickets and feedback, Zendesk or Freshdesk are stronger choices.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Canny with my existing support ticketing system?
Yes, Canny integrates with popular helpdesks like Zendesk and Intercom. You can keep using your current ticketing tool while Canny handles feedback boards and idea voting separately.
Is Freshdesk a good replacement for UserVoice if I need advanced feedback roadmaps?
Freshdesk offers basic feedback portals and surveys, but its roadmap and prioritization features are not as detailed as UserVoice’s. If roadmapping is critical, consider Canny for feedback or look at Freshdesk’s marketplace for add-on apps.
