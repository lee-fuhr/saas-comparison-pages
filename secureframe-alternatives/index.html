If you are evaluating compliance automation platforms for SOC 2, ISO 27001, or GDPR, SecureFrame is a strong choice. But you may also want to compare a few SecureFrame alternatives to see which turnkey solution best fits your budget, team size, or specific auditor requirements. This article covers four popular alternatives: Vanta, Drata, Tugboat Logic, and TrustArc, and includes a quick comparison and answers to common questions.

alternative

Vanta

. Vanta automates evidence collection and monitoring for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and HIPAA. It offers a large library of prebuilt policies and integrates with over 100 services. Many teams appreciate its straightforward dashboard and clear remediation steps.

alternative

Drata

. Drata provides continuous compliance monitoring and automates evidence gathering for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR. It stands out for its deep integration with popular tools like AWS, GCP, and Slack, and it includes a built-in auditor network to help you find a qualified reviewer.

alternative

Tugboat Logic

. Tugboat Logic focuses on simplifying the compliance process with guided questionnaires and a risk assessment builder. It supports SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR, and it offers a library of customizable control templates. This option is often preferred by smaller teams that want a very structured, step by step workflow.

alternative

TrustArc

. TrustArc is a broader privacy and compliance platform that covers GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection frameworks in addition to SOC 2 and ISO. It includes a privacy impact assessment tool and automated data mapping. Organizations with heavy privacy requirements may find TrustArc a more comprehensive fit than a pure SOC 2 tool.

How they compare

All four alternatives are turnkey tools that help you prepare for audits, but they differ in focus. Vanta and Drata are strong for continuous monitoring and integration breadth. Tugboat Logic is more guided and policy focused. TrustArc adds deep privacy management. When choosing among SecureFrame alternatives, consider your primary compliance goal: if you need broad privacy support, TrustArc may be best. If you want a simple, automated SOC 2 path, Vanta or Drata are good matches. If you prefer a structured, template driven workflow, Tugboat Logic might be the right fit.

Frequently asked questions

How do pricing models compare among SecureFrame alternatives?
Most alternatives charge per framework per year, with tiered plans based on number of employees or integrations. Vanta and Drata both offer monthly and annual subscriptions, while Tugboat Logic and TrustArc often require an annual commitment. Contact each vendor for a custom quote since exact pricing changes frequently.
Which alternative is easiest to set up for a small team?
Many small teams find Drata and Tugboat Logic the quickest to implement. Drata’s automated integrations and guided wizard reduce manual work, while Tugboat Logic provides a checklist style workflow that is easy to follow. Vanta also has a straightforward setup but may require more initial policy customization.
