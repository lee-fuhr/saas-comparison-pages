Best Mailchimp alternatives

If you started with Mailchimp because it was free or simple, you have likely outgrown it. The pricing gets confusing and expensive as your list grows, and the templates can feel restrictive. Whether you are a creator sending newsletters or a small business running email campaigns, there are better tools that offer more flexibility, better deliverability, or a simpler flat fee. Here are five solid Mailchimp alternatives built for people who need to send emails without the frustrations.

alternative

ConvertKit

. ConvertKit is designed for creators who want to build relationships with their audience through newsletters and automated sequences. It uses a simple visual automation builder and tags instead of lists, making it easy to segment subscribers based on their interests. The pricing is predictable, starting at a flat monthly rate for up to 1,000 subscribers, with no per-email fees.

Try ConvertKit →
alternative

ActiveCampaign

. ActiveCampaign is a powerful choice if you need advanced automation, CRM features, and deep email segmentation. It excels at sending behavior-based follow-ups and scoring leads, which is great for ecommerce or service businesses. The learning curve is steeper than Mailchimp, but the flexibility and reporting are far superior for growing companies.

Try ActiveCampaign →
alternative

Klaviyo

. Klaviyo is the top alternative for ecommerce stores, especially those on Shopify or WooCommerce. It pulls in purchase data to trigger abandoned cart emails, product recommendations, and reorder reminders automatically. Pricing is based on contacts and email sends, but the ROI from its targeted campaigns usually outweighs the cost for online sellers.

Try Klaviyo →
alternative

Flodesk

. Flodesk offers a flat monthly rate for unlimited subscribers and emails, which solves Mailchimp’s expensive scaling problem. Its templates are clean and design-focused, making it a favorite for photographers, wedding planners, and other visual creators. However, it lacks advanced automation and deep integrations, so it works best for simple email marketing needs.

Try Flodesk →
alternative

MailerLite

. MailerLite is the most affordable alternative with a free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers and a simple drag-and-drop builder. It includes useful features like landing pages, pop-ups, and auto-resend for unopened emails that Mailchimp charges extra for. The interface is clean and fast, making it a solid choice for small businesses that want robust features without the high price tag.

Try MailerLite →
