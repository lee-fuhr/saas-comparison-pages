If you use Better Proposals but find it lacking in features, pricing, or integration options, you are not alone. GetAccept

. GetAccept combines proposals with video messaging, document tracking, and e-signatures in one tool. Freelancers who often need to explain complex ideas can use the video feature to add a personal touch alongside the proposal text.

alternative

Nusii

. Nusii is a simpler, more affordable option built specifically for freelancers and small agencies. It offers clean templates, online signatures, and basic analytics without the heavy learning curve of larger platforms.

How they compare

All five alternatives provide e-signature capabilities and proposal tracking, but they differ in pricing and focus. PandaDoc and GetAccept are best for freelancers who need integrated payment and video features. Proposify and Qwilr excel at visual design and interactive content. Nusii is the most budget friendly choice for basic proposal management. If you need simple templates and low cost, start with Nusii. If you want advanced analytics and integrations, look at PandaDoc or Qwilr.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my existing Better Proposals templates into these alternatives?
Most tools allow you to upload PDFs or copy content, but you will need to rebuild the exact layout manually because template structures are not directly transferable between platforms.
Which alternative is best for sending proposals on a tight budget?
Nusii has the lowest starting price among these options, and PandaDoc offers a free plan with limited usage. Both are cost effective for freelancers who send only a few proposals each month.
