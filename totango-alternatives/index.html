Best Totango alternatives

Mid-size SaaS firms often start with Totango for customer success because it is easy to set up and works well for smaller teams. But as your customer base grows and workflows become more complex, you may need a platform that scales without adding manual work. Totango alternatives like Gainsight, ChurnZero, and Planhat each offer different strengths for building scalable success workflows. This guide compares them so you can choose the right fit for your growth stage, budget, and team capabilities.

alternative

Gainsight

: Gainsight is a comprehensive customer success platform built for enterprise-level automation and deep integrations. It offers powerful journey orchestration and advanced health scoring, which helps mid-size firms manage complex customer segments at scale. However, its extensive feature set often requires dedicated administrators and a larger implementation effort.

alternative

ChurnZero

: ChurnZero focuses on real-time customer engagement and automated playbooks to reduce churn. Its intuitive interface and quick setup let success teams act on health signals immediately without heavy technical overhead. This makes it a strong choice for mid-size firms that want proactive workflows without the complexity of a full enterprise suite.

alternative

Planhat

: Planhat combines customer success with a flexible data model that adapts to your existing tools and processes. It emphasizes revenue-based health scores and clear analytics, helping teams tie success activities directly to business outcomes. For mid-size SaaS firms that need a customizable, modern platform that scales with their tech stack, Planhat is a solid alternative.

How they compare

Gainsight offers the most robust automation and integration capabilities but demands more time and resources to deploy. ChurnZero provides faster time to value with an emphasis on real-time churn prevention and ease of use. Planhat strikes a balance between customization and simplicity, with a strong focus on revenue alignment. All three scale well for mid-size firms, but your choice should depend on your team size, technical resources, and whether your priority is deep automation, quick rollout, or flexible data modeling.

Frequently asked questions

Is Totango easier to use than these alternatives for a small success team?
Yes, Totango is often simpler to get started with for very small teams. But as you add more customers and workflows, tools like ChurnZero or Planhat can offer better automation without requiring a large admin team.
Which alternative is best for integrating with a CRM like Salesforce or HubSpot?
Gainsight has the deepest native integrations with major CRMs and can handle complex data syncs. Planhat also connects well with common CRMs and offers flexible mapping. ChurnZero’s integrations are solid but more focused on real-time triggers than on full data synchronization.
