If your marketing team manages multiple social accounts, you know Hootsuite is a popular choice. But it may not fit every workflow or budget. You might need better collaboration features, a simpler interface, or more affordable pricing. That is why exploring Hootsuite alternatives can help you find a tool that matches your team’s specific needs.

alternative

Buffer

focuses on simplicity and clean scheduling. It allows you to plan posts across Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Instagram with a clear calendar view. Buffer also offers a basic analytics dashboard and a free tier for small teams.

Try Buffer →
alternative

Sprout Social

is a full fledged platform for social media management, listening, and analytics. It provides robust reporting, team collaboration tools, and a unified smart inbox for engaging with audiences. Its higher price point makes it best for larger marketing teams that need deep insights.

Try Sprout Social →
alternative

Later

specializes in visual content scheduling, especially for Instagram. You can drag and drop images onto a calendar, preview your feed, and use a built in media library. Later also supports Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and TikTok, with a free plan for up to one social set.

Try Later →
alternative

Planable

puts collaboration at the center. You can create, review, and approve posts with your team in real time, using comments and version history. It works with major platforms including Instagram, LinkedIn, and Facebook, and is ideal for teams that need a streamlined approval workflow.

Try Planable →
alternative

Zoho Social

offers affordable social media management with a strong focus on scheduling and monitoring. It includes a built in CRM integration, hashtag recommendations, and a unified dashboard for multiple accounts. Zoho Social is a good choice for budget conscious teams that already use other Zoho products.

Frequently asked questions

Why would my team switch from Hootsuite to another tool?
Many teams switch because Hootsuite can become expensive as you add users or accounts. Alternatives like Buffer or Later offer simpler interfaces at lower costs. Others choose Planable for better team collaboration or Sprout Social for deeper analytics.
Can I keep my existing scheduled posts when moving from Hootsuite?
Most tools let you export your Hootsuite data or manually import post content. However, scheduling dates and times often need to be re entered. Check each tool’s import options or use a CSV file to streamline the process.
