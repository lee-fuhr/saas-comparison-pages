If you are a Shopify brand using Postscript for SMS marketing, you might be looking for alternatives due to pricing shifts, feature gaps, or a desire for tighter integration with your existing stack. Postscript is strong for stand-alone SMS and MMS, but other platforms offer different pricing models (like flat-rate tiers), deeper email-SMS cross-channel features, or native Shopify checkout integration. This article covers five solid Postscript alternatives to help you compare what fits your brand.

alternative

Attentive

. Attentive is a high-end SMS platform with advanced segmentation, AI-driven creative, and a dedicated account management approach. It works well for larger brands that need sophisticated flows and personalization, but its pricing is typically higher and less transparent than Postscript.

alternative

Klaviyo SMS

. Klaviyo SMS is built directly into the Klaviyo email platform, making it a seamless choice for brands already using Klaviyo for email. You can unify email and SMS audiences, triggers, and reporting in one place, though SMS-specific features like two-way conversations are less robust than Postscript.

alternative

SMSBump

. SMSBump (now part of Yotpo) focuses on simplicity and affordability, with a pay-per-use pricing model that can be cheaper for smaller volumes. It offers basic automation and flows but lacks the advanced segmentation and personalization tools that Postscript provides.

alternative

Yotpo SMS

. Yotpo SMS combines SMS marketing with Yotpo’s loyalty, reviews, and visual UGC tools. This is ideal if you already use Yotpo for post-purchase engagement, but its SMS features alone may not match Postscript’s depth in strategic flows and A/B testing.

alternative

Emotive

. Emotive specializes in conversational SMS with an emphasis on two-way texting and direct-to-customer communication. It is a good fit for brands that prioritize personal chats over broad blast campaigns, though its automation and flow builder are less mature than Postscript’s.

How they compare

Postscript and these alternatives differ most in pricing structure (per-segment vs. per-message) and integration approach. Attentive and Klaviyo SMS excel at cross-channel orchestration, while SMSBump and Yotpo SMS offer lower entry costs or loyalty synergies. Emotive stands out for conversational marketing. No single tool is best for every brand, so match your priority (price, features, or ecosystem) to the alternative above.

Frequently asked questions

Is switching from Postscript to an alternative difficult for a mid-size Shopify brand?
It depends on the tool. Many platforms offer one-click Postscript migration or import via CSV. You will need to rebuild flows and segments manually, but most alternatives have template libraries to speed that up.
Which alternative is the most affordable for a brand sending under 5,000 SMS per month?
SMSBump typically offers the lowest per-message cost for low volumes because it charges based on usage rather than a flat monthly fee. Klaviyo SMS can also be cost-effective if you already pay for Klaviyo email.
