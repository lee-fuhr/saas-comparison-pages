Best EasyPost alternatives

EasyPost is a popular shipping API that helps fulfillment managers integrate multiple carriers into their order management systems. However, you might look for alternatives if EasyPost’s pricing structure, feature set, or carrier coverage doesn’t match your specific operational needs. Factors like volume discounts, ease of use, inventory management, or international shipping capabilities often drive the search for a better fit.

alternative

ShipBob

is a full-service fulfillment partner, not just a shipping software, so it handles storage, picking, packing, and shipping. It works best for merchants who want to outsource logistics entirely rather than manage their own warehouse and carrier contracts. ShipBob offers built-in rate negotiation and inventory distribution across multiple locations.

alternative

ShipStation

is a multi-carrier shipping platform designed for ecommerce businesses that process a high volume of orders from multiple sales channels. It provides batch printing, custom workflows, and deep integrations with marketplaces like Amazon and Shopify. Fulfillment managers often choose ShipStation for its user-friendly dashboard and detailed reporting.

alternative

EasyShip

focuses on cross-border shipping and offers competitive rates from international carriers like DHL, FedEx, and UPS. It simplifies customs documentation and duty calculations for fulfillment managers who ship frequently to overseas customers. EasyShip also provides a rate comparison table to help you pick the cheapest option for each destination.

alternative

Shippo

is a shipping API and web app that gives you discounted rates from USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL. It integrates with major ecommerce platforms and offers features like address validation and tracking updates. Shippo is a good choice if you want a lightweight, pay-as-you-go solution without long-term contracts.

alternative

Pirateship

is a simple, no-frills shipping platform that provides deeply discounted USPS rates, especially for Priority Mail and Cubic pricing. It works well for small to medium fulfillment operations that ship primarily domestically with USPS. PirateShip does not offer inventory management or multi-carrier support beyond USPS and a few others.

How they compare

For fulfillment managers evaluating these alternatives, consider your operational model. ShipBob is best for full outsourcing, while ShipStation shines in multi-channel order handling. EasyShip leads for international shipments, Shippo offers a flexible API and low commitment, and PirateShip delivers the lowest USPS rates for simple domestic needs. None of these tools are direct replacements for the API-centric approach of EasyPost; each serves a different balance of integration depth, carrier breadth, and hands-on shipping versus fulfillment outsourcing.
