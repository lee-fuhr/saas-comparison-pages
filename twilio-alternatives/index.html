Best Twilio alternatives

Twilio is a well known platform for embedding SMS, voice, and video into applications, but it is not the only option. Developers often look for a Twilio alternative to reduce costs, access better support, or find features that better fit their use case. Below we compare four strong alternatives: Vonage, MessageBird, Plivo, and Sinch.

alternative

Vonage

. Vonage offers a full set of communication APIs for SMS, voice, and video, with a particular strength in enterprise grade reliability and global carrier coverage. Its voice APIs include programmable IVR and call recording, while the video API uses WebRTC and supports up to 25 participants. Pricing is per message or per minute, and Vonage provides a dashboard for monitoring usage.

alternative

MessageBird

. MessageBird is known for its easy to use REST APIs and excellent deliverability for SMS, especially in Europe and Asia. It also provides voice calling and video (through a separate product called Bird), but its core focus is on messaging and two way conversations. Developers appreciate the clear documentation and ability to send messages to over 200 countries.

alternative

Plivo

. Plivo provides SMS and voice APIs at competitive rates, often cheaper than Twilio for high volume traffic. Its voice features include call tracking, conferencing, and SIP trunking, while SMS supports both toll free and short code numbers. Plivo also offers a video API, though it is less feature rich than Twilio’s video offering.

alternative

Sinch

. Sinch specializes in SMS and voice with a strong emphasis on global reach and reliable delivery, including support for rich communication services (RCS). Its voice APIs cover call connect, messaging, and verification, but video is not a major focus. Sinch is often chosen by developers who need high throughput SMS and can accept a simpler feature set.

How they compare

All four alternatives support SMS and voice APIs, but video capabilities vary. Vonage and Plivo have dedicated video APIs, MessageBird offers video through a separate product, and Sinch does not emphasize video. For pricing, Plivo and MessageBird are frequently cheaper than Twilio on SMS in specific regions, while Vonage and Sinch target enterprise clients with stable pricing. Developer experience is strong across all, with MessageBird’s documentation being particularly clean and Plivo’s being straightforward for high volume use. Choose Vonage for a full voice and video suite, MessageBird for messaging focused projects, Plivo for budget conscious SMS, and Sinch for reliability and global SMS.

Frequently asked questions

Which Twilio alternative is best for video calls?
Vonage is the strongest alternative for video because its Video API includes WebRTC support, group calls up to 25 participants, and screen sharing. Plivo’s video offering is simpler but still usable for basic conferencing.
Can I switch from Twilio to another provider without rewriting all my code?
Not completely. Each provider has its own API structure, but most share similar concepts like sending a message via a REST POST request. You will need to update your HTTP endpoints, authentication, and response parsing. Many libraries exist for each provider to speed up the migration.
