ClickFunnels is a popular tool for building sales funnels, but it might not fit every online course creator or coach. You may find its pricing high, its all in one approach limiting in certain areas, or its email marketing less robust than you need. Fortunately, several solid alternatives offer similar funnel building capabilities with features tailored to course creation and client management. This article covers five strong options: Kartra, Kajabi, GoHighLevel, Systeme.io, and Builderall.

alternative

Kartra

is an all in one marketing platform that includes funnel building, email automation, membership sites, and help desk support. It works well for coaches who want to sell courses and manage client interactions from one dashboard, though its learning curve can be steeper than ClickFunnels.

alternative

Kajabi

focuses specifically on online course creators, offering a complete ecosystem to build courses, host a website, create funnels, and manage email marketing. Its user interface is clean and intuitive, making it a strong choice if your primary goal is course delivery rather than complex lead generation.

alternative

GoHighLevel

is built for agencies and service providers, but it works great for solo coaches who need CRM, funnel building, appointment scheduling, and text messaging all in one. Its unlimited contact pricing makes it a cost effective alternative for high volume funnels.

Try GoHighLevel →
alternative

Systeme.io

is a budget friendly all in one platform that covers funnels, email automation, course hosting, and affiliate management. It offers a generous free plan and low tier paid plans, making it ideal for creators just starting out who want to test funnel marketing without a high upfront cost.

alternative

Builderall

provides a vast set of tools including a visual funnel builder, website creator, email marketing, and even a webinar platform. It is a good option for tech savvy creators who want many integrated features, though the interface can feel cluttered compared to ClickFunnels.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the most affordable replacement for ClickFunnels?
Systeme.io offers the lowest cost option with a free plan and paid plans starting around $27 per month. You still get funnel building, email marketing, and course hosting.
Can I migrate my existing ClickFunnels funnels to these platforms?
Most platforms allow you to recreate funnels manually. Some like Kajabi and Kartra offer import tools for landing pages, but full automation migrations usually require rebuilding. Check each tool’s support documentation for specific steps.
