As a compliance director at a large healthcare organization, you know that employee screening is not just about speed it is about accuracy, FCRA compliance, and managing risk across thousands of hires. While HireRight is a well known provider, it may not always fit your specific needs for cost, integration, or customer support. Evaluating alternatives like Sterling, Checkr, and Accurate Background can help you find a solution that meets your regulatory obligations while streamlining your hiring workflow.

alternative

Sterling

. Sterling is a strong alternative for large healthcare organizations because it offers deep industry expertise in healthcare credentialing and background checks. Their platform integrates with major applicant tracking systems and provides dedicated compliance support to help you navigate FCRA requirements. Sterling also offers continuous monitoring and a broad network of court and database sources tailored to healthcare roles.

alternative

Checkr

. Checkr uses a modern, API first platform that can speed up screening turnaround times without sacrificing compliance. Their system is designed to flag potential issues early and provides clear, FCRA compliant dispute processes for candidates. For healthcare employers, Checkr offers specific packages that include professional license verifications and OIG/SAM exclusion checks.

alternative

Accurate Background

. Accurate Background focuses on customizable screening tools that scale to large workforces, with a strong emphasis on FCRA compliance and audit readiness. They offer healthcare specific services such as immunization record checks and clinical reference verifications. Their client success team works closely with compliance directors to ensure that all adverse action workflows meet regulatory standards.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer FCRA compliant screening, healthcare specific checks, and integration capabilities. Sterling provides the deepest healthcare credentialing expertise, Checkr leads in technology speed and developer friendliness, and Accurate Background offers maximum customization and audit support. Your choice should depend on whether your priority is industry specialization, integration ease, or flexible compliance workflows.
