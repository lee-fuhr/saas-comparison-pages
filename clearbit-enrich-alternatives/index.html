Best Clearbit Enrich alternatives

Clearbit Enrich is a popular real-time API for appending firmographic and contact data to leads, which helps SaaS companies power lead scoring and personalization. However, some teams look for alternatives due to pricing changes, data gaps in certain regions, compliance concerns, or the need for different data types (such as technographic or intent signals). The right alternative should deliver accurate, real-time enrichment that integrates smoothly with your CRM or marketing automation platform.

alternative

ZoomInfo Enrich

. ZoomInfo Enrich provides a massive B2B database with detailed company and contact profiles. Its API delivers real-time data but may have slower response times and a higher price point compared to some competitors. It works best for sales-led organizations that need deep firmographic and direct dial data for lead scoring.

alternative

Cognism

. Cognism focuses on GDPR-compliant data enrichment with strong coverage in Europe and the United Kingdom. Their real-time API supplies verified phone numbers and email addresses, making it suitable for lead scoring and outreach in highly regulated markets. It is a solid choice for SaaS companies with international sales teams.

alternative

BuiltWith

. BuiltWith is primarily a technology lookup tool that reveals the tech stack of any website. Its enrichment API provides technographic data (e.g., which analytics, marketing, or infrastructure tools a company uses) in real time for lead scoring. It is not ideal for contact-level data but excellent for identifying account fit based on technology adoption.

alternative

Leadspace

. Leadspace offers AI-driven data enrichment and audience analytics through its real-time API. It combines multiple data sources and predictive models to score and personalize leads. This tool is best for SaaS companies that need advanced segmentation, intent data, and a dynamic enrichment layer for account-based marketing.

How they compare

When choosing among these Clearbit Enrich alternatives, consider your primary data need. ZoomInfo Enrich excels in breadth of firmographic and contact data but comes at a higher cost. Cognism is the top pick for GDPR compliance and European coverage. BuiltWith is unmatched for technographic enrichment but lacks contact data. Leadspace delivers the most sophisticated AI-powered scoring and segmentation, though its pricing may be less transparent. All four offer real-time APIs, but response speeds and data freshness vary; test each against your typical lead volume and enrichment latency requirements.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for real-time lead scoring and personalization in global markets?
Cognism is a strong choice if you need GDPR-compliant data for European and UK leads. For broader global coverage with deep firmographic data, ZoomInfo Enrich is reliable, but consider the cost and integration complexity.
Do these alternatives integrate with common CRM and marketing automation tools like Salesforce or HubSpot?
Yes, all four tools offer native integrations or API-based connections to major systems. ZoomInfo Enrich and Cognism have pre-built connectors, while BuiltWith and Leadspace typically require custom API work for CRM sync. Check each platform’s integration marketplace for your specific tech stack before committing.
