Best Yodeck alternatives

If you run a retail location and use Yodeck for digital signage, you already know how well it works with Raspberry Pi hardware. But maybe you are looking for a different pricing model, more templates, or simpler setup. Several other platforms also support Raspberry Pi, and many offer free or low-cost plans that fit a small retail budget. Below we compare four strong Yodeck alternatives: ScreenCloud, Rise Vision, OptiSigns, and NoviSign.

alternative

ScreenCloud

. ScreenCloud offers a cloud based signage solution that runs on Raspberry Pi, and it provides a free player app for a single screen. Its interface is designed for non technical staff, so you can update menus or promotions quickly from any device. ScreenCloud also includes a large template library and direct integrations with tools like Canva and Google Slides.

alternative

Rise Vision

. Rise Vision is a web based platform that gives you a free plan for up to one screen, making it very affordable for a single retail display. It works with Raspberry Pi through its own OS image, and you can manage content from any browser. The free tier includes a good selection of templates and the ability to schedule content, though advanced analytics require a paid upgrade.

alternative

OptiSigns

. OptiSigns supports Raspberry Pi devices and focuses on ease of use with a drag and drop editor. Its pricing is per screen per month with no long term contract, and it includes built in integrations for social media, weather, and news feeds. For retail locations, OptiSigns also offers coupon and QR code widgets to drive in store engagement.

alternative

NoviSign

. NoviSign provides a cloud based signage platform that works on Raspberry Pi and offers a free trial with no credit card required. Its editor is based on a slide show approach, which can feel familiar if you already use presentation software. NoviSign also includes a library of retail specific templates for sales, promotions, and event announcements.

How they compare

All four alternatives support Raspberry Pi and are designed for small retail use. ScreenCloud and Rise Vision both offer a completely free plan for a single screen, which is ideal if you only need one display. OptiSigns keeps pricing flat per screen without contracts, while NoviSign gives you a free trial to test features. For template quality and ease of use, ScreenCloud and Rise Vision are strong choices. If you need more advanced widgets like social media feeds or coupon distribution, OptiSigns and NoviSign have those built in. No platform locks you into a long term commitment, so you can switch if your needs change.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the cheapest for a single screen retail display?
Both ScreenCloud (with its free player app) and Rise Vision (with its free plan) allow you to run one screen at no cost. You only pay if you need additional screens or premium features like advanced analytics.
Do these alternatives still work on older Raspberry Pi models like the Pi 3?
Yes, all four platforms support Raspberry Pi 3 and newer models. Check each provider’s hardware requirements page for the specific OS image or app they recommend, but most will run smoothly on a Pi 3B+ or Pi 4.
