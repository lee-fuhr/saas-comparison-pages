Best SMSBump alternatives

If you run an online store and rely on SMSBump for checkout recovery and cart abandonment flows, you might be exploring other options. SMSBump is a solid tool, but your needs may have changed, or you might want better segmentation, deliverability, or pricing. Below we compare seven SMSBump alternatives designed for ecommerce retailers who need to win back lost sales.

alternative

Attentive

. Attentive is a top choice for brands that want deep personalization and advanced segmentation in their SMS campaigns. It offers prebuilt cart abandonment flows and integrates directly with Shopify and Magento, making setup straightforward. Its focus on compliance and list growth through popups can help you capture more shoppers at the moment they browse.

alternative

Postscript

. Postscript is built specifically for Shopify stores and excels at two way messaging and automated flows for abandoned carts. It includes a native review and coupon feature that lets you send a follow up message after a purchase. Its pricing is based on how many messages you send, which can keep costs low for smaller stores.

alternative

Klaviyo SMS

. Klaviyo SMS is part of the Klaviyo marketing platform, so you can use your existing email segments and triggers to power SMS flows. It synchs seamlessly with your customer data, allowing you to send personalized cart recovery texts based on browsing behavior. The flat rate per message (plus a small monthly fee) makes it easy to predict your costs.

alternative

Twilio

. Twilio is a developer friendly platform that gives you full control over your SMS infrastructure. You can build custom cart abandonment flows using their API, but you will need technical resources to set up and maintain the system. It is best for large retailers that already have an in house development team and want the lowest per message cost.

alternative

MessageBird

. MessageBird offers a global reach with strong deliverability, making it a good alternative if you sell to customers outside the United States. It provides a drag and drop flow builder for cart recovery, as well as two way messaging and A/B testing. Their integration with Shopify is less direct than some competitors, so you might need a bit of setup.

alternative

Zipwhip

. Zipwhip focuses on business texting with a local phone number, so your cart recovery texts appear from a real 10 digit number rather than a short code. This can improve trust and reply rates. It works well for retailers that want a simple, no code approach and already use an existing CRM or help desk.

alternative

TextMagic

. TextMagic is a budget friendly option for retailers that need basic cart recovery messages without complex automation. It supports bulk SMS and scheduling, and you can import customer phone numbers from a CSV. Its lack of ecommerce specific integrations means you will have to manually trigger flows or use third party middleware.
