OneTrust has long been a leading platform for privacy management, but enterprises now face new challenges in security and AI governance. As regulations evolve and AI adoption accelerates, many organizations are evaluating alternatives that offer tighter integration between privacy, security, and AI risk controls. This article reviews four strong options Vanta, Drata, SecureFrame, and TrustArc and explains how they compare to OneTrust for enterprise needs.

alternative

Vanta

. Vanta automates security and compliance for frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, and HIPAA, and it includes AI governance modules to monitor model risks. It is a strong choice for enterprises that want a unified view of security posture alongside privacy controls, though its privacy-specific features are less mature than OneTrust’s.

alternative

Drata

. Drata focuses on continuous compliance monitoring and evidence collection, with integrations for over 100 SaaS tools. It offers automated control testing and a clear dashboard for security teams, but enterprises needing deep privacy impact assessments or cookie consent management may find Drata’s privacy capabilities limited compared to OneTrust.

alternative

SecureFrame

. SecureFrame provides a modular compliance platform that supports multiple frameworks (SOC 2, PCI, FedRAMP, GDPR) and includes vendor risk management. It excels in flexibility and customization for complex enterprise environments, but its AI governance features are still emerging and may require additional tools for full coverage.

alternative

TrustArc

. TrustArc is the closest direct competitor to OneTrust, with mature privacy compliance, data mapping, and consent management. It also offers an AI governance module that aligns with emerging regulations like the EU AI Act. TrustArc is ideal for enterprises that prioritize privacy first, but its security monitoring capabilities are less integrated than those of Vanta or Drata.

How they compare

For enterprises that need a broad privacy and consent management suite, TrustArc is the most direct OneTrust alternative. Vanta and Drata are better suited for security-first teams that also want basic privacy support. SecureFrame offers the most customizable framework coverage for complex regulatory environments. All four platforms lack the full breadth of OneTrust’s privacy impact assessments and cookie consent tools, but they compensate with stronger automation in security compliance and, in TrustArc’s case, a dedicated AI governance module that OneTrust is still building out.
