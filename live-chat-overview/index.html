As a customer support manager, you know that the right live chat tool can turn casual website visitors into loyal customers. But with so many options on the market, picking the best one for your team can be overwhelming. This guide walks you through the most popular live chat software alternatives, so you can compare features, pricing, and ease of use at a glance.

alternative

Olark

. Olark focuses on simplicity and a human-first approach. It offers a clean chat widget, real-time visitor monitoring, and easy integrations with CRMs like HubSpot. Its straightforward pricing and strong reporting make it a good fit for small to midsize teams that want a no-fuss setup.

alternative

Chaport

. Chaport combines a free forever plan with powerful automation features like chatbots and triggers. It supports multiple channels, including email and Facebook Messenger, from one dashboard. The tool is especially popular among startups and growing companies that need affordable scalability.

alternative

JivoChat

. JivoChat is built for omnichannel support, letting agents handle chats, phone calls, and emails in a single interface. It offers a robust mobile app and a built-in knowledge base for self-service. Teams with high call volumes will appreciate its softphone integration.

alternative

Pure Chat

. Pure Chat provides a free plan that includes unlimited chats and agents, with optional paid upgrades for extras like reports and offline forms. Its widget is highly customizable and integrates with major CRMs. It is a solid choice for budget-conscious teams that need a basic but reliable chat solution.

alternative

Smartsupp

. Smartsupp stands out with its free live chat and built-in chatbot that handles common questions automatically. It records visitor sessions to help you understand user behavior, and it supports multiple languages. Ecommerce stores often prefer Smartsupp for its simple design and video replay features.

alternative

LiveChat

. LiveChat is a mature, feature-rich platform with a polished agent interface, extensive app marketplace, and real-time visitor tracking. It excels in customization and offers powerful reporting and ticketing features. Larger support teams with complex workflows typically choose LiveChat for its reliability and depth.

alternative

Intercom

. Intercom goes beyond live chat to offer a full customer communications platform with chatbots, email campaigns, in-app messages, and a help center. It uses AI to route conversations and suggest replies. Businesses that want a unified customer engagement hub rather than just a chat widget should consider Intercom.

Frequently asked questions

Which live chat alternative is the easiest to set up for a non-technical team?
Olark and Pure Chat are often the simplest to configure. Both require minimal coding and offer drag and drop widget customization. Olark’s onboarding is especially fast for teams that just want to add a chat button and start talking to visitors.
Do any of these alternatives offer a free plan that includes chatbot features?
Yes. Chaport and Smartsupp both provide free plans that include basic chatbot functionality. Chaport’s free version allows you to create simple automated replies, while Smartsupp’s free chatbot can answer common questions without human intervention.
