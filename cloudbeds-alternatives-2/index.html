Best Cloudbeds alternatives

If you manage a chain of hotels, a central reservation system with strong OTA connectivity is essential to streamline bookings and maximize direct channels. Cloudbeds is a popular choice, but it is not the only option. Depending on your property size, budget, and feature needs, alternatives like Mews, Hotelogix, and RMS Cloud may offer a better fit. This review compares these tools from the perspective of hotel chains, focusing on their central reservation capabilities and integration with online travel agencies.

alternative

Mews

. Mews is a cloud-based property management system designed for modern, tech-forward hotel chains. It offers a centralized dashboard for managing multiple properties, direct OTA connectivity through two-way integration with major OTAs like Booking.com and Expedia, and a flexible open API for custom builds. Its channel manager is built in, so you do not need a separate third-party tool.

alternative

Hotelogix

. Hotelogix is a multi-property PMS aimed at mid-size chains and independent groups. It provides a central reservation module that lets you manage inventory, rates, and bookings across all properties from one place. Its built-in channel manager connects to over 350 OTAs and supports real-time availability updates, making it a solid choice for chains that rely heavily on OTA distribution.

alternative

RMS Cloud

. RMS Cloud is a comprehensive hospitality management system designed for large resorts and multi-property chains. It offers a powerful central reservation engine with advanced rate management and inventory control across hotels, villas, and other units. Its channel manager integrates with major OTAs and global distribution systems, and the system includes robust reporting tools for chain-level analytics.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer strong central reservation system capabilities and OTA connectivity, but they differ in focus. Mews excels with a modern user interface and open API for custom integrations, ideal for tech-savvy chains. Hotelogix provides a more affordable, straightforward solution with broad OTA coverage for mid-tier chains. RMS Cloud is best for large, complex operations with high-volume inventory and detailed reporting needs. Cloudbeds remains a strong competitor, especially for smaller chains or those wanting an all-in-one platform, but the alternatives may offer deeper chain-specific features or more flexible pricing.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Mews, Hotelogix, or RMS Cloud without a separate channel manager for OTA connectivity?
Yes, each of these systems includes a built-in channel manager that connects directly to major OTAs like Booking.com, Expedia, and others. You do not need to buy an additional third-party channel manager to manage OTA bookings across your chain.
Which alternative is best for a chain with more than 10 properties?
RMS Cloud is often a strong fit for larger chains due to its advanced multi-property central reservation engine, detailed reporting, and ability to handle complex rate and inventory rules. Mews also works well for scaling chains that want a modern, API-first platform, while Hotelogix is a good choice for budget-conscious chains with fewer than 20 properties.
