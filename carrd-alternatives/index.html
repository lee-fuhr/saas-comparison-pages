If you are a freelancer looking for a simple one-page site, Carrd is a popular choice. It is affordable and easy to use. But you might need more features, better design options, or room to grow. That is why exploring Carrd alternatives makes sense. Below we compare three solid options: Webflow, Wix, and Squarespace. Each has strengths for different needs.

alternative

Webflow

. Webflow gives you full control over design without writing code. It works well if you want a custom one-page site with animations and responsive layouts. The learning curve is steeper than Carrd, but the flexibility is unmatched for freelancers who want a unique brand.

alternative

Wix

. Wix offers a drag-and-drop editor that is very beginner friendly. You can start with a one-page template and add sections like portfolio, contact, or testimonials easily. It includes built-in SEO and hosting, but free plans show Wix ads.

alternative

Squarespace

. Squarespace is known for its beautiful, professional templates. It is ideal for freelancers who want a polished one-page site quickly. The editor is less flexible than Webflow but more structured than Wix, and it handles blogging and ecommerce well if you ever need to expand.

How they compare

Carrd is the simplest, cheapest, and fastest for a single page. Webflow offers the most design freedom but takes time to learn. Wix is easy to use and highly customizable with many apps. Squarespace provides the best out-of-the-box design quality. Your choice depends on budget, design skill, and whether you might need more pages later.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use these alternatives for free?
Wix has a free plan with ads and a Wix subdomain. Squarespace and Webflow offer free trials but no permanent free plan. Carrd is free for simple sites but has paid pro plans.
Which alternative is best for a freelance portfolio site?
Squarespace is a great fit because of its polished templates. Webflow works if you want a unique custom design. Wix is also good if you prefer a simple drag-and-drop builder with many portfolio layouts.
