Best Housecall Pro alternatives

Housecall Pro is a popular field service management tool that helps small businesses schedule jobs, send invoices, and accept payments on the go. Its mobile-first design makes it a good fit for many trades, but it may not suit every budget or workflow. If you are looking for a different feature set, pricing, or user experience, several solid alternatives can meet your needs. Below we cover four of the most common options, each with its own strengths for small service businesses that rely on mobile software.

alternative

ServiceTitan

. This platform is built for larger field service companies with complex needs, but it also offers robust mobile features for scheduling, dispatching, and customer management. It can be expensive for a single operator or very small team, and the learning curve is steeper than most alternatives. If you plan to scale quickly and need advanced reporting and inventory tracking, it is worth a look.

alternative

Jobber

. Jobber is designed with small service businesses in mind, offering a clean mobile app for managing jobs, quotes, and payments on the road. It includes features like automated follow-ups, online booking, and client communication, all at a price point that works for teams of one to ten. Many users find it easier to set up and use than Housecall Pro.

alternative

FieldEdge

. FieldEdge provides a comprehensive mobile solution for scheduling, dispatching, and invoicing, with a focus on HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors. Its mobile app lets technicians view job details, capture signatures, and process credit cards in the field. While it is now part of the ServiceTitan family, it remains a standalone option with a simpler interface than its parent product.

alternative

ServiceFusion

. This software is a cloud-based, mobile-first tool that covers scheduling, dispatching, estimates, and invoicing for small to midsize service companies. It offers a straightforward pricing model with no long contracts, and its app works well for both office staff and field workers. ServiceFusion is particularly good for businesses that want a no-fuss system without a lot of extra features they do not need.

How they compare

Housecall Pro is known for its easy mobile app and flat monthly pricing, but it can lack advanced features like inventory management or custom reporting. ServiceTitan offers the most power and analytics but at a higher cost and complexity, making it better for growing companies with multiple crews. Jobber balances ease of use, mobile functionality, and price, making it a strong alternative for solo operators or small teams. FieldEdge provides industry-specific tools for trades like HVAC and plumbing, with a mobile app that is more robust than some competitors. ServiceFusion offers a simple, affordable mobile solution ideal for very small businesses that want to avoid extra features and high prices.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free alternative to Housecall Pro?
Most full-featured field service tools do not offer a free plan, but some have free trials or limited free tiers. Jobber and ServiceFusion each offer a free trial period, and you can test their mobile apps before committing to a paid plan.
Which alternative is best for a one-person business?
Jobber is often the top choice for solo operators because it is affordable, easy to learn, and includes a strong mobile app for scheduling and payments. ServiceFusion is another good option if you want a simple, low-cost setup without extra bells and whistles.
