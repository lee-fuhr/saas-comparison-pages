Thinkific is a popular platform for creating and selling online courses, but it may not suit every educator or coach. You might need different pricing, more marketing features, or simpler membership management. Exploring alternatives helps you find the best tool for your specific goals. Below we compare Thinkific with five other course creation platforms.

alternative

Teachable

offers a similar all-in-one course building experience with a focus on ease of use. It has a straightforward pricing structure and strong student management features. Compared to Thinkific, Teachable may have a simpler interface but fewer built-in marketing automation options.

Try Teachable →
alternative

Kajabi

is a comprehensive platform that combines course creation with email marketing, sales funnels, and website building. It is more expensive than Thinkific but provides more advanced marketing tools for growing your business. Kajabi is ideal for coaches who want a full sales and marketing suite.

Try Kajabi →
alternative

Podia

is a simpler, more affordable alternative that lets you sell courses, memberships, and digital downloads in one place. It has a clean interface and no transaction fees on its higher plans. Compared to Thinkific, Podia has fewer customization options but is easier to set up for beginners.

Try Podia →
alternative

LearnWorlds

stands out with its interactive video features and built-in community tools. It allows you to create a more engaging learning experience with features like note-taking and quizzes. Thinkific offers similar core features, but LearnWorlds provides more advanced course interactivity and student analytics.

alternative

MemberSpace

is different because it focuses on adding a membership area to an existing website rather than being a full course platform. It works with WordPress, Squarespace, and other site builders to restrict content to paying members. If you already have a website and only need membership management, MemberSpace is a lighter alternative to Thinkific.

How they compare

When choosing between Thinkific and these alternatives, consider your priorities. Thinkific offers a strong balance of features, customization, and pricing for most course creators. Teachable is a close competitor with a simpler workflow. Kajabi is best for those who want built-in marketing and sales funnels. Podia is a budget-friendly option with no frills. LearnWorlds excels at interactive course design. MemberSpace is ideal for adding memberships to an existing site. Each platform has unique strengths, so think about your specific needs for course creation, marketing, and student engagement.

Frequently asked questions

Which Thinkific alternative is the cheapest?
Podia is generally the most affordable alternative with its low monthly pricing and no transaction fees on the higher plan. However, its features are more basic compared to Thinkific.
Can I migrate my courses from Thinkific to another platform?
Yes, most platforms like Teachable, Kajabi, and LearnWorlds offer import tools or support to help you move your course content. You will need to export your course files and data from Thinkific and then upload them to the new platform. Check each tool’s documentation for specific migration steps.
