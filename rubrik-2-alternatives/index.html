Best Rubrik alternatives

Converged data protection and management means unifying backup, recovery, disaster recovery, and data governance into one platform. Rubrik is a leader in this space, offering a cloud-native, API-first approach that simplifies operations for your data across on-premises, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments. For companies looking to reduce complexity and accelerate recovery, Rubrik’s zero-trust architecture and automated policy engine deliver consistent protection. However, you may also be evaluating other tools that excel in specific use cases. This article covers Rubrik and four key alternatives, Zerto, Veeam, Druva, and Acronis, to help you choose the right fit.

alternative

Zerto

specializes in continuous data protection and orchestrated disaster recovery for virtualized and cloud environments. Its near-synchronous replication gives you recovery point objectives measured in seconds, making it a strong option for mission-critical applications. Zerto is less suited for traditional backup and archiving, but it shines where minimal data loss is non-negotiable.

alternative

Veeam

is a well-established backup and recovery solution with deep support for VMware, Hyper-V, and cloud workloads. It offers flexible backup scheduling, instant VM recovery, and broad third-party storage integration. While Veeam is highly capable, it often requires additional components and manual configuration to achieve the same level of converged management that Rubrik delivers out of the box.

alternative

Druva

provides a fully SaaS-based backup and data management platform with no on-premises infrastructure. It excels in protecting endpoints, SaaS applications like Microsoft 365, and cloud-native workloads. Druva is ideal for companies that want to offload hardware maintenance, but it may not offer the same granular control or offline recovery capabilities as Rubrik.

alternative

Acronis

focuses on cyber protection, combining backup, anti-ransomware, and disaster recovery in a single suite. It is especially popular with small to medium businesses and managed service providers due to its easy setup and affordable licensing. Acronis lacks some of the advanced orchestration and scale that larger enterprises require from a converged data protection platform.

How they compare

Rubrik offers the most complete converged experience with built-in global search, automated policy orchestration, and instant recovery for any workload. Zerto is unmatched for low-RPO disaster recovery. Veeam provides deep granularity and a mature ecosystem. Druva gives you a cloud-only, zero-infrastructure model. Acronis bundles cybersecurity features for smaller deployments. Your choice depends on your primary need, full convergence, disaster recovery speed, granularity, cloud simplicity, or integrated security.
