As a general manager at a casual dining chain, you know that tip reporting is a critical part of payroll and compliance. 7shifts is a popular scheduling tool, but you might be exploring alternatives if you need more specialized tip reporting features, better integration with your POS, or a different pricing structure. This article compares three strong alternatives: Deputy, When I Work, and Homebase. Each offers employee scheduling with built in tip management, helping you streamline operations and stay compliant.

alternative

Deputy

focuses on accurate tip pooling and allocation based on hours worked or role. Its scheduling integrates with time clocks to capture exact shift times, making tip reporting straightforward for your staff. Deputy also offers real time labor cost tracking to help you manage budgets.

alternative

When I Work

provides simple tip tracking where employees can record tips received each shift, and managers can review totals. Its scheduling views help you assign shifts and ensure tip reporting is linked to each employee’s work record. The tool is known for its easy to use mobile app for staff.

alternative

Homebase

includes tip reporting as part of its all in one platform for scheduling, time tracking, and payroll. You can set up tip pooling rules by percentage or by hours, and the system calculates tips automatically. Homebase also offers free scheduling for small teams, making it a cost effective option.

How they compare

All three tools offer employee scheduling and tip reporting, but they differ in focus. Deputy excels in complex tip pooling and labor cost integration, ideal for chains with multiple roles. When I Work is simpler and more budget friendly, good for smaller operations or those needing a straightforward tip tracking interface. Homebase provides the widest feature set including free tier and robust tip calculation, but may require more setup for advanced pooling rules. Consider your chain’s size, tip complexity, and budget when choosing.

Frequently asked questions

What should I look for in a 7shifts alternative for tip reporting?
Look for tools that automatically calculate tips based on hours worked or role, allow you to set tip pooling rules, and integrate with your POS or time clock. Also check that the tool supports tip allocation compliance with local labor laws.
Can I migrate my scheduling data from 7shifts to another tool?
Most alternatives offer data import from CSV files. You can export your employee list and schedules from 7shifts and upload them to Deputy, When I Work, or Homebase. Check with each provider for specific import instructions.
