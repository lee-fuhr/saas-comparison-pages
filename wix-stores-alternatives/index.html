You love having a website that looks how you imagine it, and you need a built‑in store to sell your work. Wix Stores is a popular choice for creatives because of its drag‑and‑drop ease. But maybe you are looking for other options that still give you creative freedom while making selling simple. Here are three strong alternatives that keep the visual builder experience and add their own strengths for running an online shop.

alternative

Shopify

. Shopify is built primarily for ecommerce, but its drag‑and‑drop theme editor (Online Store 2.0) lets you customize your site without code. You can add a store to any page, manage inventory easily, and use hundreds of apps to extend functionality. Many creatives choose Shopify when they want a robust checkout experience and powerful shipping tools, though the builder is less flexible than Wix for full‑page design.

alternative

Squarespace Commerce

. Squarespace offers beautiful, designer‑made templates and a true drag‑and‑drop interface that is perfect for creatives. Its built‑in store features are seamless, with options for digital downloads, subscriptions, and physical products. You get a polished, typography‑focused site that looks great out of the box, but customization beyond the provided sections can be more limited than with Wix.

Try Squarespace Commerce →
alternative

BigCommerce

. BigCommerce combines a visual, page‑builder style editor with a store that scales well as you grow. It does not charge transaction fees on most plans, which helps creatives keep more profit. The drag‑and‑drop tools are easy to learn, and you get strong, built‑in features for SEO and multi‑channel selling without needing extra apps.

Try BigCommerce →

How they compare

All three alternatives offer an easy drag‑and‑drop experience with a built‑in store, but they differ in focus. Shopify is the most powerful for selling and shipping, Squarespace gives you the most beautiful starting templates for a portfolio‑plus‑store, and BigCommerce provides the best value with no transaction fees and strong built‑in features. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize design flexibility, selling capabilities, or long‑term cost.

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch from Wix Stores to one of these alternatives without losing my design work?
Yes, but you will need to rebuild your site in the new platform. You can export your product data as a CSV file and import it into Shopify, Squarespace Commerce, or BigCommerce. Your layout and styling will not transfer, so plan time to recreate the look you want.
Which alternative is best for selling digital downloads like art files or printables?
Both Squarespace Commerce and Shopify handle digital downloads well. Squarespace has built‑in digital product delivery with no extra app. Shopify requires a free or paid app like Digital Downloads to deliver files automatically. BigCommerce also supports digital products but may need a third‑party app for automatic delivery.
