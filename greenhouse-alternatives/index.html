Greenhouse is a powerful applicant tracking system (ATS) that many HR teams rely on. But it may not fit every budget, and some teams want a simpler tool. If you are looking for cost savings or an easier setup, there are several greenhouse alternatives to consider. Below you will find options that balance features with affordability and ease of use.

alternative

Lever

. Lever offers a user friendly interface with a focus on collaboration and candidate relationship management. It can be a good fit for teams that want a modern tool without the complexity of Greenhouse’s advanced workflows.

alternative

Workday

. Workday is a comprehensive HCM platform that includes recruiting, making it ideal for large enterprises already using Workday for HR. It is typically more expensive and complex, so it may not be the best choice for cost conscious teams.

alternative

BambooHR

. BambooHR provides a simple, affordable ATS that works well for small to midsize businesses. Its straightforward design makes it easy to use without extensive training.

alternative

Teamtailor

. Teamtailor combines an ATS with a branded career page builder, helping companies attract candidates with a modern employer brand. It is known for a simple setup and a lower price point than Greenhouse.

alternative

Ashby

. Ashby is a newer ATS that offers robust analytics and automation at a competitive price. It is designed for teams that want data driven hiring without the high cost of enterprise tools.

Try Ashby →

How they compare

The main differences among these alternatives come down to cost, simplicity, and feature depth. Lever and Ashby strike a balance between advanced features and moderate pricing. BambooHR and Teamtailor prioritize simplicity and lower costs, making them ideal for smaller teams. Workday is the most comprehensive option but is best suited for large organizations that already use its wider HR system. No single tool is right for everyone, so focus on your team’s size, budget, and need for simplicity.
