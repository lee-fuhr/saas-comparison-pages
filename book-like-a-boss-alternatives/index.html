If you have been using Book Like A Boss for scheduling client calls, you might be wondering if there is a better fit for your growing business. Entrepreneurs and freelancers often outgrow a tool or need different features like advanced team scheduling, open-source flexibility, or lower pricing. This guide covers four solid alternatives: Calendly, Cal.com, Acuity Scheduling, and Appointy. Each one offers unique strengths, so you can pick the option that matches your workflow and budget.

alternative

Calendly

. Calendly is the most popular scheduling tool and works well for solo entrepreneurs and small teams. It integrates with hundreds of apps, handles time zone differences automatically, and offers a free plan that covers basic one-on-one meetings. Paid plans add group events, round-robin routing, and custom branding.

alternative

Cal.com

. Cal.com is an open-source alternative that gives you full control over your data and customization. You can self-host it or use their cloud version, and it offers features like video conferencing links, workflow automation, and team scheduling. It is a strong choice if you value privacy, transparency, or want to avoid vendor lock-in.

alternative

Acuity Scheduling

. Acuity Scheduling is a feature-rich tool built for service-based businesses like coaches, consultants, and wellness practitioners. It includes intake forms, payment collection, client management, and appointment reminders. The interface is more complex than Calendly, but the customization options make it a powerful choice for businesses that need detailed client intake.

alternative

Appointy

. Appointy is a budget-friendly scheduler that works for freelancers and larger teams alike. It offers appointment booking, waitlist management, staff scheduling, and promotion codes. The free plan includes unlimited appointments and basic features, making it a good starting point if you are just beginning to manage bookings.

How they compare

For simplicity and integrations, Calendly is the easiest to set up and use. For open-source freedom and data control, Cal.com is the clear winner. For in-depth client management and payment processing, Acuity Scheduling offers the most features. For a low-cost or free solution with solid functionality, Appointy is a reliable choice. Your decision should depend on whether you need basic scheduling or a full client management system.
