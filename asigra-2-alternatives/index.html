If you are a service provider delivering backup as a service (BaaS), you need a platform that handles multiple clients from a single console without sacrificing performance or security. Asigra backup solution is built specifically for multi-tenant environments. It gives you agentless backup for physical and virtual workloads, a single pane of glass for all tenants, and flexible licensing that scales with your business. You can offer different service tiers to each client while keeping their data isolated and encrypted.

alternative

Zerto

is primarily a disaster recovery platform with continuous replication. Its multi-tenancy is strong for high availability scenarios, but it is less suited for long term backup retention or archiving. Service providers often use it alongside a separate backup tool.

alternative

Veeam

is a widely used backup solution that offers multi-tenant management through its service provider console. It works well for VMware and Hyper-V environments but may need additional licensing for cloud or SaaS workloads. Setup and maintenance can be complex when handling many different tenant configurations.

alternative

Druva

is a cloud native backup service that eliminates on premises infrastructure. Its multi-tenant architecture is straightforward for endpoint and SaaS data protection, but it relies on a single public cloud backend. Service providers have less control over where data is stored compared to on premises or hybrid options.

alternative

Acronis

offers backup with integrated cybersecurity features like anti malware and vulnerability assessments. Its multi-tenant portal works for small to midsize clients, but the feature set can be overwhelming for customers that only need simple backup. Licensing is per workload, which may lead to higher costs as you scale.

How they compare

Asigra stands out for its pure multi-tenant architecture and agentless approach – you do not need to install software on every server or endpoint. Zerto is better for continuous replication but not for long term archiving. Veeam gives you strong hypervisor support but requires more manual tenant segmentation. Druva is easy to deploy for cloud only backups but limits storage control. Acronis adds security features but can become expensive per workload. For a service provider that wants one platform for many clients with flexible retention and recovery options, Asigra offers the most straightforward multi-tenant experience.

Frequently asked questions

Does Asigra support billing per tenant or per gigabyte?
Asigra offers a consumption based licensing model, so you pay for the amount of data you protect per month. This makes it easy to bill each tenant based on their actual storage usage and service tier.
Can I recover a single file for a tenant without restoring the whole server?
Yes. Asigra uses a granular recovery feature that lets you restore individual files, folders, or application items without having to recover the entire virtual machine or physical system. This speeds up support requests for your clients.
