Best Pushwoosh alternatives

Pushwoosh is a solid push notification platform, but enterprises with complex needs across iOS, Android, and web often look for alternatives. You may need better deep linking, more flexible pricing, superior analytics, or a stronger enterprise support model. Below we compare seven alternatives: OneSignal, PushEngage, VWO Engage, Airship, CleverTap, Kumulos, and Pusher. Each tool offers multi-platform SDKs and deep linking capabilities, but their strengths differ.

alternative

OneSignal

. OneSignal supports iOS, Android, and web push with robust deep linking and automation. It offers a generous free tier and enterprise-level features like A/B testing and segmentation. Its SDK is easy to integrate, making it a direct alternative to Pushwoosh for most use cases.

alternative

PushEngage

. PushEngage focuses heavily on web push but also provides mobile push SDKs for iOS and Android. It includes deep linking support and advanced targeting features such as drip campaigns. Enterprises with heavy web push reliance find it a cost-effective option.

alternative

VWO Engage

. VWO Engage, part of the VWO suite, provides push notifications for mobile apps and websites with built-in deep linking. It integrates tightly with VWO’s A/B testing and personalization tools. This makes it ideal for enterprises already using VWO for experimentation.

alternative

Airship

. Airship is an enterprise-grade platform offering rich push notifications, deep linking, and orchestration across mobile and web. It excels in handling high volumes and complex customer journeys. Airship’s SDKs are well-documented, and its support for custom deep link schemas is strong.

alternative

CleverTap

. CleverTap combines push notifications with a full lifecycle marketing engine, supporting iOS, Android, and web with deep linking. It offers predictive analytics and multi-channel campaigns, making it a powerful choice for data-driven enterprises. Its segmentation and retargeting capabilities are especially advanced.

alternative

Kumulos

. Kumulos provides a mobile backend as a service with push notification SDKs for iOS and Android, plus web push support. It includes deep linking and user segmentation out of the box. Kumulos is a good fit for enterprises that want an all-in-one backend along with push notifications.

alternative

Pusher

. Pusher offers Pusher Beams, a service for sending push notifications to iOS, Android, and web apps with deep linking support. It is designed for developers who need a reliable real-time infrastructure alongside push. Pusher’s API is clean and scalable, but its feature set is leaner than full marketing platforms.

How they compare

All seven alternatives support iOS, Android, and web push SDKs as well as deep linking. The main differences lie in additional features, pricing models, and target audience. OneSignal is the most accessible with a free tier, while Airship and CleverTap cater to enterprise-scale marketing and analytics. PushEngage and VWO Engage are strong for web-first strategies. Kumulos and Pusher appeal to development teams wanting a lightweight backend or real-time layer. Enterprises should evaluate based on integration complexity, data retention limits, and the need for built-in analytics or A/B testing.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative has the best deep linking support for mobile apps?
Airship and CleverTap both offer highly configurable deep linking. Airship supports custom URI schemes and deferred deep linking, while CleverTap integrates deep linking with user lifecycle events for better retargeting.
Are there any free or low-cost alternatives suitable for testing?
Yes, OneSignal has a generous free tier that includes up to 10,000 subscribers and basic features. PushEngage also offers a free plan for up to 200 subscribers. Both are good for initial pilots before committing to a paid enterprise plan.
