Best AuditBoard alternatives

Finance teams today are expected to manage audit, risk, and compliance reporting without juggling multiple disconnected systems. AuditBoard audit management software brings these functions into one unified user interface, making it easier to track findings, monitor controls, and produce board-ready reports. Whether you are preparing for an external audit or strengthening internal controls, AuditBoard helps you move from spreadsheets and email chains to a single source of truth. This article compares AuditBoard with four common alternatives so you can decide which platform fits your finance team’s workflow.

alternative

ServiceNow GRC

. ServiceNow GRC uses the same platform as its IT service management product, which can be helpful if your organization already runs ServiceNow. It offers risk assessment, policy management, and audit workflows, but finance teams sometimes find the interface less intuitive for non-IT users. Configuration and customization often require dedicated IT support.

alternative

MetricStream

. MetricStream is a mature governance, risk, and compliance platform with deep modules for audit management and regulatory reporting. Its breadth of features can be an advantage for large enterprises, though the learning curve is steeper than AuditBoard. Finance teams may need more training to navigate the many configuration options.

alternative

RSA Archer

. RSA Archer provides a highly customizable framework for audit, risk, and compliance management. It is strong in large-scale deployments with complex hierarchies, but the setup and maintenance can be resource-intensive. Finance teams often report that the user interface feels dated compared to modern cloud-native tools.

alternative

LogicGate

. LogicGate is a low-code risk and compliance platform that allows you to build custom workflows without heavy developer involvement. It is flexible for finance teams that need to tailor processes, but out-of-the-box audit reporting may require more configuration than AuditBoard. It is a good fit for teams that value rapid prototyping over a pre-built audit module.
