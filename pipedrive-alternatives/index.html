If you are a small sales team using Pipedrive, you may be looking for a CRM that better fits your budget, workflow, or feature needs. Pipedrive is solid for pipeline management, but it can become expensive as your team grows or lack certain automation and marketing tools. This article covers five strong Pipedrive alternatives: HubSpot, Salesforce, Freshsales, Close CRM, and Nutshell. Each one is built for different sales styles, so you can find the right match.

alternative

HubSpot

offers a free CRM with robust contact management and email tracking, making it a low risk starting point for small teams. Its paid plans add marketing, sales, and service hubs, but the all in one approach can get pricey if you need many features.

Salesforce

is a powerful enterprise CRM with deep customization and a huge app marketplace. For small teams, the learning curve and cost can be steep, but it scales well if you plan to grow quickly and need advanced reporting.

Freshsales

Close CRM

focuses on outbound sales with features like call tracking, automated sequences, and built in VOIP. It is ideal for small teams that make many calls and want to reduce manual data entry.

alternative

Nutshell

combines CRM with email marketing and list management in a simple, affordable package. It works well for small businesses that want to manage leads and send campaigns from one place without extra integrations.

How they compare

If you want a free starting point, choose HubSpot. For low cost outbound calling, pick Close CRM. Freshsales offers a balanced feature set at a reasonable price. Nutshell works best if you need email marketing built in. Salesforce is only recommended if your team has a large budget and needs enterprise grade customization.

Frequently asked questions

Which Pipedrive alternative is best for a very small sales team on a tight budget?
HubSpot’s free CRM is a strong choice because it includes contact management, deal tracking, and email integration at no cost. If you can pay a small monthly fee, Freshsales and Nutshell also offer affordable entry level plans.
Can I migrate my data from Pipedrive to these alternatives easily?
Most of these tools provide import tools for CSV files or offer direct integrations with Pipedrive. HubSpot, Freshsales, and Close CRM have dedicated migration guides. Always test a small data set first to check formatting and avoid errors.
