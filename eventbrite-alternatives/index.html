Best Eventbrite alternatives

Eventbrite is a popular ticketing platform, but many organizers find its fees add up quickly or miss features like advanced attendee networking. Whether you run small local workshops or large hybrid conferences, there are alternatives that offer lower transaction costs, stronger branding tools, or specialized capabilities. This article covers five Eventbrite alternatives for organizers who want more control over their events and budget.

alternative

Cvent

is a comprehensive event management platform built for large corporate events and conferences. It offers robust registration, attendee management, and on-site check-in tools, but its pricing is typically higher and geared toward enterprise users.

alternative

Hopin

focuses on virtual and hybrid events with interactive features like virtual stages, networking lounges, and breakout sessions. It works well for organizers who need a fully digital experience, though its pricing can be steep for smaller events.

alternative

Splash

is designed for branded, visually rich event pages and seamless email marketing integration. It is a strong choice for companies that prioritize design and attendee engagement, but it lacks some of the ticketing and payment features found in Eventbrite.

Try Splash →
alternative

Whova

specializes in community building and attendee networking, offering features like a mobile app for agendas, messaging, and virtual meetups. It is ideal for multi-day conferences and events where interaction is key, although its fees can be higher than some alternatives.

alternative

Ticket Tailor

is a low cost, no frills ticketing platform that lets you keep more of your ticket revenue. It provides full control over your event pages and checkout flow, but it does not include built-in marketing or networking tools.
