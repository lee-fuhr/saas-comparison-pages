Best Zuora CPQ alternatives

Zuora CPQ is a configure price quote tool designed for subscription and usage based billing. However, some businesses find it complex or expensive, or need tighter integration with other systems. For subscription businesses seeking integrated billing and quoting, several alternatives offer strong capabilities. This article explores five Zuora CPQ alternatives: Salesforce CPQ, Apttus, Conga CPQ, Oracle CPQ, and DealHub.

alternative

Salesforce CPQ

. Salesforce CPQ, formerly known as Steelbrick, is a native solution on the Salesforce platform. It works well for companies already using Salesforce CRM and offers quote to cash automation with subscription support. Its deep integration with Salesforce makes it a popular choice for subscription businesses.

alternative

Apttus

. Apttus (now part of Conga, but often still referred to separately) provides a quote to cash platform with strong contract lifecycle management. It is suitable for complex pricing models including subscriptions and usage based billing. Its integration with Salesforce and other systems supports end to end revenue operations.

alternative

Conga CPQ

. Conga CPQ (formed from the merger of Apttus and Conga) offers advanced quoting and proposal automation. It handles subscription pricing, discounts, and renewals with seamless integration to billing systems. Conga is known for its document generation capabilities which help in creating subscription agreements.

alternative

Oracle CPQ

. Oracle CPQ is a robust enterprise solution that integrates with Oracle ERP and other Oracle cloud applications. It supports subscription and recurring revenue models with complex pricing rules. For businesses already using Oracle, this provides a tightly integrated quote to cash process.

alternative

DealHub

. DealHub is a CPQ platform focused on sales enablement and subscription quoting. It offers guided selling, subscription management, and integrates with popular billing systems like Stripe and Recurly. DealHub is designed to be user friendly and quick to deploy for subscription based businesses.

How they compare

Each alternative has strengths depending on your tech stack and needs. Salesforce CPQ is best for Salesforce users, while Oracle CPQ suits Oracle environments. Apttus and Conga excel in complex contracts and document generation. DealHub offers a modern, easy to use interface for smaller teams. Zuora CPQ remains a strong option but these alternatives provide different approaches to integrated billing and quoting.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative integrates best with existing billing systems?
The best integration depends on your billing platform. Salesforce CPQ works well with Zuora Revenue and other Salesforce connected billing tools. DealHub directly integrates with Stripe and Recurly. Oracle CPQ integrates with Oracle Billing. For Conga and Apttus, integration often requires middleware or custom connectors.
Are these alternatives suitable for usage based billing models?
Yes, most alternatives support usage based and consumption pricing. Salesforce CPQ and Oracle CPQ offer advanced usage rating capabilities. DealHub provides usage tracking through integrations. However, Zuora CPQ is known for its strong usage and subscription billing features, so evaluate your specific billing complexity.
