Best HubSpot CDP alternatives

Inbound marketers need powerful segmentation to deliver the right message to the right person at the right time. HubSpot CDP is a strong choice if you are already deep in the HubSpot ecosystem, offering unified customer profiles and basic segmentation tied to your CRM and marketing tools. But as your data sources grow or your segmentation needs become more complex, you may want a tool with more advanced built-in segmentation features. Here are four alternatives that prioritize segmentation capabilities for inbound marketers.

alternative

Segment

focuses on collecting, unifying, and routing data from websites, apps, and offline sources to hundreds of destinations. Its Personas product gives you real-time audience segmentation and computed traits, making it easy to build granular segments without manual data work.

alternative

mParticle

is built for omnichannel customer data and offers event-level segmentation with attributes that update in real time. You can create complex audiences using rules, calculated metrics, and behavioral conditions, all within a visual interface that does not require coding.

alternative

Snowplow

provides a behavioral data platform that captures every event at a granular level. You define your own data model, then use SQL or its query interface to build custom segments directly from raw data, giving you full control over segmentation logic.

alternative

Adobe Experience Platform

is an enterprise grade CDP that powers the Adobe ecosystem. It includes a Real-Time Customer Profile and a Segment Builder that lets you combine online and offline data with millions of attributes for precise targeting.

How they compare

HubSpot CDP is easiest to use if you already rely on HubSpot for CRM and marketing, but its segmentation is less flexible than dedicated CDPs. Segment and mParticle offer similar ease of integration with many tools and provide robust real-time segmentation out of the box. Snowplow gives you maximum control over data and segmentation but requires more technical setup. Adobe Experience Platform is the most comprehensive option for large enterprises, though it has a steep learning curve and higher cost. For inbound marketers who want built-in segmentation without heavy engineering, Segment and mParticle are the strongest alternatives.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Segment or mParticle alongside my existing HubSpot marketing tools?
Yes. Both Segment and mParticle have pre-built connectors that send segmented audiences and event data directly into HubSpot. You can enrich your HubSpot contacts with more detailed segments from other sources without leaving your current workflow.
Does Snowplow require a data engineer to create segments?
Snowplow does require more technical effort to deploy and model your data compared to Segment or mParticle. However, once the pipeline is set up, you can define segments using SQL or a visual query builder, giving you maximum flexibility for custom segmentation logic.
