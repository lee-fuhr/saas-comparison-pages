Best Rippling Recruiting alternatives

Rippling Recruiting is a strong choice if you already use Rippling’s unified HR and payroll platform. It lets you manage job postings, candidate tracking, and onboarding within one system. However, some organizations need more advanced recruiting features, better integrations with third party tools, or a solution that better fits their hiring volume and budget. This article covers four popular alternatives to Rippling Recruiting: Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, and SmartRecruiters. Each tool offers unique strengths for organizations that want to unify recruiting with their existing HR and payroll workflows.

alternative

Greenhouse

is built for structured, data driven hiring at scale. It offers robust interview kits, scorecards, and reporting that help teams make consistent hiring decisions. Greenhouse integrates with many HR and payroll platforms, but it does not include built-in payroll or core HR features, so you will need a separate system for those.

alternative

Lever

focuses on relationship building throughout the hiring process. Its built-in CRM helps you nurture passive candidates and track referrals alongside active applicants. Lever also offers a unified pipeline view, making it easier for teams to collaborate, but like Greenhouse it works best when paired with a separate HR and payroll provider.

alternative

Workable

is designed for ease of use and quick setup, especially for small to mid size businesses. It includes AI powered sourcing suggestions and one click job posting to hundreds of boards. Workable integrates with many HR and payroll tools, but it does not offer its own payroll functionality, so you will need to connect it to your existing system.

alternative

SmartRecruiters

is a hiring platform built for enterprise teams that need flexibility. It uses a hiring plan approach to break down roles into stages and tasks, and its marketplace connects you to hundreds of third party services. SmartRecruiters integrates with many HR and payroll systems, but it does not replace them, so you will still manage those workflows separately.

How they compare

Rippling Recruiting is the only option that includes built-in payroll and core HR within the same platform. The alternatives all require you to integrate with a separate HR and payroll system. Greenhouse and SmartRecruiters are best for larger, process driven teams that need deep reporting and structured workflows. Lever is ideal for teams that value candidate relationship management. Workable is the simplest and most affordable choice for smaller teams. All four alternatives offer stronger recruiting specific features than Rippling Recruiting, but none provide the same level of unified HR and payroll integration out of the box.
