If your B2B team relies on ZoomInfo Enrich for company and contact data enrichment, you may be exploring alternatives due to pricing changes, data accuracy concerns, or a need for specialized coverage. ZoomInfo Enrich offers broad B2B data, but its cost and occasional over-reliance on inferred data can push marketers toward other tools. Below we compare four strong alternatives that often provide better fit for specific use cases like technographic intelligence, GDPR compliance, or real-time enrichment at scale.

alternative

Clearbit Enrich

. Clearbit focuses on real-time enrichment using verified data sources, making it ideal for companies that need instant, accurate contact and company details during lead capture. It integrates deeply with popular CRMs and marketing automation platforms, but its pricing can scale quickly for high-volume needs.

alternative

Cognism

. Cognism combines AI-powered prospect data with built-in compliance features for GDPR and CCPA, which is a key advantage for European markets. It offers phone-verified contact data and intent signals, though its coverage outside of EMEA may be less comprehensive than some competitors.

alternative

BuiltWith

. BuiltWith specializes in technographic enrichment, revealing what technologies a company uses (like CRM, analytics, or ecommerce platforms). This makes it powerful for account-based marketing and competitive analysis, but it lacks the direct contact-level data that sales teams expect from tools like ZoomInfo.

alternative

Leadspace

. Leadspace uses machine learning to score and enrich leads based on predictive models, often pulling from public and third-party sources. It works well for account-based strategies and data hygiene, but its enrichment speed and depth of individual contact details can be slower than real-time alternatives.

How they compare

ZoomInfo Enrich is a mature, broad platform good for volume but can be expensive and less precise. Clearbit Enrich offers real-time, verified data and smooth integrations, ideal for high-intent lead capture. Cognism excels in compliance and phone-verified contacts, especially for EMEA. BuiltWith is best for technographic insights, not contact data. Leadspace brings predictive scoring but may not match the depth of direct enrichment from other tools. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize data freshness, compliance, technology signals, or cost.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free alternative to ZoomInfo Enrich for small teams?
Most enrichment tools like Clearbit and Cognism offer limited free tiers or trial credits, but full-scale enrichment at high volume typically requires a paid subscription. For small teams, consider starting with a usage-based plan or tools that charge per enriched record instead of a flat seat license.
Which alternative is best for GDPR-compliant enrichment?
Cognism is generally the strongest choice for GDPR compliance, as it builds its database with opt-in and verified sources, includes consent management features, and offers data deletion workflows. Clearbit also provides compliance controls, but Cognism’s European roots give it an edge in regulatory confidence.
