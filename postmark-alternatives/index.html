Postmark is a popular choice for transactional email, known for its high deliverability and simple pricing. However, your SaaS team might need more features (like marketing automation), a lower cost at high volume, or different integrations. This guide covers five strong alternatives: SendGrid, Mailgun, SparkPost, Amazon SES, and Brevo. Each option excels in a specific area, so you can choose the one that best fits your sending profile and budget.

alternative

SendGrid

(now part of Twilio) is a full service email platform that offers both transactional and marketing email. It provides robust analytics, dynamic templates, and a generous free tier (100 emails/day forever) making it a great choice for startups that want to grow without switching providers.

Mailgun

focuses on developer friendly APIs and advanced email validation. It gives you granular control over sending and suppression, and it works well for high volume sending where you need to manage multiple domains and IP pools.

alternative

SparkPost

is a pure deliverability powerhouse built for high throughput and low latency. It offers real time engagement data and a webhook system that lets you react to bounces and opens instantly, ideal for SaaS teams that depend on reliable inbox placement.

alternative

Amazon SES

is the most cost effective option, with pay as you go pricing that starts at $0.10 per 1,000 emails. It integrates deeply with AWS services but requires more manual configuration for reputation management and dedicated IPs.

alternative

Brevo

(formerly Sendinblue) combines transactional email with marketing automation, CRM, and SMS. Its flat rate pricing for unlimited emails up to a daily limit makes it attractive for small to mid sized teams that want an all in one solution.

How they compare

Postmark is best for teams that prioritize simplicity and consistent deliverability without needing marketing tools. SendGrid offers the widest feature set and scalability. Mailgun gives developers fine grained control over email infrastructure. SparkPost is the top choice for raw speed and deliverability at scale. Amazon SES is the cheapest option for high volume senders who can handle setup. Brevo is the most versatile for teams that want email plus marketing automation and CRM under one roof.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use these alternatives for both transactional and marketing emails?
Yes, all five support transactional email. SendGrid, Mailgun, SparkPost, and Brevo also support marketing campaigns and automation. Amazon SES focuses on transactional email but can be used for bulk sending with additional setup.
Which alternative has the easiest migration from Postmark?
SendGrid and Mailgun have similar API concepts (webhooks, templates, SMTP). Both offer import tools for suppression lists and dedicated support for migrations. SparkPost’s API is also straightforward but its focus on high volume may require tuning your sending patterns.
