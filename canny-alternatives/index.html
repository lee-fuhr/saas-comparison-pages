Best Canny alternatives

If you are a product team collecting feature requests, Canny is a popular choice for gathering and prioritizing user feedback. But you might be looking for Canny alternatives that offer different integrations, pricing, or workflows. This article compares five strong options: ProductBoard, Aha!, UserVoice, Pendo, and Nolt. Each tool has its own strengths, so you can find the one that fits your team’s specific needs.

alternative

ProductBoard

. ProductBoard focuses on connecting user feedback directly to your product roadmap. It lets you centralize ideas from multiple sources, prioritize them based on impact and effort, and then visualize how they map to strategic goals. This makes it a good alternative for teams that want a clear link between feature requests and high-level product strategy.

Try ProductBoard →
alternative

Aha!

. Aha! is a comprehensive product management platform that includes feedback collection, roadmapping, and strategic planning. It offers more advanced features for setting goals, scoring ideas, and reporting progress. Teams that need a full suite beyond just feedback management often choose Aha! over Canny.

Try Aha! →
alternative

UserVoice

. UserVoice specializes in capturing, organizing, and prioritizing customer feedback at scale. It provides a voting board where users can submit and upvote ideas, and it integrates with popular project management tools. Large organizations with high volumes of incoming requests may prefer UserVoice for its robust moderation and analytics.

alternative

Pendo

. Pendo is primarily a product analytics and user engagement platform, but it includes a feedback widget and idea management features. It allows you to tie feedback to specific user behavior data and in-app guides. Product teams that already use Pendo for analytics can use its feedback capabilities without adding another tool.

alternative

Nolt

. Nolt is a lightweight, no-code feedback board that is simple to set up and embed on your site. It supports custom branding, voting, and status updates, and it costs less than many competitors. Small teams or startups looking for a straightforward, affordable alternative to Canny often turn to Nolt.

How they compare

- **Best for strategic roadmapping**: ProductBoard and Aha! (both connect feedback to high-level goals). - **Best for high-volume feedback**: UserVoice (scalable moderation and voting). - **Best if you already use analytics**: Pendo (combines feedback with user behavior data). - **Best for simplicity and low cost**: Nolt (easy setup, no complexity). - **Canny** remains a solid midpoint: good feedback boards, voting, and integrations, but these alternatives offer different strengths depending on your team’s size and workflow.
