If you are evaluating white-label marketplace platforms for services or rentals, you have likely come across Near Me. While Near Me offers a solid foundation for location-based marketplaces, your specific business model may require different features, pricing, or scalability. This guide presents seven proven alternatives so you can compare their strengths and choose the platform that best fits your service or rental marketplace.

alternative

Arcadier

. Arcadier is a cloud-based marketplace builder that supports both services and physical rentals with a drag-and-drop interface. It offers extensive customization, multi-currency support, and built-in payment processing, making it a strong candidate for startups and growing businesses.

alternative

Sharetribe

. Sharetribe specializes in peer-to-peer rental and service marketplaces with two product tiers: a hosted version for quick launches and a flexible codebase for full customization. Its intuitive admin panel and integrated transaction system help you get a marketplace running without heavy development.

alternative

Kreezalid

. Kreezalid provides a headless marketplace solution that lets you separate the front-end from the back-end, giving developers full control over the user experience. It includes features like vendor management, booking calendars, and commission tracking, ideal for service and rental marketplaces that need a unique look.

alternative

Marketplacer

. Marketplacer is an enterprise-grade platform built for large-scale service and rental marketplaces, offering robust vendor onboarding, inventory management, and advanced analytics. Its marketplace-as-a-service model suits companies that need to handle high transaction volumes and complex fee structures.

alternative

Mirakl

. Mirakl is a leading enterprise marketplace platform that enables businesses to launch and scale third-party selling of services and rentals. It provides powerful seller management, automated workflows, and deep integration with existing e-commerce systems, making it best for established companies with substantial resources.

alternative

Yo!Kart

. Yo!Kart is a self-hosted marketplace script that supports services and rentals with features such as location-based search, booking slots, and vendor dashboards. It offers a one-time license fee rather than monthly subscriptions, which can be cost-effective for businesses that prefer full ownership.

alternative

SpurtCommerce

. SpurtCommerce is an open-source B2B marketplace platform that can be adapted for service and rental marketplaces. Its modular architecture allows you to add the features you need, and it supports multi-vendor management, custom pricing, and commission models.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a service marketplace with complex scheduling needs?
Kreezalid and Yo!Kart both offer built-in booking calendars and slot management. Kreezalid gives more design freedom, while Yo!Kart provides a one-time license fee.
Can I migrate from Near Me to one of these alternatives easily?
Migration difficulty depends on the platform. Arcadier and Sharetribe provide data import tools, while self-hosted options like Yo!Kart and SpurtCommerce may require custom scripting. Always check each platform’s migration documentation before making a switch.
