If you are using RollWorks for account based marketing but need more flexibility in how you sync with your CRM or how you connect with your existing ad tech stack, you are not alone. Many companies find that RollWorks works well for basic targeting but leaves gaps when it comes to deep custom integrations, multi-channel orchestration, or advanced AI-driven intent data. This article covers five strong RollWorks alternatives: Demandbase, Terminus, 6sense, Triblio, and Advertise. Each offers a different mix of CRM integration, ad tech compatibility, and campaign flexibility to help you find the right fit for your ABM strategy.

alternative

Demandbase

. Demandbase provides a full ABM platform with built-in intelligence and ad tech. It connects deeply with Salesforce and other CRMs, and its advertising engine covers display, social, and connected TV. If you need a single platform that combines account identification, engagement, and measurement, Demandbase is a strong RollWorks alternative with more advanced personalization features.

alternative

Terminus

. Terminus focuses on streamlining the ABM workflow from targeting to reporting. It integrates natively with HubSpot, Salesforce, and major ad platforms, making it easier to agree your sales and marketing data. Terminus also offers a flexible account hierarchy structure, which can be useful for companies managing complex buying groups. **6sense**. 6sense uses AI to predict which accounts are in market and recommend next steps across email, ads, and web personalization. Its CRM and ad tech integrations are robust, and it provides deep intent data without requiring a separate data source. For teams that want predictive analytics alongside their ad campaigns, 6sense is a more sophisticated alternative to RollWorks.

Triblio

. Triblio (now part of IDG) offers a straightforward ABM platform that focuses on account based advertising and website personalization. It integrates with major CRMs and ad networks, and its campaign builder is user friendly for marketers who want to launch quickly. Triblio is a good fit if you value simplicity and do not need the full intent data stack that larger platforms provide.

alternative

Advertise

. Advertise (also known as Madison Logic) specializes in B2B account based advertising with a strong emphasis on lead scoring and conversion tracking. It integrates with CRMs like Salesforce and Microsoft Dynamics, and it connects with programmatic ad exchanges for granular targeting. If your primary need is ad delivery and attribution rather than a full ABM suite, Advertise can be a focused RollWorks alternative.

Frequently asked questions

When should I consider switching from RollWorks to an alternative?
Consider switching when you need deeper CRM integration, such as syncing custom objects or managing complex account hierarchies. Also consider it if you require predictive intent data, multi-channel orchestration beyond display, or a more flexible ad tech stack that connects with your existing tools like Google Ads or LinkedIn.
Do any of these alternatives work well with a HubSpot CRM?
Yes. Terminus offers native HubSpot integration and is often recommended for HubSpot users. Demandbase and 6sense also connect with HubSpot through API or third party connectors, but Terminus has the most seamless workflow for HubSpot based teams. Triblio and Advertise support HubSpot as well, though with fewer advanced features.
