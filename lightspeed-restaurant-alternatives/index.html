Best Lightspeed Restaurant alternatives

For full-service restaurants, Lightspeed Restaurant offers solid point of sale features, but its inventory and table management tools may not meet every operator’s needs. You might be looking for deeper stock control, smoother floor plan mapping, or pricing that fits your budget. This article covers four strong alternatives that specialize in advanced inventory tracking and table management for full-service dining.

alternative

Toast POS

. Toast is built specifically for restaurants and gives you powerful inventory controls including recipe costing and automatic low stock alerts. Its table management module lets you drag and drop reservations, split checks by seat, and track server performance in real time. The system integrates with popular third party tools like payroll and online ordering out of the box.

alternative

Square Restaurants

. Square Restaurants offers free table management with floor plan views and course-based ordering that works well for busy dining rooms. Its inventory feature tracks ingredient usage automatically as you sell menu items and can sync with suppliers for reorder suggestions. The pay-as-you-go pricing and no long term contract make it a flexible choice for growing restaurants.

alternative

TouchBistro

. TouchBistro runs on iPads and includes a dedicated table management tool that shows live table status, wait times, and server assignments. Its inventory module supports multi location stock tracking, recipe costing, and can generate purchase orders directly from low stock alerts. The offline mode ensures you keep taking orders even if the internet goes down.

alternative

Revel Systems

. Revel Systems provides a robust inventory engine with real time stock counts, waste tracking, and the ability to set par levels for each ingredient. Its table management includes floor plan customization, reservation seating, and split payment handling for large parties. The cloud based system works well for multi unit full service chains that need centralized reporting.
