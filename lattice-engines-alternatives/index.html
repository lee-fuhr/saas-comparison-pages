Lattice Engines has long been a choice for B2B teams that want to enrich and score leads from multiple data sources. However, as your data needs grow, you may find that another platform better fits your budget, integration requirements, or predictive modeling approach. Below we compare five proven alternatives: MadKudu, 6sense, Mintigo, Infer, and Leadspace. Each offers a distinct mix of data enrichment, lead scoring, and account intelligence, helping you decide which tool aligns with your go-to-market strategy.

alternative

MadKudu

. MadKudu specializes in predictive lead scoring by analyzing your historical conversion data and surfacing high-fit prospects early. It integrates with modern marketing and sales stacks like Marketo, HubSpot, and Salesforce, and it provides transparent scoring logic so you can see why a lead scored a certain way. **6sense**. 6sense is an account-based intelligence platform that uses AI to identify anonymous buyers and predict which accounts are in market. It enriches leads with firmographic and intent data, and it can trigger workflows across ads, email, and sales outreach based on buying signals.

Mintigo

. Mintigo combines predictive lead scoring with deep data enrichment, pulling from thousands of third-party sources to build comprehensive profiles. It emphasizes account-level insights and can help you prioritize leads that are most likely to convert based on behavioral and demographic patterns.

alternative

Infer

. Infer (now part of Dun & Bradstreet) offers predictive scoring that learns from your CRM data to rank leads by likelihood to convert. It focuses on simplicity, with a straightforward model that requires minimal manual tuning, and it enriches leads with D&B’s firmographic data.

alternative

Leadspace

. Leadspace provides a customer data platform (CDP) that unifies and enriches lead data from multiple sources, then applies AI scoring to identify your best opportunities. It supports B2B and B2C use cases, and its enrichment layer can fill gaps in contact and account records automatically.

How they compare

The main differences among these alternatives come down to data sources, scoring transparency, and account-based orientation. MadKudu offers clear scoring logic and easy CRM integration, making it ideal for teams that want to understand why a lead scored high. 6sense is built for account-based marketing and excels at detecting intent signals, while Mintigo brings a large enrichment database and account-level focus. Infer provides a simpler, more hands-off model backed by Dun & Bradstreet data. Leadspace functions as a full CDP with enrichment and scoring, best for companies that need a central data hub. All five can replace Lattice Engines, but your choice depends on whether you prioritize transparency (MadKudu), intent data (6sense), enrichment breadth (Mintigo and Infer), or data unification (Leadspace).
