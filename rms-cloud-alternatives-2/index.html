Best RMS Cloud alternatives

If you run an independent inn and are looking for a subscription based property management system, you have likely come across RMS Cloud. It offers solid features for reservations, housekeeping, and billing. But it may not fit every inn’s budget or workflow. This article reviews three strong RMS Cloud alternatives: Cloudbeds, Mews, and Hotelogix. Each is designed for small to mid sized properties and charges a monthly subscription fee.

alternative

Cloudbeds

is a popular choice for inns because it combines a channel manager, booking engine, and front desk tools in one platform. Its dashboard is clean and easy to navigate, and it connects with many online travel agencies. Cloudbeds also includes basic reporting and automation features that help reduce manual data entry.

alternative

Mews

focuses on being a modern, cloud native system with a strong emphasis on automation and guest self service. It works well for independent inns that want to limit desk staff and let guests check in online or use a kiosk. Mews also integrates with a wide range of third party apps for payments, analytics, and housekeeping.

alternative

Hotelogix

is built for small to mid sized properties and offers a straightforward subscription model with no hidden fees. Its core features include reservation management, invoicing, and a built in channel manager. Hotelogix also provides a mobile app that lets you manage bookings and guest profiles from anywhere.

How they compare

All three alternatives are subscription based and target independent inns. Cloudbeds gives you a full suite of tools in one package. Mews leads in automation and self service options. Hotelogix keeps pricing simple and offers strong mobile functionality. RMS Cloud remains a solid option, but these alternatives may better match specific needs like lower cost, easier onboarding, or deeper integration with smart locks or payment gateways.

Frequently asked questions

Are there any setup fees for these alternatives?
Cloudbeds, Mews, and Hotelogix typically charge only monthly subscription fees for their standard plans. Some may ask for a one time onboarding payment for training or data migration, so it is wise to ask during a free trial.
Can I switch from RMS Cloud to one of these alternatives without losing my data?
Yes, most modern PMS providers offer free or paid data migration services. They can import your reservation history, guest profiles, and rates from RMS Cloud. You should confirm the scope of migration with the new provider before signing up.
