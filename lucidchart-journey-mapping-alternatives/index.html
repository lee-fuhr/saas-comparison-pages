Best Lucidchart alternatives

Journey mapping helps your team visualize customer experiences, identify pain points, and improve workflows. Lucidchart is a popular diagramming tool, but it may not fit every team’s budget, collaboration style, or feature needs. As a team lead, you want a tool that keeps everyone aligned without slowing down your process. Here are four solid alternatives to consider: Miro, Mural, Microsoft Visio, and Creately. Each brings different strengths to journey mapping, from real-time collaboration to detailed process diagrams.

alternative

Miro

. Miro is a digital whiteboard built for real-time collaboration, making it ideal for team workshops and brainstorming. Its journey map templates are easy to adapt, and sticky notes, comments, and voting features let your whole team contribute. The infinite canvas gives you room to expand maps as you discover new touchpoints.

alternative

Mural

. Mural focuses on structured facilitation, with frameworks for journey mapping, design thinking, and agile workflows. It offers guided templates and timers to keep remote sessions productive, plus sticky notes and diagramming shapes. Team leads appreciate the built-in retrospectives and facilitation tools that turn loose ideas into clear maps.

alternative

Microsoft Visio

. Visio is a professional diagramming tool that excels at process flows, system diagrams, and detailed technical maps. It integrates deeply with Microsoft 365, so if your team already uses Teams, SharePoint, or Excel, Visio fits right in. For journey mapping, it works best when you need precise, data-linked diagrams and less emphasis on free-form brainstorming.

alternative

Creately

. Creately combines diagramming with visual collaboration, offering smart shapes and a library of journey map templates. Its “smart canvas” automatically aligns and connects objects, saving time when you build complex maps. Creately also supports real-time editing, comments, and version history, making it a balanced choice for both structured diagrams and team input.

How they compare

Miro and Mural excel at collaborative, workshop-style journey mapping with lots of whiteboard freedom. Microsoft Visio is best for technical, process-heavy diagrams with Microsoft integration. Creately offers a middle ground with more structured diagramming than Miro but less complexity than Visio. All four tools support journey mapping, but your choice depends on whether your team prioritizes brainstorming, structured diagrams, or enterprise compatibility.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import Lucidchart files into these alternatives?
Most alternatives support common file imports. Miro and Creately accept Visio files, but Lucidchart exports are usually in PDF or image format. You may need to rebuild maps manually if you switch, but many tools offer copy-paste support for shapes and text.
Which alternative is best for a team that already uses Microsoft 365?
Microsoft Visio is the strongest choice because it integrates natively with Teams, SharePoint, and Excel. Miro and Mural also have Microsoft 365 integrations, but Visio gives you seamless single sign-on and direct access to organizational diagrams.
