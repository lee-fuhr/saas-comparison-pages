Where Lusha wins

Lusha is built for speed and simplicity. If your main job is to find a phone number or email in seconds while browsing a LinkedIn profile or a company website, Lusha does that faster than any alternative. The extension works with a single click, and the data fields are clean and minimal. There is no clutter, no extra tabs, just the contact info you need.

Lusha also wins on data accuracy for direct phone numbers. Many sales reps report that Lusha’s mobile and direct dial numbers connect more often than Apollo.io’s numbers. If you are a high volume cold caller who needs a working line right now, Lusha tends to deliver a higher pick up rate.

Another area where Lusha wins is simplicity of pricing. You can buy credits as needed without committing to a long term plan. For a solo rep or a small team that does not need a full CRM or sequence tool, Lusha’s pay as you go model is straightforward.

Where Apollo.io wins

Apollo.io is a full stack sales engagement platform, not just a contact data provider. If you need to build a list, enrich it, and then run email sequences or call scripts from the same tool, Apollo.io wins hands down. It gives you a built in CRM, workflow automation, and a massive database of over 275 million contacts. You can filter by job title, company size, technology used, and many other criteria without leaving the platform.

Apollo.io also wins when it comes to volume. For a sales team that has to export thousands of leads into a separate CRM, Apollo.io offers unlimited email credits on some plans and much lower per contact costs at scale. The data enrichment happens in bulk, and you can pull entire lists in minutes.

Another area where Apollo.io wins is the ability to verify and refresh contacts automatically. The platform runs a continuous data update process, so you are less likely to work with stale emails. Lusha relies more on you manually checking each contact.

Verdict

Choose Lusha versus Apollo.io based on your role. If you are an individual rep who needs a fast, reliable dialer tool to get immediate phone numbers while prospecting on LinkedIn, Lusha is your best bet. It is lean, accurate for direct dials, and easy to budget.

If you are part of a larger sales team that needs to manage sequences, track outreach, and scale prospecting across thousands of contacts, Apollo.io wins. The extra features may feel heavy for a solo user, but they make the difference when coordinating with a CRM and running automated campaigns.

Both tools are excellent, but they serve different stages of the sales process. Lusha for quick hits, Apollo.io for full pipeline management. Pick the one that matches your daily routine.
