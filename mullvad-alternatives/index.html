Mullvad has earned a strong reputation among privacy focused users thanks to its strict no-logs policy, anonymous sign-up (no email required, only an account number), and acceptance of cash payments. However, even the best VPN might not fit every need. You may want a larger server network, faster speeds for streaming, or a provider with a more polished app. If you are looking for Mullvad alternatives that still prioritize no-logs and anonymous sign-up, three well known services come close: NordVPN, ExpressVPN, and Surfshark. Below we break down how each compares for tech oriented users who value privacy above all.

alternative

NordVPN

. NordVPN offers a verified no-logs policy and supports anonymous sign-up using cryptocurrency. Its large server fleet and specialty servers (like obfuscated or double VPN) give you more options for bypassing restrictions, though its standard pricing is higher than Mullvad. The trade off is a more feature rich app with split tunneling and a kill switch built in.

alternative

ExpressVPN

. ExpressVPN is headquartered in the British Virgin Islands, outside intelligence sharing alliances, and uses a RAM only server architecture that prevents data storage. Anonymous sign-up is possible with Bitcoin payments, and its proprietary protocol (Lightway) delivers consistent speed. The main drawback is the higher per month price, but the no-logs claim has been validated by multiple independent audits.

alternative

Surfshark

. Surfshark allows anonymous sign-up with cryptocurrency and publishes a transparency report alongside regular security audits. It supports unlimited simultaneous connections, which is useful if you have many devices, and its CleanWeb feature blocks ads and trackers. While its no-logs policy is strong, the company is based in the Netherlands (a Nine Eyes jurisdiction), which privacy purists may note as a difference from Mullvad’s Swedish base.

How they compare

All three alternatives maintain a no-logs policy that has been audited or legally tested, and each accepts cryptocurrency for anonymous sign-up. Mullvad remains the simplest for pure anonymity (no email, cash payments) and has a lower monthly price if you commit long term. NordVPN provides the most server locations and advanced features, ExpressVPN prioritizes speed and a proven privacy jurisdiction, and Surfshark wins on value with unlimited connections. For the core requirements of no-logs and anonymous sign-up, any of these will serve a tech oriented user well, though Mullvad is still the gold standard for minimal data collection.
