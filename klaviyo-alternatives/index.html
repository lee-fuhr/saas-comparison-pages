Best Klaviyo alternatives

If you run an ecommerce brand, you know Klaviyo is powerful for email and SMS marketing. But its pricing can climb fast as your list grows. Many store owners look for a Klaviyo alternative that still offers solid automation, segmentation, and ecommerce integrations – but at a lower price point. Below we compare five popular options: Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Drip, Omnisend, and Brevo. Each has strengths and tradeoffs, especially for cost‑conscious ecommerce brands.

alternative

Mailchimp

. Mailchimp offers a generous free plan and lower starting tiers than Klaviyo, but its ecommerce features are less advanced. You can still build automations based on purchase behavior, though reporting and segmentation are more limited unless you pay for higher plans.

alternative

ActiveCampaign

. ActiveCampaign combines email marketing with a powerful CRM and conditional automation. It costs less than Klaviyo for many list sizes and lets you create deep customer journeys, but its built‑in ecommerce features (like product feeds and browse abandonment) require additional setup.

Try ActiveCampaign →
alternative

Drip

. Drip is purpose‑built for ecommerce and offers flexible automation workflows with a flat‑rate pricing model based on contacts instead of email sends. It can be cheaper than Klaviyo for medium‑sized lists, though its UI feels less modern and integrations are narrower.

alternative

Omnisend

. Omnisend is an ecommerce‑first platform that includes email, SMS, and push notifications. Its pricing is often lower than Klaviyo, especially for stores with under 10,000 contacts, and it offers pre‑built automation flows like cart recovery and welcome series right out of the box.

Try Omnisend →
alternative

Brevo

. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) offers a very affordable per‑email pricing model alongside a free tier. It includes transactional emails and basic marketing automation, but its ecommerce segmentation and product‑level triggers are less refined than Klaviyo’s.

Try Brevo →

How they compare

Klaviyo excels at deep ecommerce integration and advanced segmentation, but it is often the most expensive option as your list grows. Mailchimp and Brevo are the cheapest for small lists, while Omnisend and Drip offer ecommerce‑specific workflows at lower costs than Klaviyo for many mid‑size stores. ActiveCampaign gives you powerful CRM‑driven automation at a moderate price, though it may require extra effort to match Klaviyo’s native ecommerce data. Your choice depends on whether your priority is cost savings, ease of setup, or feature parity with Klaviyo.
