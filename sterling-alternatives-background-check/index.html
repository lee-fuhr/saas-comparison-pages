If you are a talent acquisition specialist at a financial services firm, you know that speed and accuracy in background checks are critical for hiring compliance professionals and support staff. Sterling is a well known provider, but sometimes you need a faster turnaround or a more flexible solution for specific roles. This article explores three credible alternatives: GoodHire, Checkr, and Accurate Background. Each offers rapid screening options while still meeting the regulatory requirements your firm demands.

alternative

GoodHire

offers a fully automated screening platform that can deliver most criminal and employment checks within 24 to 48 hours. It includes a dedicated compliance team to help financial services clients stay aligned with FCRA and ban the box regulations. GoodHire also provides customizable packages, so you can add specialized checks like credit reports or FINRA searches.

Checkr

uses AI driven technology to process background checks quickly, often returning results in under a day for standard searches. It offers a specialized financial services package that includes SEC, FINRA, and credit history checks. Checkr’s developer friendly API makes integration with your existing ATS seamless, reducing manual data entry.

alternative

Accurate Background

provides a global reach with rapid turnaround on U.S. based checks, typically within one to three business days. It features a dedicated account manager for financial services clients and offers compliance tools like adverse action workflows. Accurate Background also supports continuous monitoring, which can help you stay ahead of regulatory changes without re screening.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer faster turnaround times than traditional providers, but they differ in specialization. GoodHire balances speed with strong compliance support. Checkr excels in tech integration and speed for standard checks. Accurate Background stands out for global capability and dedicated account management. For financial services firms, the best choice depends on whether you prioritize integration ease, international coverage, or regulatory hand holding.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives replace Sterling for FINRA and SEC required checks?
Yes. GoodHire, Checkr, and Accurate Background all offer customized packages that include FINRA, SEC, and credit history screenings. You should confirm with each vendor that their source databases and turnaround times meet your firm’s compliance standards.
How do these tools handle the faster turnaround without sacrificing accuracy?
Each provider uses automated data retrieval from court databases, county records, and credit bureaus. They also employ manual verification steps to catch errors. No background check is 100% instant, but these platforms reduce wait times by prioritizing digital records and using real time database searches.
