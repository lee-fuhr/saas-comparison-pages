If your enterprise revenue team relies on Leadspace for AI driven intent scoring and data unification, you know the value of turning raw signals into prioritized accounts. But maybe you need better third party coverage, a different pricing model, or stronger integrations with your existing stack. Here are four solid alternatives that serve similar use cases, each with its own strengths and trade offs.

alternative

ZoomInfo Enrich

gives you access to ZoomInfo’s massive B2B contact and company database, enriched with firmographic and technographic data. Its intent signals come from content consumption and buying stage data, but the AI scoring is less transparent than Leadspace’s and the platform leans heavily on its own proprietary data rather than unifying external sources.

alternative

Clearbit Enrich

offers real time company and person enrichment with a strong API first approach, making it easy to layer into existing workflows. Its intent scoring is driven by web traffic and content engagement, though the data depth for late stage buying signals often requires additional tools to achieve the same level of unification that Leadspace provides.

alternative

Cognism

specializes in GDPR compliant data for European markets, combining firmographic data with phone verified contacts and intent signals from its own buyer intent index. While its AI scores are useful for outbound prioritization, the platform is less focused on unifying disparate data sources across your entire revenue stack compared to Leadspace’s holistic approach.

alternative

BuiltWith

is primarily a technographic and web technology profiler, not a full intent scoring platform. It identifies which technologies prospects use and when they adopt new tools, which can serve as a proxy for intent, but it lacks the advanced AI models and data unification capabilities that enterprise revenue teams expect from Leadspace.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use BuiltWith as a direct replacement for Leadspace’s intent scoring?
No. BuiltWith tells you what technologies a company uses and when they add new tools, which can hint at intent but does not provide the predictive AI scores or data unification that Leadspace delivers. It is best used as a complementary data source, not a standalone alternative for revenue teams.
Which alternative offers the strongest data unification capabilities similar to Leadspace?
None of the listed alternatives fully replicate Leadspace’s unified approach to combining CRM, marketing automation, and external intent data. ZoomInfo Enrich comes closest for organizations already using ZoomInfo’s ecosystem, but its unification is limited to ZoomInfo’s own datasets rather than bridging multiple third party sources.
