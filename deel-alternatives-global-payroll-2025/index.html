If your global HR team is evaluating international payroll providers, you have likely come across Deel as a popular choice. However, depending on your company size, compliance needs, or budget, a Deel alternative may better suit your operations. Below we compare three strong competitors: Remote, Papaya Global, and Oyster HR. Each offers distinct strengths in payroll, contractor management, and global compliance.

alternative

Remote

. Remote focuses on simplicity and transparent pricing for both employees and contractors. It provides local entity support in over 60 countries and handles payroll, benefits, and equity, making it a strong choice for teams that want one flat fee per worker.

alternative

Papaya Global

. Papaya Global is built for larger organizations that need advanced payroll orchestration across multiple countries. Its platform emphasizes compliance automation and real-time analytics, and it supports a wide range of payment methods and currencies.

alternative

Oyster HR

. Oyster HR is designed for companies that prioritize employee experience alongside compliance. It offers a self-serve platform for hiring and paying workers in over 180 countries, with built-in benefits and a strong focus on local market knowledge.

Frequently asked questions

Which Deel alternative is best for a small remote team?
Remote is often a good fit for small teams because it offers flat per-worker pricing with no hidden fees and supports both contractors and full-time employees in many countries.
Do any of these alternatives offer an Employer of Record (EOR) service like Deel?
Yes, all three alternatives (Remote, Papaya Global, and Oyster HR) provide EOR services, meaning they act as the legal employer in countries where you do not have your own entity.
