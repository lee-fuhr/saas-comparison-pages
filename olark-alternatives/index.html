Olark is a popular live chat tool, but it may not fit every budget or workflow. Many small to mid sized businesses find that Olark’s pricing, limited automation, or lack of integrated marketing features do not match their needs. This article covers seven Olark alternatives that offer simple website live chat widgets, along with a quick comparison and answers to common questions.

alternative

Chaport

. Chaport provides a free plan with unlimited chats, a shared inbox, and basic automation. It is a good choice for teams that want a low cost entry point without sacrificing core chat features.

alternative

JivoChat

. JivoChat combines live chat with a help desk ticketing system and supports multiple channels like email and Facebook. Its mobile app and team management tools make it useful for businesses that need omnichannel support.

alternative

Pure Chat

. Pure Chat focuses on simplicity with a clean interface, unlimited chat history, and real time visitor monitoring. It is ideal for small businesses that want a straightforward, no frills chat widget.

alternative

Smartsupp

. Smartsupp offers live chat plus video recording of visitor sessions to understand user behavior. Its free plan includes basic chat and recordings, making it a solid option for businesses that want to improve conversion rates.

alternative

LiveChat

. LiveChat is a feature rich platform with ticketing, chatbots, and integration with over 200 apps. It is best for growing companies that need advanced reporting and customization.

alternative

Tidio

. Tidio provides a visual chatbot builder alongside live chat, with a generous free plan that covers up to three operators. It works well for e commerce stores and businesses that want to automate common questions.

alternative

Intercom

. Intercom is a full customer communications platform with live chat, chatbots, email campaigns, and product tours. It is better suited for larger teams or businesses that need a complete CRM and automation suite.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Olark alternative for a very small business on a tight budget?
Tidio or Chaport are good options because they both offer free plans with core live chat features and no limit on the number of chats you can receive.
Do any of these alternatives work with Shopify or WooCommerce?
Yes, most of them have direct integrations. Tidio and LiveChat are especially popular with e commerce stores, and both offer easy setup for Shopify and WooCommerce.
