If you are a marketer who wants to create polished presentations and visuals without hiring a designer, Beautiful.ai has been a go to tool. Its AI slides auto-format your content, saving you time on layout. But maybe you need different features, a lower price, or more flexibility. Here are five strong alternatives that also use AI driven design to help you look professional without a design background.

alternative

Slidebean

focuses on pitch decks and investor presentations. You start by picking a template, then add your text and images. The AI arranges the slides so your story flows clearly, and you can export to PowerPoint or share online.

alternative

Canva

is the most versatile option for marketers. It offers thousands of templates for social media, documents, and presentations. The AI tools include Magic Design, which generates layouts from your prompts, and Magic Write for copy suggestions, all inside a simple drag and drop interface.

alternative

Visme

combines presentations, infographics, and videos in one platform. Its AI design assistant helps you choose color schemes and fonts, then re-arranges content for readability. You can add interactive elements like polls or embedded web pages, which is useful for live or self paced presentations.

alternative

Powtoon

specializes in animated videos and explainer content. Instead of static slides, you build scenes with moveable characters, text, and audio. The AI suggests transitions and timing, making it easy for non-designers to create engaging motion graphics for webinars or social ads.

alternative

Crello

, now part of VistaCreate, is a Canva competitor with a large library of animated templates. Its AI tool resizes designs for different platforms automatically. You get professional looking banners, story posts, and short videos without touching complex software.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my existing PowerPoint slides into these alternatives?
Most tools allow import. Canva and Visme accept PPTX files, while Slidebean and Beautiful.ai offer import options. Powtoon and Crello typically require you to start from scratch or use templates.
Do these tools work for team collaboration and brand consistency?
Yes. Canva, Visme, and Slidebean have team plans with shared brand kits, folders, and approval workflows. Beautiful.ai also offers team features. Powtoon and Crello allow sharing but are less focused on multi user editing.
