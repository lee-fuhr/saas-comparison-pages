Best Marketo alternatives

Choosing the right enterprise marketing automation platform can feel overwhelming. Marketo and Eloqua are two of the most established options, but they are not the only choices. This article focuses on Marketo as your primary reference and explores real alternatives including Eloqua, Pardot, HubSpot Marketing Hub, and ActiveCampaign. Each tool has strengths depending on your company size, budget, and marketing maturity.

alternative

Pardot

. Pardot is Salesforce’s B2B marketing automation solution. It integrates deeply with Salesforce CRM, making it a strong choice for companies already using that sales ecosystem. It offers lead scoring, email nurturing, and ROI reporting but can be complex to set up without dedicated support.

alternative

HubSpot Marketing Hub

. HubSpot is known for its user-friendly interface and all-in-one CRM platform. It combines email marketing, lead management, and analytics with built-in SEO and content tools. Enterprise tiers add advanced automation and custom reporting, making it a good fit for companies that want an easier onboarding experience.

alternative

ActiveCampaign

. ActiveCampaign is a more cost-effective alternative that still offers powerful automation and email marketing features. It excels at conditional triggers and customer experience mapping, and it includes a built-in CRM. While it may lack some advanced enterprise reporting, it works well for mid-market and growing businesses.

alternative

Eloqua

. Eloqua is a mature platform focused on large-scale B2B lead management and account-based marketing. It offers robust segmentation, lead scoring, and campaign orchestration. However, it often requires a dedicated admin team and can have higher total cost of ownership compared to newer platforms.

How they compare

All four alternatives can replace or compete with Marketo, but the best fit depends on your needs. Pardot and Eloqua are strongest for Salesforce-centric enterprises that need complex lead routing. HubSpot offers the easiest user experience and a unified CRM. ActiveCampaign delivers strong automation at a lower price point for mid-market teams. Marketo remains a leader in advanced lead management and multi-channel campaigns, but its complexity and cost may push some buyers toward these alternatives.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for an enterprise with a large sales team?
Pardot and Eloqua both work well for large sales teams because they integrate tightly with Salesforce (Pardot) or offer deep lead scoring and account-based features (Eloqua). HubSpot Marketing Hub is also enterprise-ready but may require a higher tier for advanced reporting.
Can I get the same features as Marketo at a lower cost?
ActiveCampaign offers many automation features at a fraction of Marketo’s price, but it lacks some enterprise-grade reporting and integrations. For a closer match to Marketo’s full feature set, consider HubSpot Marketing Hub Enterprise or Pardot, though neither is significantly cheaper once you add necessary add-ons.
