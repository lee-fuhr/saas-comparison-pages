Marketing ops teams rely on activity-based revenue data to connect sales actions to pipeline and closed won deals. People.ai has been a popular choice for automatically capturing sales activities from CRM, email, and calendars to surface rep behaviors and forecast accuracy. However, your team might be exploring alternatives due to budget constraints, a need for deeper conversation analysis, or a desire for tighter integration with your existing tech stack. This article covers four strong options – Gong, Chorus.ai, Clari, and Outreach Signal – to help you evaluate which tool best supports your activity tracking and revenue intelligence goals.

alternative

Gong

captures revenue intelligence by recording and analyzing sales conversations from calls, meetings, and emails. It excels at revealing talk patterns, objection handling, and deal risks, which helps marketing ops understand the qualitative side of activity data. For teams that prioritize conversation insights over granular CRM activity logs, Gong provides a rich dataset but may require additional effort to map to specific revenue events.

alternative

Chorus.ai

offers conversation intelligence focused on calls and demos, using AI to surface coaching moments and deal momentum. It is well suited for teams that want to improve rep performance through activity-based feedback, though its strength lies more in speech analytics than in tracking every CRM action. Marketing ops can use Chorus to bridge the gap between talk-level data and pipeline outcomes.

alternative

Clari

specializes in revenue operations and predictive forecasting by combining data from CRM, email, and calendar activities. It gives marketing ops a holistic view of pipeline health and rep engagement without relying heavily on conversation recording. Clari is a strong fit for teams that need accurate deal stage progression and activity attribution across multiple sources from a single platform.

alternative

Outreach Signal

is part of the Outreach sales engagement platform and focuses on activity signals from email opens, clicks, and meeting responses. It helps marketing ops teams see which sequence actions drive pipeline and revenue. For organizations already using Outreach for cadences, Signal adds a layer of activity-based intelligence, though it may not capture the full breadth of CRM-level events that People.ai logs.

How they compare

- **Data focus**: Gong and Chorus prioritize conversation recordings and analysis. Clari emphasizes CRM and calendar activity for forecasting. Outreach Signal concentrates on email and sequence engagement signals. - **Primary use case**: Gong and Chorus support coaching and deal inspection. Clari drives pipeline accuracy and forecasting. Outreach Signal optimizes outreach cadences. - **Integration depth**: Clari and Outreach Signal seamlessly connect to Salesforce and other CRMs. Gong and Chorus integrate with CRMs but add a conversation layer on top.

Frequently asked questions

Can any of these alternatives fully replicate People.ai’s automatic CRM activity capture?
Not entirely. People.ai is known for logging every CRM field change, email, and calendar event without manual entry. Clari comes closest for pipeline forecasting activities, while Gong and Chorus excel at conversation data. Outreach Signal covers sequence-specific actions. Your choice depends on which activities matter most for your revenue analysis.
Which alternative should I pick if my team’s main goal is to improve forecast accuracy from activity data?
Clari is the best fit because it directly connects rep activities to deal stages and probability-based forecasting. It ingests CRM, email, and calendar data to surface pipeline gaps and historical patterns. Gong and Chorus can support forecasting indirectly through conversation insights, but Clari is purpose built for revenue operations teams.
