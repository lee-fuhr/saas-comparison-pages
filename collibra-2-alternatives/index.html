Collibra is a comprehensive data governance suite designed for large enterprises that need to manage, protect, and activate their data at scale. It combines a robust data catalog, business glossary, policy management, and data lineage capabilities into a single platform. For organizations with complex data landscapes, Collibra provides the governance framework to ensure compliance, improve data quality, and empower data citizens across the business. Its strong automation, workflow engine, and integration with popular data tools make it a leading choice for enterprise data governance.

alternative

Alation

. Alation focuses on data cataloging and collaboration with a strong emphasis on user adoption and behavioral analytics. It offers a data marketplace and automated lineage, but its governance and policy enforcement features are less mature than Collibra’s, making it better suited for organizations that prioritize discovery over strict control.

alternative

Atlan

. Atlan is a modern data catalog and governance platform that emphasizes a user-friendly interface and active metadata management. It integrates well with cloud data stacks and supports data quality and lineage, but its governance depth, especially around policy enforcement and risk management, is less comprehensive than Collibra’s.

alternative

DataHub

. DataHub is an open source metadata platform originally built by LinkedIn. It provides powerful lineage, search, and discovery but requires significant engineering effort to customize and maintain. It is a strong option for tech forward teams that want flexibility and cost control, though it lacks the out of the box governance workflows and policy tools of Collibra.

alternative

Secoda

. Secoda is a lightweight, AI powered data discovery and cataloging tool aimed at mid market and smaller enterprises. It offers automated documentation and a simple interface, but its governance features, such as policy enforcement and stewardship, are limited compared to Collibra, making it less suitable for large scale enterprise compliance needs.

How they compare

Collibra leads in enterprise grade governance, policy management, and compliance readiness, with deep integrations and extensive workflow automation. Alation and Atlan offer better user experience and faster time to value but lack some of Collibra’s governance depth. DataHub provides unmatched customizability for technical teams but demands more setup and maintenance. Secoda is the simplest option but is not designed for complex, large scale governance requirements. The right choice depends on your organization’s need for governance rigor versus ease of use and cost.

Frequently asked questions

What makes Collibra suitable for large enterprises?
Collibra is built for scale, with support for multiple data domains, complex role based access controls, and automated compliance reporting. It integrates with major data platforms, ERP systems, and cloud services, and its workflow engine allows organizations to enforce data policies consistently across thousands of users.
How does Collibra compare to open source options like DataHub?
Collibra offers a complete, ready to use governance suite with policy enforcement, stewardship, and audit trails out of the box. DataHub provides strong metadata management and lineage but requires custom development for governance features, making it more suitable for organizations with dedicated engineering resources and simpler compliance needs.
