If you run a startup and need to handle sales tax without a big budget or a dedicated tax team, you want a low cost tool that works on a simple subscription. SimplyTax is built for this exact need: it automates tax calculations, filing, and returns at a flat monthly rate with no hidden fees. But you might also be comparing it to Sovos and other platforms. This article helps you understand the differences so you can choose the best fit for your business.

alternative

Avalara

is a powerful, enterprise grade tax solution that handles complex product taxability and multi state compliance. Its pricing tends to be higher and is often transaction based, which can quickly become expensive for a growing startup.

alternative

TaxJar

is a popular choice for ecommerce sellers, especially those using platforms like Shopify or WooCommerce. It offers strong integration and simple reporting, but its pricing can scale with transaction volume and does not include all state filing in every plan.

alternative

Vertex

offers a robust tax engine with deep customization for industries like manufacturing and telecom. It is not typically the best fit for early stage startups due to its higher cost and more complex implementation.

alternative

Sovos

is a full service tax compliance platform that covers returns, exemption certificate management, and indirect tax. It is aimed at mid market and enterprise companies, so its subscription pricing and features may be overkill for a small startup with basic needs.

How they compare

SimplyTax beats Sovos for startups because it offers a flat fee subscription that covers unlimited transaction calculations and state filings. Sovos often requires a longer contract and charges extra for each state or return. SimplyTax also integrates with common ecommerce and accounting platforms, while Sovos targets larger, more complex tax scenarios. For a low cost, predictable monthly cost, SimplyTax is the simpler and more affordable choice.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use SimplyTax if I only sell in one state?
Yes. SimplyTax works for any number of states, and its flat fee stays the same whether you have one state or fifty. You do not pay extra for adding more states later.
Does Sovos offer a free trial or startup discount?
Sovos typically does not offer a free trial and its pricing is not publicly listed. Most startups find it easier to start with SimplyTax because you can sign up online, get a free trial, and see the exact monthly cost upfront.
