Deel is a popular employer of record (EOR) platform for hiring international contractors and employees. However, some companies find its pricing, feature set, or support model doesn’t fit their needs. This article explores five Deel alternatives – Remote, Oyster, Papaya Global, Rippling, and Multiplier – to help you choose the right EOR provider for your global team.

alternative

Remote

focuses on simplicity and transparency, offering flat monthly fees per employee per country. It covers both contractors and EOR employees in over 60 countries, with strong support for payroll, benefits, and compliance. Remote is a good choice for companies that want predictable pricing and a straightforward onboarding process.

alternative

Oyster

is built for distributed teams and emphasizes a candidate‑friendly experience. It provides EOR services in more than 180 countries, along with tools for contract management, time tracking, and equity administration. Oyster works well for companies that prioritize global benefits and employee self‑service.

Try Oyster →
alternative

Papaya Global

combines EOR with a global payroll platform that handles payments in over 160 countries. Its strength lies in consolidating multiple payroll entities into one dashboard, making it ideal for companies that already have in‑country subsidiaries. Papaya also offers strong compliance features and real‑time reporting.

alternative

Rippling

integrates EOR with its all‑in‑one HR and IT platform. You can manage payroll, devices, apps, and benefits for international employees from the same system. Rippling suits companies that want unified employee management across both domestic and international teams.

Try Rippling →
alternative

Multiplier

provides EOR services in over 150 countries with a focus on fast onboarding and local expertise. It supports both full‑time employees and contractors, handling contracts, payroll, tax filings, and benefits. Multiplier is a solid option for companies that need quick setup and local market know‑how without a large upfront commitment.

How they compare

Each alternative has a different strength. Remote offers flat fees and simplicity. Oyster emphasizes employee experience and broad coverage. Papaya Global excels at global payroll consolidation. Rippling adds device and app management. Multiplier provides fast local setup. Your choice depends on whether you need transparent pricing, integrated tools, payroll consolidation, or quick expansion into new countries. No single provider is best for every company, so evaluate trial accounts or demos based on your specific country list, contractor vs. employee mix, and budget.
