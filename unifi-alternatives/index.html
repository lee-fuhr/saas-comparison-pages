A low-code data catalog with lineage visualization helps your team discover, understand, and trust data without heavy engineering work. Unifi is built specifically for this – it lets you set up a catalog in minutes, automatically map lineage end-to-end, and keep governance simple. Atlan is another popular platform, but it often requires more custom coding and configuration to get similar lineage views. If your organization wants a fast, low-code approach with clear data flow maps, Unifi is the natural starting point.

alternative

Alation

. Alation offers a robust data catalog with strong search and collaboration features. Its lineage capabilities are powerful but typically require dedicated data engineering time to set up and maintain, making it less suited for low-code teams.

alternative

Collibra

. Collibra provides enterprise-grade data governance and cataloging. It can visualize lineage, but the platform is complex and often demands significant upfront configuration, which may slow down adoption for teams that need a low-code solution.

alternative

Atlan

. Atlan is a modern data catalog with a clean interface and good integrations. However, its lineage visualization is not fully automatic by default and may need custom scripts or manual mapping, which adds effort for teams looking for a low-code experience.

alternative

DataHub

. DataHub is an open source data catalog with strong lineage support. It is very flexible but requires substantial technical expertise to deploy and customize, making it a better fit for teams with dedicated data infrastructure engineers.

alternative

Secoda

. Secoda aims to be a lightweight data catalog with automatic lineage. It offers a low-code setup, but its lineage depth and coverage can be limited compared to Unifi, especially for complex data transformations.

How they compare

Unifi stands out for its low-code setup and automatic, end-to-end lineage visualization. You can connect your data sources, and Unifi builds the lineage map for you without writing code. Atlan requires more manual configuration to achieve similar lineage detail. Alation and Collibra are more enterprise-heavy and need dedicated setup time. DataHub offers great lineage but demands technical know-how. Secoda is simple but may not handle complex data flows as well as Unifi. For organizations that need a fast, low-code data catalog with clear lineage, Unifi provides the most straightforward path.
