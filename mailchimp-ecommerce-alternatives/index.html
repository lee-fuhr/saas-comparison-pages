Best Mailchimp alternatives

Mailchimp is a popular email marketing platform, but as your online store grows, its pricing or eCommerce features might not fit your needs. Many store owners find that Mailchimp’s default templates, limited automation for buyers, or cost increases after you pass a certain subscriber count push them to look elsewhere. This article covers four strong Mailchimp eCommerce alternatives that are built specifically for online stores and offer deeper integration with your shopping cart data.

alternative

Klaviyo

is designed for eCommerce from the ground up, with powerful segmentation based on purchase behavior and browsing history. It connects directly to Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms, letting you send automated flows like abandoned cart emails or post purchase follow ups without manual effort. Many growing brands choose Klaviyo because it scales with your revenue rather than your subscriber count, and its reporting focuses on metrics that matter to online stores.

alternative

Privy

combines email marketing with onsite pop ups and exit intent offers to capture more subscribers and drive conversions. It is a good fit if you want an all in one tool that also handles sms campaigns and social proof notifications. While its email automation is simpler than Klaviyo, Privy’s strength is helping you build your list quickly and then retarget those leads with targeted campaigns.

alternative

Omnisend

specializes in omnichannel campaigns that include email, sms, push notifications, and even Facebook ads. It offers pre built automation workflows specifically for eCommerce scenarios like welcome series, order follow ups, and product recommendations. Omnisend is often more affordable than Klaviyo for smaller stores, and its drag and drop editor makes it easy to create multi step journeys without coding.

alternative

Drip

focuses on advanced customer relationship management and deep personalization, making it a good choice if you want to send highly tailored messages based on customer lifecycles. It connects with popular eCommerce platforms and gives you tools to score leads, tag contacts, and build complex automations. Drip is particularly useful for stores that sell higher priced products or have a longer sales cycle where nurturing is critical.
