If you run a restaurant or hospitality venue, you know that scheduling staff is one of the trickiest parts of the job. 7shifts is a popular choice for shift management, but it might not be the perfect fit for every business. Maybe you need a lower price, different features, or simpler setup. This article covers four strong 7shifts alternatives: Deputy, When I Work, Homebase, and Shiftboard. Each one brings something different to the table, so you can find the tool that matches your team’s workflow.

alternative

Deputy

is a workforce management platform that works well for both small cafes and large hotel groups. It offers smart scheduling, time tracking, and payroll integration, all with a clean interface that staff can use on their phones. Deputy also includes real-time alerts when someone clocks in late, which helps keep a busy kitchen on schedule.

alternative

When I Work

focuses on simplicity and ease of use, making it a good choice for venues with many part-time or seasonal workers. You can build schedules quickly with drag-and-drop, send shift reminders, and let staff swap shifts without manager approval. The app also includes a basic time clock and team messaging, though advanced features like labor forecasting cost extra.

alternative

Homebase

is built for small to medium businesses and combines scheduling, time clocks, and hiring tools in one system. It offers free basic scheduling for a single location, which is great for a new restaurant testing the waters. Paid plans add things like team communication, task lists, and overtime alerts, all at a lower monthly cost than 7shifts.

alternative

Shiftboard

is designed for larger, more complex operations such as hotels, event spaces, or multi-site restaurant groups. It handles high volume scheduling, shift bidding, and strong compliance tracking for wage and hour rules. Shiftboard’s reporting is more detailed than most alternatives, but it comes with a steeper learning curve and a higher price point.

Frequently asked questions

Which 7shifts alternative is best for a small bar with only 10 employees?
Homebase is a strong choice because it offers a free plan for a single location, so you can try scheduling, time clocks, and team chat without spending anything. The simple setup works well for a small team that does not need advanced reporting.
Can I import my existing schedule from 7shifts into these alternatives?
Most alternatives accept a CSV or Excel file export from 7shifts. You can usually export your schedule from 7shifts as a spreadsheet and then import it into Deputy, When I Work, Homebase, or Shiftboard during setup. Check each tool’s import guide for specific formatting requirements.
