Businesses evaluating cloud productivity suites beyond Google Workspace have several strong options. Whether you need better offline tools, advanced privacy controls, or a lower per-user cost, each alternative offers distinct strengths. This article reviews five major replacements: Microsoft 365, Zoho Workplace, Fastmail, Proton Business, and Lark.

alternative

Microsoft 365

. This is the most established alternative with familiar desktop apps like Word, Excel, and Outlook. It offers deep integration with Windows and enterprise-grade security, but its pricing can be higher than Google Workspace for similar storage.

alternative

Zoho Workplace

. Designed for small to medium businesses, Zoho Workplace bundles email, docs, and over 40 productivity apps at a very competitive price. Its interface feels less polished than Google’s, but the value per dollar is hard to beat for lean teams.

Try Zoho Workplace →
alternative

Fastmail

. Fastmail focuses on fast, reliable email and calendar with a strong emphasis on privacy and no advertising. It lacks document editing and many collaborative features, making it best for businesses that mainly need secure email.

alternative

Proton Business

. Proton brings its renowned end-to-end encryption to business email, calendar, and file storage. It is ideal for companies that require maximum privacy, but its suite is still growing and has fewer collaboration tools than mainstream options.

Try Proton Business →
alternative

Lark

. Originally developed by ByteDance, Lark combines messaging, video conferencing, docs, and project management in one platform. It excels at real-time collaboration and has a generous free tier, though its user base is smaller outside Asia.

How they compare

Microsoft 365 is the strongest match for teams that need desktop Office apps and advanced admin controls. Zoho Workplace offers the best value for budget-conscious small businesses. Fastmail and Proton Business prioritize privacy and simplicity, with Proton adding encrypted storage and calendar. Lark provides an all-in-one collaboration experience with built-in chat and video, similar to a mix of Slack and Google Workspace. Your choice should depend on your primary need: traditional office tools, low cost, privacy, or an integrated workspace.

Frequently asked questions

What is the easiest alternative to switch to from Google Workspace?
Microsoft 365 has the simplest migration path for email and documents because many users are already familiar with Outlook and Word. Its admin tools also offer direct import options for Google data.
Which alternative offers the best privacy protection for business data?
Proton Business provides end-to-end encryption for email, calendar, and files, meaning Proton cannot read your data. Fastmail also has strong privacy policies but does not offer the same level of encryption on stored content.
