Best Divvy alternatives

Divvy is a popular expense management platform that offers virtual cards and real time tracking. However, some SMBs find that Divvy’s reporting features or card controls don’t fit their exact workflow. If you need a tool that integrates differently, offers more automation, or supports international spending, exploring alternatives can help you find a better match for your team.

alternative

Expensify

focuses on receipt scanning and expense report automation, which removes manual data entry for your team. It provides virtual cards through its Expensify Card program, plus real time tracking and policy controls. Expensify is a strong choice if your main need is straightforward expense reporting with optional card spending.

alternative

Ramp

offers corporate cards with real time spending limits and automatic receipt matching. Its platform analyzes transaction patterns to suggest cost savings, such as unused subscriptions. Ramp works well for SMBs that want built in procurement controls and insights without additional software.

alternative

Spendesk

is designed for companies that manage multiple budgets and approval workflows. It gives each employee a virtual card with preset spending limits and real time balance updates. Spendesk also supports prepaid cards for one time or recurring expenses, making it suitable for teams that need strict budget oversight.

How they compare

Divvy combines virtual cards with expense management and offers bill pay features. Expensify leans heavily on receipt automation and travel booking but has fewer card controls. Ramp provides smart card limits and savings insights, while Spendesk emphasizes budget specific cards and approval chains. Choose based on whether you prioritize reporting automation, cost optimization, or tight budget controls.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use real time expense tracking with virtual cards in all of these alternatives?
Yes, each alternative offers real time tracking and virtual cards. Expensify, Ramp, and Spendesk all update spending data immediately and let you set rules that block or approve transactions as they happen.
Which alternative is easiest to set up for a small team without a dedicated finance person?
Ramp and Spendesk both have simple onboarding with minimal manual configuration. Expensify also works well for small teams, especially if you already use its receipt scanning features. Each tool provides support to help you get started quickly.
