Best Salesforce alternatives

If your mid-market team finds Salesforce too complex or expensive, you are not alone. Many growing businesses need a CRM that is simpler to set up, easier to use, and more affordable. This article compares five popular Salesforce alternatives: HubSpot, Pipedrive, Close, Zoho CRM, and Freshsales. Each one offers a different balance of features, pricing, and ease of use, so you can find the right fit for your sales process without the overhead of a full enterprise platform.

alternative

HubSpot

. HubSpot’s free CRM is a strong starting point for mid-market teams that want marketing, sales, and service tools in one place. It scales easily with paid tiers, but costs can rise quickly as you add advanced features or more users.

Try HubSpot →
alternative

Pipedrive

. Pipedrive is built around a visual sales pipeline that makes it easy to track deals and tasks. It is known for its simple interface and automation, making it a good choice for teams that want a straightforward, deal-focused CRM without extra complexity.

Try Pipedrive →
alternative

Close

. Close is designed for outbound sales teams that make a high volume of calls and emails. It includes built-in calling, SMS, and email sequencing, so you can work directly inside the CRM without switching tools.

Try Close →
alternative

Zoho CRM

. Zoho CRM offers a wide range of features at a low price, including AI-powered insights and integrations with other Zoho apps. It can be customized heavily, which is great for teams that need flexibility but may require more setup time.

alternative

Freshsales

. Freshsales provides an intuitive interface with built-in phone, email, and lead scoring. It is part of the Freshworks suite, so it integrates easily with their support and marketing products, and its pricing is transparent and budget friendly.

Try Freshsales →
