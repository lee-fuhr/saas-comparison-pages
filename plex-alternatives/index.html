Best Plex alternatives

Plex is a well known manufacturing execution system (MES) that helps manufacturers manage production, quality, and inventory in real time. But as the manufacturing technology landscape evolves, some teams find that Plex no longer fits their budget, integration needs, or industry specific workflows. If you are evaluating MES tools for your facility, it is wise to compare Plex with other platforms that offer deep MES functionality and seamless integration with enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems. Below we look at four strong alternatives: Epicor, Infor CloudSuite, DELMIAworks, and IQMS.

alternative

Epicor

. Epicor provides a comprehensive MES module within its ERP suite, designed for discrete and mixed mode manufacturers. It offers real time shop floor visibility, production scheduling, and quality management that can be tightly integrated with financials and supply chain data. Many manufacturers appreciate Epicor’s flexibility to configure workflows without heavy customization.

alternative

Infor CloudSuite

. Infor CloudSuite delivers a cloud based MES that connects production execution to broader business processes through its multi tenant platform. It is well suited for complex manufacturing environments that need strong traceability, compliance tracking, and Industry 4.0 capabilities. The system uses a modern user interface and can scale across multiple plants with a unified data model.

alternative

DELMIAworks

. DELMIAworks, part of Dassault Systèmes, offers a manufacturing operations management (MOM) solution that includes MES, quality, and planning tools. It is especially strong in industries like aerospace, automotive, and industrial equipment where detailed process control and digital twin integration are required. The platform supports both on premise and cloud deployment, giving manufacturers deployment flexibility.

alternative

IQMS

. IQMS, now part of Dassault Systèmes as well, is a manufacturing ERP with a robust built in MES for discrete and process manufacturers. It focuses on real time production monitoring, scheduling, and traceability, and it includes features tailored to plastics, packaging, and metal forming. IQMS is often chosen by mid sized manufacturers that want a single system to handle both ERP and MES needs.

How they compare

Plex remains a strong cloud MES option for manufacturers that want easy deployment and a wide ecosystem. Epicor offers deeper ERP integration for discrete industries. Infor CloudSuite excels in multi plant environments with complex compliance needs. DELMIAworks is ideal for companies that already use Dassault tools or need advanced simulation capabilities. IQMS is a solid all in one ERP/MES for mid sized process and discrete manufacturers. The best choice depends on your industry, plant complexity, and whether you prefer a unified ERP/MES or a standalone MES that connects to your existing ERP.
