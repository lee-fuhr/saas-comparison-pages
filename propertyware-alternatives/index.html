If you manage a growing portfolio of single-family rentals, you may have started with Propertyware. It offers solid features for landlords, but as your business scales you might need software that is more intuitive, more affordable, or better aligned with single-family workflows. This article covers four strong Propertyware alternatives: Buildium, AppFolio, Rent Manager, and TenantCloud. Each is designed to help property managers handle a rising number of units without losing control or efficiency.

alternative

Buildium

. Buildium is a popular choice for residential property managers, including those focused on single-family homes. It provides accounting, tenant screening, maintenance tracking, and owner portals, with pricing that scales predictably as you add units.

alternative

AppFolio

. AppFolio targets professional property management firms and offers automation features like online leasing, rent collection, and maintenance request tracking. Its interface is modern and user-friendly, making it a good fit for managers who want to reduce manual tasks.

alternative

Rent Manager

. Rent Manager is a highly customizable platform that works well for single-family portfolios of all sizes. It includes advanced accounting, marketing tools, and a built-in website builder, though its depth can require a longer setup time.

alternative

TenantCloud

. TenantCloud is a budget-friendly option for smaller to mid-sized portfolios. It covers core needs such as listing syndication, online payments, and maintenance management, with a free tier for managers with fewer than 75 units.

How they compare

Propertyware is strong for large-scale single-family management but can feel dated in its interface and pricing. Buildium offers a balanced mix of features and support for growing portfolios. AppFolio excels in automation and modern design but may be more expensive for smaller teams. Rent Manager provides deep customization but requires more time to learn. TenantCloud is the most affordable entry point, though it lacks some advanced reporting and accounting features. Choose based on your portfolio size, budget, and need for automation versus control.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a property manager with 50 single-family units?
TenantCloud offers a free plan for up to 75 units and covers essential features like online payments and maintenance requests, making it a cost-effective choice. Buildium also works well and gives you room to scale without a steep learning curve.
Does any of these alternatives offer a free trial or demo?
Yes, all four tools provide free trials or live demos. Buildium, AppFolio, and Rent Manager typically offer a 14-day trial, while TenantCloud has a free tier indefinitely for smaller portfolios. Check their websites for current offers.
