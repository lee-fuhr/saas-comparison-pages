Growth marketers often need to enrich raw contact or company data to personalize campaigns and score leads. Two popular options are Clearbit Enrich and ZoomInfo Enrich. Clearbit Enrich focuses on real-time API enrichment, returning data in milliseconds as a user fills out a form or visits a page. ZoomInfo Enrich provides bulk data services, letting you upload lists and receive enriched records with deep firmographic and technographic details. Choosing between them depends on whether your priority is instant, event-driven enrichment or comprehensive, batch-processed data sets.

alternative

ZoomInfo Enrich

. ZoomInfo Enrich offers bulk batch enrichment where you upload a CSV of contacts or companies and receive matched records with extensive firmographics, technographics, and direct dials. It excels when you need to enrich large existing lists or build complete account profiles for ABM programs. ZoomInfo’s data is sourced from a proprietary database, providing depth but with less real-time flexibility than an API-first solution.

How they compare

- **Enrichment method**: Clearbit Enrich is a real-time API, ZoomInfo Enrich is a batch upload service. - **Speed**: Clearbit returns data in under 100 milliseconds, ZoomInfo takes minutes to hours for large batches. - **Use case fit**: Clearbit fits event-driven enrichment (forms, signups, lead scoring), ZoomInfo fits list cleaning and account-level profiling. - **Data coverage**: Clearbit focuses on email, company name, domain, role, and social profiles. ZoomInfo includes direct dials, technographics, and intent signals from its database. - **Pricing model**: Clearbit charges per lookup or monthly subscription based on API calls. ZoomInfo charges per record or annual subscription with credits. - **Integration ease**: Clearbit offers native integrations with marketing automation and CRM tools. ZoomInfo integrates with Salesforce and HubSpot but often requires an export/import step for bulk work.
