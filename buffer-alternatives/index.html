Best Buffer alternatives

Buffer is a popular social media scheduling tool, but it may not fit every budget or workflow. Solo creators and small teams often look for buffer alternatives that offer more features, better pricing, or simpler interfaces. This article compares five strong options: Hootsuite, Later, Sprout Social, Metricool, and Publer. Each has its own strengths for scheduling posts, analyzing performance, and collaborating with a small team.

alternative

Hootsuite

. Hootsuite supports a wide range of social networks and includes a unified dashboard for scheduling and monitoring. Its free plan covers up to two social accounts, making it a good starting point for solo creators, though advanced analytics and team features require paid tiers.

Try Hootsuite →
alternative

Later

. Later focuses on visual content, especially for Instagram, with a drag-and-drop calendar and media library. It offers a free plan for one profile and limited posts, plus paid plans that include linkin.bio and basic analytics, ideal for creators who prioritize image and video scheduling.

Try Later →
alternative

Sprout Social

. Sprout Social provides robust analytics, smart inbox features, and team collaboration tools. It is more expensive than other alternatives, so it suits small teams with a budget who need deep reporting and customer engagement across multiple networks.

Try Sprout Social →
alternative

Metricool

. Metricool combines scheduling, analytics, and ad management in one platform at a competitive price. Its free tier includes up to 50 posts per month and one user, making it a cost-effective alternative for solo creators and small teams who want performance data without a big investment.

alternative

Publer

. Publer offers a generous free plan for three social accounts and unlimited posts, plus features like recycling posts and collaboration. It is simple to use and includes Canva integration, which appeals to creators who want flexibility without paying for a premium subscription.
