Compliance and reporting teams need a single source of truth for financial statements, regulatory filings, and operational data. Workiva is widely used for its controlled workflow, audit trail, and real-time collaboration, but it may not fit every organization’s budget, data volume, or integration needs. This article reviews Workiva alongside four strong alternatives and answers common questions to help you choose the right platform.

alternative

Planful

combines financial planning, consolidation, and reporting in one cloud platform. It offers strong driver-based modeling and automated data collection, which helps compliance teams reduce manual work. Planful’s reporting module supports XBRL tagging and SOX controls, making it a practical fit for companies that want integrated planning and compliance.

alternative

Adaptive Insights

focuses on agile financial planning and analysis with a user-friendly spreadsheet-like interface. Its reporting capabilities include dashboards, variance analysis, and drill-downs, though compliance-specific features like audit trails are less robust than Workiva’s. Adaptive Insights works well for teams that prioritize quick forecasting and collaboration over heavy document control.

alternative

Anaplan

is a connected planning platform that handles large, complex data models across finance, supply chain, and sales. Its flexibility allows compliance teams to build custom reporting frameworks, but setting up controlled workflows and linking disclosures can require more technical effort. Anaplan is best for organizations that need to model many scenarios and already have strong analytics support.

How they compare

Workiva excels at document-centric reporting with embedded controls and linked data, making it ideal for teams that produce filings, board presentations, and audit-ready statements. Vena tools offers similar workflow controls but relies on Excel familiarity, while Planful, Adaptive Insights, and Anaplan lean more toward planning and analysis. For compliance teams, Workiva and Vena are the strongest options when regulatory filing and audit trail are top priorities. The other tools are better suited when scenario modeling and forecasting are equally important.

Frequently asked questions

Which tool is best for automating SOX compliance and internal control reporting?
Workiva and Vena tools both provide detailed audit trails, version history, and approval workflows that support SOX compliance. Workiva is stronger for linking financial data directly to disclosures, while Vena’s Excel integration may be easier for teams already using spreadsheets for control testing.
Can I use Planful or Anaplan for external financial filings like 10-K or 10-Q?
Planful supports XBRL tagging and can produce basic statements, but its primary strength is planning and consolidation. Anaplan lacks native filing features and would require a separate tool for document creation and regulatory formatting. Workiva and Vena are more complete tools for end-to-end filing workflows.
