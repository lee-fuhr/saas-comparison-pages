Best ReferenceHero alternatives

ReferenceHero helps companies manage customer testimonials and case study requests. But you might need a different approach if your team wants deeper advocacy automation, referral incentives, or more flexible display options. Below we review five alternatives that serve similar goals, from reference management to referral marketing. Each tool focuses on capturing, organizing, and showcasing customer success stories in its own way.

alternative

ReferenceEdge

. This platform specializes in automating reference and case study requests so sales and marketing teams can find the right customer fast. It integrates with CRM systems to track customer willingness and availability, then surfaces the best matches for each request. ReferenceEdge also offers a public testimonial hub where you can display approved quotes and video clips.

alternative

Influitive

. Built around advocacy programs, Influitive lets you engage your happiest customers with challenges, rewards, and exclusive content. The tool captures success stories organically as customers share their experiences in response to activities. You can then display these stories on a branded community hub or use them in sales enablement materials.

alternative

Referral Rock

. While focused on referral marketing, Referral Rock also helps you collect and showcase customer success stories through its testimonial and review request features. You can set up automated email campaigns that ask satisfied customers for a short quote, then publish those quotes on your website or landing pages. It works well for companies that want both referrals and story collection from a single tool.

alternative

Extole

. Extole is a referral and advocacy platform that allows you to turn loyal customers into brand ambassadors. You can capture success stories by prompting advocates during the referral flow to share their experience. The platform then organizes those stories into a gallery or widget that you can embed on your site, helping you combine advocacy with reference content.

alternative

Champion

. This tool is designed to identify and mobilize your best customers for references, case studies, and reviews. Champion uses a scoring system to find who is most likely to say yes, then automates outreach and follow-ups. The resulting success stories can be stored in a searchable library and shared with sales teams or on your marketing site.

How they compare

ReferenceEdge is closest to ReferenceHero for pure reference management and case study requests, while Influitive and Champion lean more toward active advocacy and engagement. Referral Rock and Extole are better if you want to combine customer story collection with referral incentives. Consider your primary need: automated reference matching (ReferenceEdge), community-driven advocacy (Influitive), or full referral programs (Referral Rock, Extole). Champion offers a balance of scoring and automation for customer mobilization.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a small team with limited budget?
Referral Rock often has a lower starting price and simpler setup, making it a good fit for smaller teams that want to collect and display stories without heavy automation. You can start with its testimonial request feature and upgrade as you grow.
Can I display customer stories on my website with these tools?
Yes, most of these tools offer embeddable widgets or public gallery pages. ReferenceEdge and Influitive both provide hosted testimonial hubs, while Referral Rock and Extole let you place quotes or review collections on your own site via code snippets.
