Choosing between Collibra and Alation is a major decision for large enterprises that need a comprehensive data governance suite. Both platforms help you manage data assets, enforce policies, and improve data literacy, but they approach these goals differently. Collibra is built as a full governance hub with workflow automation and business glossary features, while Alation focuses more on data cataloging with strong search and collaboration tools. This article compares the two and also reviews other alternatives to help you find the best fit for your organization.

alternative

Alation

. Alation is a data catalog platform with a strong emphasis on search, data discovery, and user collaboration through features like crowdsourced curation and “Data anywhere” browser extensions. It offers built-in data lineage and a popular “Compliant” data warehouse assessment tool, but its governance capabilities are less out of the box compared to Collibra. Alation suits teams that prioritize self service data access and want to foster a data driven culture.

alternative

Collibra

. Collibra is an enterprise grade data governance platform that provides a central business glossary, policy management, and automated stewardship workflows. It excels at linking data lineage to governance rules, making it easier for compliance teams to enforce regulations like GDPR or CCPA. Large enterprises often choose Collibra when they need a single source of truth for both technical metadata and business definitions.

alternative

Atlan

. Atlan is a modern, cloud native data catalog and governance platform that integrates deeply with modern data stacks like Snowflake, dbt, and Fivetran. It provides automated lineage, a collaborative workspace, and policy enforcement, all with a user interface designed for data engineers and analysts. Atlan appeals to enterprises that want a lightweight yet powerful alternative to traditional governance suites.

alternative

DataHub

. DataHub is an open source metadata platform originally built by LinkedIn and now backed by Acryl Data. It offers flexible ingestion, real time lineage, and a rich search experience, but requires more technical setup and customization than proprietary tools. Large enterprises with strong engineering teams often choose DataHub for its cost effectiveness and ability to tailor the solution to unique data environments.

alternative

Secoda

. Secoda is a lightweight AI powered data catalog that focuses on automating documentation and query generation using natural language. It supports integrations with common warehouses and BI tools, and its governance features include basic lineage and access control. Secoda is best for smaller teams or as a complementary tool, but it may lack the depth needed for strict regulatory compliance at scale.
