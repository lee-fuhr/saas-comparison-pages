Businesses that run large scale live webinars need a platform that can handle high attendance, deliver detailed analytics, and allow full brand customization. Zoom Webinars meets these requirements with enterprise grade features for audiences from hundreds to tens of thousands. Demio is a solid alternative for smaller events but lacks the capacity and advanced analytics that large organizations require.

alternative

Demio

is a user friendly webinar platform built for marketing teams and smaller audiences, typically up to 1,000 live attendees. It offers simple registration pages, automated email reminders, and basic analytics like attendance and poll data. However, Demio does not support the high capacity needs or granular analytics that businesses with thousands of viewers depend on.

How they compare

Zoom Webinars supports audiences up to 50,000 view only attendees with options for panelists and Q&A, while Demio caps at 1,000 live participants. Zoom provides robust analytics including attendee engagement reports, polling results, and post event data exports, whereas Demio offers more limited tracking. For branding, Zoom allows custom registration pages, emails, and in session logos, but Demio provides similar customization within its smaller scale. Zoom also integrates with major CRM and marketing platforms, while Demio integrates with common tools but with fewer options.

Frequently asked questions

Which platform is better for webinars with more than 1,000 attendees?
Zoom Webinars is the clear choice for high capacity events. It supports up to 50,000 view only attendees with enterprise plans, while Demio is limited to 1,000 live participants.
Can I get detailed analytics from both platforms?
Zoom Webinars offers deep analytics such as attendee behavior, polling results, and question logs that can be exported. Demio provides basic attendance and engagement stats but lacks the depth needed for large scale analysis.
