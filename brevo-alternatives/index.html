If you use Brevo for email marketing but are looking for other options, you are not alone. Small businesses often outgrow a platform or need different features like better automation, lower costs, or stronger support. This article covers five solid Brevo alternatives: Mailchimp, SendGrid, ActiveCampaign, Moosend, and Omnisend. Each one has its own strengths, so you can pick the best fit for your business.

alternative

Mailchimp

is one of the most popular email marketing tools because of its user-friendly drag-and-drop builder and large template library. It works well for small businesses that want a simple way to send campaigns and track basic analytics, though advanced automation can get pricey.

alternative

SendGrid

focuses on transactional emails and deliverability, making it a strong choice if you send a lot of order confirmations or password resets. It offers a generous free tier, but its design tools are not as polished for marketing newsletters.

alternative

ActiveCampaign

excels at marketing automation and CRM features, giving you powerful ways to segment contacts and trigger personalized sequences. It is ideal if you want to build complex workflows without needing a separate CRM tool.

Try ActiveCampaign →
alternative

Moosend

is a budget-friendly option with unlimited emails on its paid plans and an easy-to-use automation builder. It is a good fit for small businesses that need solid features without a high price tag, though its integrations are less extensive than some competitors.

alternative

Omnisend

is built specifically for ecommerce, with pre-built automations for abandoned carts, welcome series, and product recommendations. If you run an online store, Omnisend can help you recover lost sales and boost repeat purchases more easily than general email tools.

Frequently asked questions

Which Brevo alternative is cheapest for a small business?
Moosend offers the lowest starting price for paid plans (about $9 per month) and includes unlimited emails. Mailchimp and SendGrid also have free tiers, but those come with sending limits and feature restrictions.
Can I switch from Brevo to one of these tools easily?
Yes, all five tools allow you to import your contact lists and templates. Most offer guides or migration support. Keep in mind you will need to update any signup forms or API integrations in your website or store after you switch.
