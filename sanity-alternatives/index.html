Best Sanity alternatives

Sanity is a flexible headless CMS with a customizable studio, real-time collaboration, and structured content. It works well for Jamstack and Next.js projects, but some teams look for alternatives due to its pricing model, the need for self-hosting the studio, or a desire for a more traditional editor experience. This article covers five popular Sanity alternatives: Contentful, Strapi, Storyblok, Prismic, and Payload CMS. Each option has its own strengths, so we compare them to help you choose the right fit for your next project.

alternative

Contentful

is a SaaS headless CMS with a strong API, a rich content model, and a visual editor for non-technical users. It offers generous free tier limits and built-in asset management, but costs can rise quickly as your team and content volume grow.

Try Contentful →
alternative

Strapi

is an open source self-hosted CMS that gives you full control over your data, infrastructure, and customization. It provides a user-friendly admin panel, supports plugins, and works well with Next.js, though you need to manage hosting and updates yourself.

Try Strapi →
alternative

Storyblok

stands out with its visual editor that allows you to create and preview components directly on the page. It uses a block-based content structure and a flexible API, making it a strong choice for teams that want designers and editors to work closely together.

Try Storyblok →
alternative

Prismic

offers a simple content modeling approach with slices and a focus on editorial workflows. It includes a built-in preview system and works seamlessly with Next.js through its official SDK, but its free tier has limited content entries.

alternative

Payload CMS

is a developer-first headless CMS that runs on Node.js and can be self-hosted or deployed on platforms like Vercel. It provides a TypeScript-native API, a customizable admin panel, and supports both REST and GraphQL without any limits on content or users.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free alternative to Sanity that works with Next.js?
Yes. Strapi is a free open source option you can self-host, and Prismic offers a free tier with limited content entries. Contentful and Storyblok also have generous free tiers. The best choice depends on your project size and whether you need self-hosting.
Which Sanity alternative is best for a small team with no dedicated backend developer?
Contentful or Storyblok are good options for small teams. They are fully managed SaaS platforms, so you do not need to manage servers or infrastructure. Their visual editors also make it easier for non-developers to create and manage content.
