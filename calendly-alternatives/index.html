Calendly is a popular scheduling tool, but it may not suit every consultant or team. You might need more flexibility, better pricing, or specific features like round robin, custom branding, or integrations. Here are five Calendly alternatives that offer different strengths for professionals who book meetings online.

alternative

Cal.com

. This open source platform gives you full control over your data and scheduling logic. It supports team features like collective availability and routing forms, and it integrates with many calendars and video tools. Cal.com is ideal for consultants and teams who want a self hosted option or a more customizable experience.

Try Cal.com →
alternative

SavvyCal

. SavvyCal focuses on effortless scheduling with a lightweight interface and polling capabilities that let invitees suggest times. It includes a built in time zone overlay and priority routing to avoid double bookings. Consultants appreciate its simplicity and the ability to send one click scheduling links without overwhelming recipients.

Try SavvyCal →
alternative

TidyCal

. TidyCal offers a low cost paid plan with unlimited events and payment integrations through Stripe and PayPal. It provides custom meeting durations, buffer times, and email reminders. This is a good choice for solo consultants who need a budget friendly alternative to Calendly without sacrificing core features.

Doodle

. Doodle is best known for group polling to find a meeting time that works for multiple participants. It also offers a simple booking page and integrates with common calendar apps. Doodle works well for teams that frequently coordinate with external groups or need to quickly compare many time options.

How they compare

Cal.com is the strongest open source option for teams who want to self host. SavvyCal excels in simplicity and polling for one on one scheduling. TidyCal provides the most affordable paid plan with unlimited events. Acuity offers the most advanced client management features. Doodle is the go to tool for group time polling. Each tool has a different price point and feature set, so your choice depends on whether you need open source, client intake, or group coordination.

Frequently asked questions

Which Calendly alternative is best for a team with multiple members?
Cal.com and Acuity both support team scheduling. Cal.com offers round robin and collective availability for free, while Acuity includes more client management and payment features. For a simple budget team, TidyCal also works but with fewer team specific options.
Are there any free Calendly alternatives that still have good features?
Cal.com has a generous free plan with unlimited events, team scheduling, and integrations. Doodle’s basic plan is also free for group polling and a simple booking page. Free versions of other tools may limit the number of events or active calendars.
