Best Dashlane alternatives

Dashlane is a popular password manager known for its built-in VPN and password health reports. However, some users look for Dashlane alternatives because of pricing changes or feature preferences. If you need a tool that checks weak passwords and comes with a VPN, there are three strong options: 1Password, LastPass, and NordPass. Each offers a different balance of security, ease of use, and extra features.

alternative

1Password

does not include a built-in VPN, but it provides a thorough Watchtower feature for password health reports. Watchtower alerts you to compromised passwords, weak credentials, and two-factor authentication options. If you already have a separate VPN service, 1Password is a solid choice for its strong security design.

alternative

LastPass

offers a built-in VPN through a partnership with Hotspot Shield, but only on its Premium and Families plans. Its Security Dashboard gives you a clear password health score and flags reused or weak passwords. Keep in mind that LastPass has had past security incidents, so some users may prefer other options.

alternative

NordPass

includes a built-in VPN as part of its premium plans, making it a direct alternative to Dashlane for users who want both in one subscription. It also provides a Password Health tool that checks for weak, reused, and old passwords. NordPass is developed by the same team behind NordVPN, so the VPN integration works smoothly.

How they compare

Dashlane stands out for its all-in-one approach with VPN and password health reports, but its price is higher. Among the alternatives, NordPass most closely matches that combined feature set. 1Password and LastPass each offer strong health reports, but only LastPass has a limited built-in VPN. The best choice depends on whether you prioritize VPN integration or a simpler password manager with robust health checks.

Frequently asked questions

Which Dashlane alternative has the best VPN integration?
NordPass offers the most seamless built-in VPN, since it is made by the same company as NordVPN. LastPass also includes a VPN, but it is provided through a third party and only on certain plans.
Do all of these alternatives provide password health reports?
Yes, 1Password, LastPass, and NordPass all include password health reports that check for weak, reused, or compromised passwords. The depth of the report varies, but each tool gives you a clear overview of your password security.
