Best HelpScout alternatives

If your team relies on email based ticketing and wants a clean, simple interface, you have likely used HelpScout. It shines for shared inboxes and ease of use. But maybe you need better reporting, deeper integrations, or a different pricing model. This article covers three popular HelpScout alternatives – Intercom, Freshdesk, and Zendesk – and helps you decide which one fits your email first approach without unnecessary complexity.

alternative

Intercom

. Intercom focuses heavily on live chat and in app messaging, but it also handles email based tickets well through its inbox. Its interface is modern and minimal, yet it can feel overwhelming if you only need simple email support without chatbot or automation layers. For teams that want a unified inbox plus customer data, Intercom is a powerful choice.

Try Intercom →
alternative

Freshdesk

. Freshdesk offers a straightforward email ticketing system with a clean UI that mimics a shared inbox, similar to HelpScout. It includes smart automations and a free tier that makes it attractive for smaller teams. The trade off is that some advanced features are tucked behind extra paid modules.

Try Freshdesk →
alternative

Zendesk

. Zendesk is a full featured help desk with robust email based ticketing and a simple enough interface for beginners. It scales well from small teams to large enterprises but often requires more setup and configuration than HelpScout. For teams that anticipate growth and need deep reporting, Zendesk is a reliable alternative.

Frequently asked questions

I only use email for customer support. Which alternative is the easiest to switch to from HelpScout?
Freshdesk tends to be the easiest switch because its interface keeps the shared inbox feel. Intercom and Zendesk can both handle email, but they add extra layers (like chat or macros) that may feel unnecessary out of the box.
Does any alternative offer a free plan like HelpScout used to?
Freshdesk offers a free tier that includes basic email ticketing for up to three agents. Intercom and Zendesk do not have a permanent free plan, only limited trials.
