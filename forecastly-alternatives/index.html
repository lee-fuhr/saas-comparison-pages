Best Forecastly alternatives

If you are a small or medium business looking for a simple, spreadsheet-style forecasting tool with AI insights, you have likely come across Forecastly. It is a popular choice for teams that want to move away from manual Excel work but still keep the familiar row-and-column view. However, there are several other tools on the market that can match or improve on that experience. This article covers the top alternatives to Forecastly: Clari, Aviso, BoostUp, People.ai, and SetSail. Each one brings a slightly different approach to predicting revenue, managing pipelines, and automating data entry. By the end you will have a clear picture of which tool best fits your team’s size, budget, and workflow.

alternative

Clari

. Clari is a revenue intelligence platform that focuses on trust in your pipeline data. It uses AI to identify deal risks and give you a more accurate forecast without extra manual work. While powerful for larger sales teams, it can be more complex than a simple spreadsheet style, so smaller SMBs should evaluate the learning curve.

alternative

Aviso

. Aviso offers AI-driven forecasting that integrates with CRM data to predict revenue outcomes. It provides a clean, visual dashboard but still allows you to drill into individual deals and time periods. For SMBs that want a balance between automation and hands-on control, Aviso is a solid choice.

alternative

BoostUp

. BoostUp specializes in cleaning up CRM data and then applying AI to forecast with higher accuracy. It highlights missing fields, overdue tasks, and stale deals so you can focus on what matters. This tool is great for teams that struggle with messy data and want a lightweight, spreadsheet-like view of their pipeline.

alternative

People.ai

. People.ai captures every sales interaction from email, calendar, and phone to automatically build activity data. Its forecasting engine uses this detailed history to predict which deals will close. Because it collects data at a granular level, it works best for teams that already use robust sales engagement tools and need deep insights.

alternative

SetSail

. SetSail is designed to measure rep performance and forecast based on behavior, not just deal stage. It uses AI to score activities and closes the gap between what reps do and what gets across the finish line. For SMBs that want to coach reps and improve forecasting at the same time, it is a strong alternative.
