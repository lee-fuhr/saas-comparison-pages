Many businesses that started with ADP payroll are now looking for alternatives. You might find ADP’s interface clunky, its pricing unclear, or its support slow. This article covers five strong options: Gusto, Rippling, Paychex, Justworks, and Patriot. Each offers better user experience, lower cost, or both for small to midsize companies.

alternative

Gusto

. Gusto is ideal for small businesses that want a clean, friendly interface and automated payroll. It bundles benefits, time tracking, and hiring tools, so you get one simple system. Pricing starts low with a basic plan, making it a budget friendly choice.

alternative

Rippling

. Rippling works best for companies that need to manage payroll, IT, and HR in one platform. It automates onboarding, device management, and app access alongside payroll. The interface is modern and fast, though pricing can increase as you add features.

Try Rippling →
alternative

Paychex

. Paychex is a good fit if you want a full service provider with a local human touch. It offers payroll, HR, and compliance support through dedicated specialists. Pricing varies by customization, so you may need to negotiate for a fair deal.

alternative

Justworks

. Justworks is a PEO (professional employer organization) that handles payroll, benefits, and risk management for growing teams. It simplifies compliance and gives you access to big business health plans. The platform is easy to use, but the all in one model has a higher base cost.

alternative

Patriot

. Patriot is a straightforward, low cost option for micro businesses and startups. It provides basic payroll, tax filing, and optional HR add ons. The interface is no frills, but the price is hard to beat for simple needs.

How they compare

Gusto offers the best UX for small teams, while Rippling excels in tech stack integration. Paychex provides dedicated support, Justworks delivers PEO benefits, and Patriot is the cheapest for basic payroll. Your choice depends on company size, desired features, and whether you need a full PEO or simple payroll.
