BambooHR is a popular HRIS for small and mid-size businesses, but as your company grows beyond around 200 employees you may find its features limiting. You might need stronger payroll integration, global compliance, advanced performance management, or industry-specific tools. This article covers five BambooHR alternatives that are well suited for mid-size companies, each with a different focus.

alternative

Rippling

. Rippling unifies HR, IT, and finance in one platform, making it ideal for mid-size companies that want to manage devices, apps, and payroll alongside employee records. Its automated onboarding and offboarding workflows save time, and it supports US and global payroll with country-specific compliance.

Try Rippling →
alternative

Gusto

. Gusto excels at payroll, benefits administration, and compliance for US-based teams. It is a strong choice if your main need is accurate, automated payroll and easy health insurance setup, though its HRIS features are less comprehensive than BambooHR’s for global or highly structured organizations.

Try Gusto →
alternative

Workday

. Workday is designed for larger enterprises but offers a scalable HCM core that mid-size fast-growing companies can adopt. Its strength is in robust analytics, talent management, and global HR, but the cost and implementation complexity are higher than most alternatives on this list.

alternative

Personio

. Personio targets European mid-size companies with strong localization for German, Austrian, and other EU labor laws. It combines core HR, recruiting, and performance management in one system, making it a top alternative for companies with a significant European workforce.

Try Personio →
alternative

Lattice

. Lattice is purpose built for performance management, goal setting, and employee engagement, not as a full HRIS. It integrates with BambooHR and other payroll systems, making it a good complement if you want to upgrade reviews, 1-on-1s, and feedback loops without replacing your core HR platform.

How they compare

For a mid-size company looking beyond BambooHR, the best choice depends on your primary pain points. Rippling offers the widest feature set (HR, IT, payroll, global) for companies that need one system to rule everything. Gusto is simpler and cheaper but US-only and lighter on HR features. Workday is powerful and scalable but expensive and heavy to implement. Personio is ideal for European compliance and recruiting. Lattice is not an HRIS replacement but the best dedicated performance tool. Consider your budget, geography, and whether you want an all-in-one system or best-in-class modules that integrate.

Frequently asked questions

Which BambooHR alternative is easiest to switch to from BambooHR?
Rippling and Gusto both offer simple migration support and similar user interfaces, making them the easiest transitions for most mid-size teams. Rippling is better if you need to keep IT and HR linked, while Gusto is simpler if payroll is your top priority.
Can I keep BambooHR for core HR and add one of these alternatives for specific features?
Yes. Lattice and Gusto are designed to integrate with BambooHR for payroll and performance. Rippling and Personio work best as standalone replacements, but they also offer APIs to sync with BambooHR during a phased migration.
