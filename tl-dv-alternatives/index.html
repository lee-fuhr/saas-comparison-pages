tl;dv is a popular tool for recording and transcribing Zoom meetings. It helps developers and product teams capture decisions and action items without manual note taking. But you might want a tool that integrates differently, offers better search, or fits your existing workflow. This article explores four solid alternatives: Otter.ai, Fireflies.ai, Grain, and Notion AI. Each brings something unique to simple video note taking within Zoom.

alternative

Otter.ai

. Otter.ai provides live transcription for Zoom meetings and can generate summary notes with action items. It works as a Zoom bot that joins your calls, so you get transcripts and highlights automatically. The free tier offers a limited number of transcription minutes per month, which can be enough for small teams.

alternative

Fireflies.ai

. Fireflies.ai records, transcribes, and enriches your Zoom meetings with searchable notes. It can create soundbites and track key topics using custom filters, making it useful for product teams that review lots of calls. Fireflies also integrates with project management tools like Jira and Notion.

alternative

Grain

. Grain focuses on capturing video highlights from Zoom meetings rather than full transcripts. It lets you clip and share short moments, which is great for user research or design feedback sessions. Grain offers a simple browser extension that runs on Zoom web and desktop clients.

alternative

Notion AI

. Notion AI includes meeting note features within its workspace platform. You can record Zoom calls directly into a Notion page, and the AI will summarize the conversation alongside your existing documents. This works best if your team already uses Notion for project management and knowledge sharing.

How they compare

Otter.ai and Fireflies.ai are strong for full transcription and search, with Fireflies adding richer integrations. Grain excels at short video clips and lightweight note sharing. Notion AI blends meeting notes into your existing document workflow, but it lacks dedicated recording features compared to the others. For a simple video note taking experience within Zoom, choose based on whether you need transcripts, clips, or tight integration with your current tools.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives work without a Zoom bot that joins my meetings?
Yes. Otter.ai, Fireflies.ai, and Grain typically add a bot to your calendar invites or use a browser extension. Notion AI works through a desktop recorder that you start manually. All require some setup but do not need the bot to be physically present in the room.
Do any of these tools offer a free tier for a small team?
Yes. Otter.ai has a free plan with limited transcription hours. Fireflies.ai offers a free tier with limited credits per month. Grain and Notion AI have free options with basic features, though Notion AI’s meeting capabilities require a paid add on after a trial.
