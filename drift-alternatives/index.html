If your B2B company is using Drift for conversational marketing but wants to explore other options, you are not alone. Many teams look for drift alternatives that better fit their budget, feature set, or integration needs. Whether you need advanced routing, native CRM sync, or a simpler chat interface, there are strong competitors worth considering. Below are five platforms that B2B marketers and sales teams often evaluate as Drift replacements.

alternative

Intercom

is a complete customer communication platform that combines live chat, email marketing, and a help desk. It works well for B2B companies that want to automate lead qualification and route conversations to the right sales rep. Intercom’s bots and workflows are powerful but can be expensive for smaller teams.

alternative

Crisp

offers a lightweight, affordable alternative with a clean chat widget and shared inbox. It includes features like chatbots, CRM integrations, and ticketing, making it a solid choice for startups and SMBs. Crisp’s simplicity and lower price point are key reasons B2B teams switch from Drift.

Try Crisp →
alternative

HubSpot Live Chat

is built into the free HubSpot CRM, which makes it ideal for companies already using HubSpot for marketing or sales. The chat tool supports automated responses, meeting scheduling, and seamless contact cookie tracking. It is a strong option for B2B organizations that want an all-in-one platform without paying extra for chat.

alternative

Tidio

combines live chat and chatbots with a visual builder that requires no coding. It integrates with many ecommerce and CRM tools, but its B2B capabilities are expanding. Tidio is a good pick if you need an affordable, easy-to-set-up conversational marketing tool for smaller B2B teams.

alternative

Qualified

is specifically built for B2B revenue teams that rely on Salesforce. It uses pipeline data to identify and engage high-value website visitors in real time. Qualified’s deep Salesforce integration and focus on converting account-level leads make it a direct Drift competitor for enterprise sales.

Frequently asked questions

What is the main reason B2B companies leave Drift for a drift alternative?
The most common reasons are cost, complexity of setup, and limited integration flexibility. Many teams find that Drift’s pricing scales quickly with their needs, and they prefer a simpler or more affordable tool that still handles conversation routing and lead qualification.
Can I migrate my existing chat bots and conversation history from Drift to another platform?
It depends on the platform you choose. Some tools like Intercom and Qualified offer migration assistance or APIs to import data, but there is no universal one-click migration. You will usually need to rebuild bots and export chat logs, then import them into the new system. Always check with your desired tool’s support team for documentation.
