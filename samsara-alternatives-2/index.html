As a fleet manager, you know that preventing accidents and reducing risk is your top priority. Samsara is a popular choice for AI-driven safety analytics, but it is not the only option. If you are evaluating alternatives, you likely want a system that delivers real-time alerts, driver coaching, and clear integration with your existing workflows. This article highlights three strong alternatives – Verizon Connect, Geotab, and GPS Insight – so you can compare their AI safety features and find the best fit for your fleet.

alternative

Verizon Connect

. Verizon Connect combines GPS tracking with advanced AI that detects risky driving behaviors like hard braking, rapid acceleration, and lane departures. Its dashcam and coaching tools give fleet managers actionable insights to reduce accidents and lower insurance costs. The platform also integrates with many third party systems, making it a flexible choice for larger fleets.

alternative

Geotab

. Geotab uses an open platform approach, allowing you to add AI safety apps from its marketplace to match your specific needs. Its hardware and cloud software process driver behavior data in real time, and you can pair it with optional third party dash cameras for video verification. This modular design works well for fleets that want to customize their safety analytics stack.

alternative

GPS Insight

. GPS Insight provides an AI-powered driver scorecard that highlights trends in speeding, idling, and harsh events. Its in-cab alerts give drivers immediate feedback, and the web dashboard lets managers review video clips and coaching recommendations. The system is designed for mid-sized fleets that need a simple, cost effective safety solution without complex setup.

How they compare

Samsara leads with a fully integrated camera and sensor ecosystem that delivers real-time AI alerts directly to your phone. Verizon Connect offers a strong all in one platform with proven insurance savings. Geotab gives you the freedom to pick and choose safety apps from its marketplace. GPS Insight focuses on ease of use and affordability for mid-sized operations. All four provide AI-driven safety analytics, but your choice depends on whether you prefer an all in one solution, a modular system, or a budget friendly option.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use these alternatives with my existing vehicle hardware?
Yes, each option supports a range of plug in OBD devices or hardwired installs. Verizon Connect, Geotab, and GPS Insight are compatible with most light and heavy duty vehicles. Check with each vendor for specific compatibility lists before purchasing.
Do these platforms include video recording for driver coaching?
Most do. Samsara and Verizon Connect offer integrated dash cameras with AI event detection. Geotab requires a third party dashcam for video. GPS Insight offers optional video bundles. All platforms allow you to review clips alongside driver behavior data for coaching sessions.
