Best Surfshark alternatives

If you are shopping for a VPN on a budget and want to connect all your devices, Surfshark is a strong choice because it offers unlimited connections. But you might want to see what else is out there. These three alternatives are also budget-friendly and let you use multiple devices, though not always unlimited.

alternative

NordVPN

allows up to 6 devices at once, and its frequent discount deals make it very affordable. It provides strong security and fast speeds, which is great for streaming or browsing. While 6 connections is less than unlimited, it works for most households.

alternative

ExpressVPN

supports up to 8 devices and is known for its speed and reliability. Its price is higher than the others, but you can often find promotions that lower the cost for budget users. It is a premium option if you can share the 8 slots.

alternative

ProtonVPN

offers up to 10 devices on its paid plans, which is a generous limit for budget users. It focuses on privacy with a transparent company model, and its pricing is competitive. This makes it a solid alternative if you need many connections without paying a lot.

How they compare

Surfshark is the only service with unlimited connections, but its price is still low. NordVPN has a 6 device limit, ExpressVPN has 8, and ProtonVPN has 10. For the best value on a high number of devices, ProtonVPN is a top pick. If you must have unlimited connections, stick with Surfshark.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the cheapest for connecting many devices?
ProtonVPN often has the lowest starting price for a plan that supports up to 10 devices. NordVPN also runs deals that make it very cheap for 6 devices.
Do any of these alternatives offer unlimited device connections like Surfshark?
No, only Surfshark gives you unlimited simultaneous connections. However, ProtonVPN allows up to 10 devices, which covers most families, while NordVPN and ExpressVPN handle smaller groups.
