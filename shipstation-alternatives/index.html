If you are an e-commerce merchant using ShipStation, you may be looking for alternatives that offer better shipping rates or more specialized integrations. ShipStation is a popular shipping platform, but it does not fit every business model. Some merchants need lower costs for high volume shipments, while others require seamless connections with specific sales channels or fulfillment services. In this article, we compare five strong alternatives: ShipBob, Shippo, EasyShip, Ordoro, and Pirate Ship. Each tool has unique strengths that might match your needs better than ShipStation.

alternative

ShipBob

is a fulfillment service that combines warehousing and shipping into one solution. It handles storage, packing, and shipping for your orders, which can save you time and money on rates. If you want to outsource logistics entirely rather than manage shipping software, ShipBob is a strong option.

Shippo

is a multi-carrier shipping platform that offers discounted rates from USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL. It integrates with major e-commerce platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and Etsy. Shippo is often more affordable than ShipStation for small to medium businesses and has a simple pay-as-you-go pricing model.

EasyShip

focuses on international shipping and cross-border e-commerce. It provides access to discounted rates from over 250 carriers and includes features like customs paperwork and tracking. EasyShip is a good choice if you ship globally and need a tool that simplifies international logistics.

Ordoro

is an order management and shipping platform designed for multi-channel sellers. It combines inventory management, dropshipping tools, and shipping discounts in one dashboard. Ordoro shines for merchants who need to manage orders from many sources without upgrading to a full ERP system.

How they compare

ShipBob is best for full fulfillment outsourcing, while Shippo and EasyShip excel at discounted rates and integrations. Ordoro adds inventory and order management for multi-channel sellers. Pirate Ship is the simplest and cheapest option for USPS and UPS flat-rate shipping. All of these tools can be cheaper or more flexible than ShipStation depending on your volume and sales channels.

Frequently asked questions

Which ShipStation alternative has the lowest shipping rates?
Pirate Ship typically offers the lowest USPS rates with no monthly fees. For discounted rates across multiple carriers like UPS and FedEx, Shippo and EasyShip are also strong contenders. Rates depend on your volume, so you should compare quotes from a few options.
Can I use these alternatives with my existing e-commerce platform?
Yes, most of these tools integrate with popular platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, and Etsy. Shippo and EasyShip have broad integration lists. Ordoro and Pirate Ship also connect to major cart systems. Check each tool’s integration page to confirm compatibility with your specific store.
