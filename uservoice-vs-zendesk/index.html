Choosing the right feedback and ticketing integration can make or break your support team’s ability to turn customer input into product improvements. UserVoice focuses on capturing and prioritizing feature requests so that product and support teams work from a single source of truth. Zendesk is a broader customer service platform with powerful ticketing, but its feedback capabilities are more of an add-on. For support leaders who want a dedicated feedback loop that directly influences the roadmap, UserVoice offers a purpose-built solution that keeps requests organized and tied to real customer impact.

alternative

Zendesk

. Zendesk is a comprehensive customer service platform with strong ticketing, automation, and analytics tools. Its feedback features come through separate add-ons like Zendesk Guide or third-party integrations, which means you may need extra configuration to get the same level of visibility into product requests. For teams that already use Zendesk for support, it can work as a lightweight feedback solution, but it lacks the native prioritization and roadmap alignment that UserVoice offers.

How they compare

UserVoice is the better choice when your main goal is to centralize feedback and link it to your product roadmap. It provides built-in voting, status tracking, and direct integration with your ticketing system so that every request is visible to both support and product teams. Zendesk excels at overall support operations, but its feedback capabilities are less integrated and require additional tools to match UserVoice’s depth. If you are a support leader looking to reduce silos between support and product, UserVoice gives you a dedicated feedback hub while Zendesk remains a strong option for teams that need a full service desk with only occasional feedback needs.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use UserVoice together with Zendesk for ticketing?
Yes, UserVoice integrates with Zendesk, allowing you to link support tickets directly to feature requests. This gives you the best of both worlds: Zendesk for day-to-day ticket management and UserVoice for prioritizing and tracking product feedback.
Does Zendesk offer built-in voting and prioritization for feature requests?
Zendesk does not include native voting or prioritization for feature requests out of the box. You would need to use a third-party app or build a custom workflow, which can be more complex than UserVoice’s dedicated voting and ideation features.
