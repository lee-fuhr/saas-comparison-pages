Best TeamGantt alternatives

TeamGantt is a popular choice for project managers who love visual Gantt chart planning. Its drag and drop timeline makes scheduling straightforward. However, not every team fits perfectly into TeamGantt’s pricing or feature set. You might need better resource management, stronger collaboration, or more flexible views. That is where alternatives come in. This article covers three strong options: GanttPRO, Smartsheet, and ClickUp. Each one keeps Gantt charts at the center but adds its own strengths.

alternative

GanttPRO

. This tool is built specifically for Gantt chart planning, with a clean, intuitive interface that feels familiar to TeamGantt users. It offers automatic task dependencies, critical path detection, and easy drag and drop editing. GanttPRO also includes resource workload management and team availability tracking, making it a solid choice for project managers who want a dedicated Gantt solution.

alternative

Smartsheet

. Smartsheet combines a spreadsheet style with powerful Gantt views, ideal for teams that already work with tables and data. You can roll up task dates, assign dependencies, and use built in automation to send alerts or update statuses. Its collaboration features, like real time commenting and file attachments, make it a good fit for cross functional teams that need both structure and flexibility.

alternative

ClickUp

. ClickUp is an all in one platform that includes a fully functional Gantt view alongside list, board, and calendar views. Its Gantt chart supports dependencies, milestones, and critical path analysis, and you can switch between views without losing data. While ClickUp offers deep customization, new users may need extra time to learn its many settings and options.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer strong Gantt chart capabilities, but they differ in focus and complexity. GanttPRO is the most direct replacement for TeamGantt because it is a dedicated Gantt tool with similar simplicity. Smartsheet works best for teams that need a spreadsheet backbone with project scheduling attached. ClickUp provides the most flexibility and can replace multiple tools, but its learning curve is steeper than the others. Pricing varies by team size and features, so you should test each one with a free trial to see which fits your workflow.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my TeamGantt projects into these alternatives?
Yes, but the method depends on the tool. Most alternatives accept CSV or Excel files, which you can export from TeamGantt. GanttPRO and Smartsheet have direct import guides for CSV data. ClickUp also supports CSV imports and offers a built in importer for several project management tools. You may need to map fields manually, but the process is straightforward.
Which alternative is best for a small team versus a large enterprise?
For small teams (up to 10 people), GanttPRO is often the best fit because it stays focused on Gantt planning without extra complexity. For large enterprises, Smartsheet scales well with its advanced automation, reporting, and integrations. ClickUp works for both, but small teams may find it overwhelming, while large teams can benefit from its all in one customization and multiple views.
