If your brand relies on online reviews to build trust and drive sales, you have likely considered Trustpilot as a primary platform. Trustpilot is well known for its large consumer reach and transparent review display. However, many businesses also evaluate alternatives like Birdeye, Podium, Grade.us, and Widewail to find a better fit for their specific needs, such as local reputation management, text-based collection, or automated support ticket reviews.

alternative

Birdeye

. Birdeye is an all-in-one reputation and customer experience platform that focuses on local businesses. It offers review generation across multiple sites, social media monitoring, and built-in survey tools. Where Trustpilot leans toward broad consumer visibility, Birdeye emphasizes local SEO and direct customer engagement.

alternative

Podium

. Podium centralizes review collection through text messaging and web chat, making it easy to request reviews from customers on their phones. It also provides payment and communication tools, so your team can manage customer interactions in one place. Podium is especially strong for service-based businesses that want a high response rate via SMS.

alternative

Grade.us

. Grade.us is a white-label reputation management platform designed for agencies and multi-location brands. It allows you to collect, manage, and display reviews under your own brand. Unlike Trustpilot, which operates its own consumer-facing review site, Grade.us focuses purely on backend management and customizable review widgets.

alternative

Widewail

. Widewail specializes in converting customer support tickets and chat transcripts into published reviews. It automatically invites customers to leave feedback after a support interaction, filling your review profile with authentic service experiences. Widewail is a good choice if your brand prioritizes consistent, high-volume review generation from existing customer service touchpoints.

How they compare

Trustpilot remains the best option for brands that want maximum consumer exposure through a well-known third party review site. Its open feedback system gives customers a public voice, which can boost credibility but also invite unverified or negative posts. Birdeye offers stronger local SEO and multi-channel management, while Podium leads in mobile and SMS collection. Grade.us gives you full control over branding and reporting, and Widewail turns customer support conversations into reviews without extra outreach. Your choice depends on whether you need brand visibility, local focus, mobile convenience, white-label flexibility, or automated volume from support interactions.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Trustpilot and one of these alternatives at the same time?
Yes, many brands use both Trustpilot and a secondary tool like Birdeye or Podium to collect reviews on a broader set of platforms. Just be careful to follow each site’s guidelines for inviting reviews to avoid duplicate or incentivized posts.
Which alternative is best for a single-location local business?
For a single-location local business, Birdeye or Podium tend to work well because they emphasize local SEO, direct customer invites, and integration with Google and Facebook reviews. Grade.us and Widewail are more suited to agencies or multi-location brands.
