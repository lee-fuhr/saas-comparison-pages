Best TenantCloud alternatives

TenantCloud offers a popular free tier that lets small landlords manage properties online, but its free plan limits the number of units and some features like online rent collection or maintenance requests. You might be looking for TenantCloud alternatives if you have outgrown the free tier, need different tools, or simply want to compare your options. Most property management software is paid, but a few offer free trials or limited free versions. Here we cover four well known alternatives: Propertyware, Buildium, AppFolio, and Rent Manager.

alternative

Propertyware

. Propertyware is built for large portfolios, often with 50 or more units, and does not offer a free tier. It includes advanced accounting, maintenance tracking, and tenant portals. For a small landlord with just a handful of units, the cost and complexity may be too high unless you are planning to grow quickly.

alternative

Buildium

. Buildium is a popular choice for landlords with between 20 and 500 units. It has no free plan but does offer a 14 day free trial. Features include online rent collection, lease management, and owner portals. Smaller landlords may find the monthly subscription price steep compared to TenantCloud’s free option.

alternative

AppFolio

. AppFolio is a cloud based tool designed for residential and commercial properties, typically for mid sized portfolios. It does not have a free tier, but you can request a demo. Its strengths are automation, marketing, and robust reporting. For a small landlord with only a few units, AppFolio is likely overkill and too expensive.

alternative

Rent Manager

. Rent Manager offers a full suite of property management features including accounting, work orders, and tenant screening. It is a paid product with no free tier, though a free trial may be available. It is more suitable for landlords managing dozens of units rather than a small number of doors.

How they compare

TenantCloud stands out as the only tool with a genuine free tier for small landlords (up to a limited number of units). Propertyware, Buildium, AppFolio, and Rent Manager all require a monthly subscription or a paid plan, with free trials only. TenantCloud’s free version is more limited in features and unit count, while the paid alternatives offer more advanced tools for a price. If you need free, stick with TenantCloud. If you can pay, Buildium and AppFolio are strong for growing portfolios.

Frequently asked questions

Does any alternative offer a completely free plan like TenantCloud does?
No. None of the alternatives listed (Propertyware, Buildium, AppFolio, Rent Manager) have a permanent free plan. A few offer free trials for a limited time, but after the trial ends you must pay to continue.
What should I do if I need more features than TenantCloud’s free tier provides?
You can upgrade to TenantCloud’s paid plan, which adds more units and features. Or you can look at a paid alternative like Buildium or AppFolio, which offer stronger accounting, maintenance, and tenant tools for a monthly fee. Compare costs and features to see which fits your portfolio size and budget.
