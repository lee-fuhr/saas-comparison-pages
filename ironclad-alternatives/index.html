Legal departments that automate contract lifecycle workflows often start with Ironclad, but it may not fit every team’s budget, integration needs, or preferred user experience. If you are evaluating options to process contracts more efficiently, consider three well known alternatives: Conga, Juro, and Contractbook. Each brings different strengths in document generation, collaboration, and data management.

alternative

Conga

is a document generation and contract management platform that integrates deeply with Salesforce. It lets legal teams automate contract creation from CRM data and manage approvals within the same ecosystem. Legal departments already using Salesforce often find Conga a natural fit for their workflow.

alternative

Juro

offers a browser based contract editor with real time collaboration and built in e signature. It is designed for teams that want to handle drafting, negotiation, and signing without switching between separate tools. Legal departments looking for a simple, intuitive interface may prefer Juro over more complex platforms.

alternative

Contractbook

provides end to end contract lifecycle management with strong data extraction and analytics from existing contracts. It helps teams surface obligations, renewals, and key terms automatically. Legal departments that need to centralize and analyze a large volume of legacy agreements might consider Contractbook as a practical alternative.

How they compare

Conga excels when your workflows are already tied to Salesforce, offering strong automation for document generation and approval routing. Juro prioritizes a clean, collaborative user experience that reduces the learning curve for legal and business teams. Contractbook stands out for its ability to pull structured data from uploaded contracts, making it easier to manage risk and deadlines. Ironclad remains a strong choice for enterprise level AI features and complex workflow design, but these three alternatives each address specific gaps in integration, usability, or data management. Your choice should depend on your existing tech stack, the volume of legacy contracts, and how much you value a streamlined editor versus deep CRM connectivity.

Frequently asked questions

How do Conga, Juro, and Contractbook compare to Ironclad for e signature?
Each alternative offers built in e signature capabilities. Conga integrates with DocuSign and Adobe Sign, Juro includes its own native e signature, and Contractbook also has a native e signature feature. Choose based on whether you prefer a fully native experience or want to keep your existing e signature provider.
Can I migrate my existing contracts from Ironclad to another platform?
Most platforms support contract import via CSV files, APIs, or direct data migration tools. Conga, Juro, and Contractbook each offer data mapping assistance, but the complexity depends on your contract volume and customization. Plan a phased migration and consult each vendor for specific migration services and timelines.
