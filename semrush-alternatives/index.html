Best Semrush alternatives

Semrush is a powerful all in one SEO platform, but its premium price tag and vast feature set can feel like overkill for smaller teams or niche projects. Many marketers and SEOs are looking for a cheaper or more focused alternative that still delivers strong keyword research, competitor analysis, and site auditing. Below we explore five popular Semrush alternatives, each with its own strengths and trade offs.

alternative

Ahrefs

. Ahrefs excels at backlink analysis and keyword research, making it a top choice for link building and content strategy. Its Site Explorer gives you comprehensive data on any domain’s organic traffic and referring domains, though the lowest paid plan starts around $129 per month, which is still less than Semrush’s Pro plan. The trade off is that Ahrefs offers fewer features for social media and advertising management, so it is best for pure SEO work.

Try Ahrefs →
alternative

Moz

. Moz is known for its user friendly interface and reliable domain authority metric, which many beginners use to gauge site strength. Its core tools include keyword explorer, site audits, and rank tracking, all wrapped in a lower price point than Semrush. However, Moz’s data freshness and database size can lag behind competitors, making it better suited for smaller sites or agencies on a tight budget.

Try Moz →
alternative

Ubersuggest

. Ubersuggest by Neil Patel is one of the cheapest options, with a free tier and paid plans starting at just $12 per month. It provides keyword suggestions, domain overviews, and content ideas, but the data accuracy and depth are limited compared to more expensive tools. Ubersuggest works well for solo freelancers or very small businesses who need basic SEO insights without a large investment.

Try Ubersuggest →
alternative

Mangools

. Mangools is a suite of five SEO tools (KWFinder, SERPWatcher, LinkMiner, SiteProfiler, and SERPChecker) that prioritizes simplicity and affordability, with plans starting around $29 per month. Its strength lies in keyword research and rank tracking, especially for local SEO, but it lacks the advanced competitor analysis and site audit depth of Semrush. Mangools is ideal for beginners and small agencies who want a clean, no frills experience.

Try Mangools →
alternative

SE Ranking

. SE Ranking offers a balanced mix of features similar to Semrush, including keyword tracking, competitor analysis, website audits, and social media management, at a lower cost (plans start at around $39 per month). It stands out for its white label reports and robust rank tracking across multiple search engines, but some users find its interface less intuitive than more polished rivals. SE Ranking is a solid choice for agencies that need client reporting and multi search engine monitoring on a budget.

Frequently asked questions

Which Semrush alternative is best for link building?
Ahrefs is widely considered the best alternative for link building and backlink analysis. Its Site Explorer provides the most detailed and up to date backlink data, including new and lost links, anchor text distribution, and competitor backlink profiles.
Can I find a reliable free alternative to Semrush?
Yes, for basic keyword research and site overviews, Ubersuggest offers a free tier that is usable for small projects. Moz also has a free browser extension (MozBar) and limited access to keyword data. However, free versions come with restrictions on daily searches, data accuracy, and report depth.
