Avoma offers AI meeting notes, call transcription, and coaching features for sales teams. But you might be looking for alternatives that better fit your budget, workflow, or specific needs. This article covers five Avoma alternatives: Chorus.ai, Gong, ExecVision, Refract, and Fireflies.ai. Each tool has its own strengths, and we will compare them to help you choose the right one for your remote sales team.

alternative

Chorus.ai

. Chorus specializes in capturing, transcribing, and analyzing sales calls. It provides automated scoring and coaching recommendations based on your team’s talk patterns and customer responses. Chorus integrates tightly with many CRMs and is a strong choice if you want deep conversation analytics.

alternative

Gong

. Gong records and transcribes all revenue-related calls and emails, then uses AI to surface deal risks and best practices. Its “customer spotlight” feature shows what prospects say, helping reps adjust their pitch. Gong is known for its advanced analytics and enterprise‑grade security.

alternative

ExecVision

. ExecVision focuses on call coaching and performance insights. It automatically tags key moments in conversations, like objection handling or competitor mentions, and provides scorecards for managers. ExecVision is a good option if your priority is detailed coaching feedback rather than just transcription.

alternative

Refract

. Refract uses AI to transcribe calls and highlight crucial conversation keywords and phrases. It offers real‑time coaching prompts during live calls and tracks rep adherence to sales scripts. Refract is ideal for teams that want to enforce consistent messaging and get immediate coaching cues.

alternative

Fireflies.ai

. Fireflies.ai provides automatic meeting notes and transcription across many meeting platforms (Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet). It can search past conversations, generate soundbites, and create action items. Fireflies is a versatile, affordable choice for teams that need quick summaries without heavy analytics.

How they compare

Chorus.ai and Gong are the most feature‑rich, with deep analytics and CRM integrations, but they are also the most expensive. ExecVision and Refract place a stronger emphasis on coaching and live feedback, making them good for teams focused on skill development. Fireflies.ai is the most budget‑friendly option, offering solid transcription and note‑taking without the advanced sales analytics of the others. If you need automated scorecards and talk‑track enforcement, Refract or ExecVision might be best. For overall conversation intelligence and deal insights, Chorus or Gong lead the pack.
