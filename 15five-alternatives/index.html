If you are evaluating 15Five for continuous performance management and employee engagement, you might also want to consider other options that fit your company’s specific size, culture, and goals. 15Five is well known for its weekly check-ins and pulse surveys, but tools like Lattice, Culture Amp, and Betterworks offer different strengths in areas such as goal alignment, advanced analytics, or integrated reviews. This article highlights three strong 15Five alternatives to help you choose the right fit.

alternative

Lattice

. Lattice combines performance reviews, goal tracking (OKRs), and employee engagement surveys in one platform. It gives managers clear visibility into progress and development, making it a solid choice for mid-sized to large teams that want structured performance cycles alongside continuous feedback.

alternative

Culture Amp

. Culture Amp focuses heavily on people analytics and in-depth engagement surveys. It provides robust benchmarks and actionable insights, ideal for organizations that prioritize data-driven decisions around culture, retention, and employee experience over simple check-in workflows.

alternative

Betterworks

. Betterworks is built around continuous goal management and performance conversations. It uses lightweight check-ins and dynamic goal setting to keep teams aligned, which works well for companies that want to replace annual reviews with ongoing coaching and business agility.

How they compare

15Five is known for its simple weekly check-in format and pulse surveys, making it easy to adopt in smaller teams. Lattice offers a more complete performance stack including reviews and OKRs. Culture Amp excels in deep engagement analytics and benchmarking. Betterworks is strongest for goal alignment and cascading objectives. Each alternative trades off some features: Lattice can feel heavier on process, Culture Amp may require more HR expertise to interpret data, and Betterworks might be less familiar for companies new to continuous performance management. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize check-in simplicity, rich analytics, or structured goal setting.

Frequently asked questions

Is Lattice easier to implement than 15Five?
Lattice has a slightly steeper learning curve because it includes built-in performance reviews and OKR tracking in addition to check-ins. However, many teams find it intuitive once set up, and it may reduce the number of separate tools you need.
Can Culture Amp replace both engagement surveys and performance reviews?
Culture Amp is primarily an engagement and people analytics platform. While it offers performance review modules, its focus is on survey data and insights rather than day-to-day continuous feedback. You would likely still need a separate tool for lightweight check-ins if that is a priority.
