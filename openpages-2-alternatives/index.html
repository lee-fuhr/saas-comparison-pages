Managing risk, compliance, and audit across a large enterprise is a complex task. The OpenPages GRC suite, now part of IBM, provides a unified platform to centralize these functions. It helps organizations identify, assess, and respond to risks while streamlining regulatory compliance and audit workflows. Built for scale, OpenPages offers pre-built content for frameworks like SOX, GDPR, and ISO, alongside robust reporting and analytics.

alternative

ServiceNow GRC

. This tool integrates risk and compliance management directly into the ServiceNow platform, which many enterprises already use for IT service management. It excels at automating workflows and connecting GRC data to operational processes. ServiceNow GRC is a strong choice for organizations looking to embed risk management into day-to-day IT operations.

alternative

MetricStream

. MetricStream provides a comprehensive governance, risk, and compliance suite with deep capabilities across risk assessment, policy management, and audit. It is known for its extensive library of regulatory content and flexible dashboards. Large enterprises in heavily regulated industries like financial services often choose MetricStream for its depth and configurability.

alternative

RSA Archer

. RSA Archer (now part of SAI Global) offers a modular GRC platform that allows organizations to build custom risk and compliance programs. It provides a wide range of use case modules, from operational risk to third party risk management. Archer is popular among large enterprises that need a highly customizable solution with a mature ecosystem of partners and templates.

alternative

LogicGate

. LogicGate is a cloud-native GRC platform that emphasizes visual process design and rapid deployment. It is more agile than traditional suites and suits enterprises that want to automate specific risk or compliance workflows without a heavy implementation. LogicGate is a good fit for organizations that prioritize speed and ease of use over pre-built content.

How they compare

When choosing between these tools, consider your organization’s priorities. OpenPages offers strong integration with IBM analytics and AI for deeper risk insights. ServiceNow GRC connects naturally if you already run ServiceNow for ITSM. MetricStream provides the broadest regulatory coverage out of the box. RSA Archer gives the most flexibility for custom builds. LogicGate is fastest to implement and easiest for business users. Price, deployment model (cloud vs on premises), and existing ecosystem should also guide your decision.
