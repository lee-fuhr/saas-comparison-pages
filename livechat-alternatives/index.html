Best LiveChat alternatives

LiveChat is a solid tool for real time support. But your e-commerce or SaaS team might be hitting its limits with robotic workflows, limited CRM depth, or pricing that climbs as you add agents. The good news is that the market has matured with platforms built for specific use cases, from proactive sales in B2B to affordable automation in small online stores. This article covers five strong LiveChat alternatives: Intercom, Drift, Tidio, Crisp and Freshchat. Each one addresses a different pain point, so you can switch to a tool that better fits how your team works and grows.

alternative

Intercom

. It is less a chat widget and more a full customer communication platform. You get a shared inbox, automated email and in-app campaigns, plus a help center builder, all tied to a detailed contact profile. It works best for SaaS teams that need to trigger messages based on user behavior and want one system for support, sales and marketing.

Try Intercom →
alternative

Drift

. This tool focuses on conversational marketing for B2B SaaS companies. It routes visitors to sales reps, books meetings directly in the chat, and uses bots that qualify leads before a human steps in. Drift is a good choice if your main goal is pipeline generation rather than high volume support tickets.

alternative

Tidio

. Tidio combines a live chat widget with powerful e-commerce bots and built-in email marketing. Its visual bot builder lets you create flows that handle abandoned cart recovery or answer shipping questions without a human. It is affordable, easy to set up, and ideal for small to midsize online stores that want automation without a complicated tool.

Try Tidio →
alternative

Crisp

. Crisp provides a unified inbox that pulls in chat, email and social media messages into one place. It includes a shared team inbox, a knowledge base and proactive chat triggers, all with a clean and minimal interface. Startups and smaller support teams appreciate how fast it is to deploy and how its pricing starts low.

Try Crisp →
alternative

Freshchat

. Freshchat is part of the Freshworks suite and offers omnichannel support with a Messenger-like experience. It includes powerful automation, a help desk integration and AI-driven intent detection to route tickets correctly. It is a strong fit for growing teams that already use Freshdesk or need a scalable solution with a traditional support backend.

How they compare

- **For deep CRM and automation**: Intercom is the most complete option but also the most expensive. - **For B2B lead generation**: Drift excels at qualifying prospects and booking meetings. - **For affordable e-commerce bots**: Tidio gives you strong automation at a low price. - **For a minimalist unified inbox**: Crisp is fast, simple and budget friendly. - **For an omnichannel suite with ticketing**: Freshchat works well alongside other Freshworks tools.

Each tool has a free or trial plan, so you can test live Chatbots and routing before you commit.
