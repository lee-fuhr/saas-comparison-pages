Best Lookback alternatives

If you are a researcher capturing user sessions, Lookback has long been a go to tool for live and moderated testing. But maybe you need different pricing, more advanced analysis features, or a platform that integrates better with your existing workflow. This article explores four strong alternatives: UserTesting, PlaybookUX, Dovetail, and dscout. We compare them on key criteria so you can decide which one fits your research needs.

alternative

UserTesting

. UserTesting offers a large panel of participants and fast turnaround for unmoderated tests. Its platform includes session recording, live interviews, and automated analysis features like sentiment detection. However, it is often more expensive than Lookback and may lock you into their panel if you want to recruit quickly.

alternative

PlaybookUX

. PlaybookUX is a simpler, more affordable option for moderated and unmoderated user sessions. It focuses on ease of use with features like screen share, recording, and basic tagging. While it lacks the advanced analytics of some competitors, it is a good choice for small teams or researchers on a budget.

alternative

Dovetail

. Dovetail is primarily a research analysis and collaboration hub rather than a session capture tool. You can import recordings from Lookback or other tools, then tag, transcribe, and search across all your data. It is ideal for teams that need to synthesize findings from multiple sources, but it does not offer built-in session recording or participant recruitment.

alternative

dscout

. dscout specializes in mobile and remote user research, offering diary studies, live sessions, and quick feedback bursts. Its “Express” feature lets you run small unmoderated tests in hours. dscout is great for in context mobile research, but its focus on mobile may limit desktop and complex website testing.

How they compare

Choose UserTesting if you need a large, vetted participant panel and are willing to pay a premium. Pick PlaybookUX for a low cost, straightforward session capture tool with no frills. Go with Dovetail when your priority is analyzing and sharing insights from many different tools, not capturing sessions itself. Select dscout for mobile first, in the moment research like diary studies or quick remote tests.

Frequently asked questions

Which Lookback alternative is best for a small research team with a tight budget?
PlaybookUX is often the most affordable option with simple session recording and no extra features you do not need. It works well for teams that want to capture user sessions without paying for large participant panels or heavy analytics.
Can I import existing Lookback recordings into any of these alternatives?
Yes, Dovetail is designed to import recordings from Lookback and other tools. You can upload your Lookback session files, then transcribe and analyze them inside Dovetail. The other alternatives (UserTesting, PlaybookUX, dscout) rely on sessions captured within their own platforms, so you would need to start fresh.
