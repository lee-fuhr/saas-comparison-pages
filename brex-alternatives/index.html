Best Brex alternatives

Brex has become a popular choice for startups because it offers high credit limits and a simple rewards program. But as your company grows, you may need different features like tighter expense controls, better accounting integrations, or lower fees. If Brex no longer fits your needs, several strong alternatives can handle corporate cards and spend management just as well.

alternative

Ramp

. Ramp focuses on saving you money by automatically finding subscription savings and negotiating vendor bills. It offers unlimited free cards, real-time expense tracking, and seamless integrations with accounting software like QuickBooks and Xero, making it a strong choice for startups that want to reduce overhead.

Try Ramp →
alternative

Mercury

. Mercury is more than a corporate card provider, it is a full digital bank for startups. You get a checking account, debit cards, and a treasury product, all in one place. Its spend management tools are simple but effective, and it does not require a personal guarantee for most accounts.

Try Mercury →
alternative

Divvy

. Divvy combines corporate cards with built-in budgeting and expense management. You can create virtual cards for specific projects or departments and set spending limits in real time. Its platform also automates receipt collection and reconciliation, which helps fast-growing teams stay organized.

alternative

Airbase

. Airbase is designed for companies that need robust procurement workflows alongside spend management. It offers corporate cards, virtual cards, and bill payments, all with custom approval flows. This makes it a good fit if your team needs to control spending across different departments and locations.

Try Airbase →
alternative

Expensify

. Expensify is best known for its expense reporting and receipt scanning capabilities. While its corporate card program is newer, it works well for companies that already use Expensify for travel and expense tracking. The SmartScan feature makes it easy to capture receipts on the go and match them to transactions.
