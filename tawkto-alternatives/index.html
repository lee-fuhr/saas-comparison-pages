Tawk.to is a popular free live chat widget, but some small businesses find its interface clunky or want different features like a simpler dashboard, better email integration, or a more modern design. While Tawk.to stays free forever, you might be looking for alternatives that also offer a completely free plan or a more tailored experience. Below we cover three alternatives: Crisp (which has a generous free tier), plus LiveChat and HelpScout (both paid but worth considering if your budget grows)., ## ALTERNATIVES

**Crisp**. Crisp offers a completely free live chat plan that includes unlimited chats, a shared inbox, and basic chatbot features. Its clean interface and built-in CRM make it easy to manage conversations without paying a cent, though the free plan limits you to 10 team members and removes the Tawk.to watermark.

**LiveChat**. LiveChat is a premium paid tool with a 14 day free trial, no permanent free plan. It excels in advanced routing, detailed analytics, and a polished agent interface, making it ideal for small businesses that outgrow free widgets and need deeper customization.

**HelpScout**. HelpScout is primarily an email help desk but includes a paid chat add on called “Beacon.” There is no free plan, just a trial. It’s best for teams that want to unify email, chat, and knowledge base under one platform, especially if you already use HelpScout for support tickets., ## COMPARISON SUMMARY

If your top priority is a completely free live chat widget, Crisp is the strongest alternative to Tawk.to. LiveChat and HelpScout are better suited once you have a budget and need more advanced features., ## FAQ

Q: Are there any other free live chat widgets besides Tawk.to and Crisp?

A: Yes, a few other free tools include Tidio (free tier with limited chats) and Chatwoot (open source, self hosted). However, Crisp and Tawk.to offer the most generous free plans for unlimited chats.

Q: Can I switch from Tawk.to to another free widget easily?

A: Most alternatives provide import tools or manual setup. You will need to copy your canned responses and install a new code snippet on your website. Chat history usually does not transfer between platforms unless you export and import manually.
