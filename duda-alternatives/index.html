If your agency builds client white label websites, Duda is a strong choice for responsive design tools and branded dashboards. But it is not the only platform that can meet your needs. You might consider alternatives when Duda’s pricing feels high, its template library feels limiting, or you need more advanced design flexibility. This article covers three Duda alternatives for agencies that want white label capabilities and responsive design: Wix, Squarespace, and Webflow. Each has unique strengths for agencies that need to deliver polished, mobile friendly sites under their own brand.

alternative

Wix

. Wix offers a white label solution through Wix Studio and the Wix Partner program. You can rebrand the editor, set your own pricing, and access a huge app market. Wix’s responsive design tools are strong, especially with its drag and drop interface and built in mobile optimization. However, the white label features require a paid partner plan and some custom work.

Try Wix →
alternative

Squarespace

. Squarespace provides white label options through its Squarespace Circle program, but full client white labeling is more limited compared to Duda. You can use your own custom domain and email branding on client invoices. Squarespace’s responsive design is automatic and polished, but agencies have less control over advanced layout tweaks without coding.

Try Squarespace →
alternative

Webflow

. Webflow gives agencies the most design control with a visual builder that generates clean, responsive code. Its white label capabilities are available through the Webflow Enterprise plan or third party tools like Memberstack. Webflow is ideal for agencies that need custom interactions and complex layouts. The learning curve is steeper and the cost can be higher than Duda for small client projects.

How they compare

- **White label capabilities**: Duda and Wix offer the most complete white label options for editor and dashboard. Webflow requires enterprise or custom integrations. Squarespace has limited white label. - **Responsive design tools**: Webflow gives the most control. Wix and Duda are both strong. Squarespace automates responsiveness but restricts manual adjustments. - **Ease of use**: Wix and Squarespace are beginner friendly. Duda is moderate. Webflow has a steeper learning curve. - **Pricing for agencies**: Duda and Wix have agency specific plans. Webflow can be expensive per site. Squarespace is affordable but lacks white label depth. - **Best for**: Duda for fast white label sites, Wix for app driven sites, Squarespace for template heavy projects, Webflow for custom coded designs.

Frequently asked questions

Which Duda alternative offers the best white label experience for agencies?
Wix provides the most complete white label experience among these alternatives, especially through Wix Studio. You can rebrand the editor, use your own domain, and manage client accounts under your own agency brand. Webflow offers white label mainly through enterprise plans and third party apps.
Can I build responsive client websites with Squarespace that look as good as Duda?
Yes, Squarespace creates responsive sites automatically, and its templates are polished. However, you have less control over mobile specific adjustments compared to Duda. If you need to fine tune breakpoints or hide elements on certain devices, Duda or Webflow give you more options.
