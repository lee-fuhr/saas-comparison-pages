Best Ludus alternatives

Ludus is a popular presentation design tool that offers powerful animation, flexible vector editing, and a wide range of customizable templates. Creative agencies often choose Ludus because it gives them full control over every element, from typography to motion. However, there are scenarios where Ludus might not be the best fit – for example, when you need faster collaboration, simpler onboarding, or specific visual features. This article explores five strong Ludus alternatives that cater to the needs of creative agencies looking for highly customizable templates.

alternative

Beautiful.ai

. This tool uses an artificial intelligence engine to automatically adjust your slides as you add content, so you never have to manually agree elements. Its template library is smart and responsive, making it easy to create polished, on brand decks quickly. Agencies that want a balance between customization and speed will find Beautiful.ai particularly useful for client work.

alternative

Canva

. Canva offers thousands of pre designed templates across presentation, social media, and print formats, all of which can be heavily customized with your own fonts, colors, and images. The drag and drop interface is intuitive, and the collaboration features allow multiple team members to edit in real time. For agencies that need a versatile, affordable tool with a massive template library, Canva is a strong alternative.

alternative

Visme

. Visme specializes in data visualization and infographics while also providing a full presentation builder. Its template engine allows you to swap layouts, adjust charts, and embed interactive elements such as video and hover effects. Creative agencies that produce reports, pitch decks, and client dashboards will appreciate Visme’s balance of design flexibility and analytical features.

alternative

Powtoon

. Powtoon is built for animated video presentations and explainers, but it also includes slide based templates that you can customize with characters, props, and voiceovers. The timeline editor gives you granular control over timing and transitions, which is ideal for storytelling. If your agency needs to create engaging, motion heavy presentations quickly, Powtoon is worth considering as a Ludus alternative.

alternative

Crello

(now part of VistaCreate). Crello offers a rich collection of animated and static templates for social media, marketing materials, and presentations. Its background remover, design objects, and font pairings make it easy to produce consistent branded content. Agencies that prioritize fast turnaround on social visuals and simple presentation templates will find Crello a lightweight but capable option.

How they compare

Ludus is best for agencies that want maximum creative control through vector tools, custom CSS, and advanced animation. Beautiful.ai automates layout so you can focus on content, while Canva provides the largest template library and easiest team collaboration. Visme excels at data rich presentations and interactive content, Powtoon is the go to for animated storytelling, and Crello (VistaCreate) offers quick, branded templates for social and short presentations. Each tool has its own strengths, and your choice depends on your agency’s workflow, template needs, and budget.

Frequently asked questions

Is it possible to import my existing Ludus templates into any of these alternatives?
Most of these tools accept common file formats like PowerPoint, PDF, or image files, but they do not support direct Ludus file imports. You may need to manually recreate your slides using the alternative’s native template editor. Canva and Visme offer import options for PowerPoint files, which can save some time.
Which alternative is best for creating highly customized, on brand templates for multiple clients?
Beautiful.ai and Canva both offer brand kit features that let you save fonts, colors, and logos for reuse across projects. For the deepest customization, Beautiful.ai’s smart templates adapt to your brand rules, while Canva’s brand hub allows you to lock certain design elements. Visme also supports brand kits, but its strength is in data driven slides rather than free form design.
