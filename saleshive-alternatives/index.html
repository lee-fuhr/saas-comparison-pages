Best SalesHive alternatives

If your team is looking for a gamified sales enablement platform with interactive learning paths, you have likely come across SalesHive. While SalesHive offers a solid mix of training tools and performance tracking, it may not fit every team’s budget, content library, or integration needs. Understanding the best SalesHive alternatives can help you find the platform that keeps your reps engaged through challenges, leaderboards, and engaging microlearning modules.

alternative

Allego

. Allego combines video-based learning with AI-powered coaching and gamification elements like quizzes and leaderboards. Its interactive learning paths allow managers to create custom courses, and the platform’s mobile-first design supports on-the-go training for field teams.

alternative

MindTickle

. MindTickle focuses heavily on gamified onboarding and continuous learning with features like certifications, challenges, and real-time leaderboards. The platform also provides interactive learning paths that adapt to each rep’s skill gaps, making it a strong choice for teams that want personalized coaching.

alternative

Showpad

. Showpad blends sales enablement with content management and gamified training. Its interactive learning paths let you create guided moments for reps, while leaderboards and badge systems keep motivation high. Showpad also integrates well with CRM tools like Salesforce and HubSpot.

alternative

Highspot

. Highspot uses AI to recommend content and learning paths, but it also includes gamification features such as contests and progress tracking. The platform is known for its user-friendly interface and ability to surface the most relevant training materials based on a rep’s current deal stage.

alternative

Seismic

. Seismic offers a powerful content management and enablement suite with gamified elements like challenges and scorecards. Its interactive learning paths can be built using bite-sized modules, and the platform’s analytics help leaders see which training drives the most revenue impact.

How they compare

All five alternatives provide some form of gamification and interactive learning, but they differ in strengths. Allego excels in video coaching and mobile learning. MindTickle offers the deepest adaptive learning paths and certification tools. Showpad balances content management with gamification and CRM integrations. Highspot focuses on AI-driven content recommendations with added gamification layers. Seismic is best for large enterprises that need robust content governance and compliance tracking alongside gamified training. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize adaptive learning, content management, or AI-powered personalization.

Frequently asked questions

Which SalesHive alternative has the best gamification features for keeping reps engaged?
MindTickle is often rated highest for gamification because it includes leaderboards, challenges, certifications, and adaptive learning paths that reset based on performance. Allego and Showpad also offer strong gamification but with a greater emphasis on video and content management.
Can I migrate my existing training content from SalesHive to one of these alternatives?
Yes, most platforms like Highspot, Showpad, and Seismic support bulk imports of SCORM files, videos, and documents. You should check each tool’s data migration services and file format compatibility before switching. MindTickle and Allego offer dedicated onboarding support to help transfer content.
