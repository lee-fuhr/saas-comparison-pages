Best EasyShip alternatives

As a fulfillment manager, you know that EasyShip offers a solid platform for global shipping with carrier integrations and duty calculations. But maybe you have outgrown its pricing structure, need more domestic focus, or want better inventory management. This article reviews five EasyShip alternatives that address different pain points, from cost savings to warehouse operations, so you can find the right fit for your fulfillment workflow.

alternative

ShipBob

. ShipBob is a full-service fulfillment provider, not just a shipping software, meaning it handles storage, picking, packing, and shipping from its network of warehouses. It suits brands that want to outsource logistics entirely while relying on ShipBob’s integrated shipping rates and inventory tracking. The tradeoff is less control over carrier selection and higher minimum volume requirements compared to a pure shipping tool.

alternative

ShipStation

. ShipStation is a multi-carrier shipping platform built for high-volume ecommerce orders, offering batch processing, custom automation rules, and deep integrations with major marketplaces and carts. It excels at streamlining manual label creation and carrier comparisons, but it does not include warehouse inventory management. Fulfillment managers who need to ship from their own facility will appreciate its robust rules engine.

alternative

Shippo

. Shippo is a lightweight shipping API and web app that focuses on discounted USPS, UPS, and DHL labels with no monthly fees for basic usage. It is ideal for smaller fulfillment teams or startups that want pay-as-you-go pricing without complex contracts. However, Shippo lacks advanced batch tools and international customs support that larger operations might require.

alternative

Pirateship

. Pirateship offers heavily discounted USPS and UPS rates with no subscription cost, making it a popular choice for low-volume shippers and small businesses. Its simplicity is a strength, but it lacks enterprise features like multi-user access, automation, or inventory tracking. Fulfillment managers focused purely on parcel cost reduction for occasional shipments will find Pirateship hard to beat.

alternative

EasyPost

. EasyPost is a developer-first shipping API that gives fulfillment teams the ability to build custom shipping workflows, compare rates across multiple carriers, and manage labels programmatically. It is not a ready-to-use app like EasyShip, so it requires technical resources to integrate. For teams that need full control and scale, EasyPost offers the deepest carrier access and flexible pricing.

How they compare

EasyShip covers global shipping with duties and taxes, while ShipBob offers a complete fulfillment solution, ShipStation focuses on rule-based batch processing, Shippo provides simple pay-as-you-go labeling, Pirateship delivers rock-bottom USPS rates, and EasyPost gives developers full API control. Your choice depends on whether you need warehouse services (ShipBob), automation at volume (ShipStation), low cost and simplicity (Shippo or Pirateship), or custom integration (EasyPost).

Frequently asked questions

What is the main reason fulfillment managers switch from EasyShip?
The most common reasons are cost for domestic US shipping, lack of advanced batch processing, or a desire for a fully managed warehouse solution. EasyShip’s strength is international shipping, so teams shipping mostly within one country often find cheaper or more efficient alternatives.
Can I use these alternatives with my existing warehouse management system?
Yes, most alternatives integrate with major WMS and ecommerce platforms. ShipStation and EasyPost have extensive APIs and prebuilt connectors, while ShipBob requires sending inventory to its warehouses. Check the specific integration documentation for your WMS to ensure compatibility.
