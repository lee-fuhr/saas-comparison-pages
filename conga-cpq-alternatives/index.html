Best Conga CPQ alternatives

If you are a mid-size firm evaluating quote-to-cash software, you have likely come across Conga CPQ. While it is a capable solution, it may not fit every workflow, budget, or integration need. This article examines five popular Conga CPQ alternatives, each with strengths that can simplify quoting, reduce errors, and accelerate deal closure. Use this guide to compare your options and choose the tool that aligns best with your team’s size, sales complexity, and existing tech stack.

alternative

Salesforce CPQ

. Salesforce CPQ is a native extension of the Salesforce platform, making it ideal if your firm already uses Salesforce CRM. It offers robust guided selling, subscription management, and approval workflows, and it requires minimal data sync because it lives inside the same ecosystem. The downside is the additional licensing cost and the need for some administrative expertise.

alternative

Apttus

. Apttus (now part of Conga’s broader portfolio) remains widely used as a standalone CPQ and contract management solution. It provides strong support for complex pricing models, multi-currency quotes, and approval chains. Mid-size firms often choose Apttus for its deep contract lifecycle management features, though the implementation can be resource-intensive.

alternative

Oracle CPQ

. Oracle CPQ (formerly BigMachines) is built for businesses that need heavy configuration and rule-based quoting, especially in manufacturing, high-tech, or industrial equipment sales. It handles complex product bundles, attribute-based pricing, and large product catalogs well. The trade-off is a steeper learning curve and higher upfront setup effort compared to simpler cloud-native tools.

alternative

DealHub

. DealHub is a cloud-native CPQ platform designed for mid-size B2B teams that want a quick, no-code setup. It integrates with common CRMs like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Microsoft Dynamics, and includes features for guided selling, e-signatures, and subscription billing. DealHub’s user-friendly interface and fast deployment make it a strong fit for firms that need to get up and running within weeks, not months.

alternative

Zuora CPQ

. Zuora CPQ is primarily aimed at subscription-based and recurring revenue models. It excels at managing usage-based pricing, tiered plans, and complex billing schedules. If your firm sells services or products as subscriptions, Zuora’s tight integration with its own billing and revenue recognition engine can streamline the entire quote-to-cash cycle. However, it may be overkill if your business relies on one-time transactions.

How they compare

When choosing a Conga CPQ alternative, your decision should hinge on your CRM environment, pricing complexity, and deployment timeline. Salesforce CPQ is the best choice for existing Salesforce users who want seamless data flow. Apttus suits firms that need advanced contract management alongside quoting. Oracle CPQ handles detailed product configuration for technical industries. DealHub offers a fast, user-friendly option for general B2B sales teams. Zuora CPQ is purpose-built for subscription and usage-based billing models. Evaluate each tool against your firm’s specific quote-to-cash pain points to find your best fit.
