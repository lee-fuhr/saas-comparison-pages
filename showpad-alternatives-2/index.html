Best Showpad alternatives

If your sales team relies on mobile devices to access and share content on the go, you already know that a platform built for desktop can hurt productivity. Showpad is a solid choice for mobile-first content delivery, but it may not fit every budget, workflow, or integration need. This article reviews five Showpad alternatives that prioritize mobile performance: Highspot, Seismic, Brainshark, Allego, and ClearSlide. We compare each tool on mobile experience, content management, and ease of use so you can find the right fit for your team.

alternative

Highspot

. Highspot delivers a fast, intuitive mobile app that lets reps find, personalize, and share content directly from their phone. Its AI-powered recommendations and real-time analytics help teams see which materials drive engagement, making it a strong choice for mobile-first sales enablement.

alternative

Seismic

. Seismic offers a mobile-optimized interface that syncs automatically with desktop, so reps always have the latest collateral at their fingertips. Its robust content governance and personalization features work well for large teams that need strict control over versioning while still enabling on-the-go access.

alternative

Brainshark

. Brainshark, now part of Bigtincan, provides a mobile-first platform focused on coaching and content readiness. Reps can access training materials and pitch decks on any device, and managers can track completion and effectiveness without requiring a laptop.

alternative

Allego

. Allego is built for mobile with a strong emphasis on video and audio content. Sales reps can record, upload, and share short presentations or demos from their phone, and the platform’s AI analyzes speech and engagement to improve messaging on the move.

alternative

ClearSlide

. ClearSlide prioritizes mobile content delivery with a lightweight app that lets reps present and track slide decks in real time. Its simplicity and focus on sharing analytics make it a good alternative for teams that want a straightforward mobile presentation tool without a steep learning curve.

How they compare

All five alternatives offer strong mobile capabilities, but they differ in depth. Highspot and Seismic provide the most comprehensive content management and analytics, ideal for larger teams. Brainshark and Allego lean into coaching and video, making them better for training-focused workflows. ClearSlide is the simplest option, perfect for teams that only need mobile presentation and tracking. Showpad remains a strong contender, but if you need deeper coaching (Allego), simpler mobile sharing (ClearSlide), or AI-powered content recommendations (Highspot), one of these alternatives may serve you better.
