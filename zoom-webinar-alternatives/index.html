Best Zoom Webinars alternatives

If you run webinars for marketing or education, you may have noticed that Zoom Webinars can feel clunky for interactive sessions or limited in customization. While Zoom is reliable, its webinar tier often lacks built-in marketing features like landing pages, email automation, or seamless attendee engagement tools. Fortunately, there are purpose-built platforms designed to make hosting, promoting, and following up with attendees easier. Below are five solid alternatives that cater specifically to the needs of marketers and educators.

alternative

Demio

This platform is built for simplicity and high-quality video, making it ideal for marketers who want a polished, no-download experience for attendees. It offers native email reminders, integrations with CRM tools, and a “lobby” feature where you can chat with viewers before going live.

Try Demio →
alternative

Livestorm

Livestorm works entirely in the browser, so neither you nor your attendees need to install software. It includes automated email sequences, analytics dashboards, and the ability to run both live and on-demand webinars from the same event.

Try Livestorm →
alternative

WebinarJam

WebinarJam focuses on live sales and conversion, with features like “pop-up” offers, countdown timers, and a real-time chat that highlights high-engagement viewers. It supports large audiences (up to thousands) and includes a built-in replay studio.

Try WebinarJam →
alternative

BigMarker

BigMarker is an all-in-one platform that allows you to host webinars, virtual conferences, and hybrid events. It offers advanced customization options, such as branded registration pages and post-event analytics, plus integrations with dozens of marketing tools.

alternative

Crowdcast

Crowdcast is designed for community building and engagement, with interactive polls, Q&A, and a “backstage” area where speakers can gather before going live. It’s a good fit for educators who want to foster discussion and for marketers who run recurring series.
