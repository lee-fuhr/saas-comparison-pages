If you are an agency or sales team that sends proposals, you have likely used Proposify or considered it. Proposify is a solid tool for creating and tracking proposals, but it is not the only option. Some teams find Proposify’s pricing high for their needs, others want more advanced eSignature features, and some need better integration with their CRM. This article covers five strong Proposify alternatives: PandaDoc, Qwilr, Better Proposals, GetAccept, and Nusii. Each tool has different strengths, so we compare them to help you choose the right fit for your workflow.

alternative

PandaDoc

. PandaDoc is a full document automation platform that goes beyond proposals to include contracts, quotes, and eSignatures. It offers rich templates, drag and drop editing, and deep integrations with CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot. Sales teams that need a complete document lifecycle tool often prefer PandaDoc over Proposify.

alternative

Qwilr

. Qwilr turns proposals into interactive web pages instead of static PDFs. This allows embedded videos, live pricing tables, and real time analytics on how clients engage with each section. Agencies that want a modern, branded client experience often choose Qwilr for its design flexibility and detailed readership data.

Try Qwilr →
alternative

Better Proposals

. Better Proposals focuses on speed and simplicity for small to mid size businesses. It provides a library of professional templates and a straightforward editor that lets you send a proposal in minutes. If your team wants a lower cost tool with fewer bells and whistles, Better Proposals is a direct Proposify alternative.

Try Better Proposals →
alternative

GetAccept

. GetAccept combines proposal creation with video recording, meeting scheduling, and deal room features. It is designed for sales teams that want to shorten the sales cycle by adding personal video introductions and automated follow ups. Its eSignature capabilities are built in, and it integrates with major CRMs.

alternative

Nusii

. Nusii is a proposal software built specifically for creative agencies and freelancers. It offers clean templates, online signatures, and payment collection through Stripe. Nusii is often praised for its intuitive interface and straightforward pricing, making it a good choice for smaller teams that send proposals frequently.

How they compare

Choose PandaDoc if you need a full document workflow with strong CRM integrations and eSignatures. Choose Qwilr if you want interactive, design driven proposals that provide deep client engagement data. Choose Better Proposals if you need a fast, affordable tool with simple templates. Choose GetAccept if you want to add video and automated follow ups to your proposal process. Choose Nusii if you are a small creative agency that wants an easy to use tool with payment collection.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free alternative to Proposify?
Most Proposify alternatives offer free trials but not permanent free plans. PandaDoc and GetAccept have limited free tiers with basic features. For a no cost option, you may need to use a Google Docs template paired with a free eSignature service like DocuSign’s free tier.
Which alternative integrates best with Salesforce?
PandaDoc offers the deepest Salesforce integration among these options. It allows you to sync templates, send proposals directly from Salesforce records, and update deal stages automatically. GetAccept also provides a strong Salesforce integration, while Qwilr and Better Proposals have more basic connections.
