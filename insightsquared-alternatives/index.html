Best InsightSquared alternatives

InsightSquared has long been a go to for sales leaders who want analytics driven forecasting and territory planning. But as your team grows or your revenue process becomes more complex, you may find that InsightSquared no longer fits your budget, your workflow, or your need for real time accuracy. That is why many sales managers are now exploring alternatives that offer stronger AI capabilities, easier integrations, and more flexible territory modeling. This article compares three leading options: Clari, Aviso, and Boostup. Each tool is built to help you predict revenue, allocate resources, and plan territories with confidence.

alternative

Clari

uses artificial intelligence to provide real time revenue intelligence across your entire sales cycle. It helps you forecast deals more accurately by analyzing signals from your CRM, email, and calendar data, and it also supports territory planning by showing you which reps are over or under performing in specific accounts or regions.

alternative

Aviso

focuses on removing biases from forecasting by using machine learning to surface the most relevant data points. Its territory planning features let you model different scenarios, such as splitting or merging sales territories, and then run what if analyses to see how those changes might affect your pipeline and revenue.

alternative

Boostup

is designed to be user friendly and integrates directly with your CRM to deliver pipeline health scores and predictive forecasts. For territory planning, it offers visual maps of account coverage and can automatically recommend adjustments based on rep capacity, deal velocity, and historical conversion rates.

How they compare

Clari excels at pulling in data from many sources to give you a single source of truth for forecasting, but it can be more expensive and complex to set up. Aviso stands out for its scenario modeling and bias reduction, making it a strong choice if your team struggles with overly optimistic or pessimistic forecasts. Boostup is the most lightweight option, ideal for mid market teams that need fast implementation and clear pipeline visibility without a steep learning curve. All three offer analytics driven forecasting and territory planning, but your choice should depend on team size, budget, and how much hands on configuration you are willing to do.
