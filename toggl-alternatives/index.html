Best Toggl alternatives

If you use Toggl for time tracking but want to explore other options, you are not alone. Many individuals and small teams look for tools that better fit their budget, workflow, or reporting needs. Toggl is reliable, but alternatives like Harvest, Clockify, RescueTime, Timely, and Paymo offer unique features that might suit you better. This article compares these tools to help you make an informed choice.

alternative

Harvest

. Harvest combines time tracking with invoicing and expense management, making it a strong choice for freelancers and small teams who need to bill clients directly. It offers a clean interface and integrates with popular project management tools, but its free plan is limited to one user and two projects.

Try Harvest →
alternative

Clockify

. Clockify is a free time tracker that works well for unlimited users and projects, which is ideal for small teams on a tight budget. It includes basic reporting, a timesheet view, and optional paid features like invoicing and project templates.

Try Clockify →
alternative

RescueTime

. RescueTime focuses on automatic time tracking and productivity analysis rather than manual entry. It runs in the background to show you how you spend your digital time, making it useful for individuals who want to reduce distractions and improve focus.

alternative

Timely

. Timely offers automatic time tracking with a visual timeline, so you can see what you worked on without starting and stopping timers. It uses AI to suggest entries, which saves time for users who dislike manual logging, but it has a higher price point than many alternatives.

Try Timely →
alternative

Paymo

. Paymo is a full project management platform with built-in time tracking, task lists, and resource scheduling. It works well for small teams that want to manage projects and track time in one tool, but its free plan is more limited than Clockify’s.
