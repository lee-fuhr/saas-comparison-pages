Crossbeam is a popular data clean room platform for partnership teams. It helps you securely match accounts with partners and discover co-selling opportunities. However, some teams look for alternatives due to pricing, integration limits, or a need for additional features like lead routing or data enrichment. This article covers three strong alternatives: LeanData, Openprise, and Reveal.

alternative

LeanData

provides a data clean room for secure partner data sharing and co-selling insights. It focuses on matching records without exposing sensitive data, and it also includes features for lead routing and attribution that go beyond clean room matching alone.

alternative

Openprise

enables partnership teams to securely share and match data with partners using a clean room approach. It automates data enrichment and lead passing, and gives you dashboards to track co-go-to-market performance across your partner ecosystem.

alternative

Reveal

specializes in secure data clean rooms for partner ecosystems. It lets you discover overlapping account territories and co-sell opportunities with self-service matching, and it integrates closely with your CRM and sales tools for real-time insights.

How they compare

Crossbeam is the most established option for partner clean rooms with broad integration support. LeanData adds lead routing and attribution on top of clean room matching. Openprise focuses on data enrichment and process automation. Reveal is a direct competitor to Crossbeam with a simpler interface and similar core clean room features. Your choice depends on whether you need extra capabilities like routing or enrichment, or if a dedicated clean room tool is enough.

Frequently asked questions

What is a data clean room?
A data clean room is a secure environment where two or more parties can share and match data without exposing sensitive information. It allows you to find overlapping accounts and other insights while keeping each partner’s data private.
How do I choose between Crossbeam and its alternatives?
Consider your existing tech stack, budget, and whether you need extra features like lead routing, data enrichment, or attribution. If you only need clean room matching and co-selling insights, Reveal or Crossbeam may be best. If you also need automation and routing, look at LeanData or Openprise.
