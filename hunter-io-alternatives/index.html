Hunter.io is a popular tool for finding email addresses, but it may not suit every outreach strategy. Some users need higher accuracy, better integration with CRM tools, or larger databases. If you are looking for hunter.io alternatives, you likely want reliable contact data that fits your workflow. This article covers three strong options: Apollo.io, Clearbit, and Lusha.

alternative

Apollo.io

offers a full sales intelligence platform that includes email finding, lead enrichment, and sequencing. Its database is vast, and it often delivers verified emails alongside direct dials and company data. Apollo works well for teams that want an all-in-one tool rather than just a standalone email finder.

alternative

Clearbit

focuses on data enrichment and company intelligence more than email discovery alone. It can append missing fields to your existing contacts and provides Chrome extension lookup. Clearbit is ideal if your main need is to enhance your CRM records rather than find cold emails from scratch.

alternative

Lusha

provides simple, fast contact lookup with a reliable free tier. It returns both email addresses and phone numbers, and it integrates directly with LinkedIn and popular CRMs. Lusha is a good choice for outreach professionals who want quick results without learning a complex platform.

How they compare

Hunter.io is best for straightforward email discovery with a clean interface. Apollo.io gives you more depth with built-in automation and lead scoring. Clearbit excels at data enrichment for existing lists. Lusha offers speed and ease of use for on-the-go prospecting. Choose based on whether you need a full sales suite, a data enhancer, or a lightweight lookup tool.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Apollo.io as a direct replacement for Hunter.io?
Yes, Apollo.io can replace Hunter.io for email finding, but it also adds many extra features like sequences and CRM sync. If you only need a simple email finder, Apollo may feel overwhelming. For a larger sales stack, it is a strong alternative.
Does Lusha have a limit on free lookups?
Yes, Lusha offers a limited number of free credits per month. After that you need a paid plan. The free tier is generous enough for light use, but heavy outreach will require a subscription.
