If you run a small business and need an easy way to find the right keywords for your website, Mangools is a popular choice because of its clear layout and simple tools. But sometimes you might want a different feature set, a different pricing model, or a tool that covers more ground. In this article we look at three strong Mangools alternatives: Ahrefs, SEMrush, and Ubersuggest. Each one can help you research keywords, but they differ in price, complexity, and depth.

alternative

Ahrefs

is a powerful SEO suite known for its huge backlink database and deep keyword data. It can feel overwhelming at first, but its keyword explorer and site explorer give small businesses very detailed insights for competitive research. The interface is polished, though the learning curve is steeper than Mangools.

alternative

SEMrush

offers an all-in-one marketing toolkit that goes beyond keyword research into content, social media, and advertising. For small businesses, it provides a solid keyword overview tool and useful competitive analysis. The dashboard is feature rich but can be busy for newcomers who just want a friendly UI.

Try SEMrush →
alternative

Ubersuggest

is a budget friendly tool by Neil Patel that focuses on keyword ideas, content suggestions, and domain overviews. It has a simpler, cleaner interface that feels closer to Mangools in ease of use. The data is less comprehensive than Ahrefs or SEMrush, but the price makes it accessible for very small businesses.

Try Ubersuggest →

How they compare

Mangools stands out for its friendly, beginner friendly interface and affordable pricing for basic keyword research. Ahrefs and SEMrush offer much larger datasets and more advanced features, but they cost more and require more time to learn. Ubersuggest is the cheapest alternative and keeps a simple layout, though its keyword data is less thorough. For a small business that needs an all-in-one suite without getting lost in menus, Mangools remains a strong choice, but Ubersuggest is a close match for simplicity and price.

Frequently asked questions

Is Ubersuggest really a good Mangools alternative for keyword research?
Yes, Ubersuggest gives you keyword ideas, search volume, and difficulty scores at a very low cost. The interface is clean and easy to navigate, so it works well for small businesses that want a simple tool similar to Mangools.
Can I get the same features as Mangools with a free plan from any of these tools?
Mangools offers a limited free trial, but full features require a paid plan. Ahrefs and SEMrush have no permanent free plans, only trials. Ubersuggest has a free tier with daily usage limits, which can be enough for occasional keyword checks.
