As a senior HR manager at a gig economy company, you know that speed and accuracy in background checks directly impact your ability to onboard drivers, couriers, or taskers. Checkr is a popular choice, but it is not the only option. Whether you need lower per check costs, stronger API documentation, or better support for state specific compliance, exploring alternatives can help you find the best fit for your platform’s scale and risk tolerance.

alternative

GoodHire

. GoodHire offers a developer friendly API with clear documentation and a sandbox environment for testing. Their platform supports continuous monitoring and has a strong reputation for customer service, making it suitable for gig companies that want a balance between automation and human support. They also provide flexible pricing tiers that scale with check volume.

alternative

HireRight

. HireRight is a well established provider with deep integrations and global screening capabilities. Their API allows for real time status updates and custom workflow triggers, which is useful for gig companies that operate across multiple states or countries. HireRight’s compliance tools are robust, but their pricing may be higher for small to mid sized volumes.

alternative

Accurate Background

. Accurate Background emphasizes speed and reliability, with an API that can return many checks within minutes. They offer a modular approach, letting you select only the services you need, such as motor vehicle records or county criminal searches. Their platform also includes built in FCRA and ban the box compliance features that help reduce legal risk.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer API first platforms with strong compliance support, but they differ in pricing flexibility and global reach. GoodHire is ideal for mid volume users who value service, HireRight suits enterprises needing international coverage, and Accurate Background works well for high volume, fast turnaround requirements. Checkr remains a strong primary option, but these alternatives can provide better value or specific features depending on your company’s size and screening priorities.

Frequently asked questions

How do these alternatives handle continuous monitoring for gig workers?
GoodHire and Accurate Background both offer continuous monitoring via their APIs, which allows you to re check workers on a schedule or event trigger. HireRight also provides this feature but typically bundles it as an add on service. Make sure to confirm whether the monitoring covers both criminal records and motor vehicle reports, as gig companies often need both.
Do these APIs support same day or instant background checks similar to Checkr?
Yes, Accurate Background and GoodHire can return instant or near instant results for certain search types, such as national criminal database and sex offender registry checks. HireRight’s speed depends on the scope of the search and the jurisdictions involved. Ask each vendor for their average turnaround times for the specific checks your gig platform requires before committing.
