If you are a security-savvy user who values Swiss data protection laws, ProtonVPN is a strong default choice. However, you might need a different provider for better speed, specialized features, or a simpler privacy model. Here are three well-known alternatives, each with its own strengths.

alternative

NordVPN

. NordVPN operates under Panama’s jurisdiction (outside surveillance alliances) and offers a massive server network with specialized servers for obfuscation, Onion over VPN, and P2P. Its double VPN feature routes traffic through two servers for extra encryption.

alternative

ExpressVPN

. ExpressVPN is based in the British Virgin Islands, which has no mandatory data retention laws. It is widely praised for its very fast server speeds and user-friendly apps, though it costs more than many competitors and does not offer a free tier.

alternative

Mullvad

. Mullvad is a Swedish provider that prioritizes anonymity: you can pay with cash and do not need an email address to create an account. It uses a simple, open-source client and publishes regular transparency reports, but it lacks server locations in some regions and has no streaming-optimized servers.

How they compare

- **Jurisdiction**: ProtonVPN is Swiss, Mullvad is Swedish, ExpressVPN is in the British Virgin Islands, NordVPN is in Panama. - **Privacy focus**: Mullvad emphasizes anonymity above all, ProtonVPN has a strong audit trail, NordVPN and ExpressVPN balance features with privacy. - **Free option**: Only ProtonVPN offers a limited free tier. - **Server count**: NordVPN has the most servers, Mullvad the fewest. - **Streaming support**: ExpressVPN and NordVPN are better for unblocking services. - **Pricing**: Mullvad and ProtonVPN (paid plans) are often cheaper than NordVPN and ExpressVPN.

Frequently asked questions

Can I trust alternatives that are not Swiss-based as much as ProtonVPN?
Trust depends on a provider’s jurisdiction, logging policy, and audit history. Mullvad operates under Sweden’s strong privacy laws and never logs, while NordVPN and ExpressVPN have headquarters in countries without data retention mandates. All three have undergone independent security audits.
Which alternative is best for someone who wants a simple, no-frills VPN with strong privacy?
Mullvad is the best choice for a Minimalist approach. It has a fixed low price, accepts anonymous payments, and its client is easy to use. However, it lacks extras like split tunneling or a kill switch on all platforms, so check compatibility first.
