If your home-building firm relies on Buildertrend for scheduling and budgeting, you may be exploring other options. While Buildertrend is a popular choice, some teams need different features, pricing models, or ease of use. This article covers four strong Buildertrend alternatives: Procore, PlanGrid, Autodesk BIM 360, and CoConstruct. Each tool offers cloud-based capabilities that help you manage timelines and costs more effectively.

alternative

Procore

. Procore is a comprehensive construction management platform that excels in large-scale project coordination. Its scheduling and budgeting tools integrate with accounting software, making it a strong fit for home builders who work on multiple complex projects simultaneously.

alternative

PlanGrid

. PlanGrid is known for its powerful blueprint and document management, but it also includes basic scheduling and cost tracking features. It works best for builders who prioritize field collaboration and real-time markups over detailed budget forecasting.

alternative

Autodesk BIM 360

. Autodesk BIM 360 provides advanced model-based scheduling and cost control for firms using building information modeling. It is ideal for home builders who need tight integration with design workflows and want to link 3D models directly to budget and timeline data.

alternative

CoConstruct

. CoConstruct is built specifically for custom home builders and remodelers, offering dedicated budgeting, scheduling, and client communication tools. It simplifies change orders and purchase orders, making it a direct competitor to Buildertrend for smaller to midsize firms.

How they compare

All four alternatives support cloud-based scheduling and budgeting, but their strengths differ. Procore handles large portfolios and complex accounting integration. PlanGrid focuses on field documents and lightweight cost tracking. Autodesk BIM 360 is best for teams using 3D models for planning. CoConstruct offers a tailored experience for custom home builders with robust change management. Choose based on your firm’s size, project complexity, and need for design integration.

Frequently asked questions

Which Buildertrend alternative is easiest for a small home builder to adopt?
CoConstruct is often the easiest for small to midsize custom home builders because it is designed for their workflows and includes straightforward budgeting and scheduling features without a steep learning curve.
Can Procore handle both scheduling and budgeting for home building projects?
Yes, Procore includes scheduling tools (via its Gantt chart view) and budget management with cost codes and change order tracking. However, its full functionality may require integration with your accounting software for detailed financial reporting.
