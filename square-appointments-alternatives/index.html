If you run a retail store that also offers services like haircuts, consultations, or fittings, you likely need a booking system that works hand in hand with your point of sale (POS). Square Appointments is a popular choice because it connects directly with Square’s payment hardware and inventory. But it may not fit every retailer’s budget or workflow. If you are looking for other options, this article compares three common alternatives: Calendly, Acuity, and SimplyBook. Each offers different levels of POS integration and booking features. Read on to find the best fit for your retail business.

alternative

Calendly

. Calendly focuses on simple, automated scheduling without built-in POS features. It works well for businesses that only need to send booking links and sync with a calendar. Retailers who use a separate POS system can link Calendly via third party tools like Zapier, but it does not handle payments or inventory directly.

alternative

Acuity

. Acuity provides a more complete booking experience with client intake forms, packages, and gift cards. It integrates with several payment processors, including Stripe and Square, so you can collect deposits or full payments at booking. However, it is not a POS system itself, so you would still need a separate retail POS for in-store sales.

alternative

SimplyBook

. SimplyBook is built for service-based businesses and offers a wide range of add-ons, including loyalty programs and membership management. It has native integrations with many POS systems, including Square and Clover, so you can sync appointments and payments. Retailers who sell both products and services may find its flexibility useful, though the interface can be less polished than Square’s.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Calendly to accept payments for appointments in my retail store?
Calendly does not process payments directly. You would need to connect it to a third party payment tool like Stripe via Zapier or use a separate checkout system. For in-store payments, you must use your existing POS separately.
Does SimplyBook replace my retail POS system?
No. SimplyBook is a booking and client management platform, not a full POS for product sales. It can integrate with your existing POS (like Square or Clover) to sync appointments and payments, but you still need a separate system for ringing up merchandise.
