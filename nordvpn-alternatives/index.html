NordVPN is a well known VPN service, but it is not the only option for privacy conscious users who need fast global servers. Whether you are looking for different features, pricing, or server locations, several strong alternatives exist. This article covers three top contenders: ExpressVPN, Surfshark, and ProtonVPN. Each offers strong security and high speed connections across the world.

alternative

ExpressVPN

stands out for its extremely fast server network spread across over 90 countries. It uses a lightweight protocol called Lightway that delivers reliable speeds for streaming and browsing. Its strict no logs policy and strong encryption make it a top choice for privacy.

alternative

Surfshark

offers unlimited simultaneous device connections, which is great for households or multiple gadgets. It maintains a global server footprint in over 100 countries with good speeds, and includes built in ad and tracker blocking. Its CleanWeb feature helps reduce online tracking without extra software.

alternative

ProtonVPN

is built by the team behind ProtonMail, focusing on privacy and transparency. It has a free tier with limited speeds, but its paid plans unlock fast servers in more than 60 countries. The service uses strong security measures and is based in Switzerland, which has strong privacy laws.

How they compare

All three alternatives provide strong privacy protections and fast global servers, but they differ in key areas. ExpressVPN leads in raw speed and server coverage, Surfshark excels in value and unlimited connections, and ProtonVPN offers the best transparency and a free option. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize maximum speed, device flexibility, or a privacy focused brand with a free tier.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use these alternatives for streaming Netflix and other services?
Yes, ExpressVPN, Surfshark, and ProtonVPN all work with major streaming platforms like Netflix, Hulu, and BBC iPlayer. However, performance may vary by server location and time. ExpressVPN and Surfshark are particularly reliable for unblocking geo restricted content.
Which alternative has the most servers worldwide?
ExpressVPN has the largest network with servers in over 90 countries, while Surfshark covers over 100 countries. ProtonVPN has servers in more than 60 countries. For maximum global reach, ExpressVPN or Surfshark are better choices.
