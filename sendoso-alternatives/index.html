Best Sendoso alternatives

Sendoso is a popular platform for sending physical gifts and swag to prospects and customers, but it may not fit every marketing team’s budget, workflow, or feature needs. If you are looking for a tool that offers better personalization, easier inventory management, or simpler integration with your CRM, exploring Sendoso alternatives can help you find the right fit. Below we cover four strong options that marketing teams can use to boost engagement with physical gifts.

alternative

Alyce

Alyce focuses on personalized gifting by letting recipients choose their own gift from a curated list, which increases the chance of a positive response. The platform integrates with major CRMs and offers AI-driven recommendations to help you select gifts that match each contact’s preferences. It is especially useful for account-based marketing campaigns where relevance matters most.

alternative

Printfection

Printfection is built for managing and shipping branded merchandise, such as t-shirts, notebooks, and other swag items. The platform includes a centralized inventory system, so you can track stock and send gifts without maintaining a physical warehouse. It works well for teams that want to scale their swag programs without manual logistics.

alternative

Reachdesk

Reachdesk combines physical gifting with direct mail automation, allowing you to send personalized gifts, handwritten notes, and brochures. The tool provides a built-in marketplace of gift options and handles fulfillment, making it easy to launch campaigns from your CRM or marketing automation platform. It is a strong choice for teams that need to coordinate both digital and physical touchpoints.

alternative

Snappy

Snappy offers a gift selection experience that lets recipients pick their own item from a themed collection, similar to Alyce but with a stronger emphasis on speed and simplicity. The platform supports both individual and bulk gifting and includes features like automated scheduling and real-time tracking. It is ideal for teams that want a low-friction way to send gifts for events, holidays, or follow-ups.

How they compare

All four alternatives help marketing teams send physical gifts, but they differ in focus. Alyce and Snappy prioritize recipient choice and personalization, while Printfection excels at branded swag inventory management. Reachdesk combines gifting with direct mail automation for integrated campaigns. Consider your team’s primary need: if you want recipients to pick their own gift, choose Alyce or Snappy. If you manage a large swag stock, go with Printfection. For a full direct mail solution, Reachdesk is the best fit.

Frequently asked questions

Can I integrate these Sendoso alternatives with my existing CRM or marketing automation tool?
Yes, all four tools offer integrations with popular CRMs such as Salesforce and HubSpot, as well as with platforms like Marketo and Pardot. Check each tool’s integration page for the full list and for any specific requirements.
Which alternative is best for sending gifts at scale to a large list of prospects?
Printfection and Reachdesk are well suited for high-volume gifting because they include inventory management and automated fulfillment. Snappy and Alyce also support bulk sending but may be more cost effective for smaller, personalized campaigns depending on your budget.
