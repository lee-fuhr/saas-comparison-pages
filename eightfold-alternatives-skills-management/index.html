Best Eightfold alternatives

Skills intelligence platforms help high growth tech companies map employee capabilities, close skill gaps, and power internal mobility. Eightfold is a strong AI driven solution, but your unique talent strategy might benefit from a different approach. This article examines three alternatives to Eightfold – Gloat, Fuel50, and Workday Skills Cloud – so you can choose the best fit for your organization.

alternative

Gloat

. Gloat focuses on talent marketplace and internal mobility, connecting employees with projects, mentors, and full time roles. Its strength lies in creating a dynamic, opportunity rich environment that keeps people engaged without relying on a single skills ontology. For a CHRO aiming to reduce turnover and boost reskilling, Gloat offers a more human centered, experiential alternative to Eightfold’s algorithm heavy matching.

Try Gloat →
alternative

Fuel50

. Fuel50 combines skills taxonomy with career pathing and internal mobility, emphasizing employee owned career development. It uses a skill cloud to recommend personalized learning and job opportunities, making it ideal for companies that want to empower individuals to drive their own growth. Compared to Eightfold, Fuel50 is often more intuitive for employees to use and easier to launch with pre built skill frameworks.

alternative

Workday Skills Cloud

. Workday Skills Cloud is a native skills engine embedded within the Workday HCM suite, using machine learning to infer skills from worker profiles and job descriptions. It provides a unified view of talent across HR processes like recruiting, performance, and succession. For organizations already on Workday, this alternative reduces integration complexity and cost, though it may lack the standalone depth of Eightfold’s AI.

How they compare

Eightfold excels at deep AI driven skill inference and external talent matching, making it a strong choice for companies with large, diverse talent pools. Gloat prioritizes internal talent mobility and employee experience through project based and mentoring opportunities. Fuel50 offers a balanced approach with clear career pathing and self directed development tools. Workday Skills Cloud is best for organizations that want seamless skill intelligence within their existing HCM system. Consider your priorities: AI power versus user experience, internal mobility versus holistic skills ontology, and integration demands versus standalone flexibility.
