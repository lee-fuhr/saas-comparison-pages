Best Zenefits alternatives

Growing startups often choose Zenefits for its all in one HR, benefits, and payroll features. But as your team expands, you might find Zenefits too expensive, too complex, or lacking the local support you need. Switching to a Zenefits alternative can give you simpler onboarding, better payroll integration, or a lower price point. The right platform should manage benefits administration and payroll in one place, saving you time and reducing errors.

alternative

BambooHR

. BambooHR is a strong alternative if your priority is employee experience and HR reporting, but its payroll and benefits features are limited outside the United States. It integrates with third party payroll providers like Gusto or ADP, so you may need two systems. For startups with fewer than 500 employees, BambooHR’s simple interface and robust time off tracking are popular, but you lose the single platform approach.

alternative

Factorial

. Factorial offers an all in one HR, payroll, and benefits solution designed for small and mid sized startups, especially those with remote or international teams. It includes document management, time tracking, and automated payroll calculations without needing separate integrations. Factorial is a good choice if you want a user friendly, affordable platform and operate in multiple countries.

alternative

Gusto HR

. Gusto is a direct competitor to Zenefits, providing integrated payroll, benefits administration, and HR tools for growing startups. Its strengths include automated tax filings, health insurance brokerage, and a simple employee self service portal. Gusto is best for US based startups that want a single system for payroll and benefits without the complexity of a full HRIS.

alternative

Namely

. Namely targets mid sized startups (50 to 500 employees) with a unified platform for payroll, benefits, and talent management. It offers a modern interface, compliance support, and a benefits broker network that helps you offer competitive packages. Namely works well if your startup needs a more customizable HR platform with strong reporting and performance management features.

How they compare

BambooHR excels in HR reporting but lacks native payroll and benefits integration, often requiring a separate partner. Factorial and Gusto both deliver true all in one platforms, with Factorial better for international teams and Gusto better for US based startups. Namely offers the most comprehensive talent management but at a higher cost, making it ideal for startups that have outgrown basic tools. Your choice depends on your team size, location, and whether you need a single system or are willing to use integrations.
