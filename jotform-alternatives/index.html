JotForm is a popular form builder, but it may not fit every business. You might find its pricing high for advanced features, or need something simpler. This article explores five JotForm alternatives: Typeform, Formstack, Cognito Forms, Tally, and Google Forms. Each offers different strengths in price and functionality. Use this guide to find the right tool for collecting data, payments, or surveys without overspending.

alternative

Typeform

focuses on conversational, one-question-at-a-time forms that boost engagement. It works well for customer feedback and quizzes, but its free plan is limited and paid plans start higher than some competitors.

Cognito Forms

provides powerful form logic, calculations, and payment processing at a competitive price. It offers a generous free tier and paid plans that scale with business needs, making it a strong JotForm alternative for cost-conscious users.

alternative

Tally

is a no-frills, free form builder with unlimited forms and responses. It lacks some advanced features like file uploads and payment integrations, but it is ideal for basic data collection without any subscription.

Frequently asked questions

Which JotForm alternative is best for cutting costs?
If you need zero cost, use Google Forms or Tally. For advanced features at a low price, Cognito Forms offers a strong free tier and affordable paid plans.
Can I migrate my existing JotForm forms to these alternatives?
Most tools offer import options for CSV or JSON data. Typeform, Cognito Forms, and Formstack have built-in importers. Google Forms and Tally may require manual copy-paste or third-party tools. Check each tool’s help documentation for specific steps.
