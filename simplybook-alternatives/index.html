SimplyBook is a popular scheduling tool for small businesses, but its free tier can feel limited when you need more appointments or custom features. Many small businesses look for alternatives that offer a truly useful free plan to manage client bookings without monthly costs. This article covers three strong alternatives: Calendly, Acuity, and Booksy. Each has a free tier built for different types of businesses, so you can find the one that fits your workflow.

alternative

Calendly

offers a generous free plan that includes one calendar connection and unlimited event types. You can set up standard one-on-one meetings and share your booking link easily with clients. It is best for businesses that need simple, time-based scheduling rather than complex service options.

Acuity

An alternative to SimplyBook.

alternative

Booksy

is built for beauty, wellness, and personal care professionals. Its free plan allows you to manage a single service and accept bookings with automated reminders. It is ideal if your business runs on appointments that require specific durations, prices, and prepayments.

How they compare

All three free tiers have limits, but each tool suits a different type of business. Calendly is the simplest for one-on-one meetings with no service categories. Acuity offers more depth for multi-service businesses but has stricter limits on free plan appointments. Booksy is the best choice for appointment-based personal services like salons or barbershops, though its free tier only supports one service. SimplyBook itself remains a good option if you need advanced features like payment integration at low cost, but its free plan can be restrictive for growing businesses.
