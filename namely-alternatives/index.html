If you are a mid-size company looking for an all-in-one HR platform that combines payroll and benefits, you may have considered Namely. Namely offers a modern interface and strong compliance tools, but its pricing and feature set may not fit every organization. Fortunately, several alternatives provide similar integrated tools, often with different strengths in usability, scalability, or cost. Below we compare four popular options: BambooHR, Factorial, Zenefits, and Gusto HR.

alternative

BambooHR

. BambooHR is best known for its user-friendly employee records and time-off tracking, and it also offers a built-in payroll module and benefits administration through partner integrations. It works well for mid-size companies that want a clean interface and strong reporting, though its benefits options may be less flexible than Namely’s for fully self-managed plans.

alternative

Factorial

. Factorial provides a centralized platform for HR, payroll, and expense management, with a focus on European and growing international teams. It includes automated payroll calculations and benefits tracking, making it a solid option if your company operates across multiple countries or needs a more budget-friendly alternative without sacrificing core features.

alternative

Zenefits

. Zenefits delivers a comprehensive suite that includes payroll, benefits, time tracking, and compliance, all in one dashboard. It is particularly strong for mid-size companies that want to streamline benefits administration and integrate with popular insurance brokers, though some users find its customer support less responsive than Namely’s.

alternative

Gusto HR

. Gusto is a popular choice for small to mid-size businesses, offering automated payroll, health benefits, and 401(k) administration. It is simpler to set up than Namely and provides excellent employee self-service, but it may lack the advanced HR analytics and customization that growing mid-size companies require.
