Revenue operations teams face a constant challenge: turning scattered CRM data into accurate, actionable forecasts. Clari has become the leading choice for consolidating pipeline health, deal risk, and revenue predictions into a single pane of glass. But with alternatives like Aviso, BoostUp, People.ai, SetSail, and Forecastly also vying for your stack, understanding the key differences is essential. This article focuses on Clari versus Aviso, while giving you a quick look at each alternative so you can decide which centralized platform best supports your forecasting needs.

alternative

Aviso

offers AI-driven forecasting that surfaces deal-level risks and coaching opportunities. It integrates directly with your CRM but can feel more tactical than strategic, making it better suited for teams that want quick rep-level insights rather than a full revenue operations layer.

alternative

BoostUp

specializes in revenue intelligence by ingesting data from CRM, email, and calendars to predict close rates. Its strength lies in its data enrichment and anomaly detection, though some users find its interface less intuitive than Clari for daily pipeline reviews.

alternative

People.ai

focuses on capturing rep activity data to measure engagement and forecast outcomes. It excels at providing activity-based signals but requires heavy data integration and may not offer the same out-of-the-box pipeline visibility that a centralized forecasting platform like Clari delivers.

alternative

SetSail

uses machine learning to identify which deals are likely to close and why. It is built for sales coaching and pipeline inspection, but its forecasting capabilities are often considered supplementary to an existing CRM rather than a standalone headquarters for revenue ops.

alternative

Forecastly

is a lightweight forecasting tool that syncs with your CRM to provide roll-up predictions. It is easy to set up and affordably priced, but it lacks the depth of deal-level risk analysis and the robust revenue intelligence features that larger teams typically require.

How they compare

Clari and Aviso both aim to improve forecast accuracy, but they differ in scope and depth. Clari acts as a complete revenue operations platform, unifying pipeline management, call intelligence, and predictive forecasting into one centralized view. Aviso is more narrowly focused on CRM-based forecasting and deal coaching, with less emphasis on the broader revenue workflow. For revenue ops teams that need a single source of truth for pipeline health, deal risk, and revenue projections, Clari offers a more comprehensive and integrated solution. Aviso may work for smaller teams that only need a lightweight forecast overlay, but it lacks the cross-source data sync and strategic reporting that Clari provides.
