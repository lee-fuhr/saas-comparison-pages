If you run a small business and need an affordable applicant tracking system, JazzHR is a popular choice. But maybe it does not fit your exact needs, or you want to compare other options. This article covers four strong JazzHR alternatives that focus on easy job posting and simple candidate tracking, without a high price tag.

alternative

Greenhouse

. Greenhouse is built for growing teams that want structured hiring workflows. It offers strong integrations with job boards and a clear pipeline view, but its pricing starts higher than JazzHR, so it works best if you plan to scale quickly.

alternative

Lever

. Lever combines an ATS with basic CRM features to help you build talent pools over time. Its interface is clean and easy to navigate, and job posting to multiple sites is straightforward, though small teams may find some advanced features unnecessary.

alternative

Workable

. Workable is a direct competitor to JazzHR with a focus on speed and simplicity. It lets you post jobs to free and paid boards in one click, and its candidate tracking feels intuitive, making it a solid choice for very small teams or solo recruiters.

alternative

BambooHR ATS

. BambooHR offers its ATS as part of a broader HR platform, but you can use the tracking module on its own. It specializes in simple candidate management and job board posting, and it syncs naturally with BambooHR’s core HR tools if you need them later.
