Mentimeter is a popular tool for live polls, quizzes, and word clouds, but it may not fit every budget, audience, or feature list. Educators and corporate trainers often look for Mentimeter alternatives that offer different pricing, question types, or integration options. This article compares three strong alternatives: Slido, Kahoot!, and Poll Everywhere. Each tool has its own strengths, and the right choice depends on your presentation style, group size, and specific needs.

alternative

Slido

is ideal for corporate meetings and webinars because it integrates directly with slide decks in PowerPoint, Google Slides, and Keynote. It offers live polls, Q&A, and word clouds, and participants can join without creating an account. Slido’s free plan limits you to three polls per event, so it works best for smaller or one-off sessions.

alternative

Kahoot!

is a game based learning platform that turns reviews and training into fast paced competitions. It works well for educators who want to boost engagement with music, timers, and leaderboards. Corporate trainers can also use Kahoot! for team building, but its quiz focused design may feel less formal than polling tools for large meetings.

alternative

Poll Everywhere

focuses on audience response with a wide variety of question types, including clickable image polls, open ended text, and ranked choices. It integrates with presentation software and offers robust reporting. Poll Everywhere is a good fit for trainers who need detailed analytics and want to collect responses before or after a session, not just live.
