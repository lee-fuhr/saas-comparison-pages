Best Clearbit alternatives

If you rely on Clearbit for B2B data enrichment but are exploring other options, you are in the right place. Marketing operations teams often need reliable APIs to append firmographic, technographic, and contact data to their records. This article covers four Clearbit alternatives: ZoomInfo, Lusha, Apollo.io, and DiscoverOrg, so you can compare features, pricing models, and data quality.

alternative

ZoomInfo

. ZoomInfo offers a large B2B contact and company database with enrichment via API and browser extensions. It excels at firmographic and intent data, but its pricing is often annual and enterprise focused, which may not suit smaller teams.

alternative

Lusha

. Lusha provides direct dial phone numbers and email addresses on demand, with a simple API for real time enrichment. It is generally more affordable than Clearbit and has a pay per contact model, but the dataset is narrower, centered on contact details rather than broad firmographics.

alternative

Apollo.io

. Apollo.io combines a database of hundreds of millions of contacts with enrichment, prospecting, and engagement features in one platform. Its API is flexible and commonly used for lead scoring and list enrichment, though data accuracy can vary depending on the market.

alternative

DiscoverOrg

. DiscoverOrg (now part of ZoomInfo) historically specialized in highly accurate contact data for specific industries like tech and finance. It still offers API access to that curated dataset, but it requires a subscription and may lack the breadth of a general purpose enrichment tool.

How they compare

Clearbit remains a strong choice for broad firmographic and technographic enrichment with a straightforward API. Among the alternatives, ZoomInfo provides the deepest firmographic and intent data but at a higher cost and longer commitment. Lusha is best when you need quick contact details on a pay as you go basis. Apollo.io is a solid all in one option if you also want CRM enrichment and sales engagement. DiscoverOrg offers niche accuracy for specific verticals but is less flexible for general use.
