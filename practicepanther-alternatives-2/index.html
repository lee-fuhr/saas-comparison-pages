PracticePanther is a popular legal practice management platform, but its time tracking and billing features may not fit every firm’s workflow. If you need a tool that automates time capture and streamlines billing cycles, it is worth comparing alternatives. This article reviews four strong contenders: Clio, MyCase, Smokeball, and Filevine. Each offers different strengths for automating time entries and invoicing, helping you choose the right fit for your firm.

alternative

Clio

. Clio’s time tracking integrates directly with calendars and email, automatically linking billable activities to matters. Its billing engine supports batch invoicing and trust accounting, making it a solid choice for firms that want a seamless timeline from time entry to payment.

alternative

MyCase

. MyCase offers a clean interface with built-in timers and a dashboard that shows unbilled time at a glance. Its billing automation includes recurring invoices and online payment reminders, which helps reduce manual follow-up and speeds up the billing cycle.

alternative

Smokeball

. Smokeball excels at automatic time capture by logging keystrokes, document activity, and phone calls. Its billing module then turns those recorded actions into draft invoices, cutting down on after-the-fact entries and ensuring no time slips through the cracks.

alternative

Filevine

. Filevine combines case management with a custom time tracking system that works with projects and tasks. Its billing automation allows you to set fixed fees or hourly rates per matter, and the platform generates invoices in bulk, which is useful for high-volume litigation firms.

How they compare

Clio offers the most mature integration across calendars and payments, while MyCase prioritizes simplicity and client-facing billing portals. Smokeball provides the deepest automatic time capture through desktop monitoring, and Filevine gives you granular control over task-based billing. Choose Clio or MyCase for all-in-one ease, Smokeball for hands-off time logging, or Filevine for complex matter structures.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives import my existing PracticePanther data?
Yes, most of them offer data import services or CSV migration tools for transferring contacts, matters, and time entries. Check with each vendor for specific support and any formatting requirements.
Do any of these tools offer a free trial for testing time tracking and billing?
Yes, Clio, MyCase, Smokeball, and Filevine all provide free trials. Trial lengths vary from 7 to 30 days. Use the trial period to test automatic time capture and billing cycle features with live matters.
