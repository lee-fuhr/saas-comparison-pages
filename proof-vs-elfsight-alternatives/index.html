If you run an e-commerce site, showing recent purchases or sign-ups can boost trust and conversions. Proof is a popular social proof widget, but you might wonder how it stacks up against Elfsight and other options. This article compares Proof to four key alternatives: Trustpilot widget, ProveSource, Elfsight, and Trustpilot itself. We focus on what matters most for online stores: easy setup, customization, and real results.

alternative

Trustpilot widget

. This tool pulls in your existing Trustpilot reviews and displays them as social proof on your site. It works best if you already use Trustpilot for collecting ratings, because the widget shows recent reviews and star counts without extra coding.

alternative

ProveSource

. ProveSource lets you show live notifications like recent purchases, sign-ups, or reviews from your own data. It offers multiple templates and triggers, making it a solid choice for stores that want to highlight both sales and user activity.

alternative

Elfsight

. Elfsight provides a wide range of widgets beyond social proof, including countdown timers and testimonials. Its social proof app is easy to embed but can feel less focused on real-time purchase alerts compared to dedicated tools.

alternative

Trustpilot

. Trustpilot itself is a review platform, not just a widget. If you embed a Trustpilot feed on your site, you get verified customer reviews and star ratings. It is excellent for building long term credibility but lacks live purchase notifications.

How they compare

Proof is built specifically for e-commerce social proof, with smart triggers based on customer behavior. Elfsight offers more general widgets but fewer real time purchase alerts. Trustpilot widget and platform are review centric, ideal for trust but not for urgency. ProveSource sits between Proof and Elfsight, giving you purchase notifications plus review pop ups. Choose Proof if you want targeted notification rules. Pick Elfsight if you need a multi purpose widget bundle. Pick Trustpilot if reviews are your main asset. Pick ProveSource if you want a balance of both.
