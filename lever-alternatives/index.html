If your team uses Lever for applicant tracking and recruiting, you might be wondering if there are better options out there. Lever is a solid ATS, but some teams find its pricing, reporting limits, or workflow flexibility don’t match their growth stage. This article covers five common Lever alternatives – Greenhouse, Ashby, Workday, Teamtailor, and Pinpoint – so you can compare value and features before making a switch.

alternative

Greenhouse

. Greenhouse offers a structured, data-driven recruiting platform with strong interview kits and scorecards. It is best for teams that want a repeatable hiring process with robust analytics, but it often costs more than Lever and can feel overwhelming for smaller teams.

alternative

Ashby

. Ashby provides a modern, flexible ATS with deep customization and a built-in analytics layer. It tends to offer better value than Lever for growing teams because its pricing includes many features that Lever charges extra for, though it has a smaller ecosystem of integrations.

Try Ashby →
alternative

Workday

. Workday is a full human capital management suite that includes recruiting, onboarding, and HR tools. It works well for large enterprises that need everything in one system, but it is overkill for most mid-market teams and typically has a longer implementation timeline than Lever.

alternative

Teamtailor

. Teamtailor combines an ATS with a branded career page and employer branding tools. It is a more affordable alternative to Lever and excels at creating a modern candidate experience, though its reporting and advanced workflow options are less mature.

alternative

Pinpoint

. Pinpoint is a purpose-built ATS for high volume and fast growing companies, with features like automated scheduling and structured hiring. It offers a simpler user interface than Lever and competitive pricing, but its customization and third party app catalog are smaller.

How they compare

Lever is a strong all around ATS, but its pricing escalates quickly and its reporting can be limited. Greenhouse provides more structure for scaling teams, Ashby offers better value and flexibility, Workday targets enterprise HR needs, Teamtailor emphasizes employer branding on a budget, and Pinpoint focuses on speed and simplicity for growing companies. Your choice depends on team size, feature priorities, and budget.

Frequently asked questions

When should I consider switching from Lever to an alternative?
You should consider switching if your team outgrows Lever’s reporting, finds the pricing too high for the features you need, or wants more flexibility in workflows. If you need better analytics or a more modern interface, tools like Ashby or Pinpoint are worth evaluating.
Are these alternatives cheaper than Lever?
Some alternatives tend to be more affordable depending on your team size. Teamtailor and Pinpoint often have lower starting prices than Lever, while Ashby offers transparent pricing including features that Lever charges as add ons. Greenhouse and Workday can be more expensive, especially for larger teams or full suite deployments.
