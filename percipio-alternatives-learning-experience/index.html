As a Learning Technology Manager in a regulated industry like healthcare or finance, you need a learning experience platform (LXP) that supports skill development while meeting strict compliance requirements. Percipio has been a popular choice, but it may not fit every organization’s needs for deep skill mapping or regulatory audit trails. If you are evaluating alternatives, three platforms worth considering are Degreed, EdCast, and Cornerstone. This article compares them on key criteria for a skill-based LXP in highly regulated environments.

alternative

Degreed

focuses on building skills through curated content, certifications, and on-the-job learning. It offers strong skills taxonomy and analytics that help track proficiency growth, which is useful for demonstrating compliance in healthcare and finance. Degreed also integrates with many content providers and learning management systems.

alternative

EdCast

positions itself as a knowledge cloud with AI-powered personalization and content curation. It provides skill assessments and gap analysis, making it easier to agree learning with regulatory requirements. EdCast’s emphasis on continuous learning and employee-driven pathways appeals to organizations wanting to move beyond traditional course completion.

alternative

Cornerstone

combines an LXP with a robust learning management system (LMS), offering deep reporting for audits in regulated sectors. Its skill-based features include competency models and career development plans, which help you map learning to job roles. Cornerstone’s compliance tracking and content management tools are well suited for industries that must prove training completion.

How they compare

All three alternatives provide skill-based learning, but they differ in their approach to compliance and integration. Degreed is strong on skill visibility and personalized pathways, yet its reporting may require additional configuration for detailed audit logs. EdCast excels in AI-driven content discovery and real-time skill insights, but it may need extra support for mandated training assignments. Cornerstone offers the most mature compliance and regulatory tracking out of the box, making it a natural fit for highly regulated environments. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize skill agility (Degreed or EdCast) or built-in compliance rigor (Cornerstone).
