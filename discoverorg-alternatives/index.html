Best DiscoverOrg alternatives

As an enterprise account executive, you rely on accurate B2B data to find the right buyers, personalize outreach, and close deals faster. DiscoverOrg has long been a staple for high quality firmographic and technographic data, but it may not fit every budget or use case. Whether you are looking for better enrichment, lower cost, or a platform with stronger sales engagement features, several alternatives can match or exceed what DiscoverOrg offers. Below we compare four top options for enterprise sales teams: ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Lusha, and Apollo.io.

alternative

ZoomInfo

offers the broadest coverage of companies and contacts in one platform, with intent signals and direct dials that enterprise teams often need. Its data is updated frequently, and the platform includes built-in workflows for list building and email sequencing. However, pricing is typically higher than other options and can require an annual commitment.

alternative

Clearbit

focuses on real time data enrichment and lead scoring, making it a strong choice for enterprise teams that use marketing automation or CRM platforms like Salesforce and HubSpot. It does not provide a large contact database on its own, but it can enhance existing records with firmographic, behavioral, and technographic data. Clearbit is better suited as a data add on rather than a standalone prospecting tool.

alternative

Lusha

provides a lightweight, easy to use solution for finding direct dials and email addresses, with a browser extension that works inside LinkedIn and CRM. Its data quality is high for individual contact details, but its firmographic coverage is limited compared to DiscoverOrg. Enterprise teams often use Lusha as a supplement rather than a primary data source.

alternative

Apollo.io

combines a large contact database with built in sales engagement tools like sequences and email tracking. Its pricing is generally lower than DiscoverOrg and ZoomInfo, making it appealing for teams that want an all in one platform. Keep in mind that Apollo’s data can sometimes be less accurate for very large enterprises, so verification steps are recommended.

How they compare

ZoomInfo gives you the deepest enterprise coverage and intent data at a premium price. Clearbit excels at enriching existing records with real time firmographic signals. Lusha is best for quick contact lookups on individual leads. Apollo.io offers a cost effective all in one platform with database and engagement features. Choose based on your primary need: breadth of data (ZoomInfo), enrichment (Clearbit), speed (Lusha), or value plus tools (Apollo.io).

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the most cost effective for a large enterprise sales team?
Apollo.io typically offers the lowest per seat pricing among these options, especially when you consider its included sales engagement tools. However, for very large teams that prioritize data accuracy and coverage, ZoomInfo’s higher cost may be justified. It is best to request trials from each vendor and compare based on your team’s specific contact volume needs.
Can I use Clearbit or Lusha as a direct replacement for DiscoverOrg?
Not . Clearbit is primarily an enrichment tool, not a full prospecting database, so you would need an existing list of names or companies to enrich. Lusha is great for finding individual contact details but lacks the firmographic and technographic depth that DiscoverOrg provides for account research. Most enterprise teams pair Clearbit or Lusha with another data source rather than using them alone.
