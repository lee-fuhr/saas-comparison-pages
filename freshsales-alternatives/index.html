Best Freshsales alternatives

Freshsales is a solid CRM for small and mid-size teams, but it may not fit every budget, workflow, or integration need. If you are evaluating modern platforms, several alternatives offer different strengths in pricing, automation, or ease of use. This article compares five popular options to help you decide which one aligns best with your sales process.

alternative

HubSpot

. HubSpot’s free CRM gives you core sales tools like contact management, deal tracking, and email integration at no cost. Paid tiers add advanced automation, reporting, and a full marketing suite, making it a strong choice if you want to scale from a small team to a larger operation.

Try HubSpot →
alternative

Pipedrive

. Pipedrive is built around a visual sales pipeline that lets you drag and drop deals through stages. Its simple interface and focus on activity tracking make it ideal for teams that want to manage high-volume leads without complex customization.

Try Pipedrive →
alternative

Close

. Close offers built-in calling, SMS, and email sequencing directly inside the CRM. It is designed for outbound sales teams that need to log calls automatically and follow up quickly, reducing manual data entry.

Try Close →
alternative

Zoho CRM

. Zoho CRM provides a wide range of features, including AI assistant Zia, workflow automation, and multi-channel communication. It is a cost-effective option for teams that need deep customization and modular add-ons for inventory, invoices, or project management.

alternative

Salesmate

. Salesmate combines pipeline management, email automation, and conversation tracking with a clean, modern interface. It supports workflows and custom fields, making it a good middle ground for teams that find Freshsales too complex or too simple.

Frequently asked questions

Which Freshsales alternative is best for a very small team with a limited budget?
HubSpot’s free CRM is a strong starting point for small teams because it covers contact management, deal tracking, and email integration with no upfront cost. Zoho CRM also offers a free plan for up to three users, though it includes fewer features than HubSpot’s free tier.
I need a CRM that focuses on phone calls and outbound sales. Which one should I choose?
Close is built specifically for outbound sales teams and includes native calling, SMS, and automatic call logging. Pipedrive also supports calling through integrations, but Close gives you the most seamless phone experience without extra setup.
