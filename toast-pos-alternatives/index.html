Best Toast POS alternatives

Toast POS is a popular choice for full service restaurants, but it isn’t the only option. Many restaurant owners look for alternatives that offer different pricing, feature sets, or hardware options. This article compares four strong competitors: Square Restaurants, Lightspeed Restaurant, TouchBistro, and Revel Systems. Each is designed for full service operations and includes back office tools for inventory, reporting, and employee management.

alternative

Square Restaurants

. Square offers a flat rate pricing model with no long term contracts, which can appeal to operators who want predictable costs. Its integrated payment processing, online ordering, and payroll tools work seamlessly together. However, some full service features like advanced table management may be less robust than Toast’s.

alternative

Lightspeed Restaurant

. Lightspeed provides a cloud based POS with strong inventory tracking and multi location management capabilities. It includes built in online ordering and a customer facing ordering app. The platform works well for full service restaurants that need detailed menu and purchase order controls.

alternative

TouchBistro

. TouchBistro is a tablet based POS that runs offline, which helps avoid disruptions if internet goes down. It focuses on table side ordering, split checks, and menu modifications common in full service settings. The back office includes inventory, labor scheduling, and reporting, though some advanced analytics require add ons.

alternative

Revel Systems

. Revel is an iPad based POS with a customizable interface and strong inventory management for complex menus. It offers offline mode and integrations with third party tools for accounting and payroll. The system can handle high volume environments but often requires professional installation and higher upfront costs.

How they compare

All four alternatives support full service table management, split checks, and back office reporting. Square Restaurants has the simplest pricing and quick setup. Lightspeed offers strong multi location and inventory features. TouchBistro excels with offline reliability and table side service. Revel provides high customization and scalability for larger operations. Pricing varies from flat rate (Square) to monthly subscriptions with transaction fees (Lightspeed, TouchBistro, Revel). Each has a free trial or demo available.
