Best TOPdesk alternatives

TOPdesk is a popular IT service management (ITSM) tool, especially for mid-sized organizations. But as your needs change, you might want a platform with more advanced automation, better integration options, or a different pricing model. This article covers four strong alternatives: BMC Remedy, ServiceNow, Cherwell, and Freshservice. Each offers unique strengths for IT service managers.

alternative

BMC Remedy

. BMC Remedy is a mature ITSM solution known for its deep customization and integration with enterprise systems. It suits large organizations that need to manage complex IT operations and prefer on-premises or hybrid deployments. However, its setup can be resource intensive.

alternative

ServiceNow

. ServiceNow is a leading cloud based ITSM platform with a broad range of modules beyond service desk like HR and security. It excels in workflow automation and scalability for large enterprises. The trade off is higher cost and potential complexity for smaller teams.

alternative

Cherwell

. Cherwell offers flexible ITSM through its low code configuration approach, allowing IT managers to tailor workflows without heavy coding. It works well for organizations that want a balance between out of the box features and customization. However, its market presence is smaller compared to others.

alternative

Freshservice

. Freshservice is a modern cloud based ITSM tool with an intuitive user interface and affordable pricing for mid-sized teams. It includes automation features like AI powered ticketing and asset management. It may lack some advanced capabilities needed by very large enterprises.

How they compare

When choosing a TOPdesk alternative, consider your organization’s size, budget, and customization needs. ServiceNow offers the broadest functionality but at a higher price point. BMC Remedy is strong for complex enterprise environments but requires more IT resources. Cherwell provides a middle ground with low code flexibility. Freshservice is the most user friendly and cost effective for growing teams. All four have good support for ITIL processes, but the right choice depends on your scaling and integration requirements.

Frequently asked questions

How do I decide between a cloud based or on-premises ITSM alternative?
Cloud based options like ServiceNow and Freshservice offer lower upfront costs and automatic updates, while on premises tools like BMC Remedy give you full control over data and security. Your decision should depend on your compliance requirements, IT team capability, and budget for infrastructure.
Can I migrate data from TOPdesk to any of these alternatives easily?
Yes, all listed alternatives provide data import tools or APIs to migrate tickets, assets, and configurations from TOPdesk. The effort varies based on data complexity, but most vendors offer professional services or documentation to assist with the migration.
