Best Chaport alternatives

Chaport is a solid live chat tool, but teams that need to manage conversations across email, social media, and chat often look for stronger multi-channel inboxes. Proactive chat triggers – messages that pop up based on visitor behavior – are another key feature that varies between tools. This article covers seven Chaport alternatives, focusing on how each handles multi-channel inboxes and proactive triggers.

alternative

Olark

. Olark offers a simple live chat with customizable proactive triggers based on page visits and time on site. Its multi-channel inbox is limited to chat only, so it works best for teams that don’t need email or social integration in one place.

alternative

JivoChat

. JivoChat provides a true multi-channel inbox that combines chat, email, phone calls, and Telegram. Its proactive triggers include timed greetings and page-specific messages, making it a strong all-in-one alternative to Chaport.

alternative

Pure Chat

. Pure Chat focuses on straightforward live chat with basic proactive triggers like time-based greetings. It does not include a multi-channel inbox, so it suits teams that only need website chat and want a simple setup.

alternative

Smartsupp

. Smartsupp offers video recordings and a basic multi-channel inbox that supports chat and email. Its proactive triggers are limited to auto-messages based on time and page visits, which is similar to Chaport’s core functionality.

alternative

LiveChat

. LiveChat is a feature-rich platform with a powerful multi-channel inbox that unifies chat, email, and social media messages. Its proactive triggers are highly customizable, including rules for cart abandonment and specific URLs, giving teams more control than Chaport.

alternative

Tidio

. Tidio combines live chat with a chatbot builder and a multi-channel inbox that covers chat, email, and Facebook Messenger. Proactive triggers can be set for time on page, scroll depth, and exit intent, providing a modern alternative with strong automation.

alternative

Freshchat

. Freshchat, part of the Freshdesk suite, offers a multi-channel inbox that includes chat, email, WhatsApp, and other messaging apps. Its proactive triggers allow rules based on customer properties and behavior, making it ideal for teams that need deep CRM integration.

How they compare

For teams that prioritize multi-channel inboxes, JivoChat, LiveChat, Tidio, and Freshchat stand out – they all combine chat with email or social channels. For proactive chat triggers, LiveChat and Tidio offer the most customization. Olark and Pure Chat are simpler choices if you only need basic live chat. Smartsupp adds video recording but lacks advanced multi-channel support. Choose an alternative based on whether you need one unified inbox or advanced automation.
