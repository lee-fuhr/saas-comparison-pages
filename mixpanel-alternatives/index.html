Best Mixpanel alternatives

If your product or growth team uses Mixpanel to track user behavior, you know how powerful behavioral analytics can be. But Mixpanel’s pricing, complexity, or feature set may no longer fit your needs. Whether you are scaling quickly, working with a smaller budget, or simply want a different approach to product analytics, there are several strong Mixpanel alternatives worth considering. Below we explore five tools that can help you understand user actions without the overhead of Mixpanel.

alternative

Amplitude

. Amplitude is a direct competitor to Mixpanel, offering similar event-based analytics with a strong focus on product-led growth. It provides advanced features like behavioral cohorts, funnel analysis, and a robust charting interface that scales well for larger teams. However, its pricing can become expensive as you add more tracked events or users.

Try Amplitude →
alternative

PostHog

. PostHog is an open source product analytics platform that gives you full control over your data and infrastructure. It includes session recording, feature flags, and heatmaps alongside traditional event tracking, making it a good fit for teams that want self-hosted or private cloud options. The community edition is free, but enterprise features require a paid license.

Try PostHog →
alternative

Heap

. Heap automates event capture so you do not need to manually instrument every click or page view. This makes it easy to retroactively analyze user behavior without writing additional code. It is particularly useful for early stage teams that want to move fast, though the automatic approach can lead to higher data volumes and costs as you scale.

Try Heap →
alternative

June.so

. June.so is a lightweight analytics tool built on top of your data warehouse, often used with tools like Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift. It focuses on simplicity and speed, letting you query user behavior using SQL or a simple interface. It is a great choice for teams that already have a warehouse and want to avoid vendor lock in.

alternative

Pendo

. Pendo combines product analytics with in app guides and feedback tools. It helps you track feature usage and user paths while also enabling you to create targeted onboarding flows and surveys. This dual capability makes it ideal for product teams that want to both analyze and influence user behavior in one platform.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free Mixpanel alternative that still offers powerful analytics?
Yes, PostHog offers a free self hosted community edition with most core analytics features, including event tracking, funnels, and session recording. Heap also has a free tier for up to 10,000 sessions per month, though its automatic capture may generate more data than you expect.
Which Mixpanel alternative is best for teams that already use a data warehouse?
June.so is designed specifically for warehouse native teams. It lets you run queries directly on your existing data in Snowflake, BigQuery, or Redshift, avoiding duplicate storage and reducing vendor lock in. Amplitude also offers warehouse connectivity, but June.so is simpler and more cost effective for this use case.
