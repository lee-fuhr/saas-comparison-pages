If you are an SDR or founder using Apollo for prospecting, you may be wondering what other options exist. Apollo is a popular all-in-one platform for lead generation, email outreach, and CRM integration. But depending on your budget, data needs, or workflow, you might benefit from exploring alternatives like ZoomInfo, Lusha, Hunter.io, Clay, and Instantly. Each tool has its own strengths and weaknesses compared to Apollo.

ZoomInfo

– A comprehensive B2B database with enriched contact and company data. It offers advanced filtering and intent signals but tends to be more expensive than Apollo, making it better suited for enterprise teams with larger budgets.

Hunter.io

– Focuses on email finding and verification, with a straightforward interface and domain search. It is ideal for SDRs who need reliable email addresses without extra features, but it does not offer the same depth of firmographic data or sequencing tools as Apollo.

Clay

– A data enrichment and automation platform that combines multiple data sources into a single workflow. It is powerful for building custom lead lists and integrating with other tools, but it requires more technical setup compared to Apollo’s out-of-the-box prospecting.

alternative

Instantly

– Specializes in cold email deliverability and infrastructure, with built-in warming and sending capabilities. It is a strong choice for SDRs focused on inbox placement, but it does not include a native lead database, so you would need to combine it with Apollo or other data sources.

How they compare

Each alternative serves a different primary function. ZoomInfo offers the largest B2B database but at a higher cost. Lusha and Hunter.io are more affordable for contact data but lack Apollo’s full suite. Clay excels at data enrichment and automation, while Instantly optimizes email delivery. Apollo itself combines data, outreach, and CRM features in one platform, so the best choice depends on whether you need depth of data, ease of use, or specialized email tools. No single tool is universally better, so consider your top priority.

Frequently asked questions

Is ZoomInfo more accurate than Apollo?
ZoomInfo is known for its verified B2B contacts and strict data maintenance, which can give it an edge in accuracy. However, Apollo also sources data from multiple providers and offers regular updates. The accuracy difference often depends on your specific industry and region.
Can I use Instantly without Apollo?
Yes, Instantly works as a standalone email outreach tool. You will need a separate source for lead data, such as ZoomInfo, Lusha, or Hunter.io, since Instantly does not have its own database. Many SDRs pair Instantly with Apollo for data and sequencing.
