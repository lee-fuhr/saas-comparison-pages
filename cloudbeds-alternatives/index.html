Best Cloudbeds alternatives

Cloudbeds is a well known all-in-one property management system and channel manager for hotels. However, boutique hotels often have specific needs such as personalized guest experiences, flexible pricing, or simpler workflows that may not fit every Cloudbeds plan. If you are looking for a platform that better matches your property size, budget, or operational style, several strong alternatives exist. Below we explore three options: Mews, Hotelogix, and RMS Cloud.

alternative

Mews

is a cloud native PMS designed for modern, tech forward hotels. It offers a clean interface, strong integrations with payment and booking tools, and a focus on automating daily tasks. Boutique hotels that value a sleek user experience and the ability to customize their tech stack often find Mews a good fit.

alternative

Hotelogix

is a scalable PMS built for independent hotels and small groups. It provides an all-in-one solution with front desk, channel manager, and revenue tools. Hotelogix is particularly suited for boutique properties that want a balance of powerful features and a clear pricing structure without long term contracts.

alternative

RMS Cloud

is a mature PMS known for its robust reservation and rate management capabilities. It supports both single properties and multi property groups. RMS Cloud appeals to boutique hotels that need detailed reporting and flexible rate rules, and it offers a strong channel manager to distribute rooms across online travel agencies.

How they compare

- **Pricing:** Mews often uses a modular pricing model, Hotelogix offers tiered plans with no lock in, and RMS Cloud typically charges per room per month. - **Ease of use:** Mews has a modern, intuitive design. Hotelogix provides a straightforward workflow. RMS Cloud is feature rich and may have a longer learning curve. - **Channel manager:** All three include native channel management. RMS Cloud and Hotelogix are known for reliable connectivity with many OTAs. Mews focuses on direct integrations with major booking platforms. - **Support:** Hotelogix and RMS Cloud offer 24/7 support. Mews has strong email and chat support with a knowledge base. - **Best for:** Mews suits design focused boutique hotels. Hotelogix works well for growing properties. RMS Cloud fits hotels that need deep rate and inventory control.

Frequently asked questions

Is Mews too expensive for a small boutique hotel?
Mews pricing can be higher than some alternatives, but it starts at a manageable level for properties with fewer rooms. Many boutique hotels find the value in its automation and guest facing features outweigh the cost. It is best to request a customized quote based on your room count and needed modules.
Which alternative gives the best value for money?
Hotelogix often provides the best price to feature ratio for smaller boutique hotels. It includes an all-in-one system without extra fees for the core PMS and channel manager. RMS Cloud is also cost effective if you need advanced rate management, though it may require a higher upfront investment in training.
