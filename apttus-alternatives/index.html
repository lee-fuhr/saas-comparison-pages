Best Apttus alternatives

Apttus was a popular choice for companies needing both contract lifecycle management (CLM) and configure price quote (CPQ) capabilities. After its acquisition by Salesforce, the platform became part of Salesforce Revenue Cloud. But many organizations now look for alternatives due to cost changes, integration complexity, or a desire for a more standalone CLM tool that still works well with CPQ. This article covers five leading alternatives that offer strong CLM and CPQ integration for your business.

alternative

Salesforce CPQ

works natively inside the Salesforce ecosystem, making it a natural choice if you already use Salesforce for CRM. Its CLM features are powered by Salesforce’s own contract management tools or third-party add-ons, and the tight integration with Salesforce Revenue Cloud can simplify quoting and approval workflows.

alternative

Conga CPQ

combines CPQ with strong document generation and CLM capabilities that extend beyond Salesforce. It offers flexible automation for contract creation, redlining, and renewal management, which can help teams that need a deeper contract lifecycle without leaving their CRM.

alternative

Oracle CPQ

provides enterprise-grade CPQ and CLM within the Oracle cloud suite. Its CLM module supports complex pricing rules, multi-language contracts, and advanced approval chains, making it a solid fit for large organizations that already use Oracle applications.

alternative

DealHub

is designed specifically for end-to-end revenue management, with built-in CLM that syncs with its CPQ engine. It offers guided selling, automated contract generation, and real-time analytics, which helps sales teams close deals faster while keeping legal and compliance in check.

alternative

Zuora CPQ

is built for subscription and recurring revenue models, with CLM capabilities that handle complex billing terms and contract amendments. Its focus on usage-based pricing and automatic contract renewal tracking makes it ideal for businesses with frequent plan changes or self-service portals.

How they compare

Salesforce CPQ is best for organizations deeply invested in the Salesforce ecosystem. Conga CPQ excels at document-heavy contract workflows. Oracle CPQ suits large enterprises needing a unified suite. DealHub offers a user-friendly all-in-one revenue platform. Zuora CPQ is tailored for subscription and usage-based businesses. All five provide CLM integrated with CPQ, but the right choice depends on your existing tech stack, contract complexity, and pricing model.
