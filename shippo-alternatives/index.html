If you manage fulfillment operations, you know that shipping software needs to balance cost, speed, and reliability. Shippo is a popular multi-carrier platform for labels and tracking. But as your order volume grows or your requirements shift, you might need different features, pricing, or integrations. Here are five practical alternatives to consider.

alternative

ShipBob

offers a full fulfillment service, not just a label platform. If you want to outsource picking, packing, and shipping to a distributed warehouse network, ShipBob handles the entire operation and includes its own shipping rates and software.

alternative

ShipStation

is a robust multi-carrier tool built for high volume ecommerce. It integrates with dozens of sales channels and provides batch label printing, custom automation rules, and advanced reporting that scales with your business.

alternative

EasyShip

specializes in international shipping with a global carrier network. It simplifies customs paperwork and duties calculation, making it a strong choice if your fulfillment team handles frequent cross-border orders.

alternative

Pirateship

is a budget friendly option for small to mid size shippers. It offers steeply discounted USPS and UPS rates without monthly fees, and its simple interface is ideal for teams that want fast label creation without extra complexity.

alternative

EasyPost

is a developer focused shipping API that lets you build custom shipping workflows. If your fulfillment team has technical resources, EasyPost gives you full control over carrier rates, label generation, and tracking automation.

How they compare

Choose **ShipBob** if you need a complete fulfillment service rather than just software. Pick **ShipStation** for high volume multi-channel order management. Use **EasyShip** for international shipping with built in customs support. Select **Pirateship** for low cost domestic shipping with no monthly commitment. Consider **EasyPost** when you need a programmable API to integrate shipping into a custom system.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a small fulfillment team on a tight budget?
Pirateship is the most cost effective option because it offers discounted rates with no monthly fees or minimum commitments. Its simple workflow also reduces training time.
Can I migrate my existing carrier contracts from Shippo to any of these tools?
Most alternatives support bringing your own carrier agreements, but check individually. ShipStation and EasyPost allow you to use pre negotiated rates, while Pirateship and EasyShip offer their own built in discounts. Contact the platform’s support to confirm.
