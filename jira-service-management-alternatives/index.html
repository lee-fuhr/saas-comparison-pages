Best Jira Service Management alternatives

Jira Service Management is a popular IT service desk tool, especially for teams already using Atlassian products. It offers strong integration with development workflows and built-in ITIL practices. However, as your organization grows or your needs become more specific, you may find that Jira Service Management lacks certain features, such as advanced automation, CMDB capabilities, or enterprise-level analytics. Evaluating alternatives can help you match tool capabilities to your team’s size, budget, and compliance requirements.

alternative

ServiceNow

. ServiceNow is a leading enterprise platform designed for complex IT service operations. It offers deep ITIL alignment, a powerful configuration management database (CMDB), and extensive automation across ITSM, ITOM, and ITBM. Many large organizations choose ServiceNow when they need a single system to manage processes beyond the service desk.

alternative

Freshservice

. Freshservice focuses on modern, user-friendly IT service management for mid-sized teams. It includes intuitive workflows, built-in AI chatbots, and an asset management module that is easy to set up. Its cloud-only deployment and transparent pricing make it a strong choice for teams that want a quick, flexible upgrade from a basic help desk.

alternative

Zendesk

. Zendesk started as a customer support tool, but its service desk module now handles IT incident, problem, and change management. It excels in ease of use, multichannel support, and integration with over 1,000 apps. IT managers with strong customer service backgrounds often prefer Zendesk for its simplicity, though it lacks advanced IT asset management and CMDB features.

alternative

BMC Remedy

. BMC Helix (formerly Remedy) is a veteran IT service management suite built for large, regulated enterprises. It offers high configurability, robust ITIL processes, and strong security controls. Organizations that need to comply with government or industry standards often turn to BMC Remedy, but it typically requires dedicated administrators to maintain and customize.

How they compare

Compared to Jira Service Management, ServiceNow and BMC Remedy are more powerful for large enterprises but cost more and require more setup. Freshservice and Zendesk are easier to deploy and more affordable, making them better fits for small to midsize teams. Jira Service Management stands out for deep integration with Jira Software and Confluence, while the alternatives offer different strengths: ServiceNow in automation, Freshservice in modern UX, Zendesk in multichannel ticketing, and BMC Remedy in compliance and customization. Your choice should balance team size, ITIL maturity, and budget.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a mid-sized IT team with limited ITIL experience?
Freshservice is often the best fit because it provides modern, guided workflows and built-in ITIL best practices without requiring a long implementation. Its pricing is transparent, and its user interface is designed for quick adoption.
Can I migrate ticket history and configurations from Jira Service Management to another tool?
Yes, most alternatives offer data migration tools or professional services. However, the complexity varies. Freshservice and Zendesk provide straightforward import utilities for tickets and users. ServiceNow and BMC Remedy may require custom scripting or consulting help for a full migration.
