If you manage an affiliate program and currently use ShareASale, you may be exploring other platforms for better features, pricing, or support. ShareASale is a solid network, but it has limitations, especially for brands that want more control over tracking, faster payouts, or advanced automation. This guide highlights five strong alternatives – Impact, PartnerStack, Refersion, Tapfiliate, and Post Affiliate Pro – each suited to different program sizes and needs.

alternative

Impact

is an enterprise-grade platform that combines affiliate tracking with partnership management for all types of partners, from influencers to B2B resellers. It offers a comprehensive suite of tools including automated fraud detection, real-time data, and customizable commission structures, making it ideal for larger programs with complex needs.

alternative

PartnerStack

focuses on partner relationship management, blending affiliate marketing with channel sales and referral programs. Its strength lies in automated onboarding, tiered rewards, and a marketplace for recruiting partners, which works well for SaaS and subscription businesses.

alternative

Refersion

is a user-friendly affiliate tracking tool built for ecommerce brands and direct-to-consumer companies. It integrates smoothly with major shopping carts, provides straightforward performance dashboards, and includes a partner marketplace to find new affiliates quickly.

alternative

Tapfiliate

is a cloud based solution that emphasizes simplicity and customizability, with support for recurring commissions, multiple commission types, and branded affiliate portals. It integrates with many platforms and is a solid choice for small to medium businesses that want an affordable, no-fuss setup.

alternative

Post Affiliate Pro

is a feature-rich, self-hosted or cloud hosted affiliate management system known for its deep customization and powerful commission rules. It supports a wide range of tracking methods, from cookies to lifetime referrals, and is often favored by companies that want full control over their program without per-transaction fees.

How they compare

ShareASale operates as a network with a built-in marketplace but takes a percentage of commissions. The alternatives above offer varying degrees of control, pricing models, and feature sets. Impact and PartnerStack excel in scalability and partner diversity, while Refersion and Tapfiliate are easier to set up for ecommerce and small programs. Post Affiliate Pro gives the most ownership but requires more technical effort. Choose based on your program size, budget, and whether you need a managed marketplace or a standalone tracking tool.

Frequently asked questions

Is it hard to migrate from ShareASale to another platform?
Migration difficulty depends on the tool you choose. Most alternatives offer migration guides or dedicated support teams to help you export affiliate lists and commission data. Plan for a transition period where you run both systems to avoid payment disruptions.
Do these alternatives work for both physical products and digital services?
Yes, all five alternatives support both physical and digital products. Impact and PartnerStack are particularly strong for software and subscription models, while Refersion and Tapfiliate are popular for ecommerce stores. Check each tool’s integration list to confirm it works with your specific shopping cart or payment system.
