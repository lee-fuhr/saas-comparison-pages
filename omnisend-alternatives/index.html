Best Omnisend alternatives

Omnisend is a popular email and SMS marketing platform built for e-commerce brands. But as your business grows, you might find that its features, pricing, or integrations no longer fit your needs. Whether you need deeper segmentation, stronger automation, or a more affordable plan, exploring Omnisend alternatives can help you find a tool that matches your multichannel strategy.

alternative

Klaviyo

is a top choice for e-commerce marketers who want advanced segmentation and powerful analytics. It integrates deeply with Shopify and other platforms, and its flows feature lets you build complex customer journeys. Klaviyo’s pricing is based on contact count and emails sent, so it can become expensive at higher volumes.

alternative

Mailchimp

is a well-known all-in-one marketing platform with email, automation, and basic CRM features. It works well for smaller e-commerce stores that need an easy setup and a wide app marketplace. However, its e-commerce specific features like product feeds and prebuilt flows are less robust than Omnisend’s.

alternative

Privy

focuses on on-site pop ups, email capture, and abandoned cart recovery for e-commerce. It is a good alternative if your main goal is growing your email list and recovering lost sales without a complex automation builder. Privy’s email capabilities are simpler, so it may not suit larger multichannel campaigns.

alternative

Drip

is designed for e-commerce brand builders who want visual automation workflows and strong segmentation. It offers excellent customer data management and personalized campaigns. Drip’s pricing is flat based on contacts, which can be more predictable than Omnisend’s volume based model.

How they compare

Omnisend stands out for its prebuilt automations and SMS integration. Klaviyo leads in segmentation and analytics but costs more. Mailchimp offers broad features at lower tiers but lacks e-commerce depth. Privy excels at list building and cart recovery. Drip provides powerful workflows for mid sized brands. Choose based on your budget, required integrations, and automation complexity.

Frequently asked questions

Which Omnisend alternative is best for a small e-commerce store?
Mailchimp is often the most affordable and easiest to set up for small stores. Its free plan for up to 500 contacts and simple drag and drop editor make it a good starting point before you need advanced automation.
Can I migrate my existing email lists and flows from Omnisend to another platform?
Yes, most platforms like Klaviyo and Drip offer import tools for contacts and basic flow migration. However, complex automations and custom fields may need manual rebuilding. Always export your data from Omnisend first and check the new platform’s migration guide.
