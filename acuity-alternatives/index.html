Best Acuity Scheduling alternatives

If you run a service business or work as a solo practitioner, you know how important it is to let clients book appointments online without back-and-forth emails. Acuity Scheduling has long been a popular choice, but it may not fit every budget or workflow. Maybe you need a simpler interface, lower cost, or tighter integration with payment systems. Whatever your reason, several strong alternatives can replace Acuity while keeping your scheduling smooth and professional. Below we walk through five options and compare them side by side.

alternative

Calendly

. Calendly is one of the most user friendly tools on the market. It excels at one on one meetings and group bookings, and its clean interface makes it easy for clients to pick a time slot. Calendly integrates with many calendar apps and video conferencing platforms, though advanced features like payment collection require a paid plan.

Try Calendly →
alternative

Square Appointments

. Square Appointments is a great choice if you already use Square for payments or point of sale. It offers built in payment processing, client management, and even a free plan for solo practitioners. The tool is especially strong for hands on services like salons, clinics, and fitness studios.

alternative

TidyCal

. TidyCal is a budget friendly alternative with a one time payment option instead of monthly fees. It includes unlimited booking types, payment integrations, and Zoom connectivity. However, its feature set is leaner than Acuity’s, so it works best for solo professionals who need a no fuss scheduler.

Try TidyCal →
alternative

Setmore

. Setmore provides a free tier that covers unlimited staff members, making it ideal for small teams. It includes video call links, payment collection, and a mobile app for on the go management. Upgrading to paid plans removes branding and adds advanced customization.

alternative

SimplyBook

. SimplyBook is a highly customizable platform aimed at service businesses with complex offerings. It supports multiple locations, staff management, coupons, and a wide range of third party integrations. The interface can feel busy, but the flexibility is unmatched for niche needs like class scheduling or membership bookings.
