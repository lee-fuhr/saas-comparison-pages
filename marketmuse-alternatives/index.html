Best MarketMuse alternatives

For enterprise content teams, MarketMuse has long been a powerful ally for scaling topic research and content strategy. But its premium pricing can strain budgets, especially when you need to cover multiple users or departments. Fortunately, several robust alternatives deliver similar capabilities without the same cost. This guide covers five strong contenders that offer advanced content optimization, keyword intelligence, and workflow features at lower price points. Each tool excels in different areas, so you can choose the one that best matches your team’s specific needs.

alternative

Clearscope

Clearscope provides a streamlined content optimization platform with a focus on readability and comprehensive topic coverage. It integrates directly with Google Docs and WordPress, making it easy for your writers to follow recommendations without leaving their workflow. While it starts at a lower monthly cost than MarketMuse, enterprise plans scale predictably and include custom reports and priority support.

Try Clearscope →
alternative

Frase

Frase combines AI-powered content brief generation with real-time optimization scoring and a built-in writer. It excels at automating research and generating outlines, which saves time for teams producing high volumes of content. Its pricing tiers are generally more affordable than MarketMuse, and its “Content AI” feature can draft paragraphs based on your target keywords and competitors.

Try Frase →
alternative

Surfer SEO

Surfer SEO offers deep on-page optimization by analyzing thousands of ranking factors to give you a precise content score. It includes a content editor, keyword research tools, and a bulk analyzer for auditing existing pages. Surfer’s pay-per-article option and lower monthly plans make it an accessible choice for enterprise teams who want to control costs while still getting granular SEO recommendations.

alternative

Content Harmony

Content Harmony is built for content operations teams that need structured briefs, interlinking suggestions, and editorial calendars. Its strengths lie in workflow management and collaboration features, giving you visibility into content production from research to publication. The pricing is typically lower than MarketMuse for comparable team sizes, and it offers a free trial to test the platform with your own projects.

alternative

Semrush

Semrush provides a full marketing suite that includes content marketing tools such as the SEO Content Template, Topic Research, and a comprehensive On-Page SEO Checker. For enterprise teams, Semrush’s all-in-one approach can replace multiple tools, reducing overall software costs. Its Content Marketplace also connects you with vetted writers, though the platform’s primary strength is its vast keyword and competitor research database.
