As a designer, you need to share your work fast – and you want clients or teammates to see video previews without downloading huge files. Jumpshare does this well, but maybe you want something lighter, cheaper, or with a different feature set. Here are five solid Jumpshare alternatives tailored for designers who rely on instant file sharing and smooth video playback.

alternative

CloudApp

. CloudApp lets you capture your screen or upload files and share them with a link that shows a live video preview. It also includes a webcam overlay option, which is useful when you want to explain your design decisions while showing the screen. The free plan gives you up to 25 captures per day and 10 GB of storage.

alternative

Soapbox

. Soapbox is a browser extension from Wistia that focuses on quick video recording and sharing with immediate previews. You can record your screen, add your webcam, and even pause and edit out mistakes before sending the link. It integrates with popular tools like Slack and Gmail, making it easy to drop a video into your workflow.

alternative

BombBomb

. BombBomb is built for personalized video messaging, and it works well for designers who want to send short walkthroughs or feedback clips. Recipients see a video preview right in the email or link, no additional software required. It offers unlimited video hosting on paid plans, but the free tier is limited to 30 second videos.

alternative

Hippo Video

. Hippo Video is an all in one video platform that includes screen recording, video hosting, and analytics. Designers can record their screen, add a webcam, draw annotations, and share a link that plays instantly in any browser. It also lets you add a call to action button inside the video, which is handy for collecting feedback or approvals.

alternative

Screencast-O-Matic

. Screencast-O-Matic (now simply called ScreenPal) offers a lightweight screen recorder with built in hosting and instant share links. You can trim, blur, and add captions to your videos, and viewers get a clean playback page without downloads. The free version caps video length at 15 minutes and adds a small watermark.
