If you are a freelancer who needs a simple booking tool without a hefty price tag, you might already know TidyCal. It offers unlimited bookings for a one time fee, but maybe you want more features, a different interface, or a tool that integrates better with your workflow. This article compares three solid TidyCal alternatives that keep costs low and complexity minimal.

alternative

Calendly

. Calendly is one of the most popular scheduling tools and offers a free plan with basic features like one event type and calendar sync. Its paid plans start at a low monthly rate and add automations, payment collection, and team scheduling. Freelancers who need a reliable, polished tool with plenty of integrations will find Calendly a solid choice.

Try Calendly →
alternative

Doodle

. Doodle focuses on finding the best meeting time across multiple people, which is perfect for group calls or client polls. Its free plan covers one to one meetings and basic polls, while the paid plans remove limits and add calendar sync. If you often coordinate with several clients or team members, Doodle’s simplicity can save you time.

Try Doodle →
alternative

Setmore

. Setmore offers a generous free plan for up to 50 appointments per month, with features like video call links, custom booking pages, and payment processing. Paid plans start at a low monthly fee and remove the appointment cap while adding more advanced tools. Freelancers who want a lightweight app that also handles staff scheduling or client reminders will appreciate Setmore.

Try Setmore →

How they compare

TidyCal gives you unlimited bookings for a one time fee, making it the cheapest option if you book many appointments. Calendly is more polished and integrates with many apps, but its free plan is limited. Doodle excels at group scheduling, but its calendar sync requires a paid plan. Setmore balances a strong free tier with low cost upgrades, but its monthly appointment cap may not suit heavy users. Choose based on whether you need unlimited bookings, group polling, or a free plan that covers most needs.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use any of these tools completely for free as a freelancer?
Yes, each tool has a free plan. TidyCal offers unlimited bookings for a one time payment, not a free plan. Calendly’s free plan allows one event type. Doodle’s free plan works for one on one meetings and basic polls. Setmore’s free plan gives you up to 50 appointments per month.
Which alternative is best if I need to collect payments during booking?
Calendly and Setmore both let you accept payments on their paid plans. TidyCal also supports payment collection with its one time license. Doodle does not include payment processing, so you would need a separate tool for that.
