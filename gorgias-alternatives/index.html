Best Gorgias alternatives

Gorgias is a popular helpdesk built specifically for Shopify stores, but it may not be the right fit for every brand. Some teams find the pricing steep as they grow, while others need deeper reporting, more advanced ticketing workflows, or a simpler interface. Whether you are looking for lower costs, different automation features, or better support for non-ecommerce channels, there are solid alternatives worth exploring. Below we compare five tools that can serve ecommerce brands on Shopify.

alternative

Zendesk

. Zendesk is a full-featured helpdesk that scales from small teams to enterprise. It offers powerful ticket management, custom automations, and a large app marketplace, but its Shopify integration requires the Plus plan or higher. It is a strong choice if you need multi-channel support (email, chat, phone, social) and robust reporting, though it can feel complex for very small stores.

alternative

Freshdesk

. Freshdesk provides an affordable, user-friendly ticketing system with good multi-channel coverage. Its automation rules and canned responses are easy to set up, and the built-in chatbot can handle common queries. The main trade-off is that its Shopify-specific features are not as deep as Gorgias, so you may need to rely on third-party integrations for order lookups.

Try Freshdesk →
alternative

Help Scout

. Help Scout focuses on simplicity and a clean, email-like experience. It is ideal for teams that want minimal setup and an intuitive dashboard. Its reports are straightforward, and the Docs feature lets you build a knowledge base. However, it does not have native Shopify order data, so customer context must be pulled via integrations or manual lookup.

alternative

Richpanel

. Richpanel is designed for ecommerce and includes a native Shopify integration that shows order history, shipping info, and customer data right inside the ticket. It also offers a returns management module and live chat. For stores that handle many return requests and want an all-in-one solution, Richpanel is a strong alternative with similar automation to Gorgias.

alternative

DelightChat

. DelightChat is built for ecommerce brands and offers shared inbox, live chat, and social messaging (Instagram, Facebook) in one place. Its strength is an affordable flat-fee pricing model that does not charge per agent. It lacks advanced reporting and complex automations, but it works well for small to mid-sized teams that want a simple, cost-effective way to manage customer conversations.

How they compare

The best alternative for your Shopify store depends on your priorities. If you need full enterprise features and a large app ecosystem, Zendesk is versatile but more expensive. Freshdesk offers good value and ease of use but limited ecommerce depth. Help Scout is the simplest choice for email-first teams that want a clean workflow. Richpanel provides the closest feature match to Gorgias with native order data and returns management, while DelightChat is the most budget-friendly option for teams that focus on chat and social messaging. Each tool has free trials, so testing two or three can help you decide.
