Best Toast POS alternatives

Toast POS is a popular choice for full-service restaurants, especially those that want payroll built directly into their point of sale system. However, it may not fit every operation. If you are looking for a different combination of features, pricing, or support, this guide covers four strong alternatives: Square Restaurants, Lightspeed Restaurant, TouchBistro, and Revel Systems. Each option offers built in payroll (or easy integration with payroll providers) and is designed for full-service dining. We compare them on key factors like ease of use, hardware costs, and payroll functionality so you can choose the best match for your restaurant.

alternative

Square Restaurants

Square provides a full-service POS with optional payroll that processes tips and handles tax filings automatically. Its flat rate pricing and no long term contracts make it budget friendly, though advanced reporting and customization are more limited than Toast.

alternative

Lightspeed Restaurant

Lightspeed offers a cloud based POS with a strong built in payroll module that tracks hours, calculates wages, and integrates with accounting software. It is especially good for multi location restaurants, but hardware costs can add up and setup may require more upfront time.

alternative

TouchBistro

TouchBistro is an iPad based POS that includes a payroll add on for time tracking and direct deposit, designed for independent fine dining and casual full service venues. It excels offline reliability and menu management, but payroll is an extra cost and the system is less scalable for large chains.

alternative

Revel Systems

Revel combines POS with an integrated payroll tool that manages labor costs and sends earnings directly to employees. It is highly customizable and works well for complex operations, but the learning curve is steeper and monthly fees tend to be higher than other options.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use my existing payroll provider instead of the built in option?
Yes. Most of these POS systems, including Toast, Square, Lightspeed, TouchBistro, and Revel, allow you to integrate with third party payroll providers like ADP, Gusto, or Paychex. Built in payroll is an option, not a requirement.
Which alternative is easiest to switch to from Toast?
Square Restaurants is often the simplest to migrate to because it has similar daily workflows and no long term contract. Lightspeed also offers a guided onboarding process. For restaurants that rely heavily on Toast’s native payroll, Lightspeed’s integrated solution may feel most familiar.
