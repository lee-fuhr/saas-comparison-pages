Geotab is a popular choice for fleet telematics and data analytics, but it is not the only option. As your fleet grows, you may need a solution that scales differently in terms of cost, hardware flexibility, or analytics depth. This article covers three strong Geotab alternatives – Samsara, Verizon Connect, and GPS Insight – to help you find a platform that fits your need for scalable vehicle data analytics.

alternative

Samsara

. Samsara offers an all in one platform that combines GPS tracking, ELD compliance, dashcams, and real time analytics in a single unified interface. Its cloud based architecture handles large fleets well, and the built in AI powered video insights can improve driver safety without requiring separate integrations.

alternative

Verizon Connect

. Verizon Connect provides a comprehensive suite that includes fleet tracking, routing optimization, and maintenance management, all backed by Verizon’s network reliability. It scales from small fleets to enterprise deployments, and its customizable dashboards allow you to focus on the metrics that matter most for your business.

alternative

GPS Insight

. GPS Insight focuses on customizable reporting and flexible integration with other business systems, making it a strong choice for fleets that need tailored data flows. Its platform can handle thousands of vehicles, and the customer support is known for helping you configure analytics to match your specific operational goals.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer scalable data analytics, but they differ in focus. Samsara excels in combining video and IoT data into one view, Verizon Connect is strong on routing and enterprise integration, and GPS Insight prioritizes custom reporting and easy data sharing. Geotab remains a solid baseline, but if you need more video intelligence, deeper Verizon network integration, or highly customizable dashboards, these alternatives are worth evaluating.

Frequently asked questions

Is Samsara more expensive than Geotab for a large fleet?
Pricing varies by fleet size and features, but Samsara tends to bundle hardware and software in a subscription, while Geotab often charges separately for the gateway and monthly service. For large fleets, Samsara’s per vehicle cost can be competitive, especially if you want integrated dashcams and analytics in one package.
Can I easily migrate my data from Geotab to Verizon Connect or GPS Insight?
Yes, most providers offer data import tools or APIs to help transfer historical trip data, driver logs, and vehicle info. However, the complexity depends on how much custom configuration you have in Geotab. Check with each provider for their migration support and timeline before switching.
