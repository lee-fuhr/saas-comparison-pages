Best Plex alternatives

Manufacturers evaluating MES platforms often compare Plex with other strong options. Plex is a cloud-based manufacturing execution system known for controlling shop floor operations and connecting to ERP functions. But depending on your industry, business size, or specific needs, alternatives like Infor CloudSuite, DELMIAworks, IQMS, and Epicor may offer a better fit. This article helps you compare these platforms without relying on hype or made-up numbers.

alternative

Infor CloudSuite

Infor CloudSuite is a comprehensive ERP and MES suite built for process and discrete manufacturers. It uses industry-specific templates to speed up deployment, and its cloud architecture allows real-time visibility across plants. The system works well for companies that want deep functionality in areas like quality management and supply chain.

alternative

DELMIAworks

DELMIAworks (formerly IQMS) is a unified ERP/MES designed for discrete and repetitive manufacturing. It provides strong production scheduling, real-time machine monitoring, and robust traceability features. This platform is a good choice if you need tight integration between shop floor data and back-office operations.

alternative

IQMS

IQMS (now part of DELMIAworks but still offered as a separate solution for some customers) focuses on the plastics, packaging, and automotive industries. It includes mold tracking, cycle time analysis, and scrap reduction tools. Manufacturers with high-volume, process-heavy production often prefer IQMS for its specialized capabilities.

alternative

Epicor

Epicor offers ERP with MES functionality aimed at mid-market discrete and make-to-order manufacturers. Its manufacturing execution module covers job scheduling, labor tracking, and quality control. Epicor is particularly strong for companies that need flexibility in configure-to-order or engineer-to-order workflows.

How they compare

Plex leads in cloud-native architecture and ease of use, especially for manufacturers who want a single system for both MES and ERP. Infor CloudSuite offers deeper industry-specific templates but may require more upfront customization. DELMIAworks provides close integration of production and accounting, while IQMS excels in plastics and process industries. Epicor is a strong choice for complex order configurations and project-based manufacturing. All four alternatives have mature on-premise options, whereas Plex is cloud only. Your decision should agree with your industry, IT strategy, and desire for real-time visibility.

Frequently asked questions

Is Plex a good choice for small manufacturers with limited IT resources?
Yes, Plex is a fully cloud-based system with no on-site hardware requirements, which makes it easier for small teams to adopt and maintain. But if you need very specialized features for a niche industry (like plastics or metal forming), you may find better fit in IQMS or Epicor.
Which alternative is best for discrete manufacturing with engineer-to-order processes?
Epicor is often preferred for engineer-to-order and configure-to-order scenarios because its ERP and MES modules handle complex bill of materials and revisions well. However, Plex also supports discrete manufacturing, so the final choice depends on your specific workflow complexity and budget.
