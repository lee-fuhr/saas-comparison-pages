If your content team uses Planable for planning and approval workflows but you want to explore other options, you have strong alternatives. Planable is known for its visual calendar and layered approval steps, but tools like Buffer, Sprout Social, and Later offer similar features with different strengths. This article compares those alternatives so you can pick the right fit for your publishing process.

alternative

Buffer

Buffer provides a simple publishing calendar and basic approval workflows in its paid plans. The approval flow lets you assign reviewers and require approval before a post goes live, which works well for small teams. It lacks the visual, multi-stage approval of Planable but is more affordable and easier to set up for straightforward schedules.

alternative

Sprout Social

Sprout Social includes robust approval workflows with customizable steps, role-based permissions, and message approval queues. You can require multiple approvers and set revision requests directly inside the platform. It is a complete social media management tool, but it costs more than Planable and has a steeper learning curve for new users.

alternative

Later

Later offers a visual content calendar with approval features in its Business and Enterprise plans. Teams can set required approvals before a post is scheduled, and reviewers can leave comments or approve directly. Its approval system is less granular than Planable’s, but Later excels at visual planning and is a good choice for brands that prioritize Instagram and visual content.

How they compare

All three alternatives support basic approval workflows, but the depth of control differs. Planable gives you multi-step approval with version history and approval states like “draft,” “in review,” and “approved.” Buffer’s approval flow is simpler and best for small teams. Sprout Social matches Planable on customization with role-based permissions and approval queues but at a higher price. Later’s approval features are tied to its visual calendar and work best for image-heavy schedules. For content teams that need detailed approval tracking, Sprout Social is closest to Planable, while Buffer and Later are better for simpler workflows.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Buffer to set up multi-step approval like in Planable?
Buffer’s approval workflow supports one-level approval where a post must be approved by a designated reviewer. It does not offer multi-layer approval chains or revision history out of the box. For more complex flows, consider Sprout Social or stay with Planable.
Does Later have approval for team members outside of scheduled posts?
Later’s approval process applies to individual posts within a calendar. You can require approval per post, but the feature does not extend to drafts or content libraries. It is a linear approval, not a multi-stage workflow like Planable uses.
