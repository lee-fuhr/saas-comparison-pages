Best Fastly alternatives

Fastly is a widely used content delivery network (CDN) and edge computing platform known for its programmable VCL and real-time performance. However, web performance engineers sometimes need alternatives due to pricing changes, feature gaps, regional coverage limitations, or a shift in infrastructure strategy. This article reviews four common alternatives (Cloudflare, Akamai, AWS CloudFront, CDN77) from a performance engineering perspective.

alternative

Cloudflare

. Cloudflare offers a massive global network with integrated security features like DDoS protection and a web application firewall (WAF). Its performance tools include Argo Smart Routing and automatic image optimization. Many engineers choose Cloudflare when they need a simple, all-in-one solution that also improves security and reduces origin load.

alternative

Akamai

. Akamai is an enterprise grade CDN with the largest deployed edge network in the world and deep customization through its Property Manager and EdgeWorkers. It excels at large scale delivery for video, software downloads, and dynamic content. Engineers working on high traffic, latency sensitive applications often prefer Akamai for its consistent performance and advanced analytics.

alternative

AWS CloudFront

. CloudFront integrates tightly with the AWS ecosystem, making it a natural choice for teams already using S3, Lambda@Edge, or other AWS services. It offers pay-as-you-go pricing with no upfront commitments and supports custom SSL certificates and origin failover. Performance engineers building on AWS often find CloudFront the most convenient and cost effective option for edge delivery.

alternative

CDN77

. CDN77 provides straightforward per terabyte pricing with no hidden fees and a focus on video streaming and large file delivery. Its network includes POPs in key regions and supports features like token authentication and real time analytics. Engineers who want transparent billing and a platform that handles high capacity video well often turn to CDN77.

How they compare

Fastly’s strength lies in its highly programmable VCL and real-time log streaming. Cloudflare offers the easiest setup and strongest integrated security. Akamai provides the largest network and deep enterprise controls, while AWS CloudFront is best for teams already invested in AWS. CDN77 appeals to those needing simple pricing and solid video performance. The right choice depends on your specific workload (static vs. dynamic), your team’s expertise, and budget constraints.
