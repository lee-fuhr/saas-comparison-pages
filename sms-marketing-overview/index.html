Best SMS Marketing alternatives

If you are looking for ways to send promotions, alerts, and cart recovery messages, you have likely outgrown a basic text service or want more than a single provider. Several SMS marketing platforms offer different strengths in automation, audience segmentation, and integrations. This article reviews seven alternatives to help you choose the right fit for your ecommerce or business needs.

alternative

Attentive

. Attentive is an enterprise focused SMS platform that specializes in two way conversations and high deliverability. It offers advanced segmentation, A/B testing, and seamless integrations with major ecommerce platforms like Shopify and Magento. Attentive works well for brands that want to scale their text marketing with a dedicated account team.

alternative

Postscript

. Postscript is built specifically for Shopify stores and focuses on automated flows for cart recovery, post purchase, and browse abandonment. It uses a pay as you go pricing model with no monthly minimums, making it a flexible choice for growing businesses. The platform also includes built in compliance tools for TCPA and text message opt ins.

alternative

SMSBump

. SMSBump, now part of Yotpo, is a tool designed for small to medium ecommerce brands. It provides automated campaigns, segmented lists, and deep integrations with Shopify, Magento, and BigCommerce. Its pricing is based on credits, and it offers free two way messaging on paid plans.

alternative

Klaviyo SMS

. Klaviyo SMS is an add on module within the Klaviyo marketing platform, which is popular for email and SMS combined campaigns. It allows you to trigger SMS messages from the same customer profiles and flows you use for email, simplifying cross channel coordination. Klaviyo SMS works best if you already use Klaviyo for email marketing.

alternative

Twilio

. Twilio is a developer friendly communication API that gives you full control over SMS delivery and custom logic. It is ideal for teams with technical resources who want to build tailored workflows, segment by custom fields, and integrate with any backend. Twilio charges per message and does not include built in marketing templates or pre built ecommerce flows.

alternative

MessageBird

. MessageBird, now also known as Bird, offers a global SMS platform with a strong focus on international reach and reliable delivery. It provides an easy to use dashboard and API for sending promotions and alerts, plus built in number verification and two factor authentication. MessageBird is a solid option if you need to send SMS to customers in multiple countries.

alternative

Zipwhip

. Zipwhip, now part of Sinch, allows you to send and receive SMS from a regular phone number or toll free number rather than a short code. This makes it a good choice for businesses that want a local presence and real time two way conversations with customers. It integrates with several CRM and helpdesk tools and supports automated replies for cart reminders.

How they compare

Each platform excels in a different area: Attentive and Postscript are top picks for Shopify heavy ecommerce, Klaviyo SMS is best for existing Klaviyo users, Twilio offers maximum customization, MessageBird covers global needs, SMSBump provides affordable credits for small stores, and Zipwhip gives you a local number for personal communication. Consider your technical resources, budget, and primary use case (cart recovery vs. general alerts) when deciding.
