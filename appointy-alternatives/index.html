If you run a small business or salon, scheduling tools save time and reduce no-shows. Appointy is a popular choice with built-in payments and marketing features. But it may not fit every budget or workflow. You might need simpler calendar sync, lower pricing, or a tool that integrates with your existing software. Here we look at four solid alternatives that many salon owners and small business operators consider.

alternative

Calendly

is the most widely used scheduling platform. It works well for one-on-one appointments and group events. Its clean interface and strong calendar integration make it easy to share your availability without back-and-forth emails.

alternative

Cal.com

is an open source alternative that gives you full control over your data and customization. It offers features like round-robin scheduling and team management. Because it is open source, you can host it yourself or use their paid cloud version.

alternative

Acuity Scheduling

is built for service based businesses like salons and spas. It includes client intake forms, package management, and automated reminders. Its robust customization helps you match the booking flow to your brand.

alternative

Book Like A Boss

focuses on simplicity and payments. It lets you sell appointments, classes, and even physical products from one dashboard. The platform is popular with solo entrepreneurs and small teams who want a straightforward booking page.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a salon with multiple stylists?
Acuity Scheduling is a strong choice because it handles service menus, staff calendars, and client history out of the box. Calendly and Book Like A Boss can work for small teams, but Acuity’s salon specific features like product add-ons and class booking make it easier to manage your daily operations.
Do any of these alternatives offer a free plan for a small business on a tight budget?
Yes, all four alternatives have free plans with limited features. Calendly’s free plan lets you set up one event type. Cal.com offers a free self hosted option if you have technical skills. Acuity’s free tier supports one calendar and basic reminders. Book Like A Boss includes one location and unlimited bookings on their free plan.
