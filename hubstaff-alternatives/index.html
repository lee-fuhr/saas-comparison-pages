If you manage a field workforce and rely on GPS tracking to monitor location, routes, and job sites, Hubstaff is a popular choice. But it is not the only option. Many companies need alternatives that offer similar or better location features, different pricing, or simpler interfaces. This article reviews three Hubstaff alternatives – Toggl, Clockify, and Timely – specifically for businesses that require GPS-based field workforce tracking.

alternative

Toggl

. Toggl Track includes GPS location capture on its mobile app, letting field workers clock in and out from a job site with a timestamped location. It works well for small to medium teams that want simple time tracking without a lot of extra features, but its GPS reporting is basic compared to Hubstaff.

alternative

Clockify

. Clockify offers a free plan with GPS tracking on its mobile app, which allows you to see where employees clock in and out and review location history. It is a strong budget-friendly option, though the GPS data is limited to check-in points and does not include live route tracking or geofencing found in Hubstaff.

alternative

Timely

. Timely focuses on automatic time tracking and does not offer native GPS location capture for field workers. While it uses calendar and app data to log time, it lacks the real-time GPS features that companies with a mobile field workforce typically need, making it less suitable for this use case.

How they compare

Hubstaff provides the most complete GPS tracking for field workers, including geofencing, route replay, and real-time location. Toggl and Clockify offer basic GPS clock-in location but no route tracking or geofencing. Timely is not designed for GPS-based field work and should only be considered if you do not need location data. For companies that require live location or geofences, Hubstaff or a dedicated field service tool would be a better fit.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Toggl or Clockify to track my field workers’ locations in real time?
No. Both Toggl and Clockify record where an employee clocks in and out, but they do not provide live location tracking or route playback. For real-time GPS monitoring, you need a tool like Hubstaff or a specialized field service app.
Does Timely offer any GPS features for field workers?
No. Timely relies on computer activity, calendar events, and manual entry to log time. It has no mobile GPS capture or location tracking, so it is not a suitable alternative for companies that need to verify worker locations.
