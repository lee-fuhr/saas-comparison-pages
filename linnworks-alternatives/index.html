If you are an e-commerce manager using Linnworks and finding it complex, expensive, or lacking the specific features your business needs, you are not alone. Many growing operations look for **Linnworks alternatives** that offer better inventory control, simpler integrations, or more affordable pricing. The right solution depends on your order volume, warehouse size, and whether you need manufacturing support, barcode scanning, or multi-location tracking. Below we compare five strong contenders: Cin7, Fishbowl, inFlow, Sortly, and SkuVault.

alternative

Cin7

. Cin7 is a cloud-based inventory and order management platform built for mid-market e-commerce brands that sell across multiple channels. It handles complex supply chains well, including dropshipping and multi-warehouse fulfillment, and it integrates with major shopping carts like Shopify and Amazon.

alternative

Fishbowl

. Fishbowl is a desktop and cloud hybrid system popular with manufacturers and wholesalers who also run e-commerce. It offers strong barcode scanning, manufacturing bill of materials, and QuickBooks synchronization, making it a good fit if you produce your own products.

alternative

inFlow

. inFlow is a user-friendly inventory management tool that is especially affordable for small to medium e-commerce businesses. It supports purchase orders, sales orders, and barcode labeling, and its cloud version works well for teams that need to manage stock from multiple locations.

alternative

Sortly

. Sortly is a visual inventory app designed for simplicity, not heavy manufacturing. It excels for e-commerce businesses that track physical items with photos and QR codes, making it ideal for small teams who want to get started quickly without a steep learning curve.

alternative

SkuVault

. SkuVault is a warehouse management system made for high-volume e-commerce operations. It emphasizes real-time inventory accuracy through barcode scanning, cycle counting, and three-way bin logic, and it integrates deeply with Amazon, eBay, and major e-commerce platforms.

Frequently asked questions

Which Linnworks alternative is best for a small e-commerce business with a tight budget?
InFlow is a solid choice for small teams because of its low monthly cost and straightforward setup. Sortly is even more affordable if you only need basic item tracking with photos and do not require purchase orders or advanced manufacturing features.
Do any of these alternatives integrate with my existing e-commerce platforms like Shopify or Magento?
Yes. Cin7, SkuVault, and inFlow all offer native integrations with Shopify, Amazon, and other major carts. Fishbowl integrates through third-party connectors or direct API. Sortly has limited native e-commerce integrations but can connect via Zapier. Always check each tool’s current integration list before committing.
