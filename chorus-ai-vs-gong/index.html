Sales leaders today rely on conversation analytics to uncover deal risks, coach reps, and scale winning behaviors. Two of the most popular platforms are Chorus.ai and Gong. Both record and transcribe calls, then use AI to surface insights. Chorus.ai is especially known for its deep integration with Salesforce, its focus on rep coaching tools like Scorecards, and its ability to let you search across your entire call library. Gong, on the other hand, is praised for its rich deal-level analytics and its “deal timeline” that tracks buyer engagement across emails, meetings, and calls. This article compares Chorus.ai with its top alternatives, including Gong, so you can choose the best fit for your team.

alternative

Gong

. Gong is the main competitor to Chorus.ai. It offers a deal timeline that pulls in emails, calendar events, and calls to show a complete picture of each opportunity. Its AI highlights stakeholder sentiment and deal momentum, and its mobile app lets reps review key moments on the go. Gong is often preferred by teams that want a single view of all buyer interactions, not just call recordings.

alternative

ExecVision

. ExecVision focuses on performance coaching with a strong library of call templates and playbooks. It lets you tag specific moments in calls and build custom scorecards that agree with your sales methodology. ExecVision is a good fit for organizations that want to standardize coaching around proven frameworks rather than rely solely on AI suggestions.

alternative

Avoma

. Avoma combines conversation intelligence with meeting notes, action items, and CRM integration. It uses AI to auto-generate summaries and extract next steps. Avoma works well for teams that need both call analytics and collaborative note taking, especially in account management or customer success roles.

alternative

Refract

. Refract is built around real time coaching during live calls. It sends in-call prompts and alerts to reps based on keywords or competitor mentions. Refract also offers post-call analytics and scorecards. It is best for teams that want to intervene in the moment rather than only review calls afterward.

alternative

Fireflies.ai

. Fireflies.ai is a more general purpose meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes calls from Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet. It includes a searchable transcript archive and can integrate with CRMs. Fireflies is a lighter, lower cost option for teams that want basic analysis without a full sales focused platform.

How they compare

Chorus.ai and Gong both lead the market, but they differ in emphasis. Chorus.ai gives sales leaders deep library search, flexible scorecards, and strong Salesforce sync, making it ideal for coaching at scale. Gong provides a richer deal level view by merging call data with email and calendar activity, which helps with forecasting and deal inspection. ExecVision offers the most structured coaching framework with pre built playbooks. Avoma stands out for adding automated meeting notes and action items. Refract is unique for its live coaching alerts. Fireflies.ai is the most affordable entry point. For most sales leaders, the choice comes down to whether you need a pure call analysis and coaching tool (Chorus.ai) or a more holistic customer interaction platform (Gong).
