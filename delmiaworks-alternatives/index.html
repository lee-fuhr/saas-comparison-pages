Best DELMIAworks alternatives

DELMIAworks (formerly IQMS) is a popular manufacturing execution system (MES) for discrete manufacturers, but its rigid architecture and complex customization can frustrate teams looking for a more flexible, modern solution. If you need an MES that adapts quickly to changing production processes, integrates easily with existing enterprise resource planning (ERP) tools, and offers strong scalability, exploring alternatives is a smart move. This article compares four leading options that discrete manufacturers often consider when moving away from DELMIAworks.

alternative

Plex

is a cloud-native MES that provides a unified platform for production monitoring, quality management, and supply chain visibility. Its flexible workflows and robust API allow discrete manufacturers to tailor the system without heavy IT support. Plex also integrates deeply with its own ERP, making it a good fit for companies that want an all-in-one solution.

alternative

Epicor

offers a manufacturing ERP with strong MES capabilities designed for make-to-stock and make-to-order discrete operations. Its modular architecture lets you add shop floor control, scheduling, and traceability features as needed. Epicor is especially strong in metal fabrication, woodworking, and industrial equipment industries.

alternative

Infor CloudSuite

leverages the AWS cloud and comes preconfigured with industry-specific best practices for discrete manufacturing. It provides built-in MES functions like real-time labor tracking, machine monitoring, and quality checks. The system’s flexibility comes from its ability to adjust processes using visual process modeling without coding.

alternative

IQMS

(now part of DELMIAworks but still available as a legacy option) remains a viable alternative for manufacturers who prefer the original on-premise deployment model. It offers strong injection molding and extrusion capabilities, along with real-time production dashboards. However, it lacks the cloud flexibility and modern user interface of newer alternatives.

How they compare

All four alternatives offer strong MES functionality, but they differ in deployment, industry focus, and ease of customization. Plex and Infor CloudSuite are cloud-native, giving you faster updates and lower IT overhead, while Epicor and IQMS provide on-premise and hybrid options. For discrete manufacturers seeking maximum flexibility, Plex’s configurable workflows and Infor’s process modeling stand out. Epicor excels in specific verticals like metal fabrication, and IQMS remains a solid choice for legacy users who do not want to migrate. The best pick depends on your production style, IT strategy, and budget.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate data from DELMIAworks to any of these alternatives?
Yes, most vendors offer data migration services or tools to transfer master data, work orders, and historical production records. The complexity depends on your data volume and the level of customization in your current DELMIAworks system. Plan for a phased migration to minimize downtime.
Which alternative is best for a small to medium sized discrete manufacturer?
For small to medium sized operations, Plex and Epicor are often the most practical choices. Plex’s cloud subscription model requires no upfront hardware investment, and Epicor offers tiered pricing based on user count. Both provide flexible MES features that scale as you grow.
