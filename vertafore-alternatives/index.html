Best Vertafore alternatives

Vertafore is a widely used insurance technology platform for carriers and agencies, offering tools like agency management systems (AMS) and comparative raters. However, some organizations find its pricing, complexity, or feature set doesn’t fully match their needs. If you are exploring other options, the following alternatives provide different strengths in integration, ease of use, and specific workflows.

alternative

Applied Systems

. Applied Systems is a leading competitor with a full suite of agency management tools including the popular Applied Epic and TAM platforms. It offers strong integration with carriers and comparative rating, and its cloud-based options support remote work. Many agencies choose Applied for its robust data management and scalability.

alternative

HawkSoft

. HawkSoft focuses on independent agencies with a document-centric, intuitive interface that reduces training time. Its system emphasizes a single-screen workflow and paperless office capabilities, making it ideal for agencies that want to simplify daily tasks. HawkSoft also provides built-in real-time carrier connectivity.

alternative

EZLynx

. EZLynx is a cloud-based comparative rater and agency management system designed for small to mid-sized agencies. It simplifies quoting for personal and commercial lines and offers integrated policy management and customer relationship features. Agencies often select EZLynx for its affordability and quick implementation.

alternative

Jenesi

. Jenesi is a cloud platform built for carriers and MGAs to automate policy administration, billing, and document management. It focuses on digital transformation and straight-through processing, helping reduce manual work. Carriers use Jenesi to modernize their core systems without a large IT overhaul.

How they compare

Applied Systems targets agencies of all sizes with a broad feature set and is typically more expensive. HawkSoft suits independent agencies that value simplicity and document management. EZLynx is best for smaller agencies needing an affordable comparative rater plus basic management features. Jenesi is carrier-focused, offering policy administration rather than agency management. Deployment options vary: Applied and HawkSoft offer cloud and on-premises, EZLynx is cloud-only, and Jenesi is cloud-native. Pricing depends on agency size and modules; none publish set rates, so you need to request quotes.
