If your company wants a live chat tool without a monthly cost and needs basic automation like triggers or simple chatbot replies, Pure Chat is a popular starting point. However, you may want to compare other options to find the best fit for your budget and feature set. Below are seven alternatives to Pure Chat that offer either a free plan or a free trial with basic automation capabilities.

alternative

Olark

offers a 14-day free trial but no permanent free plan. Its basic automation includes simple trigger-based messages and offline forms, which work well for small teams that want to test before buying.

alternative

Chaport

has a free plan that supports unlimited chats with up to two operators and includes basic chatbot builders and trigger rules. It is a strong choice for companies that want an easy setup with limited but functional automation.

alternative

JivoChat

provides a free plan with up to four operators and includes automated greeting messages and a simple trigger system. Callback and offline message features are also available at no cost.

alternative

Smartsupp

gives a free plan with up to two operators and basic automation such as auto-greetings and chat ratings. Its chatbot builder requires a paid upgrade, so basic automation here means preset messages.

alternative

LiveChat

does not offer a free plan, only a 14-day free trial. Its automation features are more advanced (e.g., ticket routing, chatbots), but the trial is short and requires a subscription after it ends.

alternative

Tidio

has a free plan that includes a visual chatbot builder with up to 100 conversations per month and basic trigger-based automation. It is a solid pick for companies that need both live chat and a simple bot without paying.

alternative

ClickDesk

offers a free plan with up to three operators and includes auto-greetings, canned responses, and basic trigger rules. Its automation is straightforward and useful for customer support and sales inquiries.

How they compare

All the tools listed above provide some form of live chat, but their free plans and automation levels vary. Pure Chat stands out for its simple, no-cost plan with basic triggers and visitor tracking. Tidio and Chaport offer the strongest free chatbot builders. Olark, LiveChat, and JivoChat focus more on paid features for larger teams. Smartsupp and ClickDesk give adequate free plans for very small teams. When choosing, consider how many operators you need and whether basic automation (trigger messages, simple bots) is enough for your daily workflow.

Frequently asked questions

Does Pure Chat have a free plan that includes automation?
Yes, Pure Chat offers a free plan with basic automation features such as automated greetings, trigger rules based on visitor actions, and proactive chat invitations. It is suitable for small teams that want simple automation without a subscription.
Which alternative has the best free plan for basic chatbot automation?
Tidio and Chaport both provide free plans with a visual chatbot builder that can handle simple conversations and trigger replies. Tidio limits you to 100 bot conversations per month, while Chaport restricts operator seats. Review each tool’s usage caps to see which fits your expected chat volume.
