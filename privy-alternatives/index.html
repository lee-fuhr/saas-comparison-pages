Best Privy alternatives

If you are a Shopify merchant using Privy for pop-ups and SMS marketing, you might be looking for alternatives that offer more advanced automation, better segmentation, or lower costs. Privy is a solid entry-level tool, but other platforms can scale with your growing email and SMS needs. This article compares four popular options: Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Omnisend, and Drip. We will focus on pop-up functionality, SMS features, and Shopify integration so you can choose the best fit for your store.

alternative

Klaviyo

. Klaviyo is a powerful email and SMS platform built specifically for ecommerce. Its pop-up builder is flexible and integrates directly with Shopify, but you may need to use a separate app (like Privy or Justuno) for advanced pop-up targeting. Klaviyo shines with its deep segmentation, predictive analytics, and automated flows, and its SMS features include two-way messaging and opt-in management.

alternative

Mailchimp

. Mailchimp offers a user-friendly pop-up builder with targeting options such as exit intent and page-specific triggers. Its SMS features are available on higher-tier plans and include automated text reminders and campaign sends. However, Mailchimp’s Shopify integration is less seamless than some competitors, and its SMS capabilities are not as robust for complex flows.

alternative

Omnisend

. Omnisend is designed for omnichannel marketing with built-in pop-ups that support coupons, exit intent, and delay triggers. It combines email, SMS, and push notifications in one platform, making it easy to create automated workflows. Omnisend’s SMS features include cart recovery and welcome series, and its Shopify sync is reliable for popup data and customer segments.

alternative

Drip

. Drip focuses on email automation and segmentation, with a pop-up feature called “form blocks” that lets you create targeted signup forms. Drip’s SMS functionality is available as an add-on with support for triggered messages and broadcasts. It integrates well with Shopify, but its pop-up builder is simpler than Privy’s, and you may need a third-party pop-up tool for advanced designs.

How they compare

For Shopify merchants who need advanced automation and deep customer profiles, Klaviyo is the strongest alternative despite its pop-up limitations. Omnisend is the closest all-in-one replacement for Privy with built-in pop-ups and SMS. Mailchimp works for budget-conscious stores with basic needs, while Drip suits content-focused brands that prioritize email over SMS. All four platforms support Shopify integration, but only Omnisend and Klaviyo offer robust two-way SMS and pop-up targeting out of the box.

Frequently asked questions

Which Privy alternative has the best SMS features for Shopify?
Klaviyo and Omnisend both offer strong SMS features with automated flows, opt-in management, and two-way messaging. Klaviyo’s SMS analytics are more detailed, while Omnisend includes SMS in its omnichannel workflows without extra add-ons.
Can I keep my existing pop-up designs if I switch from Privy?
Most alternatives let you import pop-up templates or create new ones from scratch. Klaviyo requires a third-party pop-up app for advanced designs, while Omnisend and Mailchimp provide built-in editors. You will need to rebuild your pop-ups in the new tool, but layouts and targeting settings can be matched closely.
