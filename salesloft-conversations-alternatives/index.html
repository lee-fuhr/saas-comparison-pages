Best Salesloft Conversations alternatives

If your team uses Salesloft as your sales engagement platform, you may have tried Salesloft Conversations for call recording and transcription. While it offers native integration, some teams find its analytics, coaching features, or cross-platform compatibility limited. You might need a dedicated conversational intelligence layer that works seamlessly on top of your existing engagement suite, whether that is Salesloft, Outreach, or another tool. The right alternative can give you deeper insights into buyer sentiment, more accurate coaching data, and stronger integrations with your CRM and other sales tools.

alternative

Chorus.ai

. Chorus.ai (now part of ZoomInfo) is a strong alternative that integrates with Salesloft and other engagement platforms. It excels at capturing calls, emails, and meetings, then using AI to surface deal risks, objection patterns, and competitive mentions. Its coaching and rep performance dashboards are often praised for being intuitive.

alternative

Gong

. Gong is the market leader in revenue intelligence and works as an overlay on top of sales engagement tools like Salesloft. It records and analyzes every customer interaction, including calls, emails, and video meetings, and provides automated deal insights and coaching recommendations. Many teams choose Gong for its powerful search and the ability to map conversation patterns to deal outcomes.

alternative

ExecVision

. ExecVision focuses on conversation analytics with a strong emphasis on coaching and skill development. It integrates with Salesloft and pulls in call recordings for automated transcription and analysis. Its strength lies in customizable scorecards and the ability to drill into specific talk tracks to improve rep performance.

alternative

Avoma

. Avoma combines meeting transcription, note taking, and conversation intelligence in one platform. It connects with Salesloft and other CRMs to automatically log calls and surface action items. Avoma is a good fit for teams that want both AI-powered insights and an easy way to share meeting summaries with prospects.

alternative

Refract

. Refract is a conversational intelligence platform built around coaching and compliance. It integrates with Salesloft and other dialers to record calls and provide automated feedback based on company defined playbooks. Refract is often chosen by teams that need strong call scoring and manager coaching workflows.

How they compare

When comparing these alternatives, consider how deeply each integrates with Salesloft, what type of analytics you need, and your budget. Chorus.ai and Gong are the most feature rich and scale well for large teams but come with higher price points. ExecVision and Refract offer specialized coaching tools that may appeal to organizations focused on skill development and compliance. Avoma is a strong middle ground for teams that want conversation intelligence plus meeting notes without the complexity of the top tier tools. All options can serve as a dedicated conversational intelligence layer on top of Salesloft, so your choice will depend on your team size, desired insights, and coaching maturity.
