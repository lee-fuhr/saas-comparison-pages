How they compare

All six tools help you run referral or advocacy campaigns, but they differ in complexity and target audience. Champion and ReferralCandy are the most user-friendly for small teams. ReferenceEdge and Influitive lean more toward B2B advocacy and community engagement, while Extole and Referral Rock offer more control and scalability for larger programs. Pricing varies widely: ReferralRock and ReferralCandy offer flat fees, whereas Champion and Extole often charge based on program performance or volume. Choose based on how much customization, fraud protection, and hand-holding your campaign requires.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best champion alternative for a small ecommerce brand with a limited budget?
ReferralCandy is a strong fit because it is very affordable and works directly with Shopify and other ecommerce platforms. It requires almost no setup time and automates rewards, so you can launch a basic referral program in minutes.
Which champion alternative offers the most advanced fraud detection?
Extole is known for its robust fraud prevention features, including IP tracking and behavioral analysis. If your program is likely to be targeted by abuse or you process a high volume of referrals, Extole helps protect your budget and ensure real customer engagement.
