Best Sprout Social alternatives

Sprout Social is a powerful social media management tool, but its premium pricing often puts it out of reach for agencies and mid-size brands watching their budget. If you need robust scheduling, analytics, and team collaboration without the high monthly cost, several capable alternatives exist. This article covers five strong options: Hootsuite, Buffer, Agorapulse, Zoho Social, and Later. Each offers a different mix of features and price points to help you save money while still managing your social presence effectively.

alternative

Hootsuite

. Hootsuite is one of the most established platforms, offering scheduling, analytics, and team workflows starting at a lower price than Sprout Social. Its free plan supports up to two social profiles, making it a good entry point for small teams. Paid plans add more advanced reporting and approval features.

Try Hootsuite →
alternative

Buffer

. Buffer focuses on simplicity and clean scheduling, with a generous free plan for up to three channels. Its paid plans start at a very affordable rate, making it ideal for brands that need straightforward publishing without complex analytics. Agency teams will appreciate the robust collaboration features on higher tiers.

Try Buffer →
alternative

Agorapulse

. Agorapulse combines scheduling, engagement, and reporting in one dashboard at a price point that undercuts Sprout Social. It offers strong social listening, inbox management, and customizable reports tailored for agencies. The tool also provides a unified view of comments and messages across platforms.

Try Agorapulse →
alternative

Zoho Social

. Zoho Social is a cost effective choice that integrates tightly with the broader Zoho ecosystem, including CRM and marketing tools. It supports multi brand management and bulk scheduling, which suits agencies handling multiple clients. Pricing starts well below Sprout Social, and its mobile app is solid for on the go monitoring.

alternative

Later

. Later is best known for visual content planning, especially for Instagram. Its drag and drop calendar and media library make it simple to schedule posts, and paid plans include analytics and collaboration. For brands focused on image heavy platforms, Later offers a cheaper, user friendly alternative to Sprout Social.
