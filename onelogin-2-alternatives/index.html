Mid-size firms often juggle two core identity needs: single sign-on (SSO) for quick, secure access to cloud apps, and identity lifecycle management (ILM) to automate user provisioning, role changes, and deprovisioning. OneLogin identity governance brings both capabilities together in one platform. It lets you manage user access from hire to retire, enforce policies, and simplify compliance audits. For growing teams that want to reduce helpdesk tickets and outgrow basic SSO, OneLogin offers a practical, all-in-one solution without the heavy cost of enterprise suite alternatives.

alternative

SailPoint

is a leader in identity governance but is often built for larger enterprises with complex compliance needs. It offers deep access certification and role management, though the setup and ongoing administration can require dedicated staff. Mid-size firms may find it more than they need if their main goal is SSO plus basic lifecycle automation.

alternative

Saviynt

provides a cloud-native identity governance platform with strong integration capabilities for both cloud and on-premises systems. It includes robust access review and analytics features, but its interface can be less intuitive for teams without dedicated security engineers. Saviynt works best for firms that already have a separate SSO provider and need advanced governance workflows.

alternative

BeyondTrust

focuses on privileged access management (PAM) rather than general SSO or user lifecycle management. Its strengths lie in protecting admin accounts and sessions. For a mid-size firm seeking a unified SSO plus identity governance tool, BeyondTrust would serve as a complementary layer rather than a replacement for OneLogin.

alternative

CyberArk

is the market leader in privileged access security, not in standard identity governance or SSO. It secures high-risk credentials and provides just-in-time access. While it can integrate with identity platforms, using it as a primary SSO and lifecycle solution would be overkill and miss core user provisioning capabilities that OneLogin delivers out of the box.
