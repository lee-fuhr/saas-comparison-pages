Sales enablement teams need a content hub that helps reps find the right materials quickly and uses AI to guide them toward the most effective assets. Highspot is a primary choice for teams that want an intuitive platform with strong AI recommendations and deep CRM integration. Other tools like Seismic, Showpad, Brainshark, Allego, and MindTickle also offer content management and smart suggestions, but each takes a different approach to design, compliance, and training support.

alternative

Seismic

Seismic focuses on content management and compliance, making it a strong fit for large enterprises in regulated industries. Its AI engine suggests materials based on buyer personas and stage, though the setup often requires more administrative effort than Highspot.

alternative

Showpad

Showpad combines content management with sales coaching and a CRM-native experience. It works well for teams that want a single platform for both pitch materials and rep coaching, but its AI recommendations are less personalized than Highspot’s.

alternative

Brainshark

Brainshark (now part of Bigtincan) emphasizes sales readiness through training and content delivery. It is useful for teams that need strong onboarding and assessment features, though its content hub and AI suggestions are simpler than Highspot’s.

alternative

Allego

Allego specializes in video-based sales enablement and peer learning. Its AI helps reps find recorded pitches and training content quickly, but it is less suited for teams that need a full document management and CRM-integrated hub.

alternative

MindTickle

MindTickle focuses on continuous learning and coaching with AI-driven content recommendations based on rep skill gaps. It is ideal for teams that prioritize onboarding and certification over document management and CRM workflows.

How they compare

Highspot and Seismic are the two most direct competitors for a content hub with AI recommendations. Highspot excels with an easy-to-use interface, AI that learns from rep behavior and deal stage, and deep Salesforce and HubSpot integration. Seismic offers powerful content automation and compliance controls for heavily regulated industries, but its AI features often require more manual configuration and administrator time. For teams that want fast adoption and intuitive rep guidance, Highspot is generally the better fit. Showpad, Brainshark, Allego, and MindTickle serve more specific needs such as video coaching, onboarding, or learning management.
