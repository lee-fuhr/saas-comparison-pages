Cin7 is a popular choice for inventory management, but mid-market distributors often find that its feature set or pricing does not fit their specific needs. Fishbowl

. Fishbowl offers strong inventory and manufacturing features, but its cloud version can be limited compared to native cloud platforms. It works well for distributors who need deep lot tracking and barcode scanning, though it may require additional setup for multi-location management.

inFlow

. inFlow provides an intuitive interface with order management, purchasing, and barcode support, all in the cloud. It is a good fit for mid-market distributors who want a simple, mobile-friendly system without the complexity of larger ERP platforms.

alternative

Zoho Inventory

. Zoho Inventory integrates tightly with other Zoho apps and major sales channels like Amazon and Shopify. Its cloud infrastructure offers real-time stock updates and warehouse management, making it a cost-effective choice for distributors focused on online sales and multi-warehouse operations.

How they compare

Fishbowl excels in advanced manufacturing and serial number tracking but may feel less agile as a pure cloud solution. inFlow prioritizes ease of use and quick implementation for small to mid-size distributors. Zoho Inventory delivers strong ecommerce integration and scalable pricing but can lack some heavy-duty manufacturing features. All three are viable Cin7 alternatives, and your choice should depend on whether you need deep lot control (Fishbowl), straightforward cloud inventory (inFlow), or omnichannel connectivity (Zoho Inventory).

Frequently asked questions

Is Fishbowl a true cloud inventory system?
Fishbowl offers a cloud-hosted version, but its architecture is built on an on-premise foundation. Some users report slower sync times and limited real-time visibility compared to native cloud tools like inFlow or Zoho Inventory.
Which alternative is best for a mid-market distributor with multiple warehouses?
Zoho Inventory is often the strongest choice for multi-warehouse distributors because it provides real-time stock tracking across locations and integrates with shipping carriers. inFlow is also a solid option, while Fishbowl may require additional configurations to manage multiple warehouses smoothly.
