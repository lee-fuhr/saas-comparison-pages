Best Wordfast alternatives

Wordfast is a well known translation memory tool used by many freelance translators and small teams. However, global marketers often need more than just a desktop tool. They require cloud based collaboration, real time updates, integration with content management systems, and AI driven translation quality. If you are evaluating Wordfast alternatives, you likely want a platform that scales with your marketing campaigns, supports multiple languages, and simplifies the handoff between content creators and translation teams. Below we compare four strong options that address these needs.

alternative

DeepL

. DeepL is best known for its high quality neural machine translation, often preferred by marketers for draft speed and natural sounding output. It offers a deep integration with translation management systems and a glossary feature that helps maintain brand voice and terminology across campaigns. However, its collaboration and workflow features are more limited than full localization platforms.

alternative

Smartling

. Smartling is a cloud based translation management system built for enterprise marketing teams. It uses adaptive machine translation, visual context tools for web and mobile content, and strong analytics to measure translation ROI. Marketers appreciate its direct integrations with common CMS platforms and the ability to manage multiple brands and languages from one dashboard.

alternative

Phrase

. Phrase (formerly PhraseApp) is a developer friendly localization platform that also serves marketing needs. It provides a translation memory, machine translation suggestions, and a flexible API for seamless integration into existing marketing tech stacks. For global teams, its collaboration features such as in context editing and automated workflows help keep content consistent across regions.

alternative

Lokalise

. Lokalise is a user centric localization platform that focuses on speed and ease of use for marketers and developers. It offers screenshot based context, automatic translation via integrated engines, and a mobile app for translators. Marketers like its ability to handle continuous localization for websites and apps, along with push pull integrations to tools like GitHub and Contentful.

How they compare

All four alternatives improve on Wordfast in cloud collaboration and automation. DeepL excels at raw translation quality but lacks rich project management. Smartling offers the most comprehensive enterprise grade workflow and reporting. Phrase is ideal for teams that need deep API and developer integration alongside translation features. Lokalise is the easiest to start with for small to medium marketing teams that want fast setup and a clean interface. Choose based on your team size, technical needs, and preferred balance between human review and machine translation.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best alternative to Wordfast for marketing teams?
It depends on your primary need. For high quality machine translation alone, start with DeepL. For full project management, translation memory, and marketing integrations, Smartling is a strong choice. If your team works closely with developers, Phrase offers flexible APIs. For quick onboarding and app focused localization, Lokalise is a great option.
Can these alternatives handle large volumes of marketing content?
Yes. All four platforms are built to scale. Smartling and Phrase have enterprise grade infrastructure to manage thousands of words per day across multiple languages. DeepL can handle high volume through its API and batch processing, though you may need a separate system for workflow management. Lokalise also supports large projects but is best suited for continuous rather than bulk one time uploads.
