If you are an independent creator looking for a membership site that works seamlessly with Stripe billing, Memberful is a strong option. But it is not the only one. You might want a different pricing model, simpler setup, or a platform with built-in audience discovery. This article covers the best Memberful alternatives for creators who need integrated membership sites and Stripe payments.

alternative

Patreon

. Patreon is one of the most well-known membership platforms with a built-in audience and discovery features. It offers tiered memberships, but it uses its own payment processor rather than native Stripe integration, and it takes a percentage of your revenue.

alternative

Supercast

. Supercast is designed specifically for podcasters who want to offer premium audio content via membership. It integrates with Stripe for billing and lets you create private feeds, but its focus on audio may not suit creators with broader content types.

alternative

Supporting Cast

. Supporting Cast is another podcast-focused membership tool that works with Stripe and allows you to sell subscriptions to private podcast feeds. It is a simpler alternative to Memberful if your main offering is audio, but it lacks the flexibility for video or written content.

alternative

Ko-fi

. Ko-fi is a low-cost platform that lets you accept one-time tips and recurring memberships. It supports Stripe billing and has a free tier, but its membership features are more basic than Memberful and may not scale well for complex tiered offerings.

alternative

SubscribeStar

. SubscribeStar is a membership platform popular with creators who want freedom from content restrictions. It uses Stripe for billing and offers a direct relationship with fans, though its interface and feature set are less polished than Memberful.

alternative

BuyMeACoffee

An alternative to Memberful.

alternative

Fanhouse

. Fanhouse is a membership platform that emphasizes direct fan interaction, including DMs and live streams. It integrates with Stripe for billing, but it takes a revenue share and is still a newer platform with a smaller feature set compared to Memberful.
