Best Bench alternatives

If you use Bench for online bookkeeping but have started looking for alternatives, you are not alone. Many small business owners want a service that fits their budget, offers better features, or provides more responsive support. Bench is a popular option, but it may not be the right fit for every company. This article covers five strong Bench accounting alternatives: Pilot, Bookkeeper360, QuickBooks Live, Botkeeper, and Decimal. Each one approaches online bookkeeping a little differently, so you can compare them and decide which service best matches your business needs.

alternative

Pilot

. Pilot offers bookkeeping, tax preparation, and CFO services for growing businesses. It works well for startups and companies that need more than basic data entry, and it connects with common accounting software like QuickBooks and Xero. Pilot’s plans are higher priced than Bench, but you get a dedicated team and more strategic advice.

alternative

Bookkeeper360

. Bookkeeper360 provides a full range of bookkeeping, payroll, and tax services for small businesses. It uses either QuickBooks or Xero and offers a mobile app for scanning receipts and tracking expenses. Pricing is transparent and starts lower than some other services, though advanced features cost extra.

Try Bookkeeper360 →
alternative

QuickBooks Live

. QuickBooks Live is Intuit’s own bookkeeping service that connects you with a certified bookkeeper. It works directly inside QuickBooks Online, so if you already use that software, the integration is seamless. The price is a flat monthly fee, and you can choose a plan that includes tax support or just bookkeeping.

alternative

Botkeeper

. Botkeeper uses a mix of automation and human accountants to handle bookkeeping for small businesses and accounting firms. It focuses on speed and efficiency, with features like automated data categorization and real-time reporting. Botkeeper can be a good fit if you want less manual work and more frequent financial updates.

alternative

Decimal

. Decimal (formerly known as InDinero) offers bookkeeping, payroll, and tax services tailored to small businesses and agencies. It provides a dedicated bookkeeper and uses software like QuickBooks or Xero. Decimal’s plans start at a similar price point to Bench, but it includes more direct communication and support.

How they compare

All five alternatives to Bench offer online bookkeeping with a human touch, but they differ in price, software compatibility, and extra services. Pilot and Decimal are best for growing companies that want strategic advice, while QuickBooks Live is ideal if you already use QuickBooks. Bookkeeper360 and Botkeeper are strong choices for businesses that need payroll or automation features. Prices range from around $200 to over $500 per month depending on the plan and the size of your books. Most services include a dedicated bookkeeper or team, but some (like Botkeeper) rely more on automation. Before switching, check if the service supports your accounting software and whether it offers tax filing if you need it.
