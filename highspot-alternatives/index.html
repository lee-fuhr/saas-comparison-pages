If your sales enablement team relies on AI-driven content recommendations but finds Highspot’s pricing, complexity, or specific feature gaps a challenge, it is worth exploring other platforms. The right alternative should help reps find the best assets at the moment of need, reduce time spent searching, and deliver measurable coaching insights. Below we review five top contenders that offer strong AI recommendation capabilities.

alternative

Showpad

. Showpad uses AI to score content effectiveness and suggest the right materials based on buyer engagement and rep behavior. Its coaching tools and integration with common CRMs make it a natural fit for teams that want to combine content recommendations with real-time performance data.

alternative

Seismic

. Seismic provides a predictive content engine that learns from each deal stage and recommends assets automatically. Its “Live Social” and “Dynamic Content” features adapt recommendations based on the buyer’s industry, role, and past interactions, which helps reps personalize every conversation.

alternative

Mindtickle

. Mindtickle focuses on readiness and combines AI-driven content suggestions with micro-learning and role-play feedback. Its recommendation engine surfaces the most relevant sales plays and collateral based on each rep’s proficiency gaps and deal progress.

alternative

Brainshark

. Brainshark (now part of Bigtincan) offers AI-powered content recommendations that agree with specific sales stages and buyer personas. The platform also analyzes content usage patterns to surface underused assets and suggest updates, helping teams optimize their library.

alternative

Allego

. Allego’s AI engine automatically tags and recommends sales content based on conversational keywords from calls and meetings. It pulls the most relevant assets into the rep’s workflow without manual searching, and its integration with video coaching further strengthens learning and adoption.

Frequently asked questions

Will these alternatives work with our existing CRM and sales stack?
Yes. Showpad, Seismic, Mindtickle, Brainshark, and Allego all offer native integrations with major CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot as well as common communication tools. Check each platform’s integration marketplace for your specific tools to confirm compatibility.
How do these platforms measure the success of AI content recommendations?
Each tool provides analytics on content usage, rep engagement, and win rates linked to recommended assets. Metrics include view time, sharing frequency, and correlation with closed deals. You can run A/B tests between recommended and non-recommended content to see which drives faster pipeline progression.
