Best Agiloft alternatives

Agiloft is a well known contract lifecycle management (CLM) platform that offers strong low-code customization. But it is not the only option. If you want deep customization without heavy coding, you may find that other tools fit your workflows better. This article covers five Agiloft alternatives that also emphasize low-code flexibility: Ironclad, DocuSign CLM, Conga CLM, Icertis, and Concord. We compare them on ease of configuration, integration, and overall suitability for teams that want to tailor their CLM without writing custom code.

alternative

Ironclad

. Ironclad provides a no-code workflow builder and a drag and drop editor for contract templates. It is designed for legal teams that need quick setup and strong collaboration features. Its low-code nature makes it easy to adjust approval flows and clause libraries without developer help.

alternative

DocuSign CLM

. DocuSign CLM (formerly SpringCM) offers a configurable rule engine and template builder that allows non-technical users to manage contract terms. Its integration with the broader DocuSign ecosystem is a plus for e-signature heavy environments. The low-code capabilities center on field-level controls and automated approval chains.

alternative

Conga CLM

. Conga CLM combines document generation with CLM through an intuitive, low-code interface. Users can build complex contract templates using placeholders and conditional logic without scripting. It also integrates tightly with Salesforce, making it a strong choice for sales-driven organizations.

alternative

Icertis

. Icertis is a powerful enterprise CLM with a modular low-code platform called Icertis Explorer. It enables users to configure contract types, clauses, and workflows through visual tools. However, its depth may require more initial setup time compared to simpler tools, but it offers high scalability.

alternative

Concord

. Concord is a straightforward low-code CLM that focuses on template management and approval routing. It uses a point and click interface for setting up contract lifecycle stages and permissions. It is especially good for smaller teams that want simplicity without sacrificing customization.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my existing Agiloft contracts and templates to an alternative without coding?
Yes, most alternatives offer data import tools for CSV or Excel files and support for standard contract templates. However, you may need to manually re-map custom fields and workflows because each platform structures data differently. A dedicated migration plan or using a third party integration tool can reduce manual work.
Which alternative is the easiest for a non-technical business user to customize?
Ironclad and Concord are often the easiest for non-technical users because they rely on drag and drop builders and require no scripting. Ironclad is better for complex approval chains, while Concord is simpler for basic lifecycle management. Both allow users to set up contract templates and rules without help from IT.
