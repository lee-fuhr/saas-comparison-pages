Best Eloqua alternatives

Eloqua is a powerful marketing automation platform built for large enterprises. However, some organizations with complex account based marketing (ABM) needs find that its cost, steep learning curve, or specific feature gaps do not fit their strategy. If you are evaluating alternatives, you want a tool that supports sophisticated ABM workflows, aligns with a CRM like Salesforce, and scales across multiple teams. Below we compare four popular options that enterprise buyers frequently consider.

alternative

Marketo

. Marketo is a direct competitor to Eloqua and is widely used for ABM at scale. It offers strong lead management, account scoring, and deep integrations with Salesforce and other CRMs. Marketo’s ABM module includes account targeting, engagement scoring, and multi touch attribution, making it a top choice for large enterprises with complex buying groups.

alternative

Pardot

. Pardot is Salesforce’s native B2B marketing automation platform. It works well for companies already on Salesforce, with seamless lead and account syncing. However, its ABM capabilities are less mature than Marketo’s, and some large enterprises find it lacks the advanced segmentation and reporting needed for complex ABM programs.

alternative

HubSpot Marketing Hub

. HubSpot’s Marketing Hub offers a strong all in one CRM and marketing suite, including ABM features like account profiles and custom reporting. The Enterprise tier provides behavioral event triggers and multi touch revenue attribution. While HubSpot is easier to use than Eloqua, its ABM depth may not satisfy very large organizations with intricate account hierarchies or custom data models.

alternative

ActiveCampaign

. ActiveCampaign is primarily designed for mid market and smaller businesses, offering affordable automation and email marketing. It has limited Account Based Marketing features like account scoring or dedicated ABM workflows. For large enterprises with complex ABM needs, ActiveCampaign is usually too lightweight and does not integrate well with enterprise CRMs or data enrichment systems.

How they compare

All four alternatives differ in ABM maturity, enterprise readiness, and cost. Marketo is the strongest ABM choice for large enterprises, with dedicated account targeting and reporting. Pardot works best if you are deeply invested in Salesforce, but you may need to supplement it with third party ABM tools. HubSpot Marketing Hub is simpler to deploy and good for teams that want a unified CRM and marketing suite, though its enterprise ABM features are less extensive. ActiveCampaign is not recommended for large enterprises with complex ABM needs. Pricing also varies widely: Marketo and HubSpot Enterprise are comparable to Eloqua, while Pardot and ActiveCampaign tend to be more affordable but less capable.

Frequently asked questions

What is the best Eloqua alternative for large enterprise ABM?
Marketo is generally considered the strongest alternative because it offers a dedicated ABM module, deep Salesforce integration, and advanced lead and account scoring. It can handle the complex buying groups and multi channel campaigns that large enterprises require.
Can I migrate my data from Eloqua to another platform easily?
Migration difficulty depends on the volume and cleanliness of your data. Most vendors like Marketo and HubSpot provide professional services or partner networks to help with data mapping and campaign transfer. Expect a transition period of several weeks to months for a full migration.
