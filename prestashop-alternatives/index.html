Best PrestaShop alternatives

PrestaShop is a powerful open source ecommerce platform that gives you full control over your online store. However, it requires technical knowledge for hosting, security, and updates. If you want a simpler setup, lower maintenance, or different features, you may be looking for PrestaShop alternatives. This article reviews six popular options to help you choose the right fit for your business.

alternative

BigCommerce

. BigCommerce is a hosted platform that handles all technical aspects for you. It offers built-in features like multi channel selling, SEO tools, and no transaction fees on any plan. It is a strong choice if you want scalability without needing a developer.

alternative

Shopify

. Shopify is one of the most popular ecommerce platforms due to its ease of use and large app store. You can set up a store quickly with drag and drop tools, and it handles hosting and security. It is ideal for beginners and growing businesses, but transaction fees apply unless you use Shopify Payments.

alternative

WooCommerce

. WooCommerce is a free plugin for WordPress that turns your site into an online store. You have full control over design and functionality, but you need to manage hosting, security, and updates yourself. It works best if you already use WordPress or want unlimited customization.

alternative

Ecwid

. Ecwid is a lightweight shopping cart that can be added to any existing website or social media page. It offers a free plan for small catalogs and scales with paid plans. It is a good option if you want to sell on multiple platforms without rebuilding your site.

alternative

Volusion

. Volusion is an all in one hosted ecommerce solution with built in analytics and marketing tools. It provides templates and a drag and drop builder, but its app marketplace is smaller than Shopify’s. It suits small to midsize stores that want an integrated platform.

alternative

Squarespace

. Squarespace is known for its beautiful design templates and easy page builder. Its ecommerce features include inventory management, shipping, and payment processing. It works best for stores that prioritize aesthetics and simplicity over advanced customization.

How they compare

All six alternatives remove the need to manage your own hosting, unlike PrestaShop. Shopify and BigCommerce offer the richest feature sets and third party integrations. WooCommerce gives you maximum flexibility but requires more work. Ecwid is ideal for adding a store to an existing site. Volusion and Squarespace are simpler, all in one options with less complexity. Your choice depends on your budget, technical comfort, and need for design control.
