Terminus is a popular account-based marketing (ABM) platform that helps marketing teams run account-based advertising and track analytics. However, your team may be evaluating other options because of budget constraints, feature gaps, or integration needs. This article covers five strong Terminus alternatives: Demandbase, RollWorks, 6sense, Triblio, and Madison. Each tool is compared on its account-based advertising and analytics capabilities so you can find the best fit for your ABM strategy.

alternative

Demandbase

is a comprehensive ABM platform that combines account identification, intent data, advertising, and analytics. It works well for large enterprises that need a full suite of tools from targeting to measurement. Demandbase also provides strong sales intelligence features for account engagement scoring.

alternative

RollWorks

focuses on account-based advertising and analytics for both mid-market and enterprise teams. It offers easy integration with Salesforce and other CRMs, making it simple to agree sales and marketing. RollWorks is known for its user-friendly interface and flexible pricing tiers. **6sense** uses artificial intelligence to predict buying intent and orchestrate multi-channel campaigns. Triblio

specializes in account-based advertising and marketing orchestration with a strong integration ecosystem for CRM and marketing automation platforms. It allows teams to run targeted display ads, retarget known accounts, and measure pipeline impact. Triblio was acquired by IDG, giving it access to a large B2B publisher network.

alternative

Madison

offers a programmatic account-based advertising platform built specifically for B2B. It provides high-quality account targeting, cross-device tracking, and detailed performance analytics. Madison is often chosen by teams that need a dedicated ad solution without the broader ABM suite.

How they compare

Each alternative differs in scope and target audience. Demandbase is the most complete enterprise ABM suite, combining intent, ads, and analytics. RollWorks is easier to set up for mid-market teams and has transparent pricing. 6sense leads in AI-driven intent prediction and predictive analytics. Triblio offers strong orchestration features with deep CRM integrations. Madison is a pure-play advertising solution with precise targeting and measurement. Pricing varies widely: Demandbase and 6sense tend to be higher cost for large deployments, while RollWorks and Madison offer more flexible entry points. All five tools support account-level reporting and attribution, but the depth of intent data and ad network reach differs.
