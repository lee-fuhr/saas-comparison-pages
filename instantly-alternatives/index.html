If you are a cold email sender looking for high-deliverability outreach platforms, you have probably tried or heard of Instantly. It is a popular tool for managing inbox warming, sending sequences, and tracking deliverability. But maybe you need different features, different pricing, or a better fit for your team size. Here are five solid alternatives to Instantly, each with its own strengths in deliverability and outreach capabilities.

alternative

Apollo.io

. Apollo combines a massive contact database with a built-in email sequencing engine, making it a strong all-in-one option. It handles deliverability through automatic email warmup and smart sending limits, but its primary strength is lead sourcing rather than pure deliverability optimization.

Try Apollo.io →
alternative

Reply.io

. Reply is known for its multichannel outreach and advanced sequence customization, including A/B testing and email rotation. It offers dedicated IPs and built-in spam testing, which helps maintain inbox placement even at higher sending volumes.

alternative

Lemlist

. Lemlist focuses on personalization at scale with features like custom image placeholders and video embeds in emails. It includes a warmup pool and deliverability analytics, though its sending infrastructure is less mature than dedicated deliverability-first platforms.

Try Lemlist →
alternative

Smartlead

. Smartlead is built specifically for high-volume cold email with an emphasis on deliverability through automatic warmup, smart throttling, and spam score monitoring. It supports unlimited mailbox routing and is a direct competitor to Instantly for deliverability-focused users.

Try Smartlead →
alternative

Mailshake

. Mailshake offers simple email and social outreach with a clean interface and good integrations like Salesforce and Zapier. It includes basic warmup and deliverability tools, but power users may find its advanced deliverability features more limited than Instantly or Smartlead.

How they compare

When comparing these tools, think about your primary need. If you want a combined database and outreach, Apollo.io is a good fit. For multichannel sequences and testing, choose Reply.io. For creative personalization, go with Lemlist. For pure deliverability and high-volume sending, Smartlead is the closest alternative to Instantly. Mailshake works best for simpler campaigns and smaller teams. Each tool has its own pricing model and learning curve, so check the latest plans on their websites.
