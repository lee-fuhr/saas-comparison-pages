Deputy and Homebase are two widely used shift scheduling tools, but workforce managers often need to compare them against other options to find the best fit for their team. This article looks at Deputy as the primary tool and examines four alternatives: When I Work, 7shifts, Shiftboard, and Homebase. For each alternative we highlight key strengths and ideal use cases so you can make a more informed choice.

alternative

When I Work

focuses on simplicity and ease of use for small to midsize businesses. It offers straightforward shift scheduling, time tracking, and team communication tools, making it a good choice if your main concern is getting a schedule up and running quickly. **7shifts** is built specifically for the restaurant and hospitality industry. Its features include tip pooling, sales based labor forecasting, and manager logbook functionality, which help streamline operations in food service environments.

Shiftboard

alternative

Homebase

provides an all in one solution for hourly teams, combining scheduling, time clocks, payroll, and HR features. It is popular among small retail and food service businesses that want to handle workforce management without integrating separate systems.

How they compare

Deputy stands out for its mobile first design and strong AI driven scheduling. Homebase is a close competitor with broader HR and payroll integration for smaller teams. When I Work is the simplest to set up, 7shifts is the best for restaurants, and Shiftboard is the best for large scale compliance heavy environments. Your choice should depend on industry, team size, and whether you need built in payroll or advanced forecasting.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my existing employee data into these tools?
Yes, most tools allow you to upload employee lists from a spreadsheet or integrate with payroll systems. Deputy, When I Work, and Homebase offer simple CSV imports, while 7shifts and Shiftboard may require a few extra steps for custom fields.
Which tool is best for a business with fewer than 20 employees?
Homebase or When I Work are often the most budget friendly and intuitive for small teams. Deputy also works well for small teams but can be more feature rich than needed. For restaurants, 7shifts is a strong option even at small scale.
