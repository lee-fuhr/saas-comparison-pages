Best Applied Systems alternatives

Switching agency management systems is a big decision. Applied Systems is a strong, widely used platform, but it is not the perfect fit for every agency or brokerage. Some teams need simpler workflows, lower costs, or specific features like comparative rating or cloud-only access. This article walks you through four key alternatives: Vertafore, HawkSoft, EZLynx, and Jenesi. Each offers a different balance of power, price, and ease of use.

alternative

Vertafore

offers a suite of products including Sagitta, BenefitPoint, and WorkSmart. It is best suited for mid-size to large agencies that need a full-featured management system with strong commercial lines and benefits handling. Vertafore’s integration with its own comparative rater and document management tools can reduce the number of separate vendors you manage.

alternative

HawkSoft

is a cloud-based system known for its intuitive interface and focus on personal lines. It eliminates many clicks and screens found in older systems, which can help staff onboard faster and serve clients more efficiently. While it has fewer advanced features than Applied, it works well for agencies that prioritize simplicity and speed.

alternative

EZLynx

started as a comparative rater and has expanded into a full agency management system. It is a good choice for agencies that want to run quotes from multiple carriers quickly without switching applications. Its management tools cover policy details, commissions, and basic accounting, though it may lack depth for complex commercial bookkeeping.

alternative

Jenesi

is a modern, cloud-first system designed for independent agencies. It emphasizes a clean user interface and real-time data syncing across devices. Jenesi includes built-in marketing and CRM features, making it a strong option for agencies that want to grow their book without paying extra for separate tools.

How they compare

Applied Systems offers deep functionality across personal and commercial lines but can be expensive and complex to implement. Vertafore rivals Applied in scope, especially for large brokerages, but tends to have a steeper learning curve. HawkSoft is simpler and cheaper, ideal for personal lines shops, but it sacrifices some advanced accounting and commercial features. EZLynx excels in quoting speed but its management module is less mature than Applied or HawkSoft. Jenesi provides a modern all-in-one experience with strong CRM, but its carrier integrations are not as extensive as the established players.

Frequently asked questions

Is Vertafore a good alternative to Applied Systems?
Yes, especially for larger agencies and brokerages that need robust commercial lines and employee benefits capabilities. Vertafore’s products like Sagitta and BenefitPoint can match Applied’s power, but the system requires more training and often costs more upfront.
Which alternative is best for a small agency with limited staff?
HawkSoft or Jenesi are both strong choices for small agencies. HawkSoft is very easy to learn and keeps daily tasks fast. Jenesi offers a modern interface with built-in marketing, which can reduce the need for extra software. Both are cloud-based and lower cost than Applied.
