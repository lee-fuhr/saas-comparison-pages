WalkMe is a leading digital adoption platform (DAP) that helps enterprises guide users through software and automate workflows. However, its pricing, complexity, or specific feature gaps may lead you to explore other options. This article compares four strong alternatives: Appcues, Chameleon, Gainsight PX, and UserGuiding. Each offers unique strengths for enterprise needs, from onboarding to product analytics.

alternative

Appcues

. Appcues focuses on user onboarding and in-app messaging with a no-code builder. It is well suited for SaaS companies that need quick setup and strong segmentation, but it may lack the deep analytics and enterprise-grade integration support that larger organizations require.

alternative

Chameleon

. Chameleon excels at creating interactive product tours, tooltips, and surveys without engineering help. Its flexibility appeals to product teams, though enterprises often find its reporting and user management features less mature compared to WalkMe or Gainsight PX.

alternative

Gainsight PX

. Gainsight PX is built for customer success and product-led growth, offering powerful analytics, NPS surveys, and journey mapping. It integrates deeply with Gainsight’s CRM, making it a strong choice for enterprises that want unified product and customer data.

alternative

UserGuiding

. UserGuiding provides a lightweight, affordable alternative with checklists, tooltips, and knowledge base integration. It works well for mid-market companies, but larger enterprises may miss advanced automation, custom code options, and robust permission controls.

How they compare

All four alternatives offer no-code or low-code in-app guidance, but they differ in analytics depth, enterprise readiness, and pricing. Appcues and Chameleon are best for fast, design-focused onboarding. Gainsight PX is ideal for enterprises that need tight product analytics and customer success alignment. UserGuiding is a budget-friendly entry point with solid core features. WalkMe remains the most comprehensive option for complex enterprise environments, but these alternatives provide viable paths if you outgrow it or want a simpler solution.

Frequently asked questions

Which WalkMe alternative is best for a large enterprise with multiple products?
Gainsight PX is often the best fit because it pairs strong product analytics with customer success workflows. It scales across product lines and integrates with enterprise systems like Salesforce and Zendesk.
Can I migrate my existing WalkMe content to these alternatives?
Migration is manual because each platform uses its own format for guides and segments. However, most alternatives offer import tools for user data and APIs to help recreate content. Plan for a content audit and rebuild effort.
