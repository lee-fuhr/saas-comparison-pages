Slido is a popular choice for live polling and Q&A at events, but it may not fit every budget or feature need. Event organizers sometimes look for Slido alternatives that offer different pricing plans, audience engagement styles, or integration options. Below we cover three strong alternatives: Mentimeter, Poll Everywhere, and Kahoot!. Each has its own strengths for keeping your audience involved.

alternative

Mentimeter

focuses on dynamic presentations that combine slides with real-time polls, word clouds, and quiz questions. It works well for both in-person and virtual events, and its interface is designed to be intuitive for presenters and participants alike. Mentimeter offers a free tier with limited features and paid plans that scale for larger audiences.

alternative

Poll Everywhere

specializes in audience response systems that integrate directly into presentation software like PowerPoint, Google Slides, and Keynote. It supports multiple question types, including clickable images and open-ended Q&A, and allows attendees to respond via text message or web browser. Poll Everywhere is a solid choice for corporate events and classrooms that need reliable, distraction-free polling.

alternative

Kahoot!

is best known for game-based learning but also works for live Q&A and surveys at events. Its competitive, quiz-style format boosts energy and participation, especially in settings like conferences, team meetings, or training sessions. Kahoot! offers a free basic plan and paid tiers with advanced reporting and presentation modes.

How they compare

Slido is ideal if you need a simple, dedicated Q&A tool with strong moderation features. Mentimeter excels when you want a full presentation and polling suite in one platform. Poll Everywhere is best for organizers who rely on existing slide decks and need seamless integration. Kahoot! stands out for high-energy, game-like engagement but may be less suited for formal Q&A sessions.
