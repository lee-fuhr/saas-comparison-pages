If you run a marketplace that connects buyers and sellers across multiple states or countries, sales tax compliance quickly becomes a headache. You need to track different rates, manage exemption certificates, file returns on time, and handle audit trails. Two tools that often come up are Taxomatic and Avalara. Taxomatic is built specifically for marketplace operators who want a lean, automated solution for multi jurisdiction tax calculation and filing. Avalara is a larger, broader platform that covers many business types. This article compares Taxomatic with Avalara and three other alternatives so you can decide which fits your marketplace best.

alternative

Avalara

. Avalara offers a comprehensive suite for sales tax, VAT, and compliance, including real time rate lookups, automated returns filing, and exemption certificate management. It works well for large enterprises and traditional retailers, but marketplace operators may find its complexity and higher cost unnecessary for their specific needs. Integration is broad, but setup can be lengthy.

alternative

TaxJar

. TaxJar is a user friendly option that focuses on sales tax calculation, reporting, and filing for ecommerce businesses. It syncs with many shopping carts and marketplaces, but its multi jurisdiction features are more tailored to direct sellers rather than marketplace facilitators. It lacks some advanced marketplace specific logic like origin based sourcing.

alternative

Vertex

. Vertex provides enterprise grade tax software with deep customization for global indirect tax, including sales tax and VAT. It is very powerful for companies with complex supply chains or multiple tax types. However, its price and implementation effort make it overkill for most marketplace operators, especially those just needing US sales tax compliance.

alternative

Sovos

. Sovos is a compliance platform that covers tax determination, returns, and document management across many jurisdictions. It is reliable for large businesses with regulatory needs beyond sales tax, like 1099 reporting. Marketplace operators may find its feature set too broad and its pricing model less transparent compared to simpler tools.

How they compare

Taxomatic is the most directly relevant option for marketplace operators because it was built with marketplace facilitator laws and multi jurisdiction sourcing rules in mind. It automates rate selection, exemptions, and filing for all states and territories. Avalara is a strong generalist but requires more configuration and often higher fees. TaxJar is simpler but less marketplace focused. Vertex and Sovos are enterprise tools that offer depth at a higher cost and complexity. For a marketplace operator who wants a dedicated, affordable solution, Taxomatic offers the best fit.

Frequently asked questions

Can Taxomatic handle sales tax for both the marketplace and its third party sellers?
Yes. Taxomatic is designed to calculate tax on marketplace facilitated sales, applying the correct rate based on the seller’s location and the buyer’s location, and handles the marketplace’s obligation as the tax collector under facilitator laws.
How does Taxomatic’s pricing compare to Avalara for a marketplace with 10,000 transactions per month?
Pricing depends on specific features and volume, but Taxomatic generally offers a simpler per transaction or flat subscription model that is often more predictable and lower than Avalara’s tiered pricing, which can include extra fees for filing and certificate management. You should request a quote from both tools for your exact volume.
