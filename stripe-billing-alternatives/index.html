Best Stripe Billing alternatives

Stripe Billing is a popular choice for handling subscriptions and recurring payments, especially for developers who love its clean API and tight integration with Stripe’s payment ecosystem. But it’s not always the perfect fit. You might need more advanced billing logic like usage-based pricing, dunning management, or multi-entity revenue recognition, and you want to avoid the overhead of building those features yourself. If you are a startup or a developer looking for an API-first billing solution that keeps complexity low while giving you control, here are the top Stripe Billing alternatives to consider.

alternative

Chargebee

. Chargebee offers a flexible subscription management platform with a strong API and webhooks that let you handle proration, coupons, and multi-currency billing without heavy custom code. It supports dozens of payment gateways (including Stripe) so you are not locked in, and its dunning system automates failed payment recovery to reduce churn.

alternative

Recurly

. Recurly is built for recurring billing at scale, with an API that makes it easy to model complex plans, add-ons, and usage charges. It includes built-in tax calculation and advanced analytics, plus a robust dunning engine that adapts to customer behavior, which helps you keep revenue flowing while minimizing developer time.

alternative

Zuora

. Zuora is the heavyweight option for enterprises that need full subscription lifecycle management, including rating, invoicing, and revenue recognition. Its API-first design still appeals to developers, but the system is more suited for companies with complex billing scenarios (e.g., tiered pricing, amendments, and quotes) rather than simple flat-rate subscriptions.

alternative

Paddle

. Paddle acts as both a billing platform and a merchant of record, handling sales tax, VAT, and compliance on your behalf. Its API lets you build subscription flows quickly, and because Paddle invoices customers directly, you avoid many payment and tax headaches, making it ideal for SaaS startups that want to focus on product rather than finance.

alternative

SaaSOptics

. SaaSOptics specializes in financial operations for recurring revenue businesses, with a focus on revenue recognition and reporting rather than pure subscription management. Its API integrates with your existing billing system (like Stripe) to automate deferred revenue schedules and GAAP/IFRS compliance, which is useful for startups that have outgrown spreadsheets but don’t want to switch their payment processor.

alternative

Fusebill

. Fusebill provides subscription billing with a strong emphasis on automated invoicing and collections. Its API supports custom invoice templates, recurring discounts, and flexible payment schedules, and the platform includes a built-in CRM so you can manage customer accounts and communication without stitching multiple tools together.

alternative

Aria Systems

. Aria Systems is an enterprise-grade billing platform designed for recurring revenue models, including usage-based and hybrid pricing. Its API allows developers to configure complex rating logic, create custom billing cycles, and integrate with CRMs and ERPs, but it typically requires more setup and ongoing management than simpler options like Chargebee or Recurly.
