If you build landing pages for a living, you know Instapage is a strong tool. But maybe you want better pricing, different features, or a simpler interface. Performance marketers need pages that convert, load fast, and integrate with their stack. Here are five Instapage alternatives worth testing.

alternative

Unbounce

lets you build pages with a drag and drop builder and run AI powered copy and headline suggestions. It offers smart traffic optimization and deep A/B testing, making it a solid choice for data driven marketers. Unbounce also includes sticky bars and popups for lead generation.

Try Unbounce →
alternative

Leadpages

is a budget friendly option that still provides high quality templates and a simple editor. It focuses on lead capture with features like alert bars and exit intent popups. Leadpages also includes built in analytics and split testing, though it lacks some advanced personalization.

Try Leadpages →
alternative

Swipe Pages

emphasizes speed above all, using a lightweight builder that loads pages very fast. It has a clean interface and good mobile responsiveness without sacrificing design flexibility. Swipe Pages is ideal if page load time is your top conversion factor.

alternative

Landingi

offers a large template library and a straightforward editor with many customization options. It includes built in A/B testing, forms, and integrations with common email marketing and CRM tools. Landingi provides good value for its price, especially for teams who need many landing pages.

alternative

Carrd

is a very simple and cheap tool for one page sites rather than full landing page funnels. It works well for quick micro sites, portfolio pages, or simple lead capture pages. Carrd is not a direct competitor for complex campaigns but can replace Instapage for minimal needs.

How they compare

Each tool serves a different niche. Swipe Pages and Unbounce are best for speed and testing. Leadpages and Landingi offer strong value and ease of use. Carrd is only for the simplest pages. Your choice should depend on your budget, need for A/B testing, and desire for advanced analytics versus simplicity.

Frequently asked questions

Which Instapage alternative is best for A/B testing?
Unbounce has the most mature split testing system, including AI driven optimization. Swipe Pages and Landingi also offer A/B testing, but with fewer features.
Can I build complex funnels with these alternatives?
Unbounce and Swipe Pages allow you to create multi step funnels. Leadpages and Landingi have funnel capabilities but are more basic. Carrd supports only single pages.
