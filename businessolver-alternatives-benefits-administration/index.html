If you are a VP of Total Rewards at a mid-sized enterprise with 1,000 to 5,000 employees, you know that a benefits administration platform can make or break open enrollment and year-round administration. Businessolver is a well known option, but it is not the only one. You may be looking for a platform with a different integration approach, a more consumer-friendly employee experience, or stronger spending account capabilities. This article covers three solid alternatives: Alight, Benefitfocus, and WEX Health. Each has strengths that could better match your organization’s specific needs.

alternative

Alight

. Alight offers a comprehensive benefits administration platform with deep integration into payroll and HRIS systems, making it a strong fit for companies that already use major HCM providers. The platform also includes advanced analytics and decision support tools that help total rewards leaders model costs and plan designs. Many mid-sized enterprises appreciate Alight’s ability to handle both core benefits and voluntary programs under one partner.

alternative

Benefitfocus

. Benefitfocus is known for its modern, consumer-grade employee experience that simplifies enrollment and benefits education. The platform provides flexible plan design options and a robust marketplace for voluntary benefits, which is useful for companies looking to offer a wide range of choices. Benefitfocus also delivers strong compliance support and reporting to help you manage regulatory requirements.

alternative

WEX Health

. WEX Health focuses heavily on health savings accounts, flexible spending accounts, and other consumer-directed benefits, making it an excellent choice if those accounts are a priority for your workforce. The platform also offers core benefits administration and integrates with many payroll and HCM systems, though its strength lies in the spend side of benefits. For enterprises that want a single solution for both benefits enrollment and account management, WEX Health is worth evaluating.

How they compare

All three alternatives can handle the essential tasks of benefits administration, but they differ in emphasis. Alight excels in data-driven analytics and HRIS integration. Benefitfocus stands out for its user experience and voluntary benefits marketplace. WEX Health is the clear leader when you need deep spending account functionality. Your decision should hinge on your organization’s biggest pain points: integration complexity, employee engagement, or account management.
