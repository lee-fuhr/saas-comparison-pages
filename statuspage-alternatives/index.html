If your organization relies on a status page to keep users informed during outages, you might be looking for options beyond StatusPage by Atlassian. Maybe you find the pricing too high, want more modern features, or prefer a simpler setup. Several strong alternatives offer real-time incident management, customizable pages, and integrations without the complexity or cost. Below we compare three popular choices: Status.io, Better Uptime, and Instatus.

alternative

Status.io

focuses on enterprise-grade reliability with advanced routing, scheduled maintenance, and detailed analytics. It provides a clean branded status page and supports multiple components and teams, making it a good fit for larger organizations that need granular control over incident workflows.

alternative

Better Uptime

combines status pages with built-in monitoring and on-call scheduling. It automatically creates incidents from your uptime checks and can notify your team via phone, SMS, or chat. This all-in-one approach reduces the need for separate monitoring tools, which simplifies your incident response process.

Try Better Uptime →
alternative

Instatus

emphasizes speed and simplicity with a free tier that covers unlimited subscribers and a customizable subdomain. It offers quick setup, clear incident timelines, and native integrations with tools like Slack and PagerDuty. For smaller teams or startups, Instatus delivers a polished experience without a steep learning curve.

Try Instatus →

How they compare

Status.io is best for enterprises that need deep customization and team workflows. Better Uptime suits teams that want monitoring and status pages in one platform, with strong alerting. Instatus appeals to cost-conscious teams and startups that value ease of use and a free tier. All three provide status page core functions, but differ in monitoring depth, pricing models, and scalability.
