Demandbase is a well known account based marketing (ABM) platform, but it is not the right fit for every B2B enterprise. Some teams find its pricing high for their budget, others need different advertising integrations or more flexible account orchestration workflows. If you are looking for a full stack ABM platform that handles target account identification, engagement, and measurement, these five alternatives deserve a close look. Each offers unique strengths in account advertising, sales alignment, or AI powered insights.

alternative

Terminus

is a full stack ABM platform built for account based advertising, engagement, and orchestration. It provides a unified workflow to run targeted ads, trigger sales outreach, and track account level engagement across channels. Many enterprises choose Terminus for its straightforward setup and strong integration with common CRM and marketing automation tools.

alternative

RollWorks

focuses on account based advertising and attribution, making it a strong choice for teams that want to supplement their existing stack. Its platform identifies target accounts, serves ads across major networks, and measures pipeline impact. RollWorks is often selected by enterprises that need an advertising first solution but still want basic orchestration and reporting. **6sense** is a predictive ABM platform that uses AI to identify accounts that are actively in market. It goes beyond basic firmographic data to provide intent signals, recommended next steps, and account scoring. For enterprises that prioritize data driven account selection and sales intelligence, 6sense offers deep analytics and automated orchestration.

Triblio

(now part of HubSpot) is an account based advertising and orchestration platform that integrates tightly with HubSpot’s CRM and marketing tools. It allows teams to run targeted ads, personalize web experiences, and orchestrate multi channel campaigns from a single dashboard. Enterprises already using HubSpot often find Triblio a natural fit for extending ABM capabilities.

alternative

Engagio

(acquired by Demandbase itself but still used by some legacy customers) was built around account based sales and marketing alignment. Its key features include account scoring, reporting on coverage and engagement gaps, and sales playbooks. While no longer sold as a standalone product, enterprises that already run Engagio may continue using it as part of a broader Demandbase deployment.

How they compare

All five alternatives provide account targeting, advertising, and reporting capabilities, but they differ in emphasis. Terminus and RollWorks lean heavily on advertising and ease of use, while 6sense offers the strongest predictive analytics and intent data. Triblio is the best option for HubSpot users who want seamless integration. Engagio (where still active) focuses on sales and marketing workflow alignment. For full stack orchestration that includes automated sequences, list scoring, and multi channel engagement, Terminus and 6sense are the most comparable to Demandbase. Pricing and contract flexibility vary widely, so request custom demos to see which platform matches your enterprise’s data volume and sales cycle complexity.

Frequently asked questions

What are the most important factors to evaluate when switching from Demandbase?
Focus on three areas: the quality of intent and firmographic data for account identification, the depth of advertising network coverage (especially LinkedIn and programmatic), and the ease of orchestrating multi touch campaigns across email, ads, and web personalization. Also check how well the alternative integrates with your existing CRM and marketing automation system. A trial period with a sample of your target accounts will reveal gaps in coverage or workflow fit.
Which alternative works best for enterprises with long and complex B2B sales cycles?
6sense is often the top choice for complex sales cycles because its AI identifies accounts that show early buying signals, and its orchestration rules can trigger different sequences based on account stage. Terminus also supports tiered account plays and sequences, making it a strong second option. Both platforms allow you to adjust engagement frequency and sales handoff triggers as deals progress over many months.
