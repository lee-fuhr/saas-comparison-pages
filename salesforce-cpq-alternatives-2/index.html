Best Salesforce CPQ alternatives

Salesforce CPQ is a powerful tool for quote-to-cash processes, but it may not fit every organization’s needs. As businesses grow, they often require more flexible pricing logic, better deal management, or a lighter platform. This article reviews five alternatives that offer scalable quoting beyond native Salesforce CPQ while still integrating with your existing Salesforce investment.

alternative

Apttus

. Now part of Conga, Apttus provides a comprehensive CPQ and contract lifecycle management platform. It excels in handling complex product configurations, subscription billing, and contract renewals, making it a strong choice for enterprises with intricate pricing models.

alternative

DealHub

. DealHub is known for its user-friendly interface and flexible approval workflows. It offers real-time pricing, guided selling, and seamless Salesforce integration, helping sales teams close deals faster without heavy customization.

alternative

Pricefx

. Pricefx is a cloud native pricing platform that combines CPQ with advanced price optimization and analytics. It scales easily for high transaction volumes and supports dynamic pricing strategies, ideal for B2B companies with global operations.

alternative

PROS

. PROS leverages AI to optimize pricing decisions across sales channels. Its platform includes smart CPQ capabilities that recommend optimal prices and discounts, best suited for large enterprises that need data-driven revenue management.

alternative

ConfigureOne

. ConfigureOne focuses on complex product configuration, especially for engineer-to-order and make-to-order industries. It provides guided configuration rules and real-time feasibility checks, reducing errors for products with many options.
