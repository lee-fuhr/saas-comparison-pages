Lemlist is a popular tool for running personalised cold email campaigns, but it may not fit every team’s budget, workflow, or feature needs. If you are evaluating how to scale outreach while keeping deliverability high, checking out alternatives can help you find a better match. Below we cover five strong options, each with their own strengths for outbound sales teams.

alternative

Instantly

. Instantly focuses heavily on email deliverability and warming inboxes before you send campaigns. It offers smart sending limits and a simple dashboard, making it a good fit if your main priority is keeping emails out of spam.

Klenty

. Klenty is built for high-volume personalised outreach with deep CRM integrations, especially with Salesforce. It offers custom fields and templates for personalisation, plus sequencing and meeting scheduling, making it a practical choice for teams that already use a CRM.

alternative

Mailshake

. Mailshake provides a straightforward interface for cold email and social selling campaigns. It supports A/B testing, personalisation tokens, and a built-in phone dialer, which works well for teams that want a simple, all-in-one outreach platform.

How they compare

Lemlist is strong on visual personalisation (images, GIFs) and has an engaging approach, but its deliverability features can lag behind dedicated tools. Instantly excels at inbox placement but lacks built-in data enrichment. Reply.io adds LinkedIn automation but can become complex. Apollo.io offers a huge lead database, though its email sending is not as refined. Klenty is CRM-centric and great for sales teams using Salesforce or HubSpot. Mailshake is simple and affordable, but missing advanced AI features. Your choice should depend on whether you need more data, stronger deliverability, or deeper CRM integration.

Frequently asked questions

Which Lemlist alternative is best for sending very large volumes of cold emails?
Instantly is often the best choice for high volume because of its sophisticated warmup technology and strict sending limits that protect sender reputation. It avoids sudden spikes that can hurt deliverability at scale.
Can I still personalise images or dynamic content without Lemlist?
Yes, both Reply.io and Klenty support custom fields and templates that allow you to insert personalisation like names or company details. For image-based personalisation, you can use dynamic image services (e.g., personalised GIF generators) and embed them via merge tags in any of these tools.
