Mintigo offers predictive scoring that helps marketers prioritize leads by combining firmographic data (company size, industry, revenue) with technographic data (tools and software a company uses). But as your needs evolve, you may find that Mintigo’s pricing, data coverage, or integration limits no longer fit. This article reviews five alternatives – MadKudu, 6sense, Lattice Engines, Infer, and Snowflake – each designed to deliver the deep firmographic and technographic insights your B2B team needs.

alternative

MadKudu

MadKudu specializes in predictive lead scoring that uses both firmographic and behavioral data to rank prospects. It plugs directly into your CRM or marketing automation platform, so you can quickly identify which accounts are most likely to convert. MadKudu’s model is transparent and lets you adjust scoring criteria without needing a data science team. **6sense** 6sense provides an account-based platform that predicts buying intent using firmographic, technographic, and third-party intent signals. It surfaces accounts that are actively researching tools similar to yours, which helps you prioritize outreach. 6sense also offers orchestration features that automatically tailor ads and emails based on where an account is in its buying journey.

Lattice Engines

Lattice Engines (now part of Dun & Bradstreet) delivers predictive scoring by layering firmographic and technographic data onto your existing CRM records. It identifies accounts with the highest likelihood to buy and scores them based on historical sales outcomes. The tool integrates with Salesforce and Marketo, making it a natural fit for teams that already use those platforms.

alternative

Infer

Infer focuses on predictive analytics for B2B sales and marketing, scoring leads and accounts using firmographic, technographic, and behavioral data. Its machine learning models are trained on your closed-won and closed-lost deals, so the scoring reflects your specific market. Infer also offers a simple API to push scored leads into your CRM or automation tools.

alternative

Snowflake

Snowflake is a cloud data platform that lets you store and combine firmographic, technographic, and intent data from multiple sources. While it is not a predictive scoring tool on its own, you can use Snowflake to build custom scoring models with SQL or third-party analytics tools. It works best for teams that want full control over their data and scoring logic.

How they compare

All five alternatives provide firmographic and technographic insights, but they differ in setup, data sources, and ease of use. MadKudu and Infer are purpose-built for predictive scoring and require minimal data science effort. 6sense adds intent data and account orchestration for larger account-based programs. Lattice Engines (D&B) offers deep third-party firmographic data but may need more integration work. Snowflake gives you raw data infrastructure to build your own scoring system, which requires more technical skill. Choose based on your team’s size, technical resources, and whether you need out-of-the-box scoring or custom, flexible data handling.

Frequently asked questions

Can these tools work with my existing CRM like Salesforce or HubSpot?
Yes, all five alternatives integrate with popular CRMs and marketing automation platforms. MadKudu, Infer, and Lattice Engines have native connectors for Salesforce and Marketo. 6sense offers direct integrations as well as a Zapier connection. Snowflake can connect through its data sharing or third-party ETL tools, but it requires more setup than the others.
Which alternative is best for a small marketing team without a data scientist?
MadKudu and Infer are designed for teams that want predictive scoring without hiring a data scientist. Both provide guided setup, transparent scoring logic, and support from customer success teams. 6sense also works for smaller teams if you need account-based targeting, but it typically requires more configuration and a larger budget.
