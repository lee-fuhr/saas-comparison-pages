Best Conga alternatives

Conga is a popular document generation and automation platform often used with Salesforce and e-signature tools. However, enterprises may find that Conga’s complexity, pricing, or limited contract lifecycle management features don’t fit their needs. This article explores three strong alternatives: Ironclad, Juro, and Precisely, to help you find the right fit for your document generation and e-sign integration requirements.

alternative

Ironclad

is a contract lifecycle management platform that offers powerful document generation and native e-signature capabilities. It is designed for legal and business teams to create, negotiate, and sign contracts in a single system, replacing the need for separate tools. Ironclad excels in workflows that require collaboration and approval routing across departments.

alternative

Juro

provides an all-in-one contract management platform with embedded document generation and e-signature features. Its intuitive editor allows users to create smart contracts with conditional logic, and integrations with common business tools streamline the signing process. Juro is particularly suited for mid-market to enterprise teams seeking a modern, user-friendly alternative to Conga.

alternative

Precisely

(formerly known as Informatica’s data integration tools but now focused on data integrity and document generation for enterprises) offers a robust document generation solution that can handle high-volume, data-driven document creation. It integrates with Salesforce and other CRMs and supports e-signature providers like DocuSign. Precisely is a good choice for enterprises that need to produce complex documents from large datasets while maintaining data accuracy.

How they compare

Conga is strong for Salesforce-centric document generation but can be costly and rigid. Ironclad offers a full CLM approach with built-in e-sign, ideal for legal-heavy workflows. Juro combines simplicity with powerful contract logic, suitable for teams that prioritize ease of use. Precisely focuses on data-driven document generation at scale, making it a good fit for enterprises with high-volume batch processing needs. Each tool integrates with e-signature providers, but the depth of native e-sign features varies.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives replace all Conga features for document generation?
Yes, each alternative can handle document generation, but they differ in approach. Ironclad and Juro bring contract lifecycle management into one platform, while Precisely focuses on data-driven template creation. You may need to evaluate which one aligns with your specific document types and integration needs.
Do these alternatives support integration with existing e-signature tools like DocuSign?
Yes, all three alternatives integrate with major e-signature providers. Ironclad has a native e-signature, while Juro also offers built-in e-signing. Precisely typically works with third-party e-sign providers. Confirm the specific integration details during a trial to ensure it matches your current stack.
