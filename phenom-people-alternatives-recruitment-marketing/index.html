If you are a Head of Talent Attraction at a mid-market firm with 500 to 2000 employees, you have likely evaluated Phenom People for AI-driven recruitment marketing. Phenom is a strong platform that combines career sites, candidate relationship management, and personalization. However, you might be exploring alternatives because of budget constraints, feature gaps, or a need for a more specialized fit. This article reviews three popular alternatives – SmashFly, Beamery, and Gem – to help you compare their strengths for your team’s specific goals.

alternative

SmashFly

focuses on recruitment marketing automation with an emphasis on career site optimization and multi-channel campaign management. It uses AI to personalize candidate journeys and can integrate with your existing applicant tracking system to track source effectiveness. For mid-market firms wanting a dedicated marketing-first tool without the full CRM overhead, SmashFly offers a leaner, more affordable option.

alternative

Beamery

is a talent lifecycle platform that goes beyond recruitment marketing into talent pools, internal mobility, and skills-based matching. Its AI helps you build automated nurture flows and prioritize candidates based on engagement and fit. If your organization values a unified view of candidates from attraction through hiring and retention, Beamery provides a broader scope than Phenom People.

alternative

Gem

(now part of Indeed) emphasizes outbound recruiting and team collaboration with AI-powered sourcing and sequencing. It excels at helping recruiters find and engage passive candidates across LinkedIn, email, and other channels. For talent attraction teams that rely heavily on proactive sourcing and need robust analytics on outreach effectiveness, Gem is a strong alternative to Phenom’s more inbound-focused approach.
