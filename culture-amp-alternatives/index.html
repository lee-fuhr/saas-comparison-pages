How they compare

When comparing these tools, consider your primary need: performance integration (Lattice), manager coaching (15Five), all-in-one platform (Leapsome), enterprise analytics (Glint), or continuous listening with Workday (Peakon). Culture Amp remains a strong all-rounder, but each alternative offers unique strengths for different team sizes, budgets, and HR workflows.

Frequently asked questions

How do these alternatives compare to Culture Amp in terms of cost?
Pricing varies widely. Lattice and 15Five often have lower starting prices for smaller teams. Leapsome is competitive for mid-size companies. Glint and Peakon are typically enterprise-priced and may require a minimum number of employees. Always request a custom quote based on your team size.
Can I migrate my existing survey data from Culture Amp to an alternative?
Most platforms offer data import tools, but the process depends on the format of your data. You can usually export survey results from Culture Amp as CSV files. Then work with the new vendor’s support team to map fields and upload historical data. This may require some manual cleanup.
