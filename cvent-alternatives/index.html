Best Cvent alternatives

Cvent is a powerful event management platform used by many large enterprises. But it may not fit every organization’s budget, workflow, or feature preferences. If you are evaluating your options, here are three alternatives specifically suited for large conferences and hybrid events.

alternative

Eventbrite

. Eventbrite is best known for ticketing and registration at scale, making it a solid choice for large public conferences. Its enterprise tier offers dedicated support, advanced analytics, and integrations with common marketing and CRM tools. However, its native hybrid event features are more limited than Cvent’s, so you may need to add a separate streaming partner.

alternative

Hopin

. Hopin was built from the ground up for virtual and hybrid events, which makes it attractive for enterprises moving to online formats. It includes interactive features like networking lounges, breakout sessions, and expo halls that work well for large audiences. Keep in mind that Hopin’s on-site event management tools are less mature, so it is best suited for events with a strong digital component.

alternative

Bizzabo

. Bizzabo positions itself as an all-in-one event platform with end-to-end capabilities for in-person, virtual, and hybrid events. Its strengths include a modern user interface, customizable agenda builders, and robust attendee engagement analytics. For large enterprises, Bizzabo offers enterprise licensing and dedicated account management, though some users report a steeper learning curve compared to Cvent.
