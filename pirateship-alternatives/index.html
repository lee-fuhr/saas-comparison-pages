As a fulfillment manager, you know PirateShip is a popular shipping software for small to medium volume shippers, especially those using USPS. But as your order volume grows or your shipping needs become more complex (with multi-carrier support, warehouse integrations, or automation), you may need to look at alternatives. The right platform can cut costs, reduce manual work, and help you scale without headaches. Below we compare five strong alternatives to PirateShip: ShipBob, ShipStation, EasyShip, Shippo, and EasyPost.

alternative

ShipBob

is a full fulfillment service, not just software, so it handles storage, picking, packing, and shipping from multiple warehouse locations. It is best for ecommerce brands that want to outsource logistics entirely rather than manage shipping labels in-house.

alternative

ShipStation

is a leading multi-carrier shipping platform that integrates with dozens of ecommerce carts and marketplaces. It offers powerful automation rules, batch processing, and branded tracking pages, making it a solid choice for high-volume fulfillment teams.

alternative

EasyShip

focuses on cross-border shipping with discounted rates from carriers like DHL, FedEx, and UPS along with integrated customs documentation. It is ideal for fulfillment managers who ship internationally and need simple compliance tools.

alternative

Shippo

provides a straightforward API and dashboard for comparing rates across USPS, UPS, FedEx, and DHL, with no monthly minimums. It works well for small to medium businesses that want a low-commitment alternative with batch label printing and address validation.

alternative

EasyPost

is a shipping API platform designed for developers and larger operations that need to build custom shipping workflows. It offers carrier rate shopping, address verification, and tracking insights at scale, but requires technical integration.
