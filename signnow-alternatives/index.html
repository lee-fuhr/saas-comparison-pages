If your mid-size business uses SignNow but wants to explore other options for affordable e-signature compliance, you are not alone. SignNow is a solid choice, but some teams find its interface confusing or need features that competitors offer at a similar price point. This article covers three strong SignNow alternatives that balance cost with the compliance requirements mid-size companies face.

alternative

DocuSign

. DocuSign is the industry standard for e-signature compliance, with robust audit trails and support for over 40 regional e-signature laws. Its pricing can be higher than SignNow, but mid-size businesses may find the extra investment worthwhile if they need advanced workflow automation or integration with a large number of business apps.

alternative

HelloSign

. HelloSign offers a simpler, more user-friendly experience than SignNow at a competitive price, making it a popular choice for companies that want to send and sign documents quickly. It includes strong compliance features like SOC 2 certification and tamper-evident seals, though its advanced workflow tools are less extensive than those in DocuSign or SignNow.

alternative

Adobe Sign

. Adobe Sign integrates seamlessly with the Adobe Document Cloud and other Adobe products, which is a major advantage for teams already using Adobe tools. It provides enterprise-grade compliance (including support for ESIGN, UETA, and eIDAS) and flexible pricing tiers, but its interface can feel more complex than SignNow or HelloSign.

How they compare

When comparing these SignNow alternatives, consider your team’s priorities. DocuSign leads in compliance depth and integrations but costs more. HelloSign wins on simplicity and affordability, with solid compliance for most business needs. Adobe Sign is best for organizations that rely on Adobe’s ecosystem. All three meet the legal requirements for electronic signatures, so your choice should depend on your budget and the specific workflows you use daily.

Frequently asked questions

Are these alternatives as compliant as SignNow for legal documents?
Yes. DocuSign, HelloSign, and Adobe Sign all support major e-signature laws (ESIGN, UETA, eIDAS) and provide audit trails, encryption, and identity verification. Each platform is used by regulated industries and passes independent security audits.
Which alternative is the most affordable for a mid-size business?
HelloSign typically has the lowest starting price for active users and per-envelope costs. DocuSign and Adobe Sign can be more expensive, but they often offer volume discounts for mid-size teams. Check each vendor’s current pricing page for the most accurate quote.
