Filevine is a popular case management tool for litigation firms, but it may not be the right fit for every practice. Some teams find its complexity or pricing challenging, while others need different integrations or workflows. This article explores four strong alternatives for litigation-focused firms that want case-centric task management.

alternative

Clio

offers a comprehensive legal practice management platform with robust task management and document automation. Its integration with over 200 apps makes it easy to connect billing, calendaring, and client communication. For litigation teams, Clio’s matter-centric organization and built-in time tracking help keep cases on schedule.

alternative

MyCase

provides an intuitive, all-in-one solution with a focus on client portals and task management. Its litigation tools include customizable case stages, document templates, and a built-in legal calendar. Many firms appreciate MyCase for its simple setup and strong support for billing and payment processing.

alternative

PracticePanther

emphasizes automation and efficiency, with features like workflow triggers, task templates, and two-way text messaging. Litigation teams can create repeatable task lists for common case types, reducing manual follow-up. Its integration with Zapier and other tools also extends its flexibility.

alternative

Smokeball

is built specifically for small to midsize litigation firms, with AI-powered document assembly and court rules integration. It captures time automatically from tasks and emails, which helps ensure billing is accurate. Smokeball’s emphasis on document and email management makes it a strong choice for case-centric workflows.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a litigation firm that needs strong court rules integration?
Smokeball includes built-in court rules for many jurisdictions, making it a solid choice for litigation firms. Clio and PracticePanther also offer court date calculators, but Smokeball’s integration is more deeply embedded in daily workflows.
Can I migrate my data from Filevine to these alternatives easily?
Most providers offer migration assistance or import tools for common fields like contacts, matters, and tasks. Clio and MyCase have dedicated onboarding teams, and PracticePanther provides a concierge migration service. Always check with the vendor about data format requirements before starting.
