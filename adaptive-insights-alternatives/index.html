Adaptive Insights, now part of Workday, is a popular cloud-based financial planning and analysis platform. It offers strong budgeting, forecasting, and reporting capabilities for corporate finance teams. However, some organizations look for alternatives due to cost, specific feature gaps, integration preferences, or a need for a different approach to modeling and collaboration. This article reviews four leading alternatives for finance and budgeting professionals: Planful, Anaplan, Vena tools, and Workiva.

alternative

Planful

is a cloud-based FP&A platform built for continuous planning, budgeting, and financial consolidation. It emphasizes a structured, user-friendly approach that helps finance teams speed up their planning cycles without heavy IT involvement. Many professionals choose Planful when Adaptive Insights feels too rigid or expensive for their evolving needs.

alternative

Anaplan

is a connected planning platform designed for large-scale, complex modeling across finance and operations. Its hyperscale engine allows real-time scenario analysis and driver-based forecasting, but it requires more upfront setup and training. Anaplan best suits enterprises with sophisticated modeling needs and dedicated FP&A resources.

An alternative to Adaptive Insights.

alternative

Workiva

is known for its connected reporting and compliance platform, and its planning module now links budgeting and forecasting directly to financial statements and external filings. It excels for publicly traded companies that need tight integration between internal planning and external reporting. Workiva appeals to teams that want to streamline the entire process, from planning to filing, a gap some find in Adaptive Insights.

How they compare

Planful offers a balanced mix of ease of use and structured planning, making it a strong choice for mid-market and growing firms. Anaplan provides unmatched modeling flexibility for large, complex organizations but requires more time and skill to implement. Vena focuses on minimizing disruption by keeping the spreadsheet feel while adding governance and collaboration. Workiva stands out when integration with reporting and compliance workflows is critical, particularly for regulated industries. Pricing for all four is subscription-based and varies widely by deployment size, so direct vendor quotes are recommended.
