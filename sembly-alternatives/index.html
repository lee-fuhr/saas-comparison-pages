Sembly is a popular AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and analyzes your remote team calls. However, it might not fit every team’s workflow or budget. If you are looking for alternatives that offer searchable recordings and AI powered analytics, several tools provide similar or complementary features. This article covers four strong options for remote teams.

alternative

Otter.ai

. Otter.ai delivers real time transcription and automated meeting notes that sync with your calendar. It works with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Its searchable transcript library makes it easy to find past discussions by keyword or speaker.

alternative

Fireflies.ai

. Fireflies.ai records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings from over a dozen platforms. It includes powerful search, keyword tracking, and topic detection. Team members can leave comments and create highlight clips directly on the timeline.

alternative

Grain

. Grain focuses on capturing and sharing video highlights from meetings. It automatically records and transcribes calls, then lets you cut short clips without editing software. Teams use Grain to review customer feedback, sales demos, or internal updates without watching full recordings.

alternative

Rev.ai

. Rev.ai provides high accuracy speech to text through an API and a batch upload service. It is ideal for teams that need reliable transcripts for recordings, especially those with heavy accents or technical jargon. Rev also offers human reviewed transcripts for maximum precision.

How they compare

All four tools provide searchable recordings, but they differ in focus. Otter.ai excels at live, real time transcription and has a simple free tier. Fireflies.ai offers the broadest platform integration and advanced search with custom topic tracking. Grain is best if your team wants to create and share short video clips from meetings. Rev.ai prioritizes transcription accuracy, including human reviewed options. Consider your team’s primary need: live notes, collaborative search, video highlights, or precision transcripts.
