Best Squarespace Commerce alternatives

If you are a designer using Squarespace Commerce for its elegant templates and built-in ecommerce features, you know how important a polished look and seamless shopping experience can be. However, you might be exploring other platforms that offer similar design flexibility and integrated selling tools. This article covers three strong Squarespace Commerce alternatives: Shopify, Wix Stores, and BigCommerce. Each one has its own strengths for designers who care about aesthetics and functionality.

alternative

Shopify

is a powerhouse for ecommerce with hundreds of professionally designed themes, many of which are mobile responsive and customizable for a designer’s touch. Its built-in payment processing, inventory management, and marketing tools are robust, though you may need third-party apps for more advanced design features. Shopify’s ecosystem is large, making it easy to find help or integrations, but the monthly costs can add up.

Try Shopify →
alternative

Wix Stores

offers a drag-and-drop editor with a huge library of stunning, modern templates that give designers total creative control without coding. Its ecommerce features like product galleries, abandoned cart recovery, and multi-channel selling are integrated directly into the builder. Wix is especially beginner-friendly, but some designers may find the template switching limitations a constraint after committing to a design.

Try Wix Stores →
alternative

BigCommerce

provides enterprise-grade ecommerce capabilities with a strong set of built-in features, including multi-currency support and advanced SEO tools that reduce reliance on extra apps. Its templates are attractive and responsive, though the design customization depth may not match Squarespace’s flexibility without some development work. BigCommerce scales well for growing brands, but its pricing can become steep as your revenue increases.

Try BigCommerce →

How they compare

Squarespace Commerce stands out for its minimalist, award-winning templates and all-in-one simplicity. Shopify excels in app integrations and selling across channels but may require extra design effort. Wix Stores offers maximal design freedom with a visual editor and strong built-in ecommerce, while BigCommerce provides powerful features out of the box for scaling businesses. For designers, the best choice depends on how much custom design control versus built-in ecommerce function you need.

Frequently asked questions

Can I maintain the same design quality I get with Squarespace on these alternatives?
Yes. Shopify, Wix Stores, and BigCommerce all offer high-quality, responsive templates. Wix gives you the most direct drag-and-drop design control, while Shopify and BigCommerce require some theme customization to match Squarespace’s visual polish.
Which alternative is best for a designer who wants to sell digital products?
All three support digital downloads, but Wix Stores and Shopify offer dedicated apps or built-in features for file delivery. Wix’s visual editor makes it easy to design a digital product storefront that feels cohesive with your brand.
