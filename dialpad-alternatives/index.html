Best Dialpad alternatives

If your sales or support team uses Dialpad but you are looking for a change, you are not alone. Many teams explore alternatives because of pricing changes, missing features, or a desire for simpler setup. Dialpad offers AI-powered calling and unified communications, but some users find its interface complex or its cost too high for smaller teams. Below we review five strong alternatives: Aircall, Nextiva, JustCall, OpenPhone, and CloudTalk. Each tool focuses on different strengths, so you can choose the one that fits your team’s size, budget, and workflow.

alternative

Aircall

. Aircall is a cloud-based phone system built for sales and support teams that need a simple, reliable tool. It integrates deeply with CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot, making it easy to log calls and track activity. Aircall’s pricing is per user per month, and it offers a clean interface with real-time collaboration features.

Try Aircall →
alternative

Nextiva

. Nextiva provides a full unified communications platform that goes beyond phone calls to include video meetings, team chat, and CRM integrations. It is a good choice for teams that want an all-in-one solution instead of piecing together separate tools. Nextiva’s pricing is often competitive for mid-sized businesses, and its customer support is known for being responsive.

Try Nextiva →
alternative

JustCall

. JustCall is a cost-effective VoIP solution designed for sales teams that need international calling and SMS. It offers built-in power dialers, call recording, and easy integration with many CRMs. JustCall is especially popular among small and medium businesses that want a straightforward alternative without paying for features they do not use.

alternative

OpenPhone

. OpenPhone is a modern business phone app that gives each team member a dedicated number for calls, texts, and voicemail. It works on desktop and mobile with a simple, intuitive interface that many find easier than Dialpad. OpenPhone is ideal for startups and small teams that want a low-cost, no-frills phone system.

Try OpenPhone →
alternative

CloudTalk

. CloudTalk is a cloud call center software that focuses on outbound sales and inbound support with features like smart dialers, call queues, and detailed analytics. It integrates with popular CRMs and helpdesk tools, and its pricing is based on the number of users and features needed. CloudTalk is a strong option for teams that prioritize call center functionality over general office phone features.

Frequently asked questions

Which Dialpad alternative is the most affordable for a small sales team?
OpenPhone and JustCall are typically the most affordable options. OpenPhone starts at a low per-user price with a simple plan, while JustCall offers competitive rates for teams that need international calling and SMS.
I need advanced call center features like auto dialers and queue management. Which alternative should I choose?
CloudTalk is built specifically for call centers and includes smart auto dialers, call queues, and detailed analytics. Aircall also offers some call center features but is more focused on general sales use.
