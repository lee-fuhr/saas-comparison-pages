Choosing the right performance management and employee engagement platform is a critical decision for people ops teams. Lattice is a popular option, but it might not be the perfect fit for every organization’s size, culture, or specific needs. Whether you are evaluating alternatives because of budget constraints, missing features, or a desire for a different approach to feedback and reviews, this guide will walk you through five strong competitors. We will compare 15Five, Culture Amp, Leapsome, Betterworks, and Pequity so you can find the tool that best supports your team’s growth and engagement goals.

alternative

15Five

focuses on continuous performance management with lightweight weekly check-ins and manager training built directly into the platform. It is a good fit for teams that want a simple, rhythm-based feedback cycle without the overhead of a full review system. 15Five also offers engagement surveys and a coaching component to help managers lead more effectively.

Try 15Five →
alternative

Culture Amp

is a robust analytics-first platform that excels at employee engagement surveys and advanced people analytics. It allows ops teams to run customizable engagement, lifecycle, and performance surveys, then surface actionable insights with its powerful reporting engine. Culture Amp’s performance module includes reviews and goal tracking, but its strength lies in understanding the broader employee experience.

Try Culture Amp →
alternative

Leapsome

combines performance management with learning and development features in one integrated system. It offers continuous feedback, reviews, and goal alignment alongside a learning management module for upskilling. Leapsome is ideal for companies that want to connect employee growth directly to performance conversations and still need a strong engagement survey tool.

Try Leapsome →
alternative

Betterworks

is a continuous performance management platform that emphasizes goal alignment and regular check-ins over annual reviews. It connects individual and team goals to company objectives using an OKR framework, with lightweight feedback loops and pulse surveys. Betterworks works well for organizations that already use or want to adopt an OKR-driven culture.

alternative

Pequity

takes a data-driven approach to compensation and equity planning, which is a different angle than traditional performance management. It helps ops teams model and communicate pay decisions, equity grants, and promotion budgets in a transparent way. Pequity is a strong alternative if your primary need is to integrate compensation strategy with performance and retention insights.

How they compare

Choosing between these alternatives depends on your team’s priorities. If you want simple, regular check-ins and manager training, go with 15Five. For deep engagement analytics and survey capabilities, Culture Amp leads. If you need performance management plus learning and development in one tool, Leapsome is a solid pick. Betterworks is best for OKR-aligned goal management and continuous feedback loops. And if your main pain point is linking performance to pay and equity decisions, Pequity fills that gap directly. All five tools offer integrations with common HRIS and collaboration platforms, but each has a distinct focus that your team should match against your current gaps.
