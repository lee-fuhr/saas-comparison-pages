If you manage hourly employees in retail or hospitality, you know that scheduling software can make or break your day. Deputy is a popular choice, but it might not fit every budget, workflow, or team size. Maybe you need lower pricing, easier time tracking, or stronger compliance features. This guide covers four solid Deputy alternatives: When I Work, 7shifts, Homebase, and Shiftboard. Each one has different strengths, so you can choose the tool that best matches your store or restaurant.

alternative

When I Work

. This platform focuses on simplicity and is great for small to mid-sized teams. It offers easy shift scheduling, time clocking, and team messaging, all in a clean interface. Managers appreciate the auto-scheduling rules, but advanced labor forecasting is limited compared to Deputy. **7shifts**. Built specifically for restaurants, 7shifts excels at managing tip pools, food safety manager compliance, and shift swaps. It integrates tightly with popular point of sale systems like Toast and Square. The learning curve is low, and staff love the mobile app for picking up extra shifts.

alternative

Homebase

. This tool is a strong all-rounder for retail and food service, with free options for very small teams. It includes scheduling, time clocks, hiring, and basic HR features like PTO tracking. Homebase is especially good if you want an affordable bundle that covers both scheduling and employee onboarding.

alternative

Shiftboard

. Designed for high volume, complex operations like warehouses or multi location retail chains. Shiftboard handles shift bidding, labor compliance, and integration with payroll systems. It is more powerful than Deputy for large teams, but the interface is less intuitive for simple storefronts.

How they compare

Deputy is a balanced choice for mid sized teams needing solid scheduling, time tracking, and reporting. When I Work is cheaper and simpler, best for small teams that prioritize ease of use over advanced analytics. 7shifts is the top pick for restaurants because of its restaurant specific features and POS integrations. Homebase offers a free tier and extra HR tools, making it good for very small businesses on a budget. Shiftboard is for large enterprises with complex scheduling needs, but it may be overkill for a single retail store.
