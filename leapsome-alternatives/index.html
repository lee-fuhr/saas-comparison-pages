If your People-ops team relies on Leapsome for OKR tracking and continuous performance reviews, you might be looking for alternatives that better fit your budget, feature set, or company culture. Leapsome is strong in combining employee development with goal alignment, but other tools specialize in lighter check-ins, deeper analytics, or simpler OKR workflows. Below we compare five common alternatives, focusing on how each handles both OKR tracking and continuous performance reviews.

alternative

15Five

focuses on lightweight weekly check-ins and continuous feedback, with OKR tracking available as a separate add-on module. It is best for teams that want to build a rhythm of manager-employee conversations first and layer goals on top later.

alternative

Betterworks

offers a dedicated continuous performance management platform that deeply integrates OKR tracking with review cycles, pulse surveys, and coaching. It is a strong fit for organizations that need strategic alignment across the whole company, especially if you are replacing a legacy annual review system.

alternative

Officevibe

is primarily an employee engagement and pulse survey tool, and it includes lightweight goal setting but lacks the full OKR framework and structured review cycles found in Leapsome. It works well for teams that prioritize sentiment data over formal performance processes.

alternative

Weekdone

provides a straightforward OKR-focused tool with weekly progress updates and a simple continuous feedback loop, but it does not include traditional performance reviews or 360-degree feedback. It suits small to midsize teams that want a no-frills goal tracker with periodic check-ins.

alternative

Reflektive

combines continuous performance reviews, real-time feedback, and goal tracking in one platform, similar to Leapsome. It emphasizes making reviews more agile and feedback-driven, which appeals to People-ops teams that want to move away from rigid annual cycles.

Frequently asked questions

How do I decide between Leapsome and Betterworks for OKR tracking?
Both offer strong OKR integration with performance reviews. Choose Leapsome if you prefer a simpler, all-in-one interface with built-in survey tools. Choose Betterworks if your organization needs more strategic goal cascading, advanced analytics, and a proven track record in large enterprises.
Can Officevibe replace Leapsome for performance reviews?
Not fully. Officevibe is excellent for measuring engagement and gathering real-time feedback, but it does not provide structured review templates, 360-degree reviews, or formal OKR management. You would need to pair it with another tool if continuous performance reviews are a core requirement.
