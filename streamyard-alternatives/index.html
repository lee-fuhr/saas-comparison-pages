StreamYard is a popular live streaming studio, but you might be looking for other options that offer different features, pricing, or platform integrations. Whether you need higher video quality, more guest controls, or simpler multistreaming, there are strong alternatives. Below are five tools worth comparing for your live video workflow.

alternative

Restream

is a multistreaming platform that sends your live stream to over 30 destinations at once. It offers a built-in chat room, screen sharing, and prerecorded content uploads, making it ideal for marketers who want to reach multiple audiences simultaneously.

alternative

Riverside

focuses on high quality local recording and remote guest interviews. It records each participant’s audio and video locally for post production, which is perfect for creators who want to repurpose live content into podcasts or edited videos.

alternative

Melon

emphasizes a casual, beginner friendly experience with automatic lower thirds, dynamic backgrounds, and guest chat. It is designed to help solo creators or small teams look professional on camera quickly without complex setup.

alternative

BeLive

is a multistreaming tool with strong built in show templates, countdown timers, and branded overlays. It works well for marketers who want to stream to Facebook, YouTube, and LinkedIn while controlling guests and comments from one dashboard.

alternative

Ecamm Live

is a Mac only app that gives you granular control over scenes, transition effects, and audio routing. It is a great choice for advanced creators on Apple hardware who want to integrate with iOS devices or use virtual cameras for other apps.

How they compare

- If your main need is to reach many platforms at once, **Restream** offers the widest distribution. - For high quality recorded interviews that you can edit later, **Riverside** stands out. - If you want a simple no fuss studio for solo or small team streams, **Melon** is a good pick. - Marketers who like branded templates and ready to use show elements should try **BeLive**. - For Mac users who need deep control and iOS integration, **Ecamm Live** is the strongest alternative.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for streaming to multiple social platforms at the same time?
Restream is the top choice for multistreaming because it supports more than 30 destinations including Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Twitch, plus it includes a built in chat aggregator.
Do any of these alternatives work on both Windows and Mac?
Yes, Restream is a web based tool that works on any operating system. Riverside and BeLive also run on both Windows and Mac. Melon is web based too. Ecamm Live is exclusive to Mac.
