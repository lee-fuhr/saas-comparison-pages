You run an online store on Shopify and are ready to open a physical location. Shopify POS is the obvious choice because it syncs with your existing products and orders. But maybe you need lower transaction fees, better inventory management for high volume, or hardware that works offline. This guide covers four strong alternatives, Square POS, Lightspeed Retail, Vend, and NCR, so you can compare what each offers before making a switch., ## ALTERNATIVES

**Square POS**. Square is best known for its free starter plan and simple flat rate processing, which can save money on low ticket sales. It offers a full suite of hardware like card readers and terminals with no upfront cost. However, its inventory features are limited compared to dedicated retail systems, and it does not natively integrate with Shopify’s online store.

**Lightspeed Retail**. Lightspeed is built for growing retail businesses that need advanced inventory tracking, purchase orders, and supplier management. It provides robust reporting and multi location support, making it a good fit if you plan to open several stores. The downside is higher monthly fees and a steeper learning curve than simpler options like Square.

**Vend**. Vend is a cloud based POS designed for independent retailers who want easy till management and loyalty programs. It works well with a range of barcode scanners and receipt printers, and its offline mode keeps sales running even if the internet goes down. Vend does not offer its own payment processing, so you must use a third party processor, which can add complexity.

**NCR**. NCR (formerly NCR Counterpoint) is an enterprise grade solution for retailers with high transaction volumes or complex pricing needs. It handles serialized inventory, layaways, and consignment out of the box. The trade off is a higher upfront cost for hardware and software, and it requires more time to set up and train staff., ## COMPARISON SUMMARY

Shopify POS is the easiest choice because it connects directly to your online store. But if you want lower credit card fees, Square wins. If you need deep inventory control across multiple locations, Lightspeed or NCR are better options. Vend is a strong middle ground for single store retailers who want simplicity without being locked into a payment processor. Your decision should depend on your expected sales volume, budget for monthly fees, and how many physical locations you plan to run., ## FAQ

**Q: Can I still sell online if I switch from Shopify POS to one of these alternatives?**

A: Yes, but you will need a separate ecommerce platform. Square POS includes a basic online store, Lightspeed offers its own ecommerce integration, and Vend can connect to third party platforms. If you want to keep your Shopify online store, you can use a third party app to sync inventory between Shopify and the new POS, but it is not a direct integration like Shopify POS provides.

**Q: Which alternative is best for a small pop up shop or low volume store?**

A: Square POS is the most affordable for low volume because it has no monthly fees and a simple flat rate per transaction. You can start with just a card reader and a tablet. Vend also offers a low cost plan for one register, but it requires a separate payment processor. Lightspeed and NCR are better suited for stores processing dozens of transactions per day.
