Best GanttPRO alternatives

GanttPRO is a popular Gantt chart tool for project scheduling, but it may not fit every team’s workflow or budget. If you are looking for a more intuitive, simpler, or differently featured solution, several alternatives can help. This article covers three strong options – TeamGantt, Smartsheet, and Monday.com – each offering unique strengths for teams that need clear, visual scheduling.

alternative

TeamGantt

. TeamGantt is built specifically for Gantt chart fans who want drag and drop simplicity without extra clutter. Its clean interface lets you create and adjust task dependencies, assign resources, and share live project views with stakeholders in just a few clicks. It also includes a free plan for small teams, making it a low risk way to upgrade from GanttPRO.

alternative

Smartsheet

. Smartsheet combines the familiarity of a spreadsheet with powerful project scheduling features. You can create Gantt charts directly from your task lists, automate notifications and approvals, and connect with hundreds of other tools through its integrations. It works well for teams that need both a structured data view and a visual timeline, though it has a steeper learning curve than pure Gantt tools.

alternative

Monday.com

. Monday.com offers intuitive Gantt views as one of its many project management modes. You can build timelines, set dependencies, and track progress using its colorful, customizable boards. Its strength lies in flexibility – you can switch between Gantt, Kanban, calendar, and other views without losing data – making it ideal for teams that want one tool for all their planning needs.

How they compare

TeamGantt is the most focused Gantt-only alternative, excellent for teams that want the simplest possible timeline scheduling. Smartsheet is best for spreadsheet lovers who need both rows and a chart, but it requires more setup time. Monday.com offers the broadest feature set with multiple views and strong collaboration tools, though its Gantt features are slightly less specialized than TeamGantt. Pricing varies: TeamGantt has a free tier, Smartsheet charges per user with a free trial, and Monday.com also uses per seat pricing with a free limited plan. No single tool is perfect for everyone – choose based on how much spreadsheet work, team size, and budget flexibility you have.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is easiest to learn for a team switching from GanttPRO?
TeamGantt has the simplest interface and is designed to feel familiar to anyone who already uses Gantt charts. Most teams can start scheduling within an hour, and the free plan lets you test it before committing.
Can I import my existing GanttPRO projects into Smartsheet or Monday.com?
Yes, both Smartsheet and Monday.com support CSV imports. You can export your project data from GanttPRO as a CSV file, then map the columns to task name, dates, duration, and dependencies. Note that complex dependencies may need manual adjustment after import.
