BigMarker is a popular all-in-one webinar platform with built-in landing pages, but it may not fit every marketer’s budget or workflow. If you are looking for a tool that combines webinar hosting, registration pages, and marketing automation, you have several strong alternatives. This article compares Zoom Webinars, WebinarJam, Demio, and GoToWebinar to help you find the right fit for your campaigns.

alternative

Zoom Webinars

is a reliable choice for large audiences and enterprise teams, but it lacks a built-in landing page builder. You will need to use a separate tool like Leadpages or Unbounce to create registration pages and then connect them via API or embed codes. It works best if you already have a landing page solution in place.

alternative

WebinarJam

offers a full suite of webinar tools including an integrated landing page editor, email reminders, and live chat. Its landing pages are customizable and optimized for conversions, making it a strong all-in-one option for marketers who want to manage everything from one dashboard. It also supports automated webinars and live events.

alternative

Demio

is built for simplicity and ease of use, with native landing pages that are professional and mobile-friendly. You can design registration pages, send automated email sequences, and track attendee behavior without leaving the platform. It is ideal for marketers who want a seamless experience without complex setup.

alternative

GoToWebinar

is a veteran in the webinar space, known for stability and scalability, but its landing page capabilities are limited to basic customization. You can create registration pages with your branding, but advanced design options require third-party integrations. It remains a solid choice for businesses that prioritize reliability over built-in marketing features.

How they compare

If built-in landing pages are your top priority, WebinarJam and Demio lead the group. WebinarJam gives you more design control and includes automated webinar replays. Demio offers the simplest user experience with clean landing templates. Zoom Webinars and GoToWebinar require external landing page tools, but they excel in handling large attendee counts and enterprise integrations. BigMarker itself provides a competitive all-in-one package, so evaluate which alternative’s marketing features match your specific campaign needs.

Frequently asked questions

Does Zoom Webinars include built-in landing pages?
No, Zoom Webinars does not have a built-in landing page builder. You must use a separate tool to create registration pages and then integrate them with Zoom via API or embed codes.
Which alternative has the best built-in landing page builder for marketers?
WebinarJam and Demio both offer strong built-in landing page builders. WebinarJam provides more customization options, while Demio focuses on simplicity and mobile-friendly templates.
