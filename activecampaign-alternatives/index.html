Best ActiveCampaign alternatives

ActiveCampaign is a popular marketing automation platform, but its pricing can climb quickly as you add contacts or advanced features. For small and medium businesses that need powerful automation without enterprise level costs, several alternatives offer similar capabilities at more predictable prices. This guide compares the top options so you can find the right fit for your budget and growth stage.

alternative

HubSpot

is best for businesses that want a full CRM integrated with marketing tools. Its free tier handles basic automation and contact management, while paid plans start lower than ActiveCampaign for smaller lists. However, costs can rise as you add premium features like sequences or reporting.

Try HubSpot →
alternative

Klaviyo

focuses on ecommerce and uses customer purchase data to trigger highly personalized flows. It offers a generous free plan up to 250 contacts and predictable per contact pricing. If you run an online store, Klaviyo’s built in product recommendations and abandoned cart emails often outperform ActiveCampaign out of the box.

alternative

Drip

was built for ecommerce creators but now serves service businesses too. Its automation builder is visual and intuitive, similar to ActiveCampaign’s, and it includes lead scoring and email tagging. Drip’s pricing is based on contacts, and it tends to be cheaper than ActiveCampaign for lists under 10,000.

alternative

ConvertKit

targets creators, bloggers, and course sellers who need simple automation without a steep learning curve. Its visual automation is straightforward and includes subscriber tags and sequences. ConvertKit’s pricing is flat per contact count, so you avoid surprise fees for adding features like landing pages or email sequences.

Try ConvertKit →
alternative

Brevo

(formerly Sendinblue) combines email marketing, SMS, and a CRM at a low monthly cost. Its automation builder uses a drag and drop workflow and supports transactional emails. Brevo stands out for its pay as you go credit system, making it ideal for SMBs with variable sending volumes.

Try Brevo →

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my existing ActiveCampaign automations to another tool?
Yes, most alternatives offer import tools for contacts and tags. However, complex automation workflows with multiple conditional steps may need to be rebuilt manually. Check each tool’s migration guide or look for third party services that handle the transfer.
Which alternative is cheapest for a list of 2,000 contacts that sends weekly newsletters?
Brevo’s pay as you go credits often cost less than $30 per month for that volume, and ConvertKit charges $59/month. Klaviyo’s free plan covers up to 250 contacts, so you would need a paid plan around $45/month. For the lowest predictable cost, Brevo or ConvertKit are strong contenders.
