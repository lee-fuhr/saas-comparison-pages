Best Resolver alternatives

If your company handles both incident management and governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) tasks, you need a platform that connects workflows so nothing falls through the cracks. Resolver is built for this exact need. It combines incident response with risk registers, compliance tracking, and audit management in one interface. Instead of switching between separate tools, your teams can see how a security incident relates to a control failure or a regulatory requirement in real time. This article explains how Resolver compares to other popular options and answers common questions about choosing an incident management tool with GRC capabilities.

alternative

ServiceNow GRC

. ServiceNow GRC extends the well known IT service management platform into governance and compliance. It works well if you already use ServiceNow for IT incidents, but the GRC modules are often licensed separately and can become expensive. The setup also requires more configuration and a dedicated administrator, which may slow down teams that want a faster out of the box experience.

alternative

MetricStream

. MetricStream offers a comprehensive suite for enterprise risk management, compliance, and audit. It is a strong choice for large organizations with dedicated GRC teams and complex regulatory requirements. However, its incident management features are less integrated than Resolver, and the cost and implementation time can be higher for mid sized companies.

alternative

RSA Archer

. RSA Archer is a mature GRC platform widely used in financial services and other regulated industries. It provides deep customization for risk and compliance workflows. The trade off is that incident management is often handled by a separate module or integrated through custom connectors, which can create data silos and require extra effort to maintain a single view of operations.

alternative

LogicGate

. LogicGate focuses on risk and compliance process automation with a visual, no code approach. It is easier to adapt than some enterprise GRC tools, making it a good fit for teams that want to build their own workflows quickly. However, its incident management capabilities are lighter, typically designed for lower severity events rather than end to end incident handling with response playbooks.

How they compare

Resolver stands out for teams that need incident management and GRC together without stitching multiple tools. It gives you a single record from the moment an incident is reported through root cause analysis, risk scoring, and compliance reporting. ServiceNow GRC is best if you already run on ServiceNow for other IT processes and have budget and staff for customization. MetricStream and RSA Archer are better for large enterprises with dedicated GRC departments and highly complex regulatory environments. LogicGate suits teams that want flexible risk automation but can supplement incident response elsewhere. Resolver offers the most balanced blend of out of the box incident management and GRC for mid market and growing companies.
