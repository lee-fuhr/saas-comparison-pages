Best Vidyard alternatives

Vidyard is a solid video messaging platform for sales and marketing teams, but it is not the only option. Some teams find Vidyard too expensive for their budget, too complex for quick daily use, or lacking in specific features like editing or advanced analytics. If you are looking for a tool that better fits your workflow, exploring alternatives can help you save money, save time, or get more from your video strategy. This guide covers five popular options: Loom, Wistia, Vimeo, Veed, and Sendspark.

alternative

Loom

. Loom is a straightforward video messaging tool that lets you record your screen, camera, or both and share the video instantly with a link. It integrates well with sales tools like Salesforce, Slack, and Gmail, making it easy for teams to send personalized videos. Loom’s free tier is generous, but its advanced features like custom branding and viewer analytics require a paid plan.

alternative

Wistia

. Wistia is a video hosting platform built for marketers, offering detailed analytics, lead capture forms, and customizable video players. It is ideal for teams that want to embed videos on their website and track individual viewer behavior over time. Wistia does not have a built-in screen recorder like Vidyard, but its marketing-focused features make it a strong choice for content-driven campaigns.

Try Wistia →
alternative

Vimeo

. Vimeo provides professional video hosting, live streaming, and recording tools with a clean ad-free player. It offers collaboration features and customizable video pages, which can be useful for sales presentations and marketing collateral. Vimeo’s pricing is tiered, and its recording capabilities are less integrated into daily messaging workflows compared to Vidyard.

alternative

Veed

. Veed is an online video editor that also includes screen recording and webcam capture. It is great for sales and marketing teams who need to edit videos quickly, add subtitles, or create polished content without installing software. Veed’s free plan adds a watermark, and the platform focuses more on editing than on real-time video messaging or CRM integrations.

alternative

Sendspark

. Sendspark is a video messaging platform designed specifically for sales teams, with features like personalized video landing pages, team templates, and automated follow-ups. It integrates with common CRM and email tools such as HubSpot and Outreach, making it easy to send custom videos at scale. Sendspark offers a free trial, but its advanced automation and analytics features come at a higher price point.

Try Sendspark →

How they compare

Loom and Sendspark are the closest alternatives for sales teams that rely on quick one-to-one video messaging, with Loom offering a strong free tier and Sendspark providing more customization for team workflows. Wistia and Vimeo are better suited for marketing teams that need advanced video hosting, analytics, and lead generation tools. Veed fills a niche for teams that want to edit and polish their videos before sharing. Pricing varies widely, with Loom having a generous free plan and Wistia and Vimeo charging more for marketing-grade features.

Frequently asked questions

Which Vidyard alternative is best for sales teams who send a lot of one-to-one videos?
Loom and Sendspark are both excellent choices. Loom is easier to adopt and has strong free tier support, while Sendspark offers more customization for team templates and landing pages. If you need deep CRM integrations, Sendspark may be a better fit.
Are there any free alternatives to Vidyard for video messaging?
Yes, Loom offers a free plan that allows you to record and share videos with basic features. Veed also has a free version, but it adds a watermark to your videos. Free plans typically have limits on video length, storage, or the number of team members, so check those limits before you commit.
