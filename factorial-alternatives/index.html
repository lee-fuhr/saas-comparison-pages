If you are evaluating Factorial for your European company’s human resource needs, you likely value its all-in-one approach to HR, payroll, and compliance. However, different business sizes, budgets, and local requirements may mean another tool fits better. Below we explore four strong alternatives to Factorial, each with its own strengths for European operations.

alternative

BambooHR

. Built for small and medium businesses, BambooHR offers clean employee management, time-off tracking, and reporting. It integrates well with popular payroll providers but does not include native payroll for most European countries, so you typically need a separate solution for local compliance.

alternative

Zenefits

. Zenefits provides a broad HR platform with benefits administration, payroll, and compliance support. While it is heavily focused on the US market, its core features like employee records and time tracking can work for European teams if you pair it with a local payroll partner.

alternative

Gusto HR

. Gusto is known for straightforward payroll and benefits, especially for US-based companies. For European firms, its main limitation is the lack of built-in multi-country payroll, but it can still handle basic HR tasks such as onboarding and document storage for distributed teams.

alternative

Namely

. Namely combines HR, payroll, and talent management in a single interface aimed at mid-sized companies. It offers good customization for performance reviews and time tracking, but its payroll is primarily US-centric and may require additional setup for European compliance.

How they compare

Factorial leads for European-specific compliance features and a single platform for payroll, time tracking, and document management. BambooHR excels in user experience and integrations but needs a separate payroll. Zenefits and Gusto are better suited for US-focused teams, while Namely offers robust HR tools but requires extra effort for European regulations. Your choice depends on whether all-in-one European compliance or integration flexibility matters most.

Frequently asked questions

Do any of these alternatives offer native payroll for multiple European countries?
Factorial is the strongest option here because it includes country-specific payroll for several European nations. BambooHR, Zenefits, Gusto, and Namely generally rely on third-party payroll partners or are designed for the US market, so you would need to arrange local payroll separately.
Which alternative is most similar to Factorial’s feature set?
BambooHR comes closest in terms of core HR features like time-off tracking, employee records, and reporting. However, it lacks native payroll and compliance for Europe, so you would need to add a local payroll tool to match Factorial’s all-in-one offering.
