Best Sharetribe alternatives

If you are building a peer-to-peer marketplace but don’t have a big development team, Sharetribe is a popular starting point. It offers a no-code builder and hosting, so you can launch quickly. But maybe you need more customization, lower transaction fees, or a different pricing model. Fortunately, several alternatives can help you launch without writing lots of code. Below we compare seven platforms that give creators like you the tools to run your own marketplace.

alternative

Arcadier

. Arcadier is a cloud-based marketplace builder that lets you set up a multi-vendor platform in minutes. It includes features like payment processing and a mobile-ready checkout, and it works for physical goods, services, or rentals. You can start with a free trial, but be prepared for transaction fees on lower plans.

alternative

Near Me

. Near Me specializes in location-based peer-to-peer marketplaces, such as service bookings, rentals, or local events. It provides a drag-and-drop page builder and built-in payment gateways, and it handles bookings and availability calendars out of the box. The platform is designed for non-technical founders who want a hyper-local experience.

alternative

Kreezalid

. Kreezalid offers a white-label marketplace solution with a focus on speed of setup and a clean storefront. You get tools for vendor management, commission rules, and integrated payments, and you can customize the look without coding. It is a good fit if you want to launch a simple goods or services marketplace quickly.

alternative

Marketplacer

. Marketplacer is a more enterprise-focused platform, but it still offers a no-code dashboard for managing vendors and products. It supports multiple payment methods and gives you control over commission structures and shipping rules. Because it targets larger operations, the pricing is higher, and you may need to commit to a longer contract.

alternative

Mirakl

. Mirakl powers many large B2B and B2C marketplaces and is built for scale rather than quick DIY launches. It provides advanced features like seller onboarding, catalog management, and fulfillment connectors. For creators without developer resources, Mirakl might be too complex and expensive unless you have a big budget and a dedicated admin.

alternative

Yo!Kart

. Yo!Kart is a self-hosted marketplace script that you install on your own server. It gives you full control over customization and data, but you will need some technical know-how or a hosting provider that handles installation. It works well for physical goods, digital products, and services, and the one-time license fee can be cost-effective over time.

alternative

SpurtCommerce

. SpurtCommerce is a Laravel-based marketplace platform that you can either host yourself or use their cloud version. It includes vendor dashboards, commission settings, and multi-currency support. The platform is developer-friendly, but the no-code experience is limited compared to fully hosted alternatives.

How they compare

Sharetribe is the easiest to start with for truly non-technical founders. Arcadier and Near Me also require little to no coding, while Kreezalid offers a good balance of simplicity and control. Marketplacer and Mirakl are better suited for established businesses that can afford higher costs and more complex setups. Yo!Kart and SpurtCommerce give you full ownership but demand more technical effort. Consider your budget, desired level of customization, and how much time you want to spend on setup when choosing.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the easiest to use if I have zero coding experience?
Arcadier and Near Me both offer drag-and-drop builders and hosted tools so you can launch quickly without touching any code. Sharetribe is also easy, but if you want a location-focused marketplace, Near Me is a strong choice.
Can I switch from Sharetribe to another platform later?
Yes, but migration depends on the data you need to move (listings, user accounts, orders). Most platforms offer CSV import tools for products and users, but custom data like reviews or referral programs may need manual handling. Plan ahead and check each platform’s import capabilities.
