If you are an independent hotelier exploring cloud-based property management systems, you may have come across RMS Cloud. It offers solid functionality for revenue management and operations, but its pricing model and feature set may not suit every small property. Some users find RMS Cloud’s pricing rigid or lack the integrations they need. That is why many hoteliers look for alternatives that offer more flexible pricing, simpler onboarding, or better mobile tools. In this article, we compare three popular cloud-based PMS options: Cloudbeds, Mews, and Hotelogix. Each of these caters to independent hotels and offers different pricing approaches.

alternative

Cloudbeds

. Cloudbeds provides a unified platform that combines PMS, channel manager, and booking engine. Its pricing is modular, so you pay only for the features you need, and it offers a free basic plan for very small properties. Cloudbeds is known for strong integrations and a user-friendly interface, making it a good fit for independent hotels that want to scale.

alternative

Mews

. Mews is a modern, cloud-native PMS built for automation and guest experience. It uses a flexible per-reservation pricing model rather than a flat monthly fee, which can be attractive for smaller hotels with variable occupancy. Mews excels in contactless check-in and real-time data reporting, but it may require a longer setup process compared to simpler tools.

alternative

Hotelogix

. Hotelogix is a cost-effective cloud PMS designed specifically for independent hotels and small chains. It offers transparent pricing with no hidden fees and includes essential modules like front desk, housekeeping, and channel management in its base plans. Hotelogix is ideal for budget-conscious operators who still want robust automation and 24/7 support.

How they compare

All three alternatives provide cloud-based access, mobile apps, and channel manager integrations, which are key for independent hoteliers. Cloudbeds stands out for its scalable modular pricing and free tier. Mews offers a unique per-reservation fee model that aligns costs with revenue. Hotelogix delivers a straightforward all-in-one package at a lower price point. RMS Cloud users who find its pricing inflexible or its interface outdated may prefer one of these options, depending on their property size, technical comfort level, and need for automation.
