If you are a sales team leader looking for conversation intelligence software, Chorus.ai is a popular choice. But its pricing can be a hurdle for smaller teams or budget-conscious buyers. Fortunately, there are several affordable alternatives that still offer call recording, transcription, and deal coaching features. Below we compare five tools that give you similar insights without the enterprise price tag.

alternative

Gong

. Gong is the most direct competitor to Chorus, offering rich analytics and revenue intelligence. It is not always cheaper than Chorus, but its robust deal board and pipeline insights can justify the cost for teams that need deep forecasting. Evaluate your needs carefully before assuming Gong is the low-cost option.

Try Gong →
alternative

Wingman

. Wingman focuses on real-time coaching and live call alerts. It provides a free tier with limited recordings, making it a good entry point for small sales teams. Paid plans scale affordably, and the tool integrates easily with popular CRMs and dialers.

Try Wingman →
alternative

Avoma

. Avoma combines conversation intelligence with meeting assistant and note-taking features. It offers flexible pricing tiers, including a free plan with basic transcription. Avoma is especially useful for teams that want both call analysis and automated meeting summaries in one platform.

alternative

Fireflies

. Fireflies transcribes and summarizes calls from multiple platforms like Zoom, Teams, and RingCentral. It has a generous free plan and low-cost paid options. The searchable transcript library helps reps quickly find key moments without listening to entire recordings.

Try Fireflies →
alternative

Otter.ai

. Otter.ai is known for its accurate real-time transcription and collaboration features. While it was built more for general meetings, sales teams can use it to record calls and share highlights with colleagues. Its business plan is very budget-friendly for teams that only need basic recording and transcription.
