Marketing and creative teams often choose Wrike for project management because of its robust reporting and workload views. But as your team grows or your workflows become more visual, you might find Wrike complex or expensive. This guide covers five strong Wrike alternatives that better suit creative workflows, collaboration, and budget.

alternative

Asana

focuses on clean task management and timeline views, making it a great fit for marketing teams that plan campaigns in phases. Its custom fields and templates let you track content production from draft to publish without overwhelming your team.

alternative

Monday.com

offers highly visual boards that resemble spreadsheets, ideal for creative teams who want to see progress at a glance. Its automations and integrations with tools like Slack and Adobe Creative Cloud help reduce repetitive status updates.

Try Monday.com →
alternative

ClickUp

stands out as an all-in-one platform with docs, goals, and whiteboards built in. Marketing teams can use its flexible views (list, board, calendar, Gantt) to manage both short term tasks and long term editorial calendars in one place.

Try ClickUp →
alternative

Smartsheet

feels like a spreadsheet but works like a project manager, making it useful for teams that need detailed task tracking and resource management. Its sheet based structure works well for complex production schedules and budget tracking common in larger creative departments.

alternative

Teamwork

is designed for client facing teams and includes built in time tracking and billing. Creative agencies often prefer it because you can organize projects by client and see how much time each task takes.

How they compare

Wrike is powerful but can feel clunky for visual creative work. Asana and Monday.com offer simpler interfaces and better collaboration tools. ClickUp gives you the most features for the price. Smartsheet suits data heavy teams, while Teamwork excels for agencies billing by the hour. Your choice depends on whether you need simplicity (Asana), visuals (Monday.com), all in one (ClickUp), spreadsheets (Smartsheet), or client management (Teamwork).

Frequently asked questions

Which Wrike alternative is best for a small marketing team?
Asana or ClickUp both work well for small teams. Asana is easier to learn, while ClickUp offers more features for the same price.
Can I migrate my existing Wrike data to these tools?
Yes, most alternatives offer import tools for CSV or direct integrations with Wrike. Check each tool’s import guide before switching.
