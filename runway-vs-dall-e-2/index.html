If you are an AI artist deciding between Runway and DALL·E 2, the choice mostly comes down to your need for video versus still images. Runway is built for generative video workflows, letting you create, edit, and extend clips with tools like Gen-2 and frame interpolation. DALL·E 2 is a pure image generator that excels at producing high quality, photorealistic visuals from text prompts. Understanding how each fits into your creative pipeline will help you pick the right tool for each project.

alternative

DALL·E 2

. DALL·E 2 is an image generation model by OpenAI that creates detailed, high resolution images from natural language descriptions. It supports inpainting and outpainting, allowing you to edit or extend an existing image. Its strength lies in photorealism and composition, but it cannot generate video or animation sequences.

**Key differences**: Runway gives you time based control (duration, motion, camera moves) while DALL·E 2 gives you pixel perfect stills. Both can generate images from prompts, but Runway’s strength is in sequences and DALL·E 2’s is in standalone visuals. Runway also includes a full video editor, whereas DALL·E 2 is a standalone generation tool.
