Best Rubrik alternatives

If your company is looking for a single platform that handles backup, recovery, and data management across on-premises and cloud environments, Rubrik is a strong choice. It provides a converged solution that reduces complexity by replacing multiple legacy tools with one unified system. Rubrik integrates with major cloud providers and offers built-in ransomware detection, making it easier to protect and manage your data from a central interface.

alternative

Zerto

focuses on continuous replication and disaster recovery for virtualized and cloud workloads. It offers near-zero recovery point objectives (RPOs) and fast failover, which is ideal for organizations that prioritize minimal data loss. However, Zerto is more of a disaster recovery tool than a full data management platform, so you may need additional backup software for long-term retention and archiving.

alternative

Veeam

is a widely used backup and recovery solution that supports virtual, physical, and cloud environments. It provides strong backup capabilities and a large ecosystem of integrations, but achieving a fully converged platform often requires additional Veeam components or third-party tools. This can add complexity compared to Rubrik’s all-in-one approach.

alternative

Druva

is a cloud-native data protection platform that eliminates on-premises infrastructure. It delivers backup, disaster recovery, and governance from a single SaaS console. While Druva simplifies management and reduces hardware costs, it may not fit organizations that prefer to keep some data on-premises or need tight integration with on-site workloads.

alternative

Acronis

offers a mix of backup, disaster recovery, and cybersecurity features in a single product. It is popular among small and medium businesses due to its ease of use and pricing. However, for larger enterprises seeking a converged platform with advanced automation and orchestration, Acronis may lack the depth and scalability that Rubrik provides.
