Best Drata alternatives

If your high-growth startup is evaluating compliance automation tools, you have likely come across Drata. Drata is a popular platform that connects to your cloud services and helps you achieve SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other certifications through continuous monitoring. However, Drata may not fit every budget, team size, or compliance requirement. This article covers the main alternatives to Drata, including Vanta, SecureFrame, Tugboat Logic, and TrustArc, so you can choose the solution that best matches your company’s needs.

alternative

Vanta

is the most well known alternative to Drata and offers a similar automated evidence collection model. It supports SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, and other frameworks, with a user friendly interface and a large integration library. Vanta tends to be slightly more affordable than Drata for early stage startups, but the core feature set is comparable.

alternative

SecureFrame

focuses on simplicity and affordability, making it a good fit for startups that need to pass an audit without extensive customization. It provides automated evidence collection, policy templates, and a straightforward workflow for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and PCI DSS. SecureFrame’s lower price point often appeals to bootstrapped teams, though its integration list is smaller than Drata or Vanta.

alternative

Tugboat Logic

is built around a compliance playbook that guides you step by step through framework requirements. It offers automated evidence collection, risk assessment tools, and vendor management features. Tugboat Logic is known for its strong customer support and educational resources, which can help startups that are new to compliance.

alternative

TrustArc

is more enterprise oriented and covers a broad range of privacy and security frameworks, including GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2. It provides automated assessments, policy management, and data mapping. TrustArc can be a good option if your startup deals heavily with privacy regulations, but its complexity and cost are higher than the other alternatives listed here.

How they compare

Drata and Vanta are the two most similar tools, both offering deep automation and a wide integration ecosystem. SecureFrame and Tugboat Logic are more budget friendly and simpler to start with, while TrustArc is best for companies that need advanced privacy compliance. For most high-growth startups focused on SOC 2 or ISO 27001, Vanta is the most direct Drata alternative. If you need to keep costs low, consider SecureFrame or Tugboat Logic. If privacy regulations are your main concern, evaluate TrustArc.
