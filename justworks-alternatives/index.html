If your startup is evaluating PEO services and compliance support, Justworks is a popular choice. But it may not fit every team’s budget, feature needs, or growth stage. Below you will find four strong alternatives: Gusto, Paylocity, TriNet, and ADP. Each brings different strengths for startups that need payroll, benefits, HR tools, and regulatory help.

alternative

Gusto

This platform is built for small to midsize businesses and offers a clean, user friendly interface. It includes payroll, benefits administration, and basic compliance alerts. Gusto works well for early stage startups that want an all in one HR solution without the full PEO model.

alternative

Paylocity

Paylocity provides a more customizable platform with advanced HR analytics and time tracking. It is a good fit for startups that have outgrown entry level systems and need deeper workforce management. The compliance features cover tax filing, labor law posters, and employee handbooks.

alternative

TriNet

TriNet is a full service PEO that handles payroll, benefits, risk management, and compliance for growing companies. It is ideal for startups that want to outsource most HR responsibilities. TriNet also offers industry specific packages for tech, life sciences, and professional services.

alternative

ADP

ADP offers several service tiers including a PEO option called ADP TotalSource. It combines payroll, benefits, and compliance support with a large network of HR experts. This is a solid choice for startups that need scale, dedicated support, and broad integration options.

How they compare

Justworks is a straightforward PEO that simplifies benefits and compliance for startups. Gusto is simpler and cheaper but lacks full PEO status in some states. Paylocity offers more customization but less hands on compliance guidance. TriNet provides comprehensive PEO services with industry focus. ADP delivers enterprise level support and many add on modules. The best choice depends on your startup’s headcount, budget, and how much HR work you want to outsource.

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch from Justworks to one of these alternatives without losing employee benefits?
Yes, but timing is important. Most benefits like health insurance renew on specific dates. Work with the new provider and your current broker to coordinate a smooth transition. You may need to offer a special enrollment period.
Do any of these alternatives offer compliance support for remote teams in multiple states?
Yes. TriNet and ADP have strong interstate compliance tools. Gusto and Paylocity also handle multi state payroll tax registration. However, the level of hands on guidance varies. Check if the provider assigns a dedicated compliance specialist for your account.
