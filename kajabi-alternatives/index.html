Best Kajabi alternatives

Kajabi is a powerful all-in-one platform for online course creators, but its premium pricing can be a barrier. If you are looking for a cheaper solution or a tool that excels in one specific area like course hosting, email marketing, or sales funnels, several alternatives offer excellent value. Below we compare five popular platforms that can replace or complement Kajabi without the high monthly cost.

alternative

Teachable

. Teachable is a leading course platform with robust quiz and lecture features, plus a built-in payment system. It lacks Kajabi’s full marketing suite but costs less and supports unlimited students on its basic plan. For creators who prioritize course delivery over advanced funnels, Teachable is a solid choice.

Try Teachable →
alternative

Thinkific

. Thinkific offers a free plan and transparent pricing with no transaction fees on higher tiers. It provides strong course creation tools, quizzes, and a simple website builder, but its email marketing is more limited than Kajabi. This platform works well for course creators who want to keep expenses low while still selling directly to students.

Try Thinkific →
alternative

Podia

. Podia is an all-in-one platform that includes courses, digital downloads, memberships, and email marketing at a lower price than Kajabi. Its interface is clean and beginner friendly, though it has fewer automation and funnel options. Podia is ideal if you need a simple, affordable way to sell digital products without extra complexity.

Try Podia →
alternative

Systeme.io

. Systeme.io is an ultra affordable all-in-one platform with email marketing, sales funnels, and course hosting all in one place. It offers a generous free plan and low cost paid plans, making it one of the cheapest Kajabi replacements available. However, its course features are less polished than dedicated platforms, so it suits creators who want a budget friendly marketing and course hybrid.

alternative

New Zenler

. New Zenler provides unlimited courses, students, and hosting on a single low price plan. It includes a built-in community forum and live class capability, which Kajabi charges extra for. While its design options are not as modern as Kajabi, New Zenler is a strong value pick for creators who want all features without per‑student fees.
