Best Moz alternatives

If you have been using Moz and are now exploring other options, you are not alone. Many SEOs switch tools because of budget changes, feature gaps, or a desire for fresher data. This article compares four popular keyword and backlink tools and one crawler that can complement or replace Moz’s main offerings. We focus on Ahrefs, Semrush, SE Ranking, Mangools, and Screaming Frog, highlighting what each does better than Moz and where they fall short.

alternative

Ahrefs

Ahrefs is widely considered the strongest competitor for backlink analysis. Its index is larger than Moz’s and updates more frequently, making it ideal for link building audits and competitor research. The keyword explorer is also robust, though its content gap and rank tracker features require a higher tier plan to be fully useful.

alternative

Semrush

Semrush is an all-in-one platform that excels at keyword research and competitive analysis. It offers a broader set of tools than Moz, including paid ad research, social media management, and a detailed site audit. The learning curve is steeper, but for agencies or in-house teams that need more than just backlinks and keywords, Semrush provides the most complete alternative.

Try Semrush →
alternative

SE Ranking

SE Ranking is a budget-friendly option that still covers the core Moz features: rank tracking, keyword research, and backlink monitoring. Its data freshness is comparable to Moz’s, and it adds a useful website audit tool with clear recommendations. It is a good choice for smaller teams or solo practitioners who want a simplified interface without losing essential functionality.

alternative

Mangools

Mangools is a lightweight, visual toolset that is especially beginner friendly. Its Keyword Magic tool and Domain Overview give quick insights without the noise of larger platforms. Backlink data is less comprehensive than Moz’s, but for basic link profile checks and keyword discovery on a tight budget, Mangools offers a clean experience.

Try Mangools →
alternative

Screaming Frog

Screaming Frog is not a direct replacement for Moz’s keyword or backlink tools, but it complements them perfectly. This website crawler finds technical SEO issues, duplicate content, and page title problems that Moz’s site audit may miss. It is free for up to 500 URLs, making it an essential addition to any SEO toolkit when paired with a keyword or backlink tool.

How they compare

Moz is a solid, user-friendly tool for keyword research and backlink analysis, but its data index is smaller and updates slower than Ahrefs or Semrush. If backlink depth is your priority, choose Ahrefs. If you need an all-in-one marketing suite, choose Semrush. For tight budgets, SE Ranking offers a balanced feature set, and Mangools provides a simple interface. For technical audits, add Screaming Frog. No single tool beats Moz in every category, so consider your specific needs before switching.

Frequently asked questions

How does Moz’s data freshness compare to Ahrefs and Semrush?
Moz updates its backlink index roughly once a month, while Ahrefs updates daily and Semrush updates several times a week. If you need real-time or near-real-time data for competitive analysis, Ahrefs or Semrush are better choices.
Can I use Moz alongside these alternatives?
Yes, many SEOs keep Moz for its unique metrics like Domain Authority and Spam Score, while using Ahrefs or Semrush for deeper backlink data and keyword research. Combining tools often gives you the most complete picture.
