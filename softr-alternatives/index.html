Best Softr alternatives

If you are a SaaS founder building client portals on top of Airtable, you have likely already evaluated Softr. It offers a quick way to turn Airtable bases into web apps. But as your portal requirements grow (more complex logic, deeper integrations, or a need for custom branding), you may start looking for Softr alternatives. This article compares three strong contenders: Bubble, Glide, and Buildship. Each brings a different balance of speed, flexibility, and cost. Understanding their strengths will help you pick the right platform for your next iteration.

alternative

Bubble

. Bubble is a full stack visual programming platform that gives you complete control over the front end and back end. You can build complex client portals with custom workflows, roles, and databases (or sync with Airtable via its API). The trade off is a steeper learning curve and more development time compared to Softr.

alternative

Glide

. Glide is a no code platform focused on mobile friendly data apps, originally built on Google Sheets but now supporting Airtable natively. It excels at simple, fast prototypes and lightweight client portals. However, advanced logic, custom pricing tiers, and deeply integrated backend workflows can become difficult to maintain at scale.

alternative

Buildship

. Buildship is a low code tool that turns Airtable into a custom backend with visual workflows and API endpoints. It is not a front end builder itself, but it pairs well with front ends like Bubble or Softr when you need to automate complex processes (e.g., user signups, payment flows, data transformations) that Airtable alone cannot handle.

How they compare

Softr is best when you need a rapid, Airtable native solution with minimal coding. Bubble offers the most flexibility but requires more upfront investment in learning and building. Glide is ideal for simple, fast mobile portals but limits advanced customization. Buildship complements Airtable by adding backend automation but must be combined with a separate front end tool. Your choice depends on how much logic you need, your timeline, and your willingness to trade speed for control.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my Softr client portal to Bubble or Glide later?
Yes, but it is not a one click process. You will need to rebuild your front end screens and workflows in the new tool. Your Airtable data can stay in place, but you may need to adjust field types or relationship setups to match the new platform’s expectations. Plan for a full migration rather than a simple import.
Which alternative is best for a client portal that must handle user payments and invoicing?
Bubble is the strongest choice for custom payment and invoicing logic because you can integrate directly with Stripe or other gateways and build your own billing flows. Glide offers a limited payment integration, and Buildship would require you to pair it with a separate front end. If your needs are simple, Glide may work, but for full control Bubble is recommended.
