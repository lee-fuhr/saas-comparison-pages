Gong is a powerful tool for call recording and revenue intelligence, but its premium pricing can strain your budget. If you are a sales leader looking for similar insights without the high cost, there are several robust alternatives that deliver strong analytics, conversation intelligence, and coaching features. This article will walk you through the top options and help you choose the right fit for your team.

alternative

Chorus

. Chorus (now part of ZoomInfo) offers advanced call recording, transcription, and deal tracking with a focus on pipeline analysis. It is a direct competitor to Gong and often comes at a lower price point, though you may need to negotiate for custom quotes based on team size.

alternative

Clari

. Clari specializes in revenue intelligence and forecasting rather than pure call recording. It pulls data from your CRM and conversations to give sales leaders a clear view of pipeline health, but it does not offer the same depth of call-level coaching as Gong.

Try Clari →
alternative

Wingman

. Wingman provides affordable call recording, transcription, and AI-powered scoring with a strong emphasis on coaching and deal intelligence. Its pricing is more accessible for mid-market teams, and it integrates easily with popular CRMs and dialers.

alternative

Salesloft

. Salesloft is primarily a sales engagement platform that includes call recording and conversation intelligence as part of its broader toolkit. It works well for teams that need both outreach management and some revenue insights, but its call analysis features are less in-depth than Gong’s.

alternative

Avoma

. Avoma combines meeting recording, note-taking, and revenue intelligence with built-in collaboration for sales and customer success teams. It offers a simpler interface and lower starting price than Gong, making it a strong choice for small to mid-sized sales organizations.

How they compare

All five alternatives provide call recording and some form of revenue intelligence, but they differ in focus and pricing. Chorus and Wingman are the most direct Gong competitors for conversation analytics with more flexible pricing. Clari excels at forecasting but lacks deep call coaching. Salesloft and Avoma are better suited for teams that want recording plus broader platform features. None of these tools offer Gong’s full range of AI-generated deal alerts and competitive intelligence out of the box, but they can cover 80% of use cases at a lower cost.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the easiest to implement for a small team?
Wingman and Avoma have the fastest onboarding times and simplest setups. They require minimal IT support and integrate with common CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot within a few days.
Do any of these tools offer a free trial or a pay-as-you-go plan?
Yes, most of these tools offer free trials ranging from 7 to 14 days. Wingman and Avoma also have self-serve plans with monthly pricing, while Chorus and Salesloft typically require a sales conversation for pricing.
