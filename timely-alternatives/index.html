Best Timely alternatives

If you are a consultant who needs to track billable hours without constant manual entry, Timely is a popular choice because it uses AI to automatically record your activities. But you might be looking for other options that offer similar automation, different pricing, or better integrations. This article compares three top alternatives (Harvest, Toggl, Clockify) so you can decide which one fits your workflow.

alternative

Harvest

. Harvest combines time tracking with simple invoicing and expense management, but it lacks the true AI-driven automatic logging that Timely offers. You still need to start and stop timers or enter time manually, though its browser and desktop extensions make it fast. It is a good fit if you want an all-in-one tool for projects and billing.

alternative

Toggl

. Toggl is known for its clean interface and one-click timers, but again it does not automatically capture your computer activity like Timely does. It offers a feature called Toggl Track Assistant that can suggest time entries based on calendar events, but it is not a true AI background tracker. Consultants who prefer manual control and detailed reports often choose Toggl.

alternative

Clockify

. Clockify is a free time tracker that works well for teams on a budget, yet it also relies on manual timer starts or manual entry rather than automatic logging. It provides a browser extension and a pomodoro mode, but there is no AI that records your work without you triggering it. Clockify is best for cost-conscious consultants who do not mind a little extra manual work.

How they compare

Timely stands out for its AI-powered automatic time logging, which is ideal for consultants who want to avoid stopping and starting timers. Harvest is stronger for invoicing and expense tracking but requires manual input. Toggl offers a polished manual timer with good integrations and reporting, while Clockify is the most affordable option with similar manual tracking. If automatic logging is your priority, Timely or a tool like it is your best bet. If you can live with manual timers, any of the three alternatives will serve you well.

Frequently asked questions

Can Harvest, Toggl, or Clockify automatically log my time like Timely?
No, none of these three alternatives have the same AI-driven automatic background tracking that Timely offers. They all require you to start a timer or manually add time entries. Some may have calendar-based suggestions, but they do not passively record your computer activity.
Which alternative is best for a consultant who bills by the hour and needs simple invoicing?
Harvest is the strongest choice if you need both time tracking and invoicing in one tool. Toggl has some invoicing features through third-party integrations, and Clockify offers basic invoicing on paid plans. If invoicing is not critical, Toggl or Clockify may be more flexible.
