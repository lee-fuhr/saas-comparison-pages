When I Work is a popular choice for scheduling hourly staff, but it may not fit every small business. You might want a simpler interface, lower pricing, or extra features like time tracking or team communication. Here are four strong alternatives that offer similar scheduling tools with different strengths.

alternative

Deputy

Deputy lets you build and share schedules with a simple drag and drop interface. It also tracks time and attendance, which makes payroll easier. Small teams like its clean design and easy to use mobile app. **7shifts** 7shifts is built for restaurants and shift based teams, with features like tip tracking and sales integration. It focuses on communication, letting staff swap shifts and message managers directly. This tool works well if your team handles busy or changing schedules.

Homebase

Homebase offers free scheduling for up to one location, plus paid plans with time clocks and hiring tools. It is beginner friendly and includes a mobile app for staff to pick up or trade shifts. Many small businesses start here because it is affordable and simple.

alternative

Shiftboard

Shiftboard is designed for larger organizations with complex needs, like managing multiple roles, locations, or certifications. It automates shift filling and helps avoid scheduling conflicts. Smaller businesses may find it more powerful than needed, but it supports growing teams well.

How they compare

When choosing a replacement for When I Work, think about your team size and must have features. Deputy and 7shifts offer strong scheduling and time tracking, while Homebase provides a free entry point for one location. Shiftboard is better suited for businesses that manage many employees across different roles or skills. Test free trials to see which interface your team finds easiest to use.

Frequently asked questions

Can I transfer my schedule data from When I Work to another tool?
Most alternatives let you export your schedule as a CSV file from When I Work and then import it into their system. Check each tool’s help page for specific steps, as the process can vary.
Which alternative is best for a small cafe with 10 employees?
Homebase is a popular choice because its free plan covers one location and basic scheduling for small teams. 7shifts is also a good fit for cafes that need tip tracking and shift swapping features.
