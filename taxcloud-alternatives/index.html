If you are a small business owner in the US, calculating sales tax can feel overwhelming, especially when you want a free solution. TaxJar once offered a free tier, but that plan is no longer available. TaxCloud is a strong free alternative that gives you accurate sales tax calculation for up to 100 transactions per month. This article compares TaxCloud with other popular tools so you can decide which one fits your budget and needs.

alternative

Avalara

. Avalara is a comprehensive, enterprise-grade sales tax solution that handles calculation, filing, and compliance. It offers a free trial but does not have a permanent free tier. Small businesses with very low volume may find Avalara’s pricing too high compared to free options like TaxCloud.

alternative

TaxJar

. TaxJar (now part of Stripe) provides robust sales tax automation including calculation, reporting, and filing. Its free plan was discontinued, and current plans start at around $19 per month. For businesses that need a simple free tool, TaxCloud is a more budget-friendly choice.

alternative

Vertex

. Vertex targets mid-sized to large companies with complex tax needs across multiple jurisdictions. It offers a free trial but no ongoing free plan. Small businesses with straightforward sales tax requirements will likely find Vertex’s features excessive and its cost prohibitive.

alternative

Sovos

. Sovos focuses on enterprise compliance including sales tax, VAT, and other regulatory filings. It does not have a free tier and its pricing is geared toward high-volume businesses. For a small US business seeking free calculation, Sovos is not a practical option.

How they compare

All five tools can calculate sales tax, but only TaxCloud offers a free tier for up to 100 transactions per month. TaxJar, Avalara, Vertex, and Sovos require a paid subscription for ongoing use. TaxCloud is the best fit for small businesses that want free, reliable sales tax calculation without committing to a monthly fee. If you need additional features like automated filing or handle more than 100 transactions, you may need to upgrade to a paid plan with TaxCloud or consider a paid alternative.

Frequently asked questions

Is TaxCloud really free for small businesses?
Yes, TaxCloud is free for up to 100 sales transactions per month. You only pay if you need additional services like automated tax filing or certificate management. There are no monthly subscription fees for the basic calculation feature.
What happens if I exceed 100 transactions in a month with TaxCloud?
If you go over 100 transactions, TaxCloud will ask you to upgrade to a paid plan. The paid plans start at a low monthly rate and still cost less than most other sales tax tools. You can upgrade at any time without losing your account data.
