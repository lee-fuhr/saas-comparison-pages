Best Zapier alternatives

If your operations team relies on Zapier for workflow automation but has hit its cost ceiling or needs more advanced logic, you are not alone. Many teams find Zapier’s per-task pricing adds up fast and its visual builder can feel restrictive for complex, multi-step processes. Luckily there are several powerful alternatives that offer cheaper plans, more flexible triggers and actions, or self-hosted control. This guide covers five top contenders: Make, n8n, Workato, Activepieces, and Tray.io.

alternative

Make

(formerly Integromat) focuses on visual scenario building with a drag-and-drop canvas that lets you map data fields visually. It supports more complex logic like iterators, routers, and data aggregators without needing code. Its free plan includes a generous 1,000 operations per month, and paid plans start lower than Zapier for comparable usage.

Try Make →
alternative

n8n

is an open-source, self-hostable workflow tool that gives you full control over your data and infrastructure. You can build workflows with a node-based editor, run them on your own server, and avoid per-operation fees entirely. It is ideal for teams that need HIPAA compliance, custom integrations, or unlimited internal usage without monthly surprises.

Try n8n →
alternative

Workato

is an enterprise-grade platform built for complex integration and automation scenarios. It offers robust governance, role-based access, and deep connectors for systems like Salesforce, NetSuite, and ServiceNow. While it is not the cheapest option, it provides scalability and pre-built recipe templates that operations teams in large organizations often find worth the investment.

Try Workato →
alternative

Activepieces

is an open-source, user-friendly alternative that combines ease of use with powerful features like versioning, branching, and custom code steps. It can be self-hosted or used on their cloud, and pricing is based on usage rather than per-task, which often lowers costs for teams with moderate volumes. Its active community provides regular updates and new connectors.

alternative

Tray.io

is a low-code integration platform designed for high-volume, complex workflows with advanced error handling and data transformation. It uses a visual builder but also lets you embed JavaScript for custom logic. Tray.io’s pricing is usage-based but can be more affordable than Zapier for teams moving thousands of tasks per month because it does not charge per individual operation.
