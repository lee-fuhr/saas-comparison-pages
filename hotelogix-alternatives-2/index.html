If you run a mid-size hotel, you know that managing online travel agency (OTA) channels like Booking.com and Expedia can become a complex task. Hotelogix is a popular property management system, but its pricing and feature set may not fit every budget or workflow. This guide explores three alternatives that offer strong OTA channel management without the high fees often associated with enterprise platforms. We focus on Cloudbeds, Mews, and RMS Cloud – each designed to help you control rates, availability, and reservations across multiple channels while keeping costs predictable.

alternative

Cloudbeds

is a cloud-based platform that combines a property management system with a channel manager and booking engine. It handles real-time rate and inventory syncing to over 300 OTAs, making it a solid choice for mid-size hotels that want to centralize operations. Cloudbeds offers tiered pricing based on property size, so you only pay for the features you need, without surprise add-on charges.

alternative

Mews

is a modern, open-platform solution built for hotels that value automation and ease of integration. Its channel manager connects directly to major OTAs and uses a single-rate approach to prevent overbookings. Mews charges a transparent monthly fee per room, which often works out cheaper than percentage-based systems for properties with consistent occupancy.

alternative

RMS Cloud

provides a full suite of hotel management tools with a dedicated two-way channel manager that updates availability and rates instantly across all connected OTAs. It is known for its flexible pricing models including a flat monthly rate, which helps mid-size hotels avoid high transaction fees. RMS Cloud also offers offline access, a useful feature for properties in areas with unreliable internet.

How they compare

All three alternatives focus on direct OTA connectivity and transparent pricing. Cloudbeds works well for hotels that want an all-in-one PMS with strong marketing tools. Mews appeals to tech-forward properties that want open integrations and a modern user interface. RMS Cloud suits hotels that prefer a more traditional PMS with offline capabilities and a simple flat fee. Each option avoids the high per-booking commissions that can cut into margins, making them worth considering over Hotelogix.

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch from Hotelogix to one of these alternatives without losing my data?
Yes, each alternative offers onboarding support that includes importing your reservations, rate plans, and OTA connections. Cloudbeds, Mews, and RMS Cloud all provide dedicated migration teams or step-by-step guides to ensure a smooth transition.
Which alternative has the lowest upfront cost for a mid-size hotel with 50 rooms?
RMS Cloud and Mews typically offer flat monthly pricing per room, which can be more predictable than percentage-based fees. Cloudbeds also has tiered plans, but you should request a quote based on your specific room count and feature needs to compare total costs.
