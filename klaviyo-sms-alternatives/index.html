Best Klaviyo SMS alternatives

You already use Klaviyo for email and know how powerful its segmentation and automation tools are. Now you want to add SMS to reach customers on their phones. Klaviyo offers its own SMS channel built right into the platform, but it may not fit every brand’s budget, compliance needs, or feature wish list. Several dedicated SMS marketing tools can integrate with Klaviyo or run alongside it. This article compares the most popular alternatives so you can decide which one works best for your brand.

alternative

Attentive

. Attentive is a top choice for high volume brands that want advanced mobile messaging features like two way conversations, personalization at scale, and deep integrations with ecommerce platforms. It connects natively with Klaviyo so you can sync segments and triggers. Attentive tends to have higher minimum commitments and costs, so it suits established brands with large subscriber lists.

alternative

Postscript

. Postscript is built specifically for Shopify merchants and offers seamless integration with Klaviyo email lists. It provides powerful automation workflows, opt in capture tools, and real time analytics. Postscript’s pricing is usage based with no long term contracts, making it a flexible option for growing stores.

alternative

SMSBump

. SMSBump, now part of Yotpo, focuses on Shopify and offers easy setup with Klaviyo via direct integration. It includes features like abandoned cart SMS, post purchase flows, and coupon codes sent via text. SMSBump is a strong choice for mid sized stores that want a straightforward, cost effective SMS tool.

alternative

Twilio

. Twilio is a developer focused platform that gives you full control over SMS infrastructure, APIs, and carrier routing. It is not a ready to use marketing tool, but it powers many other SMS tools. Brands with in house technical teams can use Twilio to build custom messaging that integrates with Klaviyo at the code level.

alternative

MessageBird

. MessageBird is a global SMS provider with excellent delivery rates in many countries. It offers a simple API and a cloud contact center, but lacks out of the box marketing automation. Like Twilio, it works best for brands that need custom development or have international audiences.

alternative

Zipwhip

. Zipwhip specializes in texting from a business phone number and supporting two way conversations. It integrates with Klaviyo through Zapier or a direct API connection. Zipwhip is a good fit for brands that prioritize conversational SMS with customers, not just bulk marketing blasts.

alternative

TextMagic

. TextMagic is a budget friendly SMS gateway for small to medium businesses. It provides a simple web interface, two way messaging, and scheduled sends. While it does not have deep Klaviyo integration, you can use Zapier to connect the two. It is best for teams that need basic, affordable text messaging without advanced automation.

How they compare

If you need enterprise grade automation and two way conversations, Attentive or Postscript are the most feature rich options. For Shopify stores, Postscript and SMSBump offer the smoothest native integration with Klaviyo. Developer oriented teams will prefer Twilio or MessageBird for full control and global reach. Zipwhip is ideal for conversational SMS, and TextMagic works well for budget conscious brands with simple needs. Consider your list size, budget, and whether you want a prebuilt marketing tool or a more customizable API solution.

Frequently asked questions

Can I run Klaviyo email and an outside SMS tool together without conflicts?
Yes, you can run Klaviyo for email and a separate SMS tool at the same time. Most alternatives integrate with Klaviyo through direct connection or Zapier, so you can share segments and trigger automations. Just be careful not to send duplicate messages or mix up subscriber consent across platforms.
Will using an alternative save me money compared to Klaviyo SMS?
It depends on your volume and feature needs. Klaviyo SMS charges per segment sent plus a base fee for the channel. Alternatives like TextMagic or SMSBump may have lower per message rates for low volume senders, while Attentive and Postscript often require higher minimums or monthly commits. Compare your expected monthly sends and feature requirements to find the best value.
