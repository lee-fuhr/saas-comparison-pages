If your team relies on call analytics and coaching tools, you have likely evaluated Chorus.ai. It is a strong platform for recording, transcribing, and analyzing sales calls. But every tool has gaps, and your revenue operations needs may require different features, pricing, or integrations. Whether you want deeper CRM sync, better deal intelligence, or a more coaching‑focused workflow, exploring Chorus.ai alternatives can help you find the best fit.

alternative

Gong

. Gong captures and analyzes revenue conversations across calls, emails, and web meetings. It uses AI to highlight buyer sentiment, competitive mentions, and key moments for coaching. Many teams choose Gong when they need a comprehensive view of customer interactions and advanced deal‑level analytics.

alternative

People.ai

. People.ai focuses on tracking rep activity and pipeline hygiene rather than just call recordings. It automatically logs emails, calls, and meetings to CRM, giving revenue ops visibility into what work moves deals. This tool suits teams that want to reduce manual data entry and improve forecasting accuracy.

alternative

Clari

. Clari is a revenue intelligence and forecasting platform that aggregates data from calls, CRM, and emails. It uses AI to predict deal outcomes and highlight risks, making it valuable for pipeline reviews and coaching. Revenue ops teams often turn to Clari when they need a top‑down view of pipeline health and rep performance.

alternative

Outreach Signal

. Outreach Signal offers call recording, transcription, and AI‑powered coaching insights directly within the Outreach sales engagement platform. It is a natural choice for teams already using Outreach for sequences and cadences. The tight integration reduces tool switching and keeps coaching data in the same workflow.

How they compare

Chorus.ai excels at call recording and coaching but may lack the deep CRM automation of People.ai or the forecasting power of Clari. Gong provides richer conversation intelligence across channels, while Outreach Signal is best for existing Outreach users. Your choice depends on whether your priority is call‑only analysis, full‑pipeline visibility, or seamless workflow integration. Evaluate each tool in a trial based on your team’s specific coaching and analytics workflows.
