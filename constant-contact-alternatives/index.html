Constant Contact is a popular email marketing tool for small businesses, but brick-and-mortar retailers often need easier ways to build customer lists and promote in-store events. If you are looking for alternatives, consider these three options that offer simpler list building and event marketing features.

alternative

Mailchimp

offers a free tier that is great for small lists and its signup forms are easy to embed on your website. It has event marketing tools like automated reminders and ticket sales, though some features require paid plans. Many retailers like its simple drag-and-drop editor and built-in audience segmentation.

alternative

GetResponse

focuses on automation and includes a landing page builder perfect for promoting events. It also has a feature called list building forms that can be triggered based on customer behavior, such as offering a discount to visitors who browse a specific aisle. Its event marketing tools allow you to send invitations and follow-ups with a few clicks.

Try GetResponse →
alternative

AWeber

is known for its straightforward setup and excellent customer support, which helps busy retailers get started quickly. It offers smart email design tools and simple list management, plus its event marketing features let you create and manage signup forms for in-store workshops or sales events. Many users appreciate that AWeber does not charge extra for list segmentation or tagging.

Try AWeber →

How they compare

Mailchimp is best for retailers who need a free start and advanced audience insights, but its pricing can increase with list size. GetResponse excels in automation and landing pages, making it ideal for retailers running frequent events or promotions. AWeber provides the easiest onboarding and hands-on support, which is helpful for owners who do not have much technical experience. For simple list building and event marketing, all three are solid alternatives to Constant Contact, but your choice depends on whether you prioritize free access, automation, or support.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my existing Constant Contact list into these alternatives?
Yes, all three tools allow you to import CSV or text files with your contact list. Most offer step-by-step guides to make the transfer easy.
Do these tools offer features for in-store event signups?
Yes, each tool supports creating signup forms and landing pages for events. You can share links via social media or print QR codes for customers to scan in your store.
