Frequently asked questions

Can TradeGecko handle multiple warehouse locations?
Yes, TradeGecko supports multiple warehouses and lets you track inventory quantities at each location. You can set transfer orders between warehouses and see real time stock levels per site.
Is inFlow a good fit for a wholesaler that uses many sales channels?
inFlow works best for wholesalers with one or two main sales channels and a strong need for offline desktop use. For managing many online storefronts and marketplaces simultaneously, TradeGecko’s built in multichannel order management is more efficient.
