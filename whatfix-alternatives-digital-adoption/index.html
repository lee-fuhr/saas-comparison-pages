How they compare

Whatfix offers a balanced mix of ease of use, advanced targeting, and robust reporting. WalkMe is more powerful for complex enterprise workflows but has a steeper learning curve. Pendo is best for teams that need strong analytics first, with guidance as a secondary feature. Appcues is ideal for smaller teams or simpler use cases where speed of deployment matters. Pricing varies by seat count and features; each vendor offers custom quotes for enterprise plans.
