Best Arcadier alternatives

Arcadier is a popular low-code platform for building online marketplaces, but it may not fit every niche or budget. Many entrepreneurs who run B2B or B2C marketplaces need more flexibility, lower transaction fees, or simpler setup. This article covers seven strong alternatives that offer different strengths for low-code builders.

alternative

Sharetribe

. Sharetribe is a fully hosted marketplace platform with two versions: a ready-to-launch option and a flexible one with custom code. It works well for peer-to-peer rentals, services, and products. The platform handles payments and user management out of the box.

alternative

Near Me

. Near Me focuses on location-based marketplaces for local services, events, and rentals. Its low-code builder lets you drag and drop features like maps, calendars, and chat. It is a good choice if your niche relies on proximity between buyers and sellers.

alternative

Kreezalid

. Kreezalid gives you a customizable marketplace with built-in membership tiers, subscriptions, and commissions. You can change the design without coding and add vendor dashboards. It suits creators who want to monetize through recurring revenue.

alternative

Marketplacer

. Marketplacer is a headless marketplace platform designed for larger catalogs and complex B2B needs. It integrates with existing ecommerce systems and offers advanced product data management. This tool works best for established brands scaling a multi-vendor store.

alternative

Mirakl

. Mirakl is an enterprise-grade platform for B2B and B2C marketplaces with drop shipping, vendor onboarding, and catalog management. It requires more technical integration but offers deep customization through APIs. It is ideal for businesses that already have a strong tech team.

alternative

Yo!Kart

. Yo!Kart is a self-hosted marketplace software that gives you complete control over code and data. It includes features like multiple vendor types, commission plans, and a mobile app. It is a good option if you want to avoid monthly fees and own the infrastructure.

alternative

SpurtCommerce

. SpurtCommerce is a white-label marketplace solution that runs on a subscription model. It offers vendor management, commission rules, and a responsive theme. The platform is designed for entrepreneurs who want a quick launch without deep technical work.

How they compare

The best alternative depends on your niche and technical comfort. Sharetribe and Near Me are easiest to set up for simple B2C or local marketplaces. Kreezalid and Yo!Kart offer more control and different revenue models. Marketplacer and Mirakl target larger B2B operations with deeper integrations. SpurtCommerce provides a middle ground between ease and customization. Compare pricing, transaction fees, and vendor features before you choose.

Frequently asked questions

Which Arcadier alternative is best for a low-budget startup?
Sharetribe’s ready-to-launch version has a low monthly fee and no transaction commission. Near Me also offers affordable plans for local marketplaces. Both let you go live quickly without custom development.
Can I switch from Arcadier to one of these alternatives easily?
Most platforms allow you to import product and user data via CSV or API. However, custom themes and payment flows may need to be rebuilt. Check each tool’s migration guide or ask their support team for specific steps.
