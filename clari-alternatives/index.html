Best Clari alternatives

As a forecasting manager, you need accurate revenue visibility to guide your team and leadership. Clari is a strong platform for sales forecasting and revenue intelligence, but it is not the only option. Depending on your team’s specific needs, other tools may offer better fit, lower cost, or unique capabilities. This article covers four popular Clari alternatives: Gong, Chorus.ai, People.ai, and Outreach Signal. Each brings its own approach to AI-powered revenue visibility, and understanding their strengths will help you choose the right solution.

alternative

Gong

captures and analyzes customer interactions to provide revenue intelligence. Its AI identifies patterns in sales calls and emails, then links them to deal outcomes, giving forecasting managers pipeline visibility based on actual conversation data. Gong excels in coaching and deal risk detection but requires your team to use its recording and transcription features.

alternative

Chorus.ai

, now part of ZoomInfo, focuses on conversation intelligence and deal forecasting. It automatically records and transcribes calls, then surfaces key moments and deal signals. Chorus is strong for understanding buyer sentiment and pipeline health, though its forecasting capabilities may require integration with your CRM.

alternative

People.ai

uses AI to capture sales activity data from email, calendar, and CRM to build accurate forecasts. It prioritizes activity-to-revenue mapping, showing which behaviors lead to closed deals. People.ai is ideal if you want to automate data entry and get rep-level insights, but its core strength is in sales execution rather than conversation analysis.

alternative

Outreach Signal

is a forecasting module within Outreach’s sales engagement platform. It analyzes email, meeting, and call data to predict deal outcomes and flag risks. Signal’s tight integration with Outreach’s cadence tools makes it powerful for teams already using Outreach for prospecting, but it may not offer the same breadth of call recording features as Gong or Chorus.
