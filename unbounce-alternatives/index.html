If you build landing pages regularly, you know Unbounce is a powerful tool. But its monthly price can feel steep for smaller teams or solo marketers, and some users find its page load times slower than they would like. Fortunately, there are solid alternatives that offer similar features at a lower cost or with faster performance. This article reviews five Unbounce alternatives based on price and speed so you can pick the right fit for your campaigns.

alternative

Instapage

. Instapage is a premium tool often compared to Unbounce, with a similar price point but a strong emphasis on post-click optimization and collaboration. Its pages load quickly thanks to a lightweight code structure, though the cost makes it best for teams with larger budgets.

Try Instapage →
alternative

Leadpages

. Leadpages is one of the most affordable Unbounce alternatives, with a simple drag and drop builder and a flat monthly fee that includes unlimited traffic. Its page speed is acceptable for most use cases, but it may not be the fastest option for high volume campaigns.

Try Leadpages →
alternative

Swipe Pages

. Swipe Pages is built for speed, using a mobile-first approach that delivers some of the fastest load times among landing page builders. It is also more affordable than Unbounce, making it a strong choice for marketers who prioritize performance over a large feature set.

Try Swipe Pages →
alternative

Landingi

. Landingi offers a middle ground on price, with plans that cost less than Unbounce but still include A/B testing, popups, and integrations. Its page speed is generally solid, though it can vary depending on how many elements you add to a template.

alternative

Carrd

. Carrd is a very low cost option ideal for simple, one page landing pages. It is extremely fast because the pages are lightweight and hosted on a fast content delivery network, but it lacks advanced features like A/B testing and email integrations that Unbounce provides.

How they compare

Unbounce is feature-rich and reliable, but it is one of the pricier options and its page speed can be average. Instapage matches Unbounce on price but offers faster load times. Leadpages is more affordable but slower than Unbounce. Swipe Pages is both cheaper and faster, making it a top pick for speed. Landingi is a good balance of cost and speed for most marketers. Carrd is the cheapest and fastest, but only works for very basic pages. Choose based on your budget and how much speed matters for your campaigns.

Frequently asked questions

Which Unbounce alternative is best for a low budget?
Carrd is the cheapest option, starting at a very low annual fee, but it only supports single page sites with limited features. If you need more functionality and still want to save money, Leadpages or Landingi are better choices with monthly plans under Unbounce’s entry price.
Does page speed really matter for landing page conversions?
Yes, faster load times can improve conversion rates because visitors are less likely to abandon a slow page. Swipe Pages and Carrd are built specifically for speed, so they are good picks if performance is your main concern. Unbounce and Leadpages are still effective, but their speed may not be as competitive.
