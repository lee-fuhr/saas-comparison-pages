If you manage a brand or agency that produces professional podcasts, you need hosting that supports multiple user accounts, detailed analytics, and reliable distribution. Transistor.fm is a strong choice for these needs, but it is not the only option. Depending on your budget, team size, and desired features, other platforms may offer better value or specific tools. This article covers four leading alternatives to Transistor.fm: Podbean, Libsyn, Anchor, and Buzzsprout. We compare them with your professional requirements in mind: multi-user access, scalability, and supported file formats.

alternative

Podbean

. Podbean offers unlimited storage on its Business plan and supports multiple administrators with custom permission levels. It includes a built-in ad marketplace and live streaming capabilities, which can help agencies monetize or engage audiences directly. The platform also provides detailed podcast analytics and distribution to major directories.

alternative

Libsyn

. Libsyn is one of the oldest podcast hosts and provides robust statistics and professional-grade media storage. Its multi-user support comes through a separate feature called “Team Accounts” that allows you to grant upload or administrative access. Libsyn also offers dynamic ad insertion and a simple, no-frills interface that many agencies find reliable for high-volume publishing.

alternative

Anchor

. Anchor is a free hosting service owned by Spotify, which means your podcast gets automatic distribution to Spotify and other platforms. It supports multiple users through a “team” feature where you can invite collaborators with different roles. However, its analytics are less detailed than paid alternatives, and you may have limited control over your RSS feed and monetization options.

alternative

Buzzsprout

. Buzzsprout is known for its user-friendly dashboard and clear pricing. It supports multiple users through the “Team Members” feature on paid plans, allowing you to assign roles like Editor or Administrator. The platform also offers a visual progress tracker for uploads, customizable podcast websites, and simple migration tools from other hosts.

How they compare

All four alternatives support multi-user access, but Podbean and Buzzsprout make it easier and more affordable than Libsyn. Anchor is free but sacrifices analytics depth and customization. Transistor.fm remains a strong choice for agencies that need granular user permissions and advanced marketing tools. When choosing, consider your team size, storage needs, and whether you want built-in monetization or distribution deals.
