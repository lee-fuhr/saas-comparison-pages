Modern hotels need a property management system that is cloud native and supports mobile check in. Mews is a popular choice because of its modern design and guest facing features. But you may be looking for other options that offer similar capabilities, different pricing, or better fit for your specific property type. This article compares three strong Mews alternatives: Cloudbeds, Hotelogix, and RMS Cloud.

alternative

Cloudbeds

is a cloud native PMS that offers mobile check in through its guest app and integrated channel manager. It is designed for independent hotels, hostels, and vacation rentals, and its interface is easy to navigate. Cloudbeds also includes a built in booking engine and point of sale features that work well for small to midsize properties.

alternative

Hotelogix

is a cloud based PMS that provides mobile check in via a guest portal and a dedicated hotelier mobile app. It focuses on chain hotels and independent properties, with modules for front desk, housekeeping, and revenue management. Hotelogix supports multi property management and can be customized for different room types and rate plans.

alternative

RMS Cloud

is a cloud native PMS with a strong mobile check in feature that lets guests complete pre arrival forms and access digital keys. It is built for hotels, resorts, and serviced apartments, and includes a centralized booking engine and payment processing. RMS Cloud is known for its flexibility in handling complex rate rules and multi property setups.

How they compare

All three alternatives are cloud native and support mobile check in, but they differ in target market and depth of features. Cloudbeds is best suited for independent properties that want an all in one system with strong channel management. Hotelogix is a good choice for chains and large properties that need multi property control and customization. RMS Cloud offers strong rate management and is ideal for resorts and serviced apartments that require flexible booking rules. Pricing for each varies based on property size and selected modules, so it is important to request a quote for your specific needs.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use mobile check in with all three alternatives?
Yes, Cloudbeds, Hotelogix, and RMS Cloud all offer mobile check in features. Each system allows guests to check in, submit IDs, and in some cases receive digital keys through a mobile app or web portal.
How difficult is it to migrate from Mews to one of these alternatives?
Migration difficulty depends on the amount of data you need to move, such as reservations, guest profiles, and billing history. All three vendors provide onboarding support and data import tools. It is best to discuss your current setup with each provider to understand the exact process and timeline.
