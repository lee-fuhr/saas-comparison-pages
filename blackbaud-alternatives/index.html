For decades, Blackbaud has been a dominant player in nonprofit fundraising software, offering comprehensive tools for donor management, accounting, and reporting. However, many foundations and charities are now exploring Blackbaud alternatives due to concerns about cost, complexity, or limitations in flexibility. Whether you are a small charity looking for a more intuitive system or a large foundation needing deeper integration with program management, evaluating the right fundraising suite is critical. This article highlights three strong alternatives: Salesforce Nonprofit, Bloomerang, and DonorPerfect. Each offers unique strengths to match different organizational sizes, budgets, and strategic goals.

alternative

Salesforce Nonprofit

is built on the world’s leading CRM platform, providing powerful customization and scalability for foundations and mid-size to large charities. It offers a 360-degree view of donors, integrates seamlessly with program and case management tools, and supports complex reporting without the need for separate licenses. However, it requires dedicated staff or a partner for setup and administration, making it best suited for organizations with technical resources.

alternative

Bloomerang

focuses on donor retention and ease of use, making it an excellent choice for charities that prioritize relationship building over advanced reporting. Its dashboard highlights donor engagement scores and helps automate personalized communications, while the platform’s simple interface reduces training time. Bloomerang is ideal for smaller to mid-sized nonprofits that want a straightforward, all-in-one solution without the complexity of a full CRM.

alternative

DonorPerfect

offers a flexible, feature-rich system that grows with your organization, from small charities to large foundations. It includes robust fundraising tools, online donation forms, and detailed reporting options, all backed by responsive customer support. DonorPerfect also offers add-ons for special events, grants management, and email marketing, making it a versatile alternative that balances capability with affordability.

How they compare

When choosing among these Blackbaud alternatives, consider your organization’s size, technical capacity, and budget. Salesforce Nonprofit stands out for scalability and customization but demands more investment in setup. Bloomerang excels in simplicity and donor retention, perfect for smaller teams. DonorPerfect offers a middle ground with strong features and flexibility that can accommodate growth. All three provide comprehensive fundraising suites, but the right choice depends on whether your priority is deep CRM power, user-friendly retention tools, or balanced functionality.
