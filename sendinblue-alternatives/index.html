Many marketers look for a single platform that handles email, SMS, and transactional messaging without juggling multiple tools. Sendinblue (now known as Brevo) is a popular choice, but it may not fit every team’s budget, feature needs, or workflow. If you are exploring Sendinblue alternatives, you likely want a solution that keeps all your channels in one place while staying affordable and easy to use. Below we compare three strong options for marketers who need combined messaging capabilities.

alternative

Mailchimp

. This platform is known for its robust email marketing tools and extensive library of integrations. It offers SMS and transactional email features, but those are mostly available on higher tier plans. Mailchimp is a good fit if you already have a large contact list and need advanced segmentation and analytics.

alternative

Mailerlite

. This tool is praised for its simple interface and affordable pricing, especially for small to medium businesses. It provides email marketing, basic SMS functionality, and transactional emails through its API. Mailerlite works well for teams that want a low complexity setup without sacrificing core features.

Try Mailerlite →
alternative

Brevo

(formerly Sendinblue). This all in one platform delivers email, SMS, and transactional messaging with powerful automation workflows. Its pricing is often competitive for growing businesses, and it offers a free plan with daily sending limits. Brevo is ideal if you want a unified dashboard for all your campaigns and transactional triggers.

Try Brevo →

How they compare

Mailchimp offers the deepest integration ecosystem and advanced reporting, but its SMS and transactional features come at a higher cost. Mailerlite is the most budget friendly and easiest to learn, though its SMS capabilities are limited compared to the others. Brevo provides the fullest all in one experience with strong automation and transparent pricing, making it the closest direct alternative to the original Sendinblue. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize low cost, rich integrations, or a complete channel bundle.

Frequently asked questions

Can I send transactional emails like order confirmations with these platforms?
Yes, Mailchimp, Mailerlite, and Brevo all support transactional emails through their API. Each platform charges separately for transactional volume, and your plan tier usually determines the sending limits and delivery speed.
Which platform is easiest to set up for both email and SMS campaigns?
Mailerlite has the simplest onboarding and user interface, making it quick to launch combined campaigns. Brevo also offers a straightforward setup with clear channel management, while Mailchimp may require more time to configure advanced SMS features.
