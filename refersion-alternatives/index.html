If you manage an affiliate program and currently use Refersion, you may be looking for alternatives because of cost, feature gaps, or scaling needs. Refersion is a solid entry level platform, but as your program grows you might want more automation, better reporting, or deeper integration options. This article covers five strong Refersion alternatives that affiliate managers commonly evaluate: Impact, ShareASale, PartnerStack, Tapfiliate, and Post Affiliate Pro.

alternative

Impact

is a full partnership automation platform built for high volume programs that need advanced fraud detection, custom commission rules, and deep analytics. It handles everything from affiliates to influencers to B2B partners, but its pricing and onboarding can be heavier than Refersion, so it suits established programs.

alternative

ShareASale

operates as both a network and a software solution, giving you access to a large pool of pre vetted affiliates along with tracking and reporting tools. It is a good choice if you want to recruit new partners quickly, though you trade some customization for the network ecosystem.

alternative

PartnerStack

focuses on partner relationship management with built in incentives like gamification and tiered rewards to keep affiliates engaged. It works especially well for SaaS and subscription businesses that need to track recurring commissions and manage multiple partner types.

alternative

Tapfiliate

offers a simple, affordable platform with clean tracking, customizable dashboards, and easy integration via API and plugins. It is a strong alternative for smaller or mid size programs that want Refersion like functionality without the higher price tag.

alternative

Post Affiliate Pro

is a self hosted or cloud based solution that gives you full control over commission structures, link formats, and affiliate management. It is very customizable and has a one time license option, making it budget friendly for programs that prefer not to pay monthly recurring fees.

How they compare

When comparing these alternatives to Refersion, think about your program size, budget, and complexity. Impact and ShareASale are best for scaling programs that need network reach or advanced automation. PartnerStack suits subscription models with recurring commissions and partner tiers. Tapfiliate and Post Affiliate Pro are more affordable options that still provide core tracking and management, with Tapfiliate being easier to set up and Post Affiliate Pro offering more long term ownership.

Frequently asked questions

Which Refersion alternative is easiest to migrate to from Refersion?
Tapfiliate is often the easiest because it has similar features and a simple dashboard, plus it offers import tools for existing affiliate data. Post Affiliate Pro also provides migration support, but it may take more setup time depending on your customization needs.
Do any of these alternatives include a built in affiliate marketplace like Refersion does not?
Yes, ShareASale includes a large affiliate marketplace where you can recruit new partners, which Refersion lacks. Impact also offers partner discovery through its partnership cloud, though it is more focused on automating existing relationships than a traditional marketplace.
