Homebase is a popular scheduling and time tracking tool for small businesses, but it may not fit every retail or service operation. You might look for alternatives if you need different pricing, advanced labor forecasting, or tighter integration with specific point of sale systems. The tools below offer reliable time-clock features that work well on mobile devices and desktop computers.

alternative

Deputy

focuses on real time schedule changes and automated compliance with labor laws. Its time clock captures GPS location and photo verification, making it a strong choice for service businesses with mobile teams. Deputy also connects with payroll providers like ADP and Gusto for seamless data export.

alternative

When I Work

is built for shift based teams and offers a simple clock in and out interface from any device. It includes automated break tracking and alerts when employees are about to hit overtime. The platform integrates with popular POS systems such as Square and Toast for retail and restaurant use. **7shifts** is designed primarily for restaurants but works well for retail with features like tip pooling and sales based labor forecasting. Its time clock allows managers to view live punches from a dashboard and approve time off requests. The tool syncs directly with payroll providers like QuickBooks and Heartland.

Shiftboard

specializes in managing large, rotating schedules for industries like retail, hospitality, and healthcare. Its time clock supports biometric fingerprints and facial recognition for high security environments. Shiftboard also provides advanced analytics to predict staffing needs and reduce labor costs.

How they compare

Deputy and When I Work are best for small to midsize teams needing simple mobile time tracking and payroll integration. 7shifts excels for restaurants and retail that rely on sales based labor forecasting and tip management. Shiftboard suits larger organizations with complex scheduling rules and a need for biometric verification. All four tools offer free trials, but pricing varies by number of employees and feature tier. Homebase remains strong for its all in one free plan, but these alternatives provide deeper specialization for specific business types.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a retail store with a small team of 10 employees?
When I Work or Deputy are good options. When I Work offers a straightforward interface and free plan for up to 10 employees. Deputy gives you GPS verification and stronger compliance features if you have mobile workers or strict labor laws.
Do these tools integrate with popular point of sale systems like Square or Clover?
Yes. When I Work integrates directly with Square and Toast. Deputy connects with Square through its API. 7shifts works with Toast and Clover. Shiftboard offers custom integrations for enterprise POS systems. Always check the specific app marketplace for your POS model.
