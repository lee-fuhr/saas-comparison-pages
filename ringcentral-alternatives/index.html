Best RingCentral alternatives

If your business uses RingCentral but you are looking for a change, you are not alone. Many companies switch to a different cloud phone system because of pricing, feature gaps, or customer support issues. The right alternative depends on your team size, budget, and must-have features like SMS, analytics, or integrations. Below we compare five popular RingCentral alternatives: Dialpad, Vonage, Nextiva, Aircall, and OpenPhone.

alternative

Dialpad

. Dialpad uses artificial intelligence to transcribe calls in real time and provide live coaching tips. It offers a clean desktop and mobile app, unlimited domestic calling, and deep integrations with Google Workspace and Salesforce. However, its pricing can be higher per user compared to some competitors, especially for advanced AI features.

Try Dialpad →
alternative

Vonage

. Vonage provides a flexible cloud phone system with strong SMS and MMS capabilities plus programmable APIs for custom integrations. It is a solid choice for businesses that need a full feature set including video conferencing and team messaging. Vonage’s plans can get confusing due to many add-ons, so careful comparison is needed.

alternative

Nextiva

. Nextiva bundles voice, video, team chat, and customer relationship management tools into one platform. It is known for reliable uptime and excellent customer support, making it a good fit for medium to large businesses. The platform may feel more complex for very small teams that only need basic calling.

Try Nextiva →
alternative

Aircall

. Aircall is built for sales and support teams that handle high call volumes. It offers a simple interface, easy setup, and native integrations with helpdesk and CRM tools like HubSpot and Zendesk. One limitation is that Aircall does not include video conferencing, so you may need a separate tool for that.

Try Aircall →
alternative

OpenPhone

. OpenPhone is a modern phone system designed for small businesses and startups. You get shared phone numbers, text messaging, and a simple app that works on desktop and mobile. It lacks advanced call center features like automatic call distribution and real-time analytics, which larger teams may need.

Try OpenPhone →

Frequently asked questions

Can I keep my existing phone number when switching from RingCentral to an alternative?
Yes. All five alternatives we listed support number porting. The process usually takes a few days to two weeks. You will need an account with the new provider to start the port request.
Which RingCentral alternative is easiest to set up for a small team?
OpenPhone is the simplest option for small teams. Its onboarding takes minutes, and you do not need any hardware. For a more feature-rich but still easy setup, Dialpad also offers a straightforward sign-up process with guided configuration.
