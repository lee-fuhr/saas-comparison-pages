Best Icertis alternatives

Global corporations rely on AI-driven contract lifecycle management (CLM) to ensure compliance, reduce risk, and automate workflows. Icertis is a leading platform for this purpose, but its complexity, cost, or specific feature gaps may lead organizations to explore alternatives. This article reviews five strong contenders: Ironclad, DocuSign CLM, Conga CLM, Agiloft, and Concord. Each offers AI capabilities tailored to contract compliance, but they differ in deployment, scalability, and integration depth. Below you will find a concise overview of each tool and a comparison summary to help your team evaluate the best fit.

alternative

Ironclad

. Ironclad provides a user-friendly, no-code contract management platform with AI-powered extraction and clause identification. Its workflow builder helps global teams automate approvals and compliance checks, and it integrates tightly with Salesforce and other enterprise systems. However, some users note that advanced AI features for large-scale compliance analytics may require additional customization.

alternative

DocuSign CLM

. DocuSign CLM (formerly SpringCM) combines document generation with AI-driven contract analysis and obligation tracking. It supports complex approval chains and multi-language contracts, making it suitable for global operations. The platform benefits from DocuSign’s extensive eSignature network, though its AI compliance capabilities may be less mature than Icertis’s for high-volume, unstructured data.

alternative

Conga CLM

. Conga CLM focuses on automating contract creation, redlining, and renewal management with AI-assisted clause libraries. Its compliance engine can flag non-standard terms and enforce approval policies across different regions. Conga is often praised for its tight integration with Salesforce, but its AI analytics for post-execution compliance monitoring may require third-party add-ons.

alternative

Agiloft

. Agiloft offers a highly customizable CLM platform with a strong rules engine and AI-based contract review. Global corporations can configure compliance workflows, obligation tracking, and risk scoring without extensive coding. While Agiloft’s flexibility is a major advantage, some users report that the initial setup for AI compliance models can be time-intensive.

alternative

Concord

. Concord provides a simple, cloud-based contract management solution with AI features for term extraction and version comparison. Its collaborative editing and approval workflows are designed for distributed teams, and it supports compliance by enforcing standardized templates. Concord’s AI capabilities are lighter than Icertis’s, making it more suited for mid-market firms than large-scale global compliance programs.
