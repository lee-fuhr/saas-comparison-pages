Sococo was a popular virtual office platform that used a 2D map based interface. Many enterprises now need a secure, compliance ready alternative because Sococo ended its service. This article reviews five tools: Gather.town, Teamflow, Wonder.me, SpatialChat, and VirBELA. We focus on security features, data handling, and enterprise controls that matter for regulated industries.

alternative

Gather.town

. Gather.town offers a customizable 2D map with an enterprise tier that includes single sign on (SSO), data encryption at rest and in transit, and SOC 2 compliance. The platform is well suited for teams that want a playful, game like environment but still need strong access controls and audit logs.

alternative

Teamflow

. Teamflow creates a persistent virtual office with live audio and video. The platform provides enterprise grade security including SOC 2 Type II certification, encryption, and granular admin controls for managing user permissions and data retention policies.

alternative

Wonder.me

. Wonder.me focuses on spontaneous conversations using a timer based breakout system. For enterprises, the tool offers SSO integration, data encryption, and GDPR compliance, but its security posture is generally less comprehensive than dedicated enterprise virtual office platforms.

alternative

SpatialChat

. SpatialChat uses proximity based audio with a flexible 2D canvas. The platform supports end to end encryption, SSO, and role based access controls, making it a viable option for enterprises that need a simple, visual workspace with reasonable security features.

alternative

VirBELA

. VirBELA is a full 3D virtual campus designed for large scale enterprise events and persistent offices. It offers advanced security including SOC 2 certification, HIPAA compliance, dedicated server options, and custom integrations for single sign on and identity management.

How they compare

Gather.town provides a playable map with SOC 2 and SSO, ideal for creative teams. Teamflow offers a persistent office space with strong compliance certifications and admin controls. Wonder.me is lightweight and good for ad hoc meetings but has fewer enterprise security features. SpatialChat balances simple spatial audio with encryption and access controls. VirBELA is the most comprehensive for large enterprises needing HIPAA compliance and dedicated infrastructure.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative offers the strongest compliance certifications for healthcare or finance clients?
VirBELA provides SOC 2 and HIPAA compliance, making it the strongest choice for highly regulated industries. Teamflow also offers SOC 2 Type II certification and is suitable for finance and tech enterprises.
Can we integrate any of these tools with our existing SSO provider and Active Directory?
Yes, Gather.town, Teamflow, Wonder.me, SpatialChat, and VirBELA all support SSO integration in their enterprise plans. Most can work with Okta, Azure AD, or OneLogin. Confirm specific AD support with each vendor during the evaluation process.
