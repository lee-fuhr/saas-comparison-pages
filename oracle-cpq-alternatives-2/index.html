Best Oracle CPQ alternatives

Oracle CPQ is a powerful tool for configure, price, quote processes, but enterprises with complex pricing rules and global compliance requirements often need a solution that is more flexible, easier to integrate, or better suited to their specific industry. While Oracle CPQ offers deep functionality, it can be expensive to implement and maintain, and its rigid architecture may not adapt well to rapid pricing changes or multi-country tax rules. Exploring alternatives can help your organization find a platform that reduces total cost of ownership, simplifies compliance, and supports dynamic pricing across regions.

alternative

SAP CPQ

is built on the SAP ecosystem and integrates natively with SAP S/4HANA and other SAP modules. It provides strong support for subscription and usage-based pricing, making it a natural fit for enterprises already using SAP for ERP and supply chain. Its guided selling and approval flows are customizable, but the learning curve can be steep for teams new to SAP architecture.

alternative

PROS

is a pricing and CPQ platform designed for complex, data-driven pricing strategies. It uses AI and machine learning to optimize prices in real time, which is especially helpful for enterprises with thousands of SKUs and variable global pricing rules. PROS also offers built-in compliance tools for international trade and tax regulations, reducing the burden on legal and finance teams.

alternative

Pricefx

is a cloud-native CPQ and price optimization platform known for its flexibility and rapid deployment. It supports highly configurable pricing logic, including tiered discounts, volume breaks, and customer-specific contracts, all within a unified interface. Pricefx’s global compliance features, such as multi-currency and multi-language support, make it a strong choice for enterprises operating in dozens of countries.

alternative

Zilliant

focuses on pricing optimization and CPQ for B2B manufacturers and distributors. Its strengths lie in profit-guided price recommendations and automated quote approval workflows that handle complex discount rules. Zilliant also provides robust integration with Salesforce and other CRM platforms, allowing sales teams to work within familiar tools while meeting compliance requirements.

alternative

ConfigureOne

is a specialized CPQ solution for highly complex engineered products, such as industrial machinery and telecom equipment. It excels at managing advanced product configurations with thousands of options, dependencies, and compliance constraints. ConfigureOne offers on-premise and cloud deployment, and its rule engine is designed to handle multi-country regulatory checks without custom coding.

How they compare

Each alternative addresses specific pain points that enterprises face with Oracle CPQ. SAP CPQ is ideal for organizations already invested in the SAP ecosystem, while PROS and Pricefx lead in AI-driven pricing and global compliance. Zilliant prioritizes profit optimization and CRM integration, and ConfigureOne is best for ultra-complex product configurations. Your choice should depend on your existing technology stack, pricing model complexity, and the level of customization you need for global regulatory requirements.
