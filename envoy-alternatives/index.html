If your office relies on Envoy for visitor check-ins and space management, you might be looking for alternatives that better fit your budget, feature needs, or company culture. Office managers often need tools that simplify daily operations, from greeting guests to booking desks. Here we explore four solid options: Robin, OfficeSpace, Condeco, and Teem. Each offers a different approach to visitor and space management, so you can choose what works best for your team.

alternative

Robin

is a workplace platform that focuses on desk booking and room scheduling, with visitor management as a built-in feature. It shines in hybrid environments where employees need to reserve spaces in advance and see who is in the office. The interface is clean and mobile-friendly, making it easy for staff and visitors alike.

alternative

OfficeSpace

provides a comprehensive workplace management suite that includes visitor registration, desk hoteling, and meeting room booking. It is known for its strong floor plan integrations and detailed reporting, which helps office managers track space utilization over time. OfficeScale is a good fit for companies that want a single platform to manage both people and physical spaces.

alternative

Condeco

is a mature room scheduling and desk booking solution that also handles visitor management. It integrates with popular calendar systems like Outlook and Google Calendar, making adoption smooth for teams already using those tools. Condeco is especially suited for larger organizations with multiple locations and complex meeting room configurations.

alternative

Teem

(part of WeWork) offers a visitor management and room booking platform that emphasizes simplicity and integration with Slack and other collaboration tools. It is designed to allow employees to book spaces quickly and let guests check in without friction. Teem works well for growing companies that want an easy-to-deploy solution with a modern feel.

How they compare

Choose Robin if your priority is hybrid workforce coordination and flexible desk booking. Pick OfficeSpace when you need deep space analytics and detailed floor plan management. Select Condeco for large enterprises with complex scheduling needs and heavy calendar integration. Go with Teem if you want a straightforward, scalable tool with strong collaboration app connectivity. All four alternatives cover visitor management, but their strengths differ by company size, feature emphasis, and integration preferences.
