Best Simple Analytics alternatives

If you run a website and want clear traffic reports without the complexity of Google Analytics, you likely know Simple Analytics. It focuses on privacy and simplicity, giving you the key numbers like page views and unique visitors in an easy dashboard. But you might be looking for an alternative because of pricing, feature needs, or the desire to self-host. This article covers three popular Simple Analytics alternatives: Fathom, Plausible, and Matomo. Each offers straightforward reports, so you can pick the one that fits your site best.

alternative

Fathom

is a lightweight analytics tool that puts privacy first and delivers a clean, one-page dashboard. It shows your most important metrics such as page views, bounce rate, and top pages without any clutter. Fathom is a hosted service with a simple monthly fee, and its reports load fast and are easy to read at a glance.

alternative

Plausible

is another privacy-focused analytics platform that keeps reports simple and free of cookies. It gives you real time data on visitors, referrers, and pages, all in a dashboard that loads in seconds. Plausible is open source and offers both cloud hosting and self hosting options for sites that want full control.

alternative

Matomo

is a powerful open source analytics tool that you can host on your own server. It offers detailed reports including heatmaps, campaign tracking, and ecommerce insights, but its dashboard can still be simplified. Matomo gives you full ownership of your data and is a good choice if you need advanced features beyond basic page views.

How they compare

All three tools provide easy to understand traffic reports without tracking personal data by default. Fathom and Plausible are the simplest options with minimal setup and clear dashboards, while Matomo offers more depth but requires more configuration. Pricing differs: Fathom and Plausible use flat monthly fees based on page views, while Matomo is free if you self host but charges for cloud hosting and premium plugins. If you want a no fuss experience, Fathom or Plausible are great matches. If you need advanced analytics and own your data, Matomo is the strongest alternative.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my Simple Analytics data to these alternatives?
Yes, most of these tools allow you to import historical data from Simple Analytics using CSV files. Check each tool’s documentation for specific steps on importing your traffic data.
Which alternative is best for a very small site with low traffic?
Plausible and Fathom both offer free tiers or low cost plans for sites with under 10,000 page views per month, making them ideal for small blogs or personal projects. Matomo’s self hosted version is also free, but you need to manage the server yourself.
