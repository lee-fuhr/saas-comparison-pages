Best Chargebee alternatives

If you use Chargebee for recurring billing but are looking for a change, you likely want a platform that handles subscriptions, invoices, and payment collection without forcing you into a rigid workflow. Several strong Chargebee alternatives exist, each with different strengths for SaaS companies. This guide covers five top contenders so you can compare features, pricing, and complexity before making a switch.

alternative

Recurly

. Recurly is a dedicated subscription management platform that excels at handling complex billing scenarios like dunning, invoicing, and multi-currency. Its built-in revenue recognition and accounting integrations make it a solid choice for mid-market SaaS companies that need a dedicated billing engine separate from their payment processor. However, pricing can rise quickly as your revenue grows, and it does not include direct payment gateway services.

alternative

Stripe Billing

. Stripe Billing is an add-on to the Stripe payment platform that lets you manage subscriptions, invoices, and usage-based pricing directly within Stripe’s ecosystem. It is ideal for SaaS teams already using Stripe for payments because there is no separate integration and the pricing is transparent (a small percentage on top of Stripe’s standard processing fees). The main limitation is that you are locked into Stripe’s payment methods and may lack some advanced billing features like custom dunning schedules.

alternative

Zuora

. Zuora is an enterprise-grade subscription management platform designed for large-scale, high-volume billing with support for complex pricing models (usage, tiered, hybrid). It offers deep customization, revenue automation (ASC 606/IFRS 15), and a full suite of tools for quoting, collections, and analytics. The trade-off is significant implementation time, a steep learning curve, and high pricing that makes it overkill for most small or mid-size SaaS companies.

alternative

Paddle

. Paddle is a merchant-of-record platform that handles not only billing but also sales tax compliance, VAT, and fraud prevention for digital goods and SaaS globally. It simplifies global expansion because Paddle acts as the legal seller, so you do not need to register for taxes in each country. However, you give up direct control over your customer payment data and your subscription logic is limited compared to dedicated billing engines.

Try Paddle →
alternative

Lago

. Lago is an open-source billing platform designed for developers who want full control over their subscription and usage-based billing logic. It offers a self-hosted option with a flexible API and supports features like metered billing, plans, and invoices. The main challenges are the need for engineering resources to set up and maintain it, and its current documentation is less mature than commercial alternatives.

Frequently asked questions

Which Chargebee alternative is easiest to migrate to for a small SaaS team?
Stripe Billing is usually the simplest to migrate because it integrates directly with Stripe’s existing payment infrastructure. You can start with basic subscription logic and add features over time without a large upfront investment.
Do any of these alternatives handle sales tax and VAT automatically?
Yes, Paddle acts as the merchant of record and handles all global tax compliance automatically. Zuora and Recurly offer tax integrations but require you to manage your own tax registrations. Stripe Billing has a Stripe Tax add-on that can automate tax calculation for supported jurisdictions.
