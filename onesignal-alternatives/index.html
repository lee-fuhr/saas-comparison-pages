OneSignal is a popular choice for push notifications, but many teams find they need more advanced segmentation, better analytics, or different pricing models as their user base grows. Whether you require deeper CRM features, higher delivery reliability, or tighter integration with your existing marketing stack, exploring alternatives can help you find a platform that scales with your goals. Below we review five strong contenders worth considering.

alternative

Pushwoosh

offers a cost effective entry point with solid push notification capabilities across mobile, web, and email. It includes easy-to-use templates and A/B testing, but its advanced automation and analytics are less comprehensive than some enterprise tools.

alternative

Airship

provides a robust, highly reliable push infrastructure built for large scale deployments. It excels at lifecycle marketing and real-time segmentation, though its pricing and complexity may be overkill for smaller teams.

Try Airship →
alternative

MoEngage

focuses on cross-channel personalization, combining push, in-app, email, and SMS with AI-driven insights. It is well suited for growth teams that want to unify customer engagement under one platform, though setup can take more time than simpler alternatives.

alternative

Braze

is an enterprise-grade engagement platform known for its powerful data modeling, real-time orchestration, and advanced experimentation features. It is ideal for teams with sophisticated analytics needs, but it comes with a higher price point and a steeper learning curve.

alternative

CleverTap

blends push notifications with product analytics and lifecycle optimization in a single system. It works well for mobile-first businesses that want to trigger messages based on user behavior, but its web push capabilities are less mature than its mobile offering.

How they compare

Pushwoosh is the most affordable and easiest to adopt for basic push, while Airship offers the highest delivery reliability at scale. MoEngage and CleverTap both emphasize cross-channel personalization, with CleverTap having stronger built-in analytics and MoEngage offering more flexible campaign management. Braze stands alone for advanced data manipulation and A/B testing, but at a cost that fits larger budgets. Your choice should depend on whether you prioritize price, ease of use, data depth, or omnichannel integration.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a small team on a tight budget?
Pushwoosh is often the best fit for small teams because it offers a generous free tier and simple setup. You can start with basic push notifications without committing to a high monthly spend.
Do any of these alternatives integrate with my existing analytics or CRM tools?
Yes, all five alternatives offer REST APIs and pre-built integrations with popular platforms like Segment, Amplitude, and Mixpanel. Braze and CleverTap provide especially deep integration support for enterprise stacks.
