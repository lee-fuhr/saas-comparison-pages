If your sales ops team is evaluating Highspot alternatives, you likely need a platform that delivers AI-curated content suggestions to help reps find the right assets at the right moment. Highspot is a strong player in sales enablement, but its approach to AI recommendations may not fit every workflow or budget. Below we explore five alternatives: Showpad, Seismic, Brainshark, Allego, and ClearSlide. Each offers a slightly different take on content management and intelligent suggestions, so comparing their strengths can help you choose the best fit for your team’s specific needs.

alternative

Showpad

. Showpad uses AI to recommend content based on buyer intent signals and rep behavior, making it easier for sales teams to serve relevant materials without manual searching. Its content engagement analytics give sales ops clear visibility into which assets influence deals, and the platform integrates tightly with major CRM systems.

alternative

Seismic

. Seismic offers a content recommendation engine that learns from past deal wins and rep performance to surface the best materials. It also provides lifecycle marketing capabilities, so content can be tailored to different stages of the buyer journey, and its LiveSight feature delivers real-time suggestions during customer conversations.

alternative

Brainshark

. Brainshark (now part of Bigtincan) focuses on sales coaching and content readiness, with AI that suggests training content and pitch materials based on individual rep gaps. It prioritizes onboarding and skill development alongside content curation, which can be a differentiator if your team needs both enablement and learning in one platform.

alternative

Allego

. Allego’s AI curates content from recorded sales calls and training sessions, pulling out best-practice snippets and recommended talking points. It is especially strong for organizations that rely heavily on video and audio assets, and its content recommendation engine ties directly to call analytics and rep performance data.

alternative

ClearSlide

. ClearSlide (now part of SalesLoft) uses AI to recommend content within the context of sales conversations and email sequences, focusing on real-time suggestions during presentations. Its strength lies in its presentation analytics and the ability to surface the right asset at the exact moment a rep is engaging a prospect, though its content management features are lighter than some other options.

Highspot remains a strong all-rounder, but these alternatives provide targeted strengths that may better match specific sales ops priorities.
