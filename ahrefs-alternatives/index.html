Best Ahrefs alternatives

Ahrefs is a powerful SEO tool, but its pricing can be steep for freelancers, small teams, or those who only need a few core features. Whether you are looking for a lower cost option or a tool with a different focus, there are several solid alternatives that can match or exceed Ahrefs in specific areas. This article reviews five popular choices, comparing them by price and feature set to help you find the right fit.

alternative

Semrush

. Semrush is the most direct full-feature competitor to Ahrefs, offering strong keyword research, site audit, and competitor analysis tools. Its backlink database is slightly smaller than Ahrefs, but it excels in PPC and social media features. Pricing starts around $140 per month, similar to Ahrefs, but the higher plans include more project limits and historical data.

Try Semrush →
alternative

Moz

. Moz is a longtime SEO tool known for its user-friendly interface and reliable link metrics like Domain Authority. It offers keyword research, site audits, and rank tracking, though its backlink index is smaller than Ahrefs and Semrush. Pricing begins at about $50 per month for the basic plan, making it a good mid-range choice for smaller teams.

Try Moz →
alternative

Majestic

. Majestic specializes in backlink analysis with the largest link index of any tool, focusing on link quality scores like Trust Flow and Citation Flow. It lacks the site audit and keyword research depth of Ahrefs, but it is unmatched for link prospecting and link building. Pricing starts at around $50 per month for the Basic plan, with more generous limits on higher tiers.

alternative

SE Ranking

. SE Ranking is a budget-friendly all-in-one SEO platform that covers keyword research, rank tracking, site audits, and competitor analysis. Its backlink database is not as extensive as Ahrefs, but it offers a clean interface and flexible pricing plans starting at about $30 per month. It is a great fit for agencies that manage multiple clients with limited budgets.

alternative

Mangools

. Mangools is a beginner-friendly suite of five SEO tools (KWFinder, SERPWatcher, LinkMiner, etc.) focused on ease of use and affordability. It is excellent for keyword and rank tracking but has limited backlink data and no site audit feature. Pricing starts at roughly $30 per month per tool, with a full bundle available for about $90 per month.

Frequently asked questions

Which Ahrefs alternative is best for a solo blogger on a tight budget?
Mangools and SE Ranking are both excellent budget options starting around $30 per month. Mangools is simpler and great for keyword and rank tracking, while SE Ranking offers more features like site audit and competitor analysis.
Does Semrush have a backlink database as big as Ahrefs?
No. Semrush’s backlink index is smaller than Ahrefs, but it still covers hundreds of millions of domains and is sufficient for most SEO tasks. If your work relies heavily on link building, Majestic or Ahrefs may be better choices.
