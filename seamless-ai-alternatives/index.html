Seamless.AI is a popular sales prospecting tool that helps teams find contact data and build lead lists. However, some prospecting teams look for alternatives due to pricing changes, data accuracy concerns, or the need for features like advanced filtering or CRM integrations. Whether you want a more budget friendly option, better international coverage, or a tool that focuses on verified data, the market offers several strong choices. Below we explore three leading alternatives: ZoomInfo, Lusha, and LeadGenius.

alternative

ZoomInfo

. ZoomInfo provides a massive business database with contact and company data, intent signals, and workflow automation. It is best for enterprise teams that need deep firmographic filters and account based marketing capabilities, but it comes with a higher price point and often requires a long term contract.

How they compare

ZoomInfo is the most robust alternative for large scale prospecting but requires significant budget and commitment. Lusha is the most accessible for quick, low cost data pulls and works well for smaller teams or those with variable volume. LeadGenius stands out when you need carefully researched, verified leads for specific verticals. Compared to Seamless.AI, ZoomInfo offers broader company intelligence, Lusha is more lightweight and affordable, and LeadGenius provides human verified quality for niche needs. Each alternative has trade offs, so your choice depends on team size, budget, and the level of data validation required.

Frequently asked questions

Does ZoomInfo or Lusha offer better mobile phone number coverage than Seamless.AI?
Both ZoomInfo and Lusha provide mobile phone numbers, but coverage varies by region. ZoomInfo generally has more US based mobile data, while Lusha often has stronger international coverage for direct dials. It is best to test each tool with your target market.
Can I integrate these alternatives with my existing CRM like Salesforce or HubSpot?
Yes, all three tools offer native CRM integrations. ZoomInfo and Lusha have direct connectors for Salesforce and HubSpot, and LeadGenius can export data in formats that work with most CRMs. Integration ease and sync frequency may differ, so check each tool’s current compatibility.
