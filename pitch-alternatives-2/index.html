Best Pitch alternatives

If your team uses Pitch for collaborative slide decks but you are looking for other options, you have plenty of choices. Pitch is a strong primary tool, but alternatives can offer different design features, animation styles, or pricing models. Here we cover five web-based alternatives that work well for teams: Canva, Visme, Powtoon, Prezi, and Slides.com.

alternative

Canva

is a design-first platform that makes it easy to create polished slide decks with thousands of templates and a simple drag-and-drop editor. Teams can collaborate in real time, and the free tier includes enough features for basic presentations. Canva works best when your priority is visual appeal over advanced slide transitions or data storytelling.

alternative

Visme

combines slide decks with infographics, charts, and data visualization tools, which helps teams present numbers clearly. It includes brand kits and team collaboration features, making it a good fit for marketing or reporting needs. Visme’s editor is more structured than Canva’s but offers richer data options.

alternative

Powtoon

focuses on animated presentations and explainer videos rather than static slides. Teams can create short, engaging video-style decks with characters, speech bubbles, and motion effects. Powtoon is ideal for internal training, product demos, or any pitch that benefits from movement and story.

alternative

Prezi

uses a zoomable canvas instead of linear slides, which can make presentations feel more dynamic and spatial. Teams collaborate in real time, and viewers can navigate the content at their own pace. Prezi works well for non-linear stories but may require more upfront planning to avoid disorientation.

alternative

Slides.com

is a minimalist, developer-friendly tool that supports Markdown, LaTeX, and live code embedding. It offers real-time multiuser editing and syncs with cloud storage like Google Drive. Slides.com is best for technical teams who want clean, code-based slides and don’t need heavy design features.

How they compare

All five alternatives are web-based and support real-time team collaboration. Canva and Visme lean toward design and data, while Powtoon and Prezi add motion and non-linear navigation. Slides.com is the simplest for code-savvy teams. Pitch remains a strong all-rounder, but your choice depends on whether you value animation, data visuals, or minimalism.

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my Pitch presentations into these alternatives?
Most alternatives let you upload PDFs or PowerPoint files, which you can then edit. Check each tool’s import options because direct Pitch file imports are not standard. You may need to export from Pitch as a PDF or PPTX first.
Which alternative is best for large teams with strict brand guidelines?
Visme and Canva both offer brand kits that let you lock colors, fonts, and logos. Visme’s brand tools are slightly more advanced, while Canva’s are easier to set up. Both work well for teams that need consistent branding across presentations.
