If your enterprise manages a fleet of hundreds or thousands of vehicles, you need a vehicle data platform that scales, integrates, and supports complex workflows. Geotab is a leading choice, but it is not the only option. Samsara, Verizon Connect, and GPS Insight each offer distinct strengths for large scale operations. This review compares these alternatives to Geotab, focusing on features, data volume handling, and total cost of ownership for enterprise users.

alternative

Samsara

offers a unified cloud platform that combines vehicle telematics, video safety, asset tracking, and environmental monitoring in a single dashboard. Its real time data pipeline supports high volume fleets, and the platform includes built in AI for dashcam events and driver coaching. Enterprises appreciate Samsara’s no code integrations with ERPs and its ability to scale from 50 to over 10,000 vehicles without degrading performance.

alternative

Verizon Connect

provides a mature, highly customizable telematics solution backed by Verizon’s network infrastructure. It excels in field service and mobile workforce management, with advanced route optimization, job dispatch, and compliance tools. For large enterprises, its data retention policies and API offerings allow deep integration with existing business systems, though pricing can increase significantly with add on modules.

alternative

GPS Insight

focuses on flexibility and customer service, offering modular telematics hardware and software for fleets of any size. Its platform includes customizable alerts, extensive reporting, and a strong integration marketplace. Enterprise users often choose GPS Insight for its transparent pricing and dedicated account management, making it a good fit for organizations that want to avoid long term contracts and proprietary hardware lock in.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a fleet with over 10,000 vehicles that needs real time video and safety analytics?
Samsara is often the best fit for very large fleets that require integrated AI dashcams and real time video. Its cloud native platform handles high data volumes without performance loss, and the all inclusive pricing simplifies budgeting compared to Geotab’s marketplace add on model.
How do these alternatives compare to Geotab on total cost for a large enterprise over a three year period?
Geotab can be cost effective if you use only its base telematics, but costs rise when adding video and advanced analytics through third party partners. Verizon Connect tends to have higher upfront costs but lower per vehicle fees for large contracts. GPS Insight offers the most predictable pricing with no long term commitments. Samsara’s per vehicle rate is competitive at scale, especially when you consider the included video hardware and software.
