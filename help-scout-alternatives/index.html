Best Help Scout alternatives

Help Scout is loved by small teams for its clean design and human focus. But as your team grows, the monthly cost per agent can feel steep. If you run a tight budget yet still want a simple help desk, there are good options. This article covers five popular Help Scout alternatives. Some are cheaper, some offer free tiers, and all can work for small support teams who value ease of use.

alternative

Freshdesk

. Freshdesk offers a free plan that includes email ticketing and a knowledge base. Its paid plans start lower than Help Scout and scale with more features like automation and reporting. The interface is slightly more busy but still easy to learn.

Try Freshdesk →
alternative

Zendesk

. Zendesk is the most well known help desk but its standard plan often costs more per agent than Help Scout. For very small teams the basic plan might be worth it if you need advanced integrations. However it is not the cheapest alternative on this list.

alternative

Front

. Front is built around a shared inbox that feels like email. It is priced similarly to Help Scout but adds collaboration features like internal comments and channel switching. If your team lives in email and needs shared visibility, Front is a strong contender despite similar costs.

Try Front →
alternative

Intercom

. Intercom is powerful for in app messaging and customer communication. But it is typically more expensive than Help Scout and can be overkill for simple email based support. Consider it only if you also need live chat and automation at a higher price point.

alternative

Groove

. Groove is designed to be a simple, affordable alternative to Help Scout. Its interface is minimalist and it focuses on email support with a few extra features like a knowledge base. Pricing starts lower than Help Scout making it a direct fit for budget conscious teams.

Try Groove →

How they compare

- **Simplicity**: Groove and Freshdesk come closest to Help Scout’s clean interface. Zendesk and Intercom are more complex. - **Pricing**: Freshdesk (free tier) and Groove (lower starting price) are cheapest. Front is similar to Help Scout. Zendesk and Intercom are usually more expensive. - **Best for**: Groove for email only teams on a budget. Freshdesk for teams that want a free start and room to grow. Front for shared inbox power users. Zendesk for teams needing advanced integrations. Intercom for product led support.
