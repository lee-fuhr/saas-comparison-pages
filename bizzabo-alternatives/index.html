Bizzabo is a well known event platform, but it may not fit every brand’s need for data driven attendee engagement. Many organizations find that Bizzabo’s pricing or feature set does not match their specific goals, especially when they want deeper analytics or more flexible engagement tools. If you are looking for a solution that gives you clearer insights into attendee behavior and helps you act on that data in real time, exploring alternatives like Eventbrite, Hopin and Whova can point you toward a better fit.

alternative

Eventbrite

. Eventbrite focuses on event ticketing and registration with strong analytics on ticket sales and attendee demographics. It offers basic engagement features like email reminders and check ins but lacks the advanced attendee interaction tools that data driven brands often need. For straightforward events where registration data is your main priority, Eventbrite is a solid and affordable choice.

alternative

Hopin

. Hopin provides a full virtual and hybrid event platform with built in networking tools, breakout sessions and live chat, all of which generate attendee behavior data. Its analytics dashboard tracks session attendance, engagement duration and poll responses, making it easier for brands to measure participation quality. However, Hopin can be complex to set up and may require more training to fully use its data reporting capabilities.

alternative

Whova

. Whova is built around attendee engagement, offering features like live polling, Q&A, community boards and gamification that produce rich behavioral data. Its analytics module gives you insights into which sessions were most active, which speakers drew the highest engagement and how attendees connected with each other. Whova also provides post event reports that help you compare engagement across different event segments.

How they compare

When comparing these alternatives, Eventbrite is best for simple ticketing and basic registration data, but it offers limited attendee engagement analytics. Hopin excels in virtual and hybrid event logistics, providing detailed session level data but requiring more setup effort. Whova stands out for brands that prioritize deep attendee engagement metrics, such as poll participation and community activity, with an interface that is easy for both organizers and attendees to use. Your choice depends on whether you need lightweight registration data, comprehensive virtual event analytics, or focused engagement tracking.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative gives the most detailed attendee engagement data?
Whova is designed specifically for engagement tracking, providing analytics on polls, Q&A, community interactions and gaming elements. Hopin also offers strong session level data but focuses more on virtual event attendance rather than ongoing community engagement.
Can these alternatives integrate with my existing CRM or marketing tools?
Yes, all three platforms offer integrations with common CRM and marketing systems. Eventbrite connects with tools like Salesforce and Mailchimp, Hopin has integrations with HubSpot and Marketo, and Whova supports connections to Salesforce, Dynamics 365 and Zapier. Check each platform’s integration directory for your specific tools.
