Best TechTarget Priority Engine alternatives

TechTarget Priority Engine is a popular tool for capturing purchase intent signals from a network of technology-focused media sites. However, enterprises that rely on technographic intent data for account-based marketing (ABM) often need broader coverage, different data sources, or tighter integration with their existing sales stack. This article examines three strong alternatives: Bombora, G2 Buyer Intent, and Demandbase ABM, each offering distinct strengths for enterprise ABM programs.

alternative

Bombora

provides B2B intent data based on a cooperative of thousands of premium publisher sites. Its Company Surge model aggregates topic-level reading behavior to score account interest, which can complement or replace TechTarget’s technographic focus. Bombora’s data is widely integrated with major ABM and CRM platforms, making it suitable for enterprises that need scalable, cross-industry intent signals.

alternative

G2 Buyer Intent

sources intent data from its own marketplace of software reviews and buyer research content. It tracks account-level browsing behavior on G2’s platform, revealing which products and categories a company is actively evaluating. This makes it especially useful for enterprises that want technographic signals tied directly to the buyer’s research journey, often with faster time to signal.

alternative

Demandbase ABM

combines intent data, account identification, and AI-driven orchestration in a single platform. Its intent data draws from multiple sources including Bombora and its own network, and it offers technographic attributes as part of its account profile. Demandbase is ideal for enterprises that want a unified ABM solution rather than a standalone intent feed.

How they compare

TechTarget Priority Engine excels when your ideal customers are deeply engaged with IT-specific editorial content. Bombora offers broader publisher reach across industries. G2 Buyer Intent is strongest when software reviews and direct research behavior matter most. Demandbase provides the most integrated ABM platform, but may require more investment. Choose based on where your prospects actively research and how deeply you need intent data woven into your sales process.
