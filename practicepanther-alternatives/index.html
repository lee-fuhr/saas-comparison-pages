If you run a boutique law practice, PracticePanther may feel like a solid fit for case management and billing. But as your firm grows and you need smarter automated workflow tools, you might want to explore other options. The right platform should save you time on repetitive tasks, reduce manual data entry, and keep your team on track without adding complexity. Below we compare four leading PracticePanther alternatives to help you find the best match for your firm.

alternative

Clio

. Clio offers a comprehensive suite of practice management tools with strong document automation and client intake workflows. Its built-in time tracking and billing features integrate with over 200 apps, making it easy to customize automated processes for your boutique firm.

alternative

MyCase

. MyCase focuses on ease of use and client communication, with simple workflow templates for task management and calendaring. Its automated billing and payment reminders help reduce administrative overhead, and the client portal streamlines document sharing and approvals.

alternative

Smokeball

. Smokeball is built for smaller firms that want deep document automation and intelligent workflow triggers based on case events. It automatically populates documents from your case data and tracks time as you work, reducing manual entry and helping you stay compliant.

alternative

Filevine

. Filevine excels at creating custom workflow pipelines that move cases step by step with automated triggers and notifications. Its project management style is ideal for boutique practices that handle many similar matters and want a clear, repeatable process from intake to closing.

How they compare

All four alternatives offer automated workflows, but they differ in focus. Clio provides the broadest integration ecosystem and mature automation for billing and intake. MyCase is the simplest to set up with strong client-facing automation. Smokeball leads in document automation and time capture. Filevine offers the most customizable pipeline automation for repeatable case types. Choose based on whether you prioritize integrations (Clio), ease of use (MyCase), document workflows (Smokeball), or custom pipelines (Filevine).

Frequently asked questions

Can I import my data from PracticePanther into these alternatives?
Yes, most platforms offer data migration services or CSV import tools. Clio, MyCase, Smokeball, and Filevine all have dedicated onboarding teams that can help transfer your contacts, cases, and documents. Always check with the provider about current import capabilities.
Do these tools include automated document assembly and templates?
Yes, all four include some form of document automation. Smokeball is the strongest for document generation, while Filevine and Clio offer template libraries and merge fields. MyCase has basic document assembly with its built-in editor. For advanced automation, look at Smokeball or Filevine.
