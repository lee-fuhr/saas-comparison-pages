Synthesia is a leading platform for AI-generated video avatars, but it may not suit every business. Some teams need different pricing, more languages, or unique customization options. This article covers three strong Synthesia alternatives: Rephrase.ai, Colossyan, and DeepBrain AI. Each offers its own strengths for creating professional video content with digital avatars.

alternative

Rephrase.ai

. This platform focuses on creating personalized video avatars at scale, making it a good fit for marketing campaigns and customer outreach. It offers a wide library of pre-built avatars and allows you to upload your own footage to create a custom digital twin. Rephrase.ai also supports multiple languages and provides a simple text-to-video interface.

alternative

Colossyan

. Colossyan is built for corporate training and internal communications, with an emphasis on ease of use and rapid video creation. It offers realistic avatars that can speak in over 70 languages, and you can upload a script or use its AI assistant to generate one. The platform also includes collaboration features for teams and a built-in video editor for adding images and text.

alternative

DeepBrain AI

. DeepBrain AI focuses on ultra-realistic avatars with natural expressions and voice cloning capabilities. It is well suited for news broadcasting, customer service, and high-stakes presentations where realism matters. The platform provides a range of avatar styles and supports real-time video generation through an API, which is useful for interactive applications.

How they compare

All three tools let you create AI video avatars from text, but they differ in focus. Rephrase.ai excels at personalized, campaign-style videos. Colossyan is best for training and internal use with a simple workflow. DeepBrain AI offers the highest realism and is ideal for broadcast or real-time scenarios. Pricing varies widely, so you should check each platform for current plans. Synthesia remains a strong all-rounder, but these alternatives may better match specific business needs.

Frequently asked questions

Which synthetic avatar tool is most affordable for a small business?
Pricing changes often, so visit each platform’s website for the latest plans. Generally, Colossyan and Rephrase.ai have starter tiers that work for smaller teams, while DeepBrain AI is often higher priced due to its advanced realism.
Can I create a custom avatar that looks like a real person from my company?
Yes, all three alternatives support custom avatar creation. Rephrase.ai and DeepBrain AI let you upload video footage to train a digital twin. Colossyan offers a similar option, though you may need to contact their sales team for enterprise-level customization.
