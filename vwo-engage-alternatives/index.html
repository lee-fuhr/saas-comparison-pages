VWO Engage is a push notification tool built into the VWO platform, making it easy for optimization teams to pair messaging with heatmaps and A/B testing. But not every team finds that VWO Engage meets their specific needs for segmentation, delivery volume, or cross-platform support. If you need push notifications that work hand in hand with your existing testing and analytics stack, these alternatives offer strong integrations with heatmaps and experimentation tools.

alternative

OneSignal

. OneSignal provides a free tier with high delivery volume and native integrations with Google Analytics, Mixpanel, and many testing platforms. It supports web push, mobile push, and email, and its simple API makes it easy to connect with your own heatmap tools.

alternative

PushEngage

. PushEngage focuses on web push notifications with advanced automation and drip campaigns. It offers direct integrations with Google Optimize and Hotjar, so you can trigger messages based on heatmap insights and run tests alongside your pushes.

alternative

Pushwoosh

. Pushwoosh is a cross-platform notification service that includes rich analytics and segmentation. Its API can feed data into third-party heatmap tools, and it has a dedicated WordPress plugin and integration with Google Analytics to support testing workflows.

alternative

Airship

. Airship is an enterprise-grade platform with deep lifecycle messaging and real-time segmentation. It integrates with major analytics and testing suites like Adobe Analytics and Optimizely, making it suitable for teams that want push notifications tied to experimentation data.

alternative

CleverTap

. CleverTap combines push notifications with a full customer engagement engine including analytics and AI-driven segmentation. It works with tools like Google Optimize and Mixpanel, and its built-in heatmap feature gives you visual insights without a separate tool.

alternative

Kumulos

. Kumulos offers push notifications with a focus on mobile apps and real-time user engagement. It provides server-side integrations with analytics platforms and can send event data directly to heatmap tools through webhooks.

alternative

Pusher

. Pusher specializes in real-time messaging and push notifications for web and mobile apps. Its lightweight API can be combined with any heatmap or testing tool you already use, and it offers strong support for custom integrations.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative integrates best with VWO’s heatmaps and testing features?
OneSignal and PushEngage offer direct integrations with Google Optimize and Hotjar, which many teams pair with VWO. CleverTap has its own built-in heatmap and analytics, so you might not need a separate tool at all.
Can I migrate my existing VWO Engage subscribers to another tool?
Yes, most alternatives provide import options via CSV or API. OneSignal and Pushwoosh have documented migration guides. Expect to map your subscriber segments and custom properties to the new platform’s structure.
