Best AppFolio alternatives

If you manage multifamily properties and need automated leasing and maintenance, AppFolio is a popular choice. But it may not fit every budget or workflow. This guide compares four strong AppFolio alternatives: Propertyware, Buildium, Rent Manager, and TenantCloud. Each one offers different strengths for owners who want to streamline tenant screening, online rent collection, and work order management without paying for features you do not use.

alternative

Propertyware

. Built specifically for single-family and small multifamily portfolios, Propertyware gives you automated leasing tools like online applications and tenant screening. Its maintenance module lets residents submit requests and track progress, and you can customize workflows for different property types. It works well if you own 10 to 500 units and need detailed reporting.

alternative

Buildium

. Designed for mid-sized multifamily properties, Buildium combines automated lease renewals, online rent payments, and a maintenance portal that routes work orders to vendors. It integrates with popular accounting software and provides a resident portal for 24/7 communication. Buildium is a solid choice if you want an all-in-one system with strong financial features.

alternative

Rent Manager

. This desktop and cloud hybrid gives you full control over automated leasing, including custom lease terms and automatic late fee calculations. Its maintenance tracking allows you to dispatch vendors and track costs per unit, with mobile access for on-site staff. Rent Manager is ideal for owners who need deep customization and already have established workflows.

alternative

TenantCloud

. A budget-friendly option for smaller multifamily owners, TenantCloud offers automated leasing tools like online applications, credit checks, and digital lease signing. Its maintenance request system lets tenants upload photos and get instant notifications, plus you can manage multiple properties from one dashboard. It is a lightweight alternative that covers the basics without a steep learning curve.

How they compare

All four alternatives provide automated leasing and maintenance, but they differ in scale and price. Propertyware and Buildium are best for growing portfolios of 50 or more units, offering robust integrations and accounting. Rent Manager suits owners who need highly customizable workflows, especially for mixed-use or student housing. TenantCloud works well for smaller owners who want a simple, low-cost solution. AppFolio remains strong for its all-in-one marketing and leasing automation, but these alternatives can match or beat it on specific features like resident portals or vendor management.
