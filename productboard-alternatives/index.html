Best ProductBoard alternatives

If you are a product manager using ProductBoard, you already know it helps you collect feedback and build roadmaps. But maybe you have hit limits with its pricing, flexibility, or integration depth. This article covers five popular alternatives that can handle roadmap and feedback management in different ways. Each tool has strengths and trade offs, so consider your team size, budget, and preferred workflow when exploring these options.

alternative

Aha!

is a full featured product management platform with strong roadmapping, strategy alignment, and idea management. It connects to dozens of development tools and lets you link feedback directly to features and releases. The trade off is higher cost and a steeper learning curve compared to ProductBoard.

Try Aha! →
alternative

Canny

focuses specifically on feedback collection and prioritization with a clean, user friendly interface. It is lightweight for gathering customer requests and voting, but its roadmap features are simpler than ProductBoard’s. Canny works best when you want a dedicated feedback board that integrates with your existing roadmap tool.

Try Canny →
alternative

Jira Product Discovery

is an Atlassian tool built for teams already using Jira. It captures ideas, scores them, and connects directly to Jira issues for development. The downside is that it lives inside the Jira ecosystem, so it may feel limited if you need a standalone roadmap tool or do not use Jira.

alternative

Linear

is a fast, modern project management tool that appeals to engineering led teams. It offers a clean roadmap view and lightweight feedback capture through integrations like Slack or GitHub. Linear lacks the dedicated feedback board and voting features that ProductBoard provides, so it is better for teams that prioritize speed and simplicity over deep feedback management.

Try Linear →
alternative

Roadmunk

provides visual, drag and drop roadmaps with timeline views and stakeholder sharing. It has basic feedback collection via email and public boards, but its feedback features are less advanced than ProductBoard’s. Roadmunk shines when you need beautiful roadmaps to communicate strategy, not when you need a central feedback hub.

How they compare

Aha! is best for strategic alignment at scale, Canny for standalone feedback, Jira Product Discovery for Jira heavy teams, Linear for fast engineering workflows, and Roadmunk for visual roadmaps. ProductBoard sits in the middle with strong feedback and roadmap features. Evaluate based on your need for integration depth, pricing, and whether feedback or roadmapping is your primary pain point.

Frequently asked questions

What is the easiest alternative to switch to from ProductBoard if I only need feedback management?
Canny is the most straightforward choice because it focuses purely on collecting and prioritizing feedback. You can set up a public board in minutes and import your existing feedback data with a CSV export from ProductBoard.
Can I use Jira Product Discovery without having Jira Software?
No, Jira Product Discovery requires a Jira Software subscription because it extends Jira’s project and issue system. If your team does not use Jira, consider Aha!, Linear, or Roadmunk instead.
