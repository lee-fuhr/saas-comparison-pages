Best Bubble alternatives

Bubble is a powerful no-code platform for building web apps, but it can feel overwhelming with its complex design and steep learning curve. Many entrepreneurs building web apps without a developer look for Bubble alternatives that are simpler, faster to launch, or better suited for specific app types like data dashboards or marketplaces. This article covers three popular options – Glide, Adalo, and Softr – to help you choose the right fit.

alternative

Glide

turns spreadsheets into mobile-friendly web apps with minimal effort. You connect a Google Sheet or Glide Tables, then use prebuilt components to create a polished interface. It is ideal for internal tools, customer portals, and apps that rely on data updates rather than complex logic.

alternative

Adalo

offers a visual drag-and-drop builder with a focus on mobile app design. You can create custom screens, add databases, and integrate with third-party services using native components. It works well for social apps, booking systems, and simple marketplaces, but complex workflows may require workarounds.

alternative

Softr

transforms Airtable or Google Sheets into professional web apps and client portals. It provides prebuilt blocks for user authentication, payments, and content management. Softr is a strong choice for membership sites, directories, and simple ecommerce apps with low-code needs.

How they compare

Glide excels at rapid spreadsheet-to-app conversions, making it best for data-driven tools. Adalo offers more customization for mobile-friendly interfaces but has a moderate learning curve. Softr is the simplest for building client portals and member areas from Airtable data. Bubble remains the most flexible for complex logic, but these three alternatives trade some power for speed and ease of use. Consider your primary use case: data apps (Glide), mobile apps (Adalo), or client portals (Softr).
