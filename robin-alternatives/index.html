If your company uses Robin for desk booking and meeting room scheduling, you may be exploring other options. Robin works well for many teams, but perhaps you need deeper integrations, different pricing, or more advanced workplace analytics. This article covers four strong Robin alternatives: Envoy, OfficeSpace, Condeco, and Teem. Each offers a distinct approach to managing hybrid work and office space, so you can find the right fit for your organization.

alternative

Envoy

focuses on workplace experience beyond just desk booking. It combines visitor management, room scheduling, and desk reservations in one platform, making it ideal if you already use Envoy for front desk sign in or want a unified system. The interface is clean and simple, and it integrates with common tools like Slack, Google Calendar, and Okta.

alternative

OfficeSpace

is a robust space management platform built for larger enterprises. It offers detailed floor plans, hot desking, room scheduling, and utilization analytics. If you need granular control over office layout planning and want to track real time space usage, OfficeSpace provides powerful reporting without requiring a huge tech team to set up.

alternative

Condeco

specializes in enterprise level meeting room and desk booking with a strong focus on hardware integrations. It supports touch screen panels outside rooms, digital signage, and Outlook or Exchange synchronization. Condeco is a good choice if your company has many conference rooms and wants a reliable, high uptime system with certified integrations.

alternative

Teem

(by iOffice) emphasizes simplicity and user adoption. It offers desk and room booking through mobile apps and web, plus features like wayfinding, visitor management, and space analytics. Teem is often praised for its intuitive interface and quick setup, which helps get employees comfortable with hybrid scheduling fast.
