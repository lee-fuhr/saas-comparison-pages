If you are a startup that needs a lightweight SaaS tool for pipeline visualization and forecasting, you might have come across PipelineForecast. It offers simplicity, but maybe it is not the perfect fit for your team’s budget, feature set, or scalability. Fortunately, several alternatives provide similar pipeline tracking and forecasting capabilities, often with more flexible pricing or integrations that better suit early stage companies. This article covers five popular options – Clari, Aviso, BoostUp, People.ai, and SetSail – and highlights how they compare for startups that want a lean solution.

alternative

Clari

is a well known revenue intelligence platform that gives sales teams real time pipeline visibility and AI driven forecasts. While it is powerful, it can be pricey for very early stage startups, so it works best once you have a mature sales process and a dedicated operations team to use its advanced analytics.

alternative

Aviso

focuses on predictive forecasting and deal scoring, helping startups identify which opportunities are most likely to close. It integrates with common CRM systems like Salesforce and HubSpot, and its lightweight interface makes it easier to adopt without heavy onboarding.

alternative

BoostUp

offers pipeline health analysis and revenue forecasting with a strong emphasis on data cleanliness and rep activity tracking. Startups that want to spot risks early and improve forecast accuracy without a lot of manual data entry will find BoostUp’s automation valuable.

alternative

People.ai

uses activity data from email, calendars, and calls to automatically map pipeline movements and forecast changes. It is best for startups that already generate high volumes of sales activity and need to correlate that activity with revenue outcomes.

alternative

SetSail

is a lightweight tool that uses machine learning to surface the most important pipeline signals and provide accurate forecasts. Its simple dashboard and low setup overhead make it a good fit for small teams that want to move from spreadsheets to a data driven forecast quickly.

How they compare

Clari is the most comprehensive but comes with higher cost and complexity. Aviso and BoostUp offer strong forecasting and deal scoring with moderate pricing and easy CRM integration. People.ai requires more sales activity data to shine but gives deep behavioral insights. SetSail is the simplest and most startup friendly, focusing on core forecasting without extra features. For a lightweight replacement of PipelineForecast, SetSail and Aviso are the best starting points.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is easiest to set up for a small sales team without technical support?
SetSail and BoostUp both have quick onboarding and simple dashboards that require minimal IT involvement. They are designed for teams that want to start forecasting in days rather than weeks.
Do these tools work with free or low cost CRM systems like HubSpot’s starter tier?
Yes. Most alternatives integrate with HubSpot, Salesforce, and similar platforms. Clari and People.ai tend to require more data volume and a paid CRM tier, while Aviso, BoostUp, and SetSail work well with entry level CRM plans.
