As an HR Operations Lead at a multinational corporation, you know benefits administration is complex across different countries, currencies, and compliance rules. WEX Health is a popular platform for managing consumer-directed benefits like HSAs and FSAs, but it was built primarily for the U.S. market. If your global workforce needs broader coverage, or if you are looking for tools that handle multiple country requirements out of the box, exploring WEX Health alternatives is a smart move. Below are three strong contenders: Benefitfocus, Businessolver, and Alight.

alternative

Benefitfocus

offers a cloud-based platform that supports benefits administration for large employers, including multinationals. It integrates well with payroll and HRIS systems and provides a single dashboard for enrollment, billing, and compliance across multiple countries. However, its international capabilities are more mature for certain regions, so you should verify your specific country needs during demos.

alternative

Businessolver

delivers a benefits administration solution with a strong focus on employee experience, using AI and personalization to simplify enrollment. It can manage both U.S. and select global benefits, but its multinational support is not as deep as dedicated global platforms. Businessolver is best if your primary need is a seamless user interface and you have a mostly domestic workforce with some global locations.

alternative

Alight

is a comprehensive benefits, payroll, and HR outsourcing provider with a long history of serving large multinationals. It offers end-to-end global benefits administration, including compliance, vendor management, and employee support across many countries. Alight is ideal if you want a full-service partner, but its cost and implementation complexity are higher than standalone benefits platforms.

How they compare

Benefitfocus and Businessolver are more direct alternatives to WEX Health, with similar self-service enrollment and billing features, but they offer broader benefits types (like medical, dental, life) that WEX Health does not emphasize. Alight goes further by bundling benefits with payroll and HR outsourcing, making it a strong choice for companies that want a single vendor for multiple global services. All three surpass WEX Health in supporting non-U.S. benefits, but Alight has the most extensive global reach, followed by Benefitfocus and then Businessolver. Your choice depends on your workforce distribution, required benefits types, and whether you prefer a platform solution or a full outsourced service.
