Best Harvest alternatives

Harvest is a popular time tracking tool for freelancers and agencies, but it might not fit every workflow or budget. Whether you need built-in project management, simpler pricing, or offline tracking, several Harvest alternatives offer similar features with different strengths. This article compares five solid options to help you pick the right replacement.

alternative

Toggl

Toggl focuses on one-click time tracking and provides clear reports for billable hours. It integrates with hundreds of apps like Asana and QuickBooks, making it easy to work into existing workflows. The free plan covers basic tracking for up to five users.

Try Toggl →
alternative

Clockify

Clockify offers unlimited users on its free plan, which includes time tracking, reports, and project management basics. It is ideal for agencies that need a robust free tier without per‑user costs. Paid plans add features like scheduling and auto‑tracking.

Try Clockify →
alternative

Timely

Timely uses automatic time capture to log every app and website you visit, then lets you review and bill manually. This reduces the effort of remembering tasks at the end of the day. It is best for freelancers who want accuracy without constant manual entry.

Try Timely →
alternative

Everhour

Everhour sits inside project management tools like Asana, Trello, and Basecamp, showing timesheets right in those apps. It tracks time against tasks and budgets, giving agencies a clear view of project profitability. Its simple interface works well for teams already using a PM platform.

Try Everhour →
alternative

Paymo

Paymo combines time tracking with task management, invoicing, and resource scheduling in one tool. It is built for small creative agencies that need to manage the whole project lifecycle from tracking to billing. Paymo also offers a client portal and Gantt charts for planning.

How they compare

All five alternatives track time and generate reports for billing. Toggl and Clockify emphasize simplicity and free tiers, while Everhour and Paymo add deeper project management features. Timely stands out for automatic tracking, and Paymo is the most all‑in‑one. For freelancers on a tight budget, Clockify’s unlimited free plan is hard to beat. Agencies with complex workflows may prefer Everhour or Paymo for their built‑in task and budget tools.
