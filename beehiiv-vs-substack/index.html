If you are a newsletter creator looking at the two fastest-growing platforms right now, you have probably landed on Beehiiv versus Substack. Both let you start a newsletter quickly, but they serve different kinds of creators. Substack is the older, more established name, famous for helping writers build paid subscriptions. Beehiiv is the newer player that packs in more growth tools, ad networks, and design flexibility. Choosing between them depends on what you want your newsletter to become: a simple paid writing business or a full media operation.

Where Beehiiv wins

Beehiiv wins for creators who want more than just a newsletter. It includes a built-in referral program, a recommendation network, and a native ad marketplace where you can earn money by placing ads from other newsletters. You get a web site that looks like a modern blog, not just a text feed. Beehiiv also lets you create multiple audiences with different tiers, run polls, and split-test subject lines. Its growth features, like the ability to import subscribers and set up automated onboarding sequences, are stronger than what Substack offers. For creators who plan to scale fast, turn their newsletter into a business with multiple revenue streams, or use data to improve open rates, Beehiiv is the better choice.

Where Substack wins

Substack wins on simplicity and network effects. It is famously easy to start: you write, hit publish, and the platform handles payments, formatting, and reader discovery. The Substack network itself is a powerful distribution channel. Readers on Substack can discover your newsletter through recommendations from other writers they follow. That built-in audience reach is hard to replicate. Substack also has a cleaner, more editorial feel that many writers and readers prefer. If your main goal is to build a paid subscription base around your writing, and you want the least friction possible, Substack’s one-button setup and established reader ecosystem give it an edge. Its Notes feature also adds a lightweight social layer for community engagement.

Verdict

Choose Beehiiv versus Substack based on your ambition. Pick Beehiiv if you want a full-stack growth platform with ads, referrals, and advanced analytics. Pick Substack if you want a straightforward writing tool with strong organic discovery and a loyal reader network. Both are excellent, but Beehiiv is built for builders and Substack is built for writers. There is no wrong answer, only a different path.
