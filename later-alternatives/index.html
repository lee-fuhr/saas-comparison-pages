Later is a popular choice for visual Instagram planning, but it is not the only option. Many creators and brands look for later alternatives because of pricing changes, missing features, or a need for tools that work better with other platforms. Below are five strong alternatives that focus on visual scheduling, analytics, and team collaboration.

alternative

Buffer

. Buffer offers a simple scheduling tool that works across Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Its visual calendar and post previews make planning easy, and the analytics dashboard gives clear performance data. It is a good choice if you manage multiple social networks from one place.

alternative

Planoly

. Planoly is built for visual planning on Instagram and Pinterest. It includes a drag and drop grid preview, story planning, and user-generated content tools. Many creators like its simple interface and affordable starter plan.

Try Planoly →
alternative

Tailwind

. Tailwind specializes in Pinterest and Instagram scheduling with smart posting times based on your audience. It also has a Hashtag Finder and a dedicated posting queue for Instagram. This tool works well if you rely heavily on Pinterest traffic.

Try Tailwind →
alternative

Preview App

. Preview App focuses on helping you plan your Instagram feed and stories visually before posting. It offers hashtag groups, analytics, and a first comment scheduling feature. Its visual grid editor is especially useful for brands that care about feed aesthetics.

alternative

Hopper HQ

. Hopper HQ is a robust scheduler with a drag and drop calendar for Instagram. It supports story scheduling, bio link tools, and detailed analytics reports. It is a solid choice for teams that need multi-user access and approval workflows.

How they compare

All five tools offer visual planning for Instagram, but they differ in platform focus and extra features. Buffer and Tailwind support multiple networks beyond Instagram, while Planoly and Preview App are more Instagram-first. Hopper HQ stands out for team collaboration features. Pricing varies, with Buffer and Planoly offering free tiers and Tailwind, Preview App, and Hopper HQ having paid plans that start around the same range. Your choice depends on whether you need cross-platform posting, advanced analytics, or a strong visual grid editor.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for visual feed planning?
Preview App and Planoly both offer detailed grid previews that let you rearrange posts before they go live. Later also has this feature, but among the alternatives, these two are the most similar for visual feed planning.
Can I use these tools for scheduling Instagram Stories?
Yes, most of them support story scheduling. Hopper HQ, Planoly, and Preview App all allow you to schedule and preview Instagram Stories. Buffer and Tailwind also offer story scheduling, though Buffer’s story features are more limited on the free plan.
