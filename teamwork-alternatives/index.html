Best Teamwork alternatives

If your professional services firm uses Teamwork for project management and billing, you may be looking for alternatives that better handle client-facing workflows, time tracking, and invoicing. While Teamwork offers solid task management and collaboration features, some firms need stronger customization, simpler billing integration, or more intuitive client portals. Exploring Teamwork alternatives can help you find a platform that matches how your firm manages projects, tracks billable hours, and communicates with clients from kickoff to final invoice.

alternative

Monday.com

. Monday.com provides a highly visual interface with customizable boards, making it easy to track client projects at a glance. Its built-in time tracking and integrations with tools like QuickBooks and Xero support billing workflows, though you may need to set up automated rules to replicate Teamwork’s task dependencies and billing hierarchies.

alternative

Asana

. Asana excels at organizing complex client work with project templates, recurring tasks, and timeline views. While it offers native time tracking through its premium tiers, professional services firms often rely on third-party integrations like Harvest or Toggl for billing, and its client access features are limited to guest collaborators rather than full client portals.

alternative

ClickUp

. ClickUp is a highly customizable platform that combines project management, time tracking, and goal setting in one place. For professional services firms, its built-in invoicing and budgeting features (via ClickUp’s “Everything” view) can replace separate billing tools, but the learning curve may be steeper than Teamwork, especially for staff new to flexible work management software.

alternative

Smartsheet

. Smartsheet uses a spreadsheet-style interface that appeals to firms already familiar with Excel for project planning and billing. It includes time tracking, resource management, and integrations with accounting software, but its client portal and automated invoice generation are less polished than Teamwork’s dedicated billing module, so you may need to pair it with a separate invoicing tool.

How they compare

For professional services firms, Monday.com offers the most visual client progress tracking and easy billing integrations. Asana is best for detailed task management and collaboration but requires additional billing apps. ClickUp provides the most all-in-one customization for project and billing needs, though setup takes more effort. Smartsheet works well for spreadsheet-driven teams but lacks a seamless client billing experience out of the box. Teamwork remains strong if you already rely on its specific billing and client portal features.
