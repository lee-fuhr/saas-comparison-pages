If you are a content marketer who relies on Frase for SEO research, content briefs, and AI writing, you might be looking for other options. Maybe you need a tool with stronger copywriting features, a different pricing model, or better integration with your existing workflow. The good news is that there are several strong competitors in the AI content space. This article covers the most relevant Frase alternatives: Jasper, Copy.ai, Writesonic, Anyword, Hypotenuse, Cohesive AI, and Wordtune. Each tool brings something different to the table, and we will help you understand which one might fit your needs.

alternative

Jasper

. Jasper is one of the most well known AI writing platforms, especially for long form content like blog posts and marketing copy. It offers a powerful editor with brand voice settings, a built in SEO mode, and access to the latest GPT models. While Frase excels at research and briefs, Jasper focuses more on generating high quality drafts quickly.

alternative

Copy.ai

. Copy.ai is designed for fast, conversational AI writing that works well for short form content such as social media posts, email campaigns, and ad copy. It includes a “Workflow” builder that lets you automate multiple steps in a content creation process. For content marketers who need speed and simplicity over deep SEO research, Copy.ai can be a good alternative to Frase.

alternative

Writesonic

. Writesonic provides a broad set of tools including an AI article writer, a landing page generator, and a repurposing feature that turns blog posts into other formats. It also has a built in SEO checker and integrates with Surfer SEO. If you want a single platform that covers both AI writing and SEO optimization, Writesonic is worth considering.

alternative

Anyword

. Anyword specializes in predictive performance scoring for copy, meaning it analyzes your text and predicts how likely it is to convert across different channels. This makes it a strong choice for marketers who prioritize data driven writing for ads, emails, and landing pages. It is less research heavy than Frase but offers unique insights on copy effectiveness.

alternative

Hypotenuse

. Hypotenuse focuses on long form content generation with a strong emphasis on ecommerce and product descriptions. It also has a feature called “Clusters” that helps you create content hubs around a central topic. For content marketers in the retail or product space, Hypotenuse may be a more natural fit than Frase.

alternative

Cohesive AI

. Cohesive AI is an all in one content platform that combines AI writing with project management, collaboration, and templates. It includes tools for blog posts, social media, email, and even video scripts. While it does not have the deep SEO research of Frase, it offers a more complete workflow for teams that need to move from ideation to publication.

alternative

Wordtune

. Wordtune is not a full content generator like Frase. Instead, it is a rewriting and editing tool that helps you rephrase sentences, adjust tone, and shorten or expand text. It works as a browser extension and integrates with Google Docs, email, and other platforms. For content marketers who already have a research and drafting process and just need a smarter way to polish their writing, Wordtune is a lightweight but powerful supplement.
