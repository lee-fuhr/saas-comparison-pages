Metricool is a solid choice for agencies that need to manage social media posts and track basic analytics. But as your agency grows, you may find its reporting or team features too limited. You might also want deeper cross-platform insights or more advanced scheduling workflows. That is why looking at other tools can help you find a better fit for your clients and your internal team.

alternative

Hootsuite

. Hootsuite supports a wide range of social networks and offers a powerful scheduling engine with a bulk upload feature. Its analytics dashboards provide detailed cross-platform reports that agencies can customize for different clients. Team collaboration tools, including approval workflows and role based permissions, make it easy to manage large accounts.

alternative

Sprout Social

. Sprout Social is known for its comprehensive reporting and listening capabilities across multiple platforms. The tool gives agencies a clear view of audience engagement and competitor activity, which helps when presenting results to clients. Its unified inbox and automated workflows also save time when responding to messages from different networks.

alternative

Buffer

. Buffer keeps scheduling simple and offers a clean interface that many agencies find easy to teach to clients. Its analytics focus on key performance metrics like reach and engagement, and the tool integrates with popular business platforms such as Slack and Canva. Although it lacks some advanced features like deep social listening, its pricing is straightforward and team plans are affordable.

How they compare

Metricool is a good budget option with basic scheduling and analytics. Hootsuite offers the widest network support and the most robust scheduling features for agencies with many clients. Sprout Social provides the deepest analytics and social listening tools, which is ideal if you need to prove ROI to clients. Buffer is the easiest to use and cheapest, but its analytics are simpler and it does not support as many platforms as the others.

Frequently asked questions

Which tool is best for an agency that needs detailed client reports?
Sprout Social has the most advanced reporting and listening features, making it a strong choice for agencies that must show deep insights across platforms. Hootsuite also offers customizable reports, but Sprout Social’s data visualizations are generally considered more thorough.
Can I migrate my scheduled posts from Metricool to another tool easily?
Most of these alternatives allow you to import post drafts through CSV files or direct uploads, but the process varies. Hootsuite and Buffer both support bulk uploads from a spreadsheet, while Sprout Social requires you to manually recreate posts or use its API. Check each tool’s help center for exact steps.
