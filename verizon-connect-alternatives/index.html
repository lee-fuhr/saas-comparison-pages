Best Verizon Connect alternatives

If you manage a logistics fleet, you know that routing efficiency and driver safety go hand in hand. Verizon Connect is a popular choice, but it may not fit every operation – whether due to cost, feature gaps, or integration limitations. The good news is that several strong alternatives offer integrated routing and driver safety tools. This article covers three of them: Samsara, Geotab, and GPS Insight. Each can help you reduce fuel costs, improve on-time delivery, and keep your drivers safe without locking you into a single vendor.

alternative

Samsara

combines real-time GPS tracking with a modern, user-friendly dashboard that covers both routing optimization and driver safety. Its AI-powered dashcams provide immediate coaching alerts for risky behaviors like harsh braking or following too close, and the platform integrates route planning directly with dispatch. Samsara also offers a single platform for vehicles, trailers, and equipment, which can simplify your tech stack if you run a mixed fleet.

alternative

Geotab

is a highly customizable platform built around an open telematics gateway. It supports over 200 third-party add-ins, so you can pair its driver safety scoring and real-time routing features with the exact tools your logistics team already uses. The MyGeotab Marketplace gives you flexibility to add dashcams, ELDs, or advanced route optimization apps without switching platforms. Geotab’s strength lies in data depth and scalability for large fleets.

alternative

GPS Insight

focuses on ease of use and responsive customer support, making it a good fit for mid-size logistics operations. Its route optimization engine considers traffic, stops, and driver hours, while the driver safety module includes behavior monitoring and coaching tools. GPS Insight also offers a dedicated mobile app for drivers to view routes and receive safety alerts, helping close the loop between the office and the road.

How they compare

All three alternatives beat Verizon Connect in certain areas. Samsara leads on dashcam integration and user experience. Geotab offers the most flexibility through its open platform and app marketplace. GPS Insight provides a simpler, more support-focused approach for teams that want less customization. Your choice depends on your priority: if you want all-in-one with great hardware, choose Samsara. If you need deep data control and scalability, go with Geotab. If you prefer a lower cost with strong customer service, GPS Insight is a solid option.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my existing vehicle data from Verizon Connect to one of these alternatives?
Yes, most providers offer data migration services or export tools. Samsara and Geotab have dedicated onboarding teams that can transfer historical GPS, route, and driver data. GPS Insight can also handle bulk imports, but you should check with their sales team about specific file formats.
Do these alternatives work with my current ELD or dashcam hardware?
It depends. Samsara requires its own hardware, while Geotab is a gateway that can be paired with many third-party devices through its Marketplace. GPS Insight supports a limited set of compatible hardware from its own ecosystem. Discuss your existing equipment with each vendor before committing.
