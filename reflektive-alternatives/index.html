Reflektive has long been a go to platform for performance management, but many enterprises are now reviewing their options. Whether you are facing shifting pricing, feature gaps, or a need for deeper talent analytics and calibration support, it makes sense to explore other tools. This article covers five Reflektive alternatives that can handle enterprise grade performance reviews, calibration meetings, and people analytics. Each option is evaluated for its suitability for large teams that require structured feedback cycles and data driven talent decisions.

alternative

Leapsome

. Leapsome combines performance reviews, 360 degree feedback, and goal tracking with a strong focus on employee engagement surveys. Its calibration module allows managers to compare employee ratings side by side, making it easier to agree on performance tiers across departments. The platform also offers advanced talent analytics that highlight retention risks and skill gaps, which is critical for enterprise HR teams. **15Five**. 15Five is built around continuous feedback, weekly check ins, and performance reviews that can be customized for different roles and levels. Its “Best Self” review framework supports peer feedback and self assessments, while the analytics dashboard tracks engagement trends and performance distribution. For calibration, 15Five provides a separate “Performance Review” module where managers can discuss and adjust ratings in a structured workflow.

alternative

Betterworks

. Betterworks focuses on aligning individual goals with company objectives through OKRs and continuous performance management. The platform includes a dedicated calibration session tool that lets HR set up multi rater reviews and facilitate discussion rounds with scorecards. Its analytics engine surfaces insights on goal progress, performance outliers, and promotion readiness, which helps enterprises make data backed talent decisions.

alternative

Officevibe

. Officevibe is primarily an employee engagement and feedback tool, but it also offers lightweight performance review capabilities such as pulse surveys and one on one meeting templates. While it lacks a full calibration module, its analytics provide real time team sentiment and manager effectiveness scores. Enterprises that need a simple, engaging alternative for day to day feedback rather than heavy calibration workflows may find it useful as a complementary system.

alternative

Weekdone

. Weekdone is a lean OKR and performance tracking platform that helps teams set, track, and review goals on a weekly basis. It includes basic performance review features with rating scales and comment fields, but does not offer a dedicated calibration tool. The analytics focus on goal completion rates and team productivity trends. It works best for smaller enterprises or those just starting to structure their performance process without needing complex calibration workflows.

How they compare

Leapsome offers the most complete enterprise calibration and analytics package among these alternatives. Betterworks excels at goal alignment and structured review sessions. 15Five is strongest for continuous feedback and weekly check ins, while Officevibe is best for employee engagement monitoring. Weekdone is a simpler, lower cost option for teams focused on OKR tracking without advanced calibration. Enterprises that require formal calibration processes should prioritize Leapsome or Betterworks; those needing lightweight feedback can consider 15Five or Officevibe.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for replacing Reflektive’s calibration module?
Leapsome is the closest match for calibration. It provides side by side rating comparisons, calibration meetings with discussion logs, and analytics that show rating distributions across teams. Betterworks also offers strong calibration sessions with scorecards and group reviews.
Do any of these alternatives integrate with our existing HRIS or SSO?
Yes, all five tools support major HRIS integrations such as Workday, BambooHR, and SAP SuccessFactors. They also offer SAML based single sign on. You should verify with each vendor for your specific system, but enterprise grade integration is a standard feature across these platforms.
