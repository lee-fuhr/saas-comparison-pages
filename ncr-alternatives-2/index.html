Best NCR alternatives

For large merchants with high transaction volumes and complex operations, NCR has long been a go-to provider of integrated payment hardware and point of sale software. However, rising costs, inflexible contracts, or the need for modern cloud features may lead you to explore other options. This review compares NCR with Square POS, Lightspeed Retail, Shopify POS, and Vend to help you find a reliable, scalable system that works with your existing hardware or offers new integrated terminals.

alternative

Square POS

Square offers a full integrated payment hardware ecosystem, including countertop terminals, chip readers, and contactless devices, with no long-term contracts or monthly minimums. For large merchants, Square’s advanced reporting, inventory management, and payroll features scale well, though its processing rates can be higher for high-volume businesses compared to negotiated NCR rates.

alternative

Lightspeed Retail

Lightspeed provides a comprehensive cloud-based POS with strong inventory and multi-location management, and it integrates with payment processors like Stripe or its own Lightspeed Payments hardware. Large merchants benefit from its robust reporting and B2B ecommerce capabilities, but the hardware options are less extensive than NCR’s, and you may need to source terminals separately.

alternative

Shopify POS

Shopify POS offers a unified system for retail and ecommerce, with integrated payment hardware such as the Shopify Tap & Chip reader and retail stand for in-store use. For larger merchants, its scalability, omnichannel inventory sync, and built-in payments (Shopify Payments) are major advantages, though hardware options are limited to Shopify’s own devices and the platform focuses on businesses primarily selling online.

alternative

Vend

Vend is a cloud-based POS built for retail, offering lightweight hardware compatibility (including iPads and off-the-shelf card readers) and strong inventory management across multiple locations. While it provides a simple, affordable setup, its integrated payment hardware options are more limited than NCR’s enterprise terminals, and larger merchants may find its features less robust for complex supply chains.

How they compare

NCR remains strong for dedicated, high-capacity hardware and on-premise reliability. Square and Shopify POS offer seamless, all-in-one payment hardware and software ecosystems with lower upfront costs but potentially higher transaction fees. Lightspeed and Vend provide flexible cloud tools with solid inventory and multi-store tools but require more attention to hardware compatibility and payment integration. Choose the system that best matches your hardware needs, transaction volume, and long-term contract preferences.
