Best Rollbar alternatives

Rollbar is a popular error monitoring and debugging tool used by many teams. However, site reliability engineers (SREs) may look for Rollbar alternatives due to pricing changes, specific integration gaps, or the need for different alerting workflows. The right choice depends on factors like data retention, on-premises options, and how deeply the tool connects with your existing incident response stack. This article compares four common alternatives: Sentry, Bugsnag, Raygun, and Airbrake.

alternative

Sentry

is an open source error tracking platform that offers self-hosted and cloud options, making it a strong fit for SREs who want full data control. It supports a wide range of languages and frameworks, and its performance monitoring features help correlate errors with slow transactions. Sentry’s alerting and release tracking are well suited for continuous deployment pipelines.

alternative

Bugsnag

focuses on stability metrics and provides a clear dashboard showing crash rate, user impact, and error trends over time. It integrates natively with popular CI/CD and collaboration tools, and its stability scores make it easy to set quality gates in your deployment process. Bugsnag also offers a server-side agent for backend monitoring.

alternative

Raygun

combines error monitoring with real user monitoring and performance diagnostics, giving SREs a single pane of glass for both frontend and backend issues. Its “Crash Reporting” and “Application Performance Monitoring” features help identify the root cause of user-facing errors. Raygun also provides customizable alert rules that can route notifications to your incident management tool.

alternative

Airbrake

is a straightforward error tracker that emphasizes simplicity and quick setup. It supports a variety of languages and provides clear grouping of similar errors to reduce noise. Airbrake also offers deploy tracking and a simple API, making it a good choice for teams that want a lightweight alternative without a steep learning curve.
