As a Director of Safety, you know that the right telematics and dashcam solution can reduce accidents, improve driver behavior, and lower your total cost of risk. Verizon Connect is a well known platform, but it may not fit every fleet’s specific needs, budget, or integration requirements. Evaluating alternatives like Geotab, Samsara, and Motive can help you find a system that better aligns with your safety goals, reporting demands, and operational workflows.

alternative

Geotab

offers a highly customizable open platform with a vast marketplace of third party add ons, making it a strong choice for fleets that need deep data integration and flexible reporting. Its hardware is vehicle agnostic and known for reliable GPS tracking, though its dashcam capabilities are generally separate add ons rather than built in. For safety directors who want to build a tailored telematics stack, Geotab provides the control and scalability to match complex requirements.

alternative

Samsara

combines dashcams, vehicle gateways, and environmental sensors into a single, cloud connected system that is easy to deploy and manage from one dashboard. Its AI powered dashcam alerts for events like distracted driving or following too closely are designed to coach drivers in real time, which can directly reduce risky behaviors. The platform’s user friendly interface and automatic reporting make it a popular alternative for safety teams that want a unified, low maintenance solution.

alternative

Motive

(formerly KeepTrucking) focuses heavily on driver safety and compliance, integrating dashcams with electronic logging devices (ELDs) and real time coaching tools. Its safety scorecards and video based event reviews give safety directors clear, actionable data to target high risk drivers. Motive is especially strong for fleets that prioritize hours of service compliance and want a straightforward, all in one platform that reduces administrative overhead.

How they compare

All three alternatives provide robust GPS tracking and dashcam capabilities, but they differ in approach. Geotab excels in data customization and integration, Samsara offers a seamless unified experience with strong AI dashcam features, and Motive emphasizes driver safety and compliance in a single platform. Verizon Connect users may find Samsara or Motive easier to transition to because of similar all in one interfaces, while Geotab appeals to those who want more control over data and third party tools. Your choice should depend on whether your priority is integration flexibility, ease of use, or combined ELD and safety management.
