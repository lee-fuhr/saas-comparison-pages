LinkedIn Sales Navigator is a powerful tool for B2B prospecting, but it is not the only option. Many sales teams look for alternatives to get more data, better pricing, or features that fit their specific workflow. The tools below are widely used by B2B salespeople who want to find and contact leads without relying solely on LinkedIn. Each has strengths in areas like contact data, enrichment, or multichannel outreach.

alternative

Apollo.io

Apollo combines a large B2B database with built-in email sequencing and CRM integration. It is a good choice for salespeople who want to both find leads and run outreach from a single platform, though data accuracy can be inconsistent.

alternative

ZoomInfo

ZoomInfo offers a massive, continuously updated database of company and contact information. It is best for enterprise teams that need deep firmographic data and intent signals, but the cost is higher than most alternatives.

alternative

Lusha

Lusha provides verified direct dials and email addresses for individuals and companies. It is a lightweight, pay-as-you-go solution that works well as a supplement to LinkedIn rather than a complete replacement.

alternative

Clay

Clay is a data enrichment and research platform that lets you layer multiple data sources and run personalized outreach at scale. It is ideal for power users who want to build custom workflows, but it requires more setup than out-of-box tools.

alternative

Cognism

Cognism specializes in GDPR-compliant contact data for European and global prospecting. It includes phone-number verification and intent data, making it a strong alternative for teams that prioritize compliance and data quality.

How they compare

Choose Apollo.io if you want an all-in-one prospecting and outreach tool at a moderate price. Choose ZoomInfo if you need enterprise-grade company data and can afford the premium. Choose Lusha if you only need quick, verified individual contacts and prefer a simple usage-based model. Choose Clay if you are tech-savvy and want to build custom enrichment pipelines. Choose Cognism if GDPR compliance and high-quality EU data are your top priorities.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives replace all the features of LinkedIn Sales Navigator?
Not entirely. Most alternatives focus on contact data and outreach, while Sales Navigator excels at LinkedIn-specific social selling features like relationship tracking and InMail. You may need to use a combination of tools to get the same coverage.
Which alternative is best for a small team with a tight budget?
Lusha or Apollo.io are the most cost-effective options. Lusha works on a credit system with no monthly minimum, and Apollo offers a free tier with limited data. Both let you start small and scale as your team grows.
