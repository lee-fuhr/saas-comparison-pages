Choosing the right agency management system is a big decision for an agency with over 100 staff. Applied Epic is a powerful, feature rich platform, but it is not the only option. As your agency grows, you may want to compare systems on scalability, integration ease, automation, and total cost of ownership. This article walks you through three common alternatives to Applied Epic: Vertafore, EZLynx, and HawkSoft. Each has strengths and trade offs that matter for a large agency.

alternative

Vertafore

offers a suite of management tools including AMS360, which directly competes with Applied Epic. Its cloud based platform scales well for large agencies and includes strong reporting, accounting, and download capabilities. However, the migration process can be complex and ongoing licensing costs tend to be high.

alternative

EZLynx

is known for its fast comparative rater and user friendly interface. It handles core policy management and document storage well, but agencies with over 100 staff often find its advanced reporting and workflow customization options less robust than Epic’s. Integration with third party tools may also require extra effort.

alternative

HawkSoft

emphasizes simplicity and a clean user experience. It works well for independent agencies that value a single, streamlined interface. For a larger agency, though, HawkSoft’s limited automation features and smaller partner ecosystem can be a drawback compared to Applied Epic’s extensive integrations.

How they compare

When comparing Applied Epic and its alternatives for a 100+ staff agency, consider three main areas: scalability, integration depth, and cost. Applied Epic and Vertafore (AMS360) are both built for large operations with advanced accounting, document management, and reporting. EZLynx is easier to learn but may require workarounds for complex agency needs. HawkSoft is the simplest option but lacks the scale and automation larger agencies typically require. In general, the more staff you have, the more important it becomes to choose a system that supports robust workflows, carrier downloads, and customized reporting without heavy manual work.
