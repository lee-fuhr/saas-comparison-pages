Best Flodesk alternatives

If you are a designer or visual creator who loves Flodesk’s clean templates but are looking for a similar experience with different features or pricing, you have plenty of solid options. The best Flodesk alternatives should let you build beautiful emails without wrestling with code, while still giving you the control you need over your audience and campaigns. Below we compare five popular tools that balance visual design with ease of use.

alternative

ConvertKit

. ConvertKit focuses on audience segmentation and automation, making it a strong choice if you sell digital products or run a membership. Its visual builder is simpler than Flodesk’s but still clean, and you can customize templates with basic CSS if needed.

Try ConvertKit →
alternative

Mailchimp

. Mailchimp offers a drag and drop editor with hundreds of pre-designed templates and a free plan for small lists. The interface can feel cluttered, but the design flexibility and built-in analytics are powerful for creators who want detailed reports.

Try Mailchimp →
alternative

MailerLite

. MailerLite combines a minimalist drag and drop editor with rich text blocks and image galleries, giving you a Flodesk-like aesthetic at a lower price. It also includes a landing page builder and a native pop-up form tool, all without coding.

Try MailerLite →
alternative

ActiveCampaign

. ActiveCampaign is built for advanced automation and personalization, with a visual builder that lets you map complex customer journeys. Its email designer requires a short learning curve, but it rewards you with dynamic content and split testing features.

alternative

Brevo

. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) offers a responsive drag and drop editor alongside transactional email and SMS features. Its template library is smaller than Flodesk’s, but the tool integrates with many ecommerce platforms and provides a free tier for up to 300 emails per day.
