Best Smartsupp alternatives

If you run an e-commerce store, you know how valuable it is to see what visitors do on your site while you chat with them. Smartsupp is a popular choice because it combines live chat with video session recordings (also called visitor replay). But maybe you need a different pricing model, want deeper integrations, or are just shopping around. This article looks at seven Smartsupp alternatives: Olark, Chaport, JivoChat, Pure Chat, LiveChat, Tidio, and Intercom. We will focus on how well each tool supports video recordings of visitor sessions alongside chat.

alternative

Olark

offers a straightforward live chat tool with strong automation and canned responses. It tracks visitor pages and referral sources, but it does not include video session recordings, so you cannot play back a visitor’s screen movements.

alternative

Chaport

provides a feature-rich chat platform with chatbots and team inbox features. Its visitor monitoring shows pages viewed, location, and time on site, but it lacks built-in video session replay.

alternative

JivoChat

combines live chat with callbacks and a mobile app for on-the-go support. It monitors visitor activity such as page visits and browsing history, but it does not record full video sessions.

alternative

Pure Chat

focuses on simple, affordable live chat with real-time visitor tracking. You can see which page a visitor is on and how long they have been there, but there are no video recordings of their browsing behavior.

alternative

LiveChat

is a comprehensive help desk and chat solution with extensive integrations and reporting. While it offers detailed visitor logs and screen sharing during chats, it does not provide automatic video recordings of all visitor sessions.

alternative

Tidio

brings together live chat, chatbots, and email marketing in one interface. Its visitor list shows pages viewed and engagement scores, but video session replay is not a native feature.

alternative

Intercom

is a full customer communications platform with chatbots, automated messages, and a knowledge base. It tracks visitor behavior through events and pages, yet it does not include video recordings of sessions.

How they compare

All of the alternatives listed focus on live chat and basic visitor tracking (page views, time on site, location). None of them include the video session replay feature that Smartsupp is known for. If video recordings of visitor sessions are essential for your e-commerce store, Smartsupp remains the only tool in this group that offers that capability out of the box. However, the other tools may still be a good fit if your priority is chatbot automation (Tidio, Chaport), extensive integrations (LiveChat, Intercom), or budget simplicity (Pure Chat, Olark). You can also consider pairing one of these chat tools with a dedicated session recording service like Hotjar or FullStory, but that adds cost and complexity.
