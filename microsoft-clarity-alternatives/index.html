Best Microsoft Clarity alternatives

Microsoft Clarity is a free heatmap and session recording tool that helps website owners see how visitors interact with their pages. However, you might want alternatives for reasons like privacy concerns, different feature priorities, or a need for simpler tools. Below are four free or freemium options to consider. Each has its own strengths and limitations when it comes to heatmaps.

alternative

Hotjar

. Hotjar offers a free plan that includes heatmaps, session recordings, and feedback widgets. It limits you to 35 daily sessions and 3 heatmaps, which works well for small websites testing user behavior.

alternative

Matomo

. Matomo provides a free self-hosted analytics platform where you own all your data. However, its heatmap and session recording features require a paid plugin, so the free version does not include these capabilities.

alternative

Clicky

. Clicky has a free tier with real-time analytics and uptime monitoring. Heatmaps are not part of the free plan, only available with paid subscriptions starting at a higher price point.

alternative

Simple Analytics

. Simple Analytics is a privacy-first tool that tracks page views and events without cookies. It does not offer heatmaps or session recordings, making it an option only if you do not need visual behavior data.

How they compare

Microsoft Clarity is completely free with unlimited heatmaps and session recordings, but it uses cookies and Microsoft infrastructure. Hotjar is free but limited to 35 daily sessions, suitable for small sites. Matomo is free only for basic analytics, with heatmaps locked behind a paid plugin. Clicky offers heatmaps only on paid plans. Simple Analytics lacks heatmaps entirely and focuses on simple, privacy-respecting stats. Choose based on your budget, privacy needs, and whether you require heatmaps at no cost.
