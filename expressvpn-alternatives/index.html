If you are a traveler who needs reliable streaming and strong encryption, you might already know ExpressVPN as a top choice. But maybe you are looking for a different price point or a feature set that better matches your travel habits. Here are three strong alternatives that compete with ExpressVPN for speed, security, and streaming performance.

alternative

NordVPN

. This provider offers a large server network that works well for streaming services like Netflix and BBC iPlayer. It includes strong encryption and a strict no-logs policy, plus a threat protection feature that blocks ads and malware. NordVPN also has a proprietary NordLynx protocol for fast connections.

alternative

Surfshark

. Surfshark lets you connect an unlimited number of devices on one subscription, which is great for travelers with multiple gadgets. It unblocks many streaming platforms and includes a CleanWeb feature to block ads and trackers. Surfshark also offers a camouflage mode that hides your VPN use from your internet provider.

alternative

Mullvad

. Mullvad focuses on privacy and simplicity, with a flat monthly fee and no personal information required to sign up. It uses strong encryption and supports WireGuard and OpenVPN protocols. While Mullvad does not prioritize streaming, it can still access some services and is excellent for secure browsing in countries with restricted internet.

How they compare

ExpressVPN remains a strong all rounder for streaming and encryption, but NordVPN matches it with a huge server fleet and extra security tools. Surfshark wins on value with unlimited connections and solid streaming support. Mullvad is the best choice if privacy is your top priority and streaming is secondary.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use these alternatives to watch Netflix while traveling?
Yes. NordVPN and Surfshark reliably unblock Netflix libraries in different regions. Mullvad may work with some Netflix content but is less consistent for streaming.
Which alternative has the strongest encryption for public Wi Fi?
All three use AES 256 bit encryption as the default, which is considered strong. NordVPN and Surfshark add extra features like a kill switch and DNS leak protection, while Mullvad is trusted for its transparent, no frills security.
