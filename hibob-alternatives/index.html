If your mid-size company uses HiBob, you already know it offers a modern, employee-centric HR platform. However, growing needs around payroll, IT management, or budget can lead you to explore other options. This article covers five strong alternatives that provide different features or pricing models to better match your goals.

alternative

BambooHR

. Built for growing companies, BambooHR focuses on core HR tasks like hiring, time-off tracking, and employee data management. It offers straightforward reporting and a clean interface, but its payroll and advanced analytics are more limited than HiBob’s.

alternative

Rippling

. Rippling combines HR, IT, and finance in one system, letting you manage devices, apps, and payroll alongside employee records. It automates many onboarding and offboarding steps, making it a strong choice if you want to reduce manual work across departments.

Try Rippling →
alternative

Personio

. Designed for European mid-size companies, Personio covers the full employee lifecycle with features for recruiting, performance, and absence management. Its pricing is often more transparent than HiBob’s, but its payroll integrations vary by country.

alternative

Gusto

. Best known for automated payroll and benefits administration, Gusto also offers basic HR and time tracking. It is simpler and more affordable than HiBob, but it lacks the depth of global compliance and advanced people analytics that larger organizations might need.

alternative

Namely

. Namely provides an all-in-one HR, payroll, and benefits platform with a focus on employee engagement and compliance. Its modular setup allows you to pick only the features you need, which can lower costs compared to HiBob’s broader suite.

How they compare

HiBob excels in employee experience and global flexibility, but its pricing can climb quickly as you add users. BambooHR and Gusto are more cost-effective for companies that prioritize core HR and US payroll. Rippling adds valuable IT controls, Personio offers strong European compliance, and Namely gives modular bundles. Your choice depends on whether you need deeper payroll, IT automation, or simpler administration.

Frequently asked questions

How do I migrate data from HiBob to another HR platform?
Most alternatives offer data import tools or professional services that map your employee records, time-off balances, and documents. Plan for a few weeks to clean up data and test integrations before going live.
Will switching from HiBob save my mid-size company money?
It depends on your feature needs. Simpler tools like Gusto or BambooHR often have lower per-user fees, while Rippling or Personio may cost similar to HiBob but bundle services you already pay for separately. Request custom quotes and compare total costs.
