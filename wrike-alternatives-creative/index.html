Best Wrike alternatives

Creative teams often rely on Wrike for task management, Gantt charts, and proofing tools. But some teams find Wrike too complex or expensive for their workflow. If you need a simpler or more affordable solution that still offers robust task-centric work management with Gantt chart views and built-in proofing features, here are four solid alternatives to consider: Asana, ClickUp, Monday.com, and Smartsheet.

alternative

Asana

. Asana provides strong task management with multiple project views including Gantt-style timelines and a built-in proofing tool for reviewing creative assets. It is known for its clean interface and automation rules, making it a good fit for teams that prioritize ease of use over extensive customization.

alternative

ClickUp

. ClickUp offers extensive customization including Gantt charts, task dependencies, and a dedicated proofing feature for images and PDFs. It is highly flexible and can adapt to many workflows, but its depth can be overwhelming for smaller creative teams.

alternative

Monday.com

. Monday.com features intuitive Gantt charts via its timeline view and includes a basic proofing and approval system within the platform. Its visual, board-based approach works well for teams that prefer a spreadsheet-like interface with drag-and-drop simplicity.

alternative

Smartsheet

. Smartsheet is a spreadsheet-focused tool with powerful Gantt charts and resource management, plus optional proofing through add-ons or integrations. It is best for creative teams that need detailed project scheduling and reporting, though its proofing capabilities are not as native as other options.

How they compare

All four tools offer task-centric work management and Gantt chart views, but they differ in proofing features and ease of use. Asana and ClickUp have more mature built-in proofing, while Monday.com and Smartsheet require extra steps or integrations for full asset review. Asana is the simplest, ClickUp the most customizable, Monday.com the most visual, and Smartsheet the most spreadsheet-like. Choose based on your team’s size, need for customization, and the priority of native proofing over other features.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative has the most similar feature set to Wrike for creative teams?
ClickUp is the closest match because it combines Gantt charts, task dependencies, time tracking, and integrated proofing, much like Wrike. It also allows for custom workflows and views.
Can I use these tools for video proofing and approvals?
Most of these tools support image and PDF proofing natively, but for video proofing you may need an additional integration. Asana and ClickUp offer video proofing through third party apps like Frame.io, while Monday.com and Smartsheet require separate add-ons. Check each tool’s integration marketplace to confirm.
