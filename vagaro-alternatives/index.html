Best Vagaro alternatives

If you run a wellness or beauty business and are looking for scheduling and management software, you have likely come across Vagaro. While it offers many features, some businesses find that its pricing, user interface, or specific functionality does not fit their needs. This article explores four popular Vagaro alternatives: Mindbody, Zenoti, Acuity Scheduling, and Square Appointments. Each tool is built for different business sizes and workflows, so understanding their strengths can help you choose the right fit for your spa, salon, or wellness center.

alternative

Mindbody

is a well established platform that serves both small studios and large chains in the wellness and fitness space. It offers online booking, marketing tools, and a marketplace that helps clients discover your business. Keep in mind that Mindbody often requires a contract and has higher monthly fees, which may not suit budget conscious independent operators.

alternative

Zenoti

is an enterprise grade solution designed for multi location salons, spas, and medical aesthetics practices. It provides advanced reporting, inventory management, and robust member management features. Because of its complexity and pricing, Zenoti is best suited for established businesses with multiple locations rather than single owner operators.

alternative

Acuity Scheduling

is a lightweight, user friendly tool that focuses on online booking and client self service. It integrates easily with many payment processors and calendar apps, and its pricing is transparent with no long term contracts. Acuity is ideal for solopreneurs or small teams who need simple scheduling without extra features like payroll or marketing automation.

alternative

Square Appointments

is a free or low cost option that pairs seamlessly with Square’s payment processing ecosystem. It includes basic booking, client management, and point of sale capabilities. This alternative works well for businesses that already use Square for payments and want a simple, no frills scheduler with minimal monthly costs.

How they compare

For a quick overview: Mindbody and Zenoti are powerful but expensive and target larger or multi location businesses. Acuity Scheduling offers simplicity and affordability for independent practitioners. Square Appointments is the most budget friendly option if you already use Square payments. Consider your business size, feature needs, and budget when choosing among these Vagaro alternatives.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my client data from Vagaro to another tool easily?
Most alternatives offer import tools or CSV uploads to transfer client names, contact info, and appointment history. Check each provider’s migration guide or support team for specific steps, as some data fields may not transfer automatically.
Which alternative is best for a solo esthetician or massage therapist?
Acuity Scheduling is a strong choice for solo practitioners because of its low cost, simple setup, and reliable online booking features. Square Appointments is also a good option if you already use Square for credit card processing.
