Pendo is a strong choice for onboarding analytics, but many SaaS founders eventually look for alternatives. Perhaps you outgrow its feature set, find the pricing too high at scale, or need deeper product analytics that separate user behavior from in-app guidance. Below we cover four direct alternatives to Pendo, each with a different focus. If onboarding analytics is your primary need, these tools can help you track activation funnels, measure feature adoption, and improve time to value.

alternative

Amplitude

is a product analytics platform built for teams that want deep behavioral cohorts and advanced funnel analysis. Its onboarding features include journey maps, retention curves, and the ability to group users based on specific actions. Amplitude’s free tier and generous event volume make it a practical starting point for early stage SaaS teams.

alternative

Mixpanel

specializes in granular event tracking and user segmentation, which supports onboarding analysis out of the box. You can create custom funnels for signup flows, measure drop off at each step, and trigger messages based on user behavior. Mixpanel also offers a free plan with limited monthly tracked users, making it accessible for smaller teams.

alternative

Heap

automatically captures every user interaction without manual event tagging, which simplifies setting up onboarding metrics. Its user journey visualization helps you see common paths through the product and spot where new users get stuck. Heap’s autocapture approach reduces upfront engineering work, a plus for lean teams.

alternative

Gainsight PX

combines product analytics with in-app messaging and walkthroughs, much like Pendo. It focuses heavily on customer success workflows, allowing you to score user engagement and trigger onboarding emails or tooltips based on product behavior. Gainsight PX is a good fit if your company already uses Gainsight for customer relationship management.

How they compare

All four alternatives offer funnel analysis and retention tracking for onboarding, but they differ in setup and emphasis. Amplitude and Mixpanel are general product analytics tools with deep segmentation and flexible dashboards, while Heap reduces setup time with autocapture. Gainsight PX sits closest to Pendo, pairing analytics with in-app guidance and CS integration. Your choice depends on whether you prioritize easy event tracking (Heap), behavioral modeling (Amplitude, Mixpanel), or combined analytics and onboarding features (Gainsight PX).

Frequently asked questions

Which Pendo alternative is best for startups with limited engineering time?
Heap is a strong choice because it autocaptures all user interactions. You do not need to code events upfront, which lets you start analyzing onboarding flows in minutes.
Can I use Amplitude or Mixpanel to replace Pendo’s in-app guides and walkthroughs?
Not directly. Amplitude and Mixpanel are analytics platforms, not guide builders. If you need in-app messaging alongside analytics, consider Gainsight PX or a combination of Amplitude with a dedicated walkthrough tool like Userflow.
