Best Shiftboard alternatives

Shiftboard is a workforce management platform designed for complex shift scheduling, often used by large enterprises with 24/7 operations. However, some organizations may find that its feature set or pricing does not fully agree with their needs. This article explores four popular alternatives: Deputy, When I Work, 7shifts, and Homebase, focusing on how they handle complex shift patterns for large teams.

alternative

Deputy

. Deputy offers robust scheduling, time tracking, and compliance features suitable for large enterprises. It supports complex shift patterns with auto-scheduling based on demand and employee availability, and integrates with major payroll and HR systems. Its mobile app allows employees to swap shifts and request time off, which helps manage large workforces efficiently.

alternative

When I Work

. When I Work provides shift scheduling, time clocks, and team communication tools that can handle moderate complexity. It is easier to set up than Shiftboard but may lack advanced features for rotating or overlapping shifts common in large enterprises. For very complex patterns, users might need to supplement with other tools. **7shifts**. 7shifts is built primarily for the restaurant and hospitality industry, with strong shift swapping and labor cost management. While it can manage complex schedules in those sectors, large enterprises with diverse operations may find its industry-specific focus limiting for general use. Its reporting and compliance tools are tailored to hourly workers in food service.

7shifts

. 7shifts is built primarily for the restaurant and hospitality industry, with strong shift swapping and labor cost management. While it can manage complex schedules in those sectors, large enterprises with diverse operations may find its industry-specific focus limiting for general use. Its reporting and compliance tools are tailored to hourly workers in food service.

alternative

Homebase

. Homebase offers scheduling, time clocks, and team messaging for small to medium businesses, with some capabilities for shift management. Large enterprises with complex shift patterns may outgrow Homebase quickly due to limited advanced scheduling logic and fewer integration options. It is best suited for simpler, predictable schedules.

How they compare

Among these options, Deputy is the strongest for large enterprises needing complex shift patterns and compliance, while When I Work offers a simpler interface but less depth. 7shifts excels in hospitality but lacks versatility, and Homebase is better for small teams. Shiftboard remains a more comprehensive solution for the most intricate scheduling needs, but Deputy comes closest as an alternative.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a large enterprise with rotating 12-hour shifts?
Deputy is generally the best fit for rotating 12-hour shifts because it supports advanced auto-scheduling, shift patterns, and compliance with labor laws. It also integrates with payroll systems to handle overtime calculations efficiently.
Can any of these alternatives replace Shiftboard for managing unionized workforces?
Deputy offers seniority and union rule settings that can help, but Shiftboard’s specialized features for union contracts and complex bidding may still be superior. You should evaluate Deputy’s capabilities against your specific union requirements.
