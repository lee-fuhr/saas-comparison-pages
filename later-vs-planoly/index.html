If you are an Instagram influencer trying to decide between Later and Planoly to plan your visual feed, you are not alone. Both tools let you drag and drop photos to preview your grid, schedule posts, and manage captions. But they have different strengths when it comes to analytics, video scheduling, and pricing. This article breaks down the key differences so you can pick the right one for your content strategy.

alternative

Planoly

. Planoly started as a grid planner for Instagram but now supports scheduling for multiple platforms including Pinterest and TikTok. It offers a built-in hashtag manager and a content library, plus it shows you analytics like best posting times. Planoly’s free tier is more generous with posts per month compared to Later, but its drag and drop layout can be less smooth on mobile.

How they compare

Later gives you a cleaner desktop experience for feed previews and stronger support for Instagram Reels scheduling directly from the app. Planoly offers a more generous free plan and a built-in hashtag tool, but its visual grid editor is less polished on mobile. For influencers who prioritize Reels and analytics, Later is the better choice. If you need to manage multiple platforms on a tight budget, Planoly may work better.

Frequently asked questions

Which tool is better for scheduling Instagram Reels?
Later supports direct scheduling of Reels with captions and covers, while Planoly requires you to post Reels manually and only saves captions for copy paste. Later is the stronger option for Reels.
Can I use the free version of either tool for a full month of posting?
Later’s free plan allows up to 30 posts per month across all platforms. Planoly’s free plan allows up to 30 posts on Instagram alone plus 30 on other platforms. Planoly gives you more free capacity for multi platform posting.
