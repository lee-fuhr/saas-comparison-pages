As a privacy officer managing global data regulations, you know that TrustArc has been a long-standing leader in privacy compliance software. But you may be evaluating alternatives due to cost, complexity, or a need for more integrated automation. This article covers four strong options – Vanta, Drata, SecureFrame, and OneTrust – to help you find the right fit for your organization’s privacy program.

alternative

Vanta

. Vanta is a security and compliance automation platform that focuses heavily on SOC 2, ISO 27001, and HIPAA, but it also provides privacy modules for GDPR, CCPA, and other frameworks. Its automated evidence collection and continuous monitoring can reduce manual effort for privacy teams. However, Vanta’s privacy features are less comprehensive than its security controls, so it works best when privacy is part of a broader compliance strategy.

alternative

Drata

. Drata offers continuous compliance monitoring and automation for frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR. It integrates with over 100 SaaS tools to collect evidence automatically and includes a built-in privacy impact assessment workflow. For privacy officers, Drata’s strength lies in its streamlined user experience and rapid deployment, though its global privacy coverage may not be as deep as dedicated privacy platforms.

alternative

SecureFrame

. SecureFrame is a compliance management platform that supports SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI, GDPR, and CCPA. It provides automated evidence collection, risk management, and vendor due diligence capabilities. While SecureFrame is well-regarded for its user-friendly interface and affordability, it may require more manual setup for complex, multi-country privacy programs compared to enterprise tools.

alternative

OneTrust

. OneTrust is a direct, full-featured competitor to TrustArc, offering a wide suite for privacy, security, ethics, and ESG. It includes modules for data mapping, consent management, DSAR automation, and privacy impact assessments. OneTrust is ideal for large, global organizations that need deep privacy functionality, but its scale and cost can be overkill for smaller teams.

How they compare

TrustArc is the incumbent with mature privacy capabilities for global regulations, but its cost and complexity can be drawbacks. Vanta and Drata excel at security-first automation but offer only moderate privacy depth, making them better for teams where privacy is secondary to security. SecureFrame provides a balanced, budget-friendly option with decent global compliance support, though it lacks the advanced privacy features of TrustArc or OneTrust. OneTrust is the most comprehensive alternative for privacy-only needs, matching TrustArc’s breadth at a similar price and complexity level.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my existing TrustArc data to Vanta or Drata easily?
Migration depends on the tool. Most alternatives offer data import templates or APIs for common data like vendor lists or policies, but custom integrations for historical DSR logs or data maps may require manual work. Check with each vendor’s onboarding team for a migration plan.
Which alternative is best for an organization operating in both the EU and California?
OneTrust is the strongest option because it includes dedicated modules for GDPR and CCPA compliance, including data mapping across jurisdictions. TrustArc also handles both well, but if you are leaving TrustArc, OneTrust offers the most seamless replacement for multi-regulation coverage.
