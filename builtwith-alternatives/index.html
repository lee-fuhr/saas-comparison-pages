BuiltWith is a popular tool for uncovering the technology stacks behind websites, making it useful for account targeting. However, it has gaps in firmographic data and direct contact enrichment, which is where alternatives can provide more complete insights. For tech-focused marketers who need to identify and reach the right accounts, these BuiltWith alternatives offer integrated data enrichment and better coverage of company and contact details.

alternative

ZoomInfo Enrich

. This tool appends firmographic and technographic data directly into your CRM or marketing automation platform. It goes beyond simple stack detection by providing verified contact details and company intent signals, which helps you target accounts already using specific technologies.

alternative

Clearbit Enrich

. Clearbit focuses on real-time data enrichment using website domains and email addresses. It delivers technology stack intelligence combined with company size, industry, and funding data, making it easier to segment accounts by both tech usage and fit criteria.

alternative

Cognism

. Cognism offers B2B data enrichment with a strong emphasis on GDPR compliance and contact verification. Its technology stack data is paired with phone-verified mobile numbers and intent signals, giving marketers a direct channel to decision makers at tech adopters.

alternative

Leadspace

. Leadspace uses AI to build predictive account and contact profiles from multiple data sources. It enriches your leads with technology stack information while also scoring accounts based on likelihood to convert, which helps prioritize outreach to the most promising tech users.

How they compare

BuiltWith is best for raw stack detection, but it lacks contact enrichment and firmographic depth. ZoomInfo Enrich and Clearbit Enrich both offer reliable technographic plus company data, with ZoomInfo stronger on phone numbers and Clearbit on real-time updates. Cognism adds compliance-friendly mobile contacts, while Leadspace provides predictive scoring on top of stack data. Choose based on whether you need direct contact (ZoomInfo, Cognism), real-time enrichment (Clearbit), or AI-driven prioritization (Leadspace).

Frequently asked questions

Can I use a BuiltWith alternative to see which companies are using a specific technology?
Yes, most alternatives offer technographic filters. ZoomInfo Enrich and Clearbit Enrich let you create audience segments based on technology usage, such as “companies using Salesforce.” Leadspace and Cognism also include stack intent signals but may require more setup than a simple lookup.
Do these alternatives work with my existing CRM or marketing automation tool?
Yes, all four tools offer native integrations or API access. ZoomInfo Enrich and Clearbit Enrich have direct connectors for Salesforce, HubSpot, and Marketo. Cognism and Leadspace also integrate with major platforms, though you may need to configure data sync rules for best results.
