Best Kenjo alternatives

If your Latin American business uses Kenjo but finds that its features do not fully match your local payroll, compliance, or language needs, you are not alone. Many firms in the region look for a Kenjo alternative to get better support for country specific labor laws, local currencies, and Spanish or Portuguese interfaces. This article reviews four popular options (BambooHR, Factorial, Zenefits, Gusto HR) and helps you decide which one fits your team.

alternative

BambooHR

. BambooHR is a strong choice for companies that want a clean, easy to use platform for core HR tasks like time off tracking and employee records. However, its payroll and compliance features for Latin America are limited, so you may need to pair it with a local payroll provider.

alternative

Factorial

. Factorial is built specifically for European and Latin American markets, offering native support for Spanish, Portuguese, and local labor regulations. It includes time tracking, document management, and expense reports, making it a direct competitor to Kenjo for regional firms.

alternative

Zenefits

. Zenefits provides a broad set of HR and benefits administration tools, but its focus is mainly on the United States. Latin American users often find that local compliance features, such as Mexican IMSS reporting or Brazilian eSocial, are missing or require workarounds.

alternative

Gusto HR

. Gusto HR is popular among small and medium businesses in the U.S. for its payroll and benefits features. It does not offer dedicated support for Latin American payroll or labor laws, so it works best if your company has a separate local payroll processor.

How they compare

Factorial is the most direct Kenjo alternative for Latin American firms because it offers localized compliance, multilingual support, and a similar feature set. BambooHR is excellent for core HR but lacks deep regional payroll integration. Zenefits and Gusto HR are better suited for companies with a U.S. presence that need a lightweight HR tool, not for full Latin American operations. Your choice depends on whether you need all in one local compliance or you can combine a global HR tool with a local payroll vendor.
