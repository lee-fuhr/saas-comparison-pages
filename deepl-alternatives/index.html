Best DeepL alternatives

DeepL is a strong machine translation tool known for its natural output, but global marketers often need more than raw translation. You may require project management, translation memory, real time collaboration, or integration with content systems. The alternatives below offer these features plus additional flexibility for teams of different sizes and budgets.

alternative

Smartling

is an enterprise translation management platform that combines machine and human translation with workflow automation. It connects directly to your CMS or marketing stack, making it easy to scale localization without manual handoffs. Smartling also provides detailed analytics to track translation quality and turnaround time.

alternative

Phrase

(formerly PhraseApp) specializes in localization for agile development and marketing teams. It offers a cloud based translation management system with strong API support, version control, and integrations with tools like GitHub and Figma. Phrase also includes a built-in machine translation engine selection, so you can compare DeepL results alongside others.

alternative

Lokalise

focuses on speed and collaboration for web and mobile app localization. It provides real time editing, automatic screenshot capture, and a developer friendly CLI for continuous delivery. Lokalise is a good fit for marketers who work closely with developers and need to push translations quickly.

alternative

Wordfast

is a classic translation memory tool for freelancers and small agencies. It works offline and supports a wide range of file formats, including Trados compatible TMX and bilingual DOCX. Wordfast is lighter on project management features but reliable for cost conscious teams that want to reuse past translations.

How they compare

DeepL excels at raw translation quality for many language pairs, but it lacks built-in project management, team collaboration, and advanced integration options. Smartling and Phrase are better for larger teams that need end to end workflow control. Lokalise is ideal for fast moving development projects, while Wordfast suits users who prefer offline tools and a pay once rather than subscription model. Pricing varies widely: Smartling and Phrase are enterprise level, Lokalise offers team plans, and Wordfast remains a low cost purchase.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use DeepL inside Smartling or Phrase?
Yes. Both Smartling and Phrase support DeepL as a machine translation engine option. You can enable it within their translation workflows to combine DeepL’s quality with their project management features.
Is there a free alternative to DeepL for occasional use?
Wordfast offers a free trial and a one time purchase model, but it relies on translation memory rather than AI. For occasional free machine translation, you might consider Google Translate or Microsoft Translator, though they lack the customization and workflow features of the alternatives listed here.
