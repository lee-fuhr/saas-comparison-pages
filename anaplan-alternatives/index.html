Anaplan is a powerful platform for connected planning, but it may not suit every strategic planning and modeling team. High costs, steep learning curves, or a need for simpler financial workflows can lead teams to explore other options. Below we compare four strong Anaplan alternatives: Planful, Adaptive Insights, Vena tools, and Workiva. Each offers distinct strengths for financial planning, budgeting, forecasting, and reporting.

alternative

Planful

. Planful focuses on continuous planning with an intuitive interface and strong financial consolidation features. It is well suited for finance teams that want to shorten close cycles and improve collaboration without extensive IT support.

alternative

Adaptive Insights

. Now part of Workday, Adaptive Insights provides a cloud based planning tool with prebuilt modeling templates and strong integration with existing ERP systems. It excels at driver based forecasting and scenario analysis for mid to large enterprises.

alternative

Vena Solutions

An alternative to Anaplan.

alternative

Workiva

. Workiva is built for data connectivity and reporting, with a focus on linking financial planning to external reporting and SEC filings. Its platform is excellent for teams that need to manage complex data from multiple sources and ensure version control and audit readiness.
