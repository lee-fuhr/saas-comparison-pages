Best Apttus alternatives

Apttus is a popular platform for quote-to-cash and revenue management, but it may not fit every company’s needs due to cost, complexity, or integration requirements. If you are looking for alternatives that offer stronger contract and revenue management integration, several established tools can help you streamline sales processes and billing workflows. Below we explore five notable alternatives: Salesforce CPQ, Oracle CPQ, SAP CPQ, Experlogix, and Zilliant.

alternative

Salesforce CPQ

. This native solution runs directly on the Salesforce platform, making it a natural fit for companies already using Salesforce CRM. It handles complex pricing, quoting, and contract lifecycle management, and its tight integration with Salesforce Revenue Cloud allows for seamless order-to-revenue processes.

alternative

Oracle CPQ

. Oracle’s offering is built for large enterprises with complex product configurations and global pricing rules. It integrates deeply with Oracle ERP and other Oracle cloud applications, providing a unified data model for contracts, subscriptions, and revenue recognition.

alternative

SAP CPQ

. Designed to work within SAP’s ecosystem, SAP CPQ connects with SAP S/4HANA and SAP Sales Cloud. It is well suited for organizations that need to manage intricate product bundles, subscription billing, and contract renewals while maintaining compliance with SAP’s revenue standards.

alternative

Experlogix

. Experlogix specializes in configure-price-quote (CPQ) for manufacturers and distributors, with strong document generation and contract management features. It offers flexible integration options with major CRMs and ERPs, making it a good choice for mid-market companies that require tailored quoting and contract workflows.

alternative

Zilliant

. Zilliant focuses on pricing optimization and revenue management, using data science to recommend optimal prices and contract terms. Its platform integrates with existing CRM and ERP systems to improve margin and compliance, making it ideal for companies that want to drive profit through smarter pricing rather than just quoting efficiency.

How they compare

When choosing among these alternatives, consider your existing tech stack and the complexity of your revenue processes. Salesforce CPQ is best for Salesforce-native shops. Oracle CPQ and SAP CPQ suit large enterprises within their respective ecosystems. Experlogix targets mid-market manufacturing and distribution. Zilliant is a strong choice if pricing optimization is a primary need. Evaluate integration ease, contract lifecycle management depth, and total cost of ownership for your specific use case.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative offers the strongest contract lifecycle management features?
Salesforce CPQ and Oracle CPQ both provide robust contract management including amendments, renewals, and approval workflows. Oracle CPQ has an edge for companies needing global multi-currency and multi-language contract support, while Salesforce CPQ integrates tightly with Salesforce’s document generation and e-signature tools.
Can these alternatives handle subscription-based billing and revenue recognition?
Yes, all five platforms support subscription billing and revenue recognition, but the depth of integration varies. Salesforce CPQ works with Salesforce Revenue Cloud for ASC 606 compliance. Oracle CPQ connects with Oracle Revenue Management. SAP CPQ integrates with SAP Subscription Billing. Experlogix and Zilliant typically rely on third-party billing systems or your existing ERP for revenue recognition.
