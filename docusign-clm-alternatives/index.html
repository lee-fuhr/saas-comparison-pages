Best DocuSign CLM alternatives

DocuSign CLM is a popular choice for enterprises that want to manage contracts from creation through signature and beyond. However, as your organization grows, you may find that its reporting, workflow flexibility, or integration depth no longer fit your needs. This article explores five strong alternatives that provide end-to-end contract lifecycle management (CLM) with features tailored to complex enterprise requirements.

alternative

Ironclad

offers a modern, user-friendly interface built specifically for legal and contracting teams. Its drag-and-drop workflow designer and native Salesforce integration make it easy to automate approvals and agree with sales processes. For enterprises that prioritize speed of deployment and intuitive user experience, Ironclad provides a solid balance of power and simplicity.

alternative

Conga CLM

is a mature platform that excels at document generation and bulk contract actions. It integrates tightly with Salesforce and other CRM systems, making it a strong choice if your contracting process is heavily tied to sales quotes and orders. Conga also provides robust clause libraries and approval routing, though its interface can feel less modern than newer tools.

alternative

Icertis

targets large, global enterprises with complex compliance, regulatory, and multi-language needs. Its AI-driven insights and obligation management features help you track commitments across thousands of contracts. Icertis is ideal when you need deep metadata extraction, risk scoring, and the ability to handle high transaction volumes.

alternative

Agiloft

is known for its highly customizable, no-code platform that adapts to any contract process without requiring IT support. It offers strong workflow automation, flexible reporting, and built-in AI for clause recognition and risk analysis. Agiloft suits enterprises that want total control over their CLM configuration and need to support diverse use cases across departments.

alternative

Concord

provides a straightforward, collaborative contract workspace focused on approval workflows and templates. Its clean interface makes it easy for non-legal teams to create and negotiate contracts, with version control and commenting built in. Concord is a good fit for mid-market and larger enterprises that want a simpler alternative to DocuSign CLM without sacrificing essential lifecycle features.

How they compare

All five alternatives offer end-to-end contract lifecycle tools, but they differ in customization depth, AI capabilities, and ecosystem fit. Ironclad and Concord prioritize ease of use, while Icertis and Agiloft offer the strongest customization and advanced analytics. Conga excels in Salesforce-centric environments. The right choice depends on your integration needs, team size, and how much control you want over workflows and reporting.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is the easiest to implement for a large enterprise?
Ironclad is often cited for its fast onboarding and intuitive design, making it a top choice if you want to go live quickly. For companies already deep in the Salesforce ecosystem, Conga CLM can also be deployed relatively fast because of its prebuilt integrations.
Do any of these tools include AI features comparable to DocuSign CLM’s Insight?
Yes. Icertis offers advanced AI for clause extraction, risk scoring, and obligation tracking. Agiloft also includes built-in AI for clause recognition and metadata extraction, and Ironclad provides AI-assisted search and redlining. Each tool’s AI strength varies, so evaluate based on your specific use case.
