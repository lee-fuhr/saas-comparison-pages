If you are a mid-market recruiter using Lever, you know its strength is treating candidates like contacts in a CRM. But maybe you need stronger pipeline automation, better reporting, or a tool that scales differently. Comparing alternatives like Greenhouse, Ashby, and SmartRecruiters can help you find the right fit for your hiring goals without losing the pipeline visibility you rely on.

alternative

Greenhouse

. Greenhouse offers structured hiring workflows and robust reporting, making it a strong choice for teams that value process consistency. Its pipeline management relies on stages and scorecards, which can give your team more visibility into where candidates stall compared to Lever’s more freeform CRM approach.

alternative

Ashby

. Ashby is built with data and flexibility in mind, giving you detailed pipeline analytics and customizable stages without complex setup. It often appeals to mid-market recruiters who want Lever-like ease of use but with more powerful reporting and automated sequence triggers for nurturing candidates.

alternative

SmartRecruiters

. SmartRecruiters focuses on collaboration and marketplace integrations, helping you manage candidate pipelines across multiple sources. Its visual pipeline view and collaboration tools (like in-app comments) are similar to Lever, but it offers stronger multi-channel sourcing features and a larger integration ecosystem.

How they compare

All three alternatives improve on Lever in different areas: Greenhouse provides stronger process control and compliance, Ashby offers deeper pipeline analytics and automation, and SmartRecruiters excels at multi-channel sourcing and integrations. Your choice depends on whether you want more structure (Greenhouse), more data (Ashby), or more reach (SmartRecruiters). None are perfect replacements for Lever’s CRM-first feel, so test each with your actual pipeline data.
