If you schedule meetings with more than a handful of people, you know the pain of back-and-forth emails. Doodle is a popular choice, but it is not the only one. You may need a tool that integrates with your calendar, handles payments, or works better with large groups. We have compared three strong alternatives to help your team find common meeting times quickly.

alternative

Calendly

. Calendly lets you set your availability and share a booking link. Instead of proposing multiple time slots, participants pick from your open slots. It integrates with Google, Outlook, and Office 365 calendars to avoid double-booking, and it sends automatic reminders.

Try Calendly →
alternative

Acuity Scheduling

. Acuity offers more customization for client-facing businesses. You can add intake forms, collect payments, and set buffer times between appointments. It works well when you need to handle different service types or require participants to submit information before booking.

Try Acuity Scheduling →
alternative

TidyCal

. TidyCal is a simple, low-cost option that feels similar to Calendly. It provides unlimited booking types and calendar sync for a flat yearly fee. The interface is straightforward, making it great for teams that just need a no-frills way to share availability.

Try TidyCal →

How they compare

All three tools are faster than manual Doodle polls for finding a common meeting time across many participants. Calendly is best for internal team meetings and one-on-one bookings. Acuity is better for paid appointments or client scheduling with forms. TidyCal is the budget-friendly choice when you need basic features without a monthly subscription.
