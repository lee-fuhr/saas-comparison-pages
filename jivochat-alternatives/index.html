Best JivoChat alternatives

JivoChat is a popular live chat tool, but sales and support squads that need omnichannel messaging across web, mobile, and social sometimes find its features or pricing don’t match their exact workflow. Whether you need deeper social media integration, a more affordable plan, or a simpler interface, exploring alternatives can help you find the right fit. Below we compare seven strong JivoChat alternatives to support your team’s omnichannel communication strategy.

alternative

Olark

offers a straightforward live chat experience with a focus on customization and chatbot basics. Its strengths include easy-to-build automated triggers and a clear pricing model based on agents, making it a good choice for small teams that want simplicity without sacrificing essential chat features.

alternative

Chaport

provides a unified inbox that combines website chat, Facebook Messenger, Telegram, and email into one dashboard. Its built-in chatbot builder and live visitor monitoring make it a strong option for teams that want to automate replies while keeping a human touch across multiple channels.

alternative

Pure Chat

is a budget-friendly live chat solution that includes basic routing, file sharing, and proactive chat invitations. It works well for small businesses that need a simple chat widget without complex omnichannel integrations or advanced automation.

alternative

Smartsupp

stands out with its visitor recording and replay feature, which helps teams review real user interactions to improve support quality. It also offers a free plan limited to one agent and connects with Facebook Messenger, making it suitable for startups that prioritize video-like insights.

alternative

LiveChat

is a feature-rich platform that supports web, mobile, and social channels including Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp through its integration ecosystem. It provides robust analytics, ticket management, and AI-powered chatbots, making it ideal for larger teams that want a full-service help desk.

alternative

Tidio

excels in bringing together live chat, email marketing, and chatbots in one easy-to-use interface. Its drag-and-drop chatbot builder connects with Messenger, Instagram, and email, giving sales and support teams a versatile tool for lead generation and customer service.

alternative

Zendesk Chat

(part of the Zendesk suite) offers deep integration with the broader Zendesk customer service platform, including ticketing and knowledge base features. It is best for organizations already using Zendesk that want a seamless, omnichannel experience across chat, social, and email.
