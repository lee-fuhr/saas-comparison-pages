Best Trustpilot alternatives

Trustpilot is a widely used review platform, but it may not fit every business. Some companies find the pricing too high for their volume of reviews. Others want more control over how reviews are collected and displayed. If you need a solution that offers lower costs, better targeting for your industry, or more ownership of your review data, these alternatives can help.

alternative

G2

. G2 focuses on software and technology reviews, making it ideal for B2B companies. It uses a verified review system to maintain trust and provides detailed comparison pages. Businesses gain control by managing profiles and responding directly to reviewers, though pricing can be higher than some alternatives.

Try G2 →
alternative

Capterra

. Capterra is another B2B review platform owned by Gartner, with a strong emphasis on software categories. It offers a free basic listing with optional paid features for more exposure. Businesses get access to a large audience of buyers, but they have less control over review moderation compared to self-hosted tools.

alternative

Birdeye

. Birdeye is an all in one reputation management platform that goes beyond reviews to include surveys, social media, and messaging. It helps local businesses collect reviews from multiple sites and track feedback in one dashboard. The cost is often lower than Trustpilot for small and medium companies, and you have better control over review invitations.

Try Birdeye →
alternative

Podium

. Podium specializes in text message based review collection, making it easy to reach customers on their phones. It integrates with payment systems and offers tools for customer communication. Pricing is per location, which can be cost effective for multi location businesses that want direct control over the review funnel.

Try Podium →
alternative

ReviewTrackers

. ReviewTrackers aggregates reviews from over 100 sites including Google, Facebook, and Yelp. It provides analytics and reporting to help businesses understand trends and respond quickly. The platform gives you a central hub for managing all reviews, reducing the need to rely on any single primary platform like Trustpilot.

How they compare

Each platform serves a different need. G2 and Capterra are best for B2B software companies seeking targeted buyer traffic. Birdeye and Podium focus on local businesses and direct customer engagement through text and surveys. ReviewTrackers is a monitoring and analytics tool rather than a primary review collection platform. For cost and control, Birdeye and Podium often offer lower per location pricing and more customizable review invitation workflows. Trustpilot remains strong for large scale consumer facing brands, but these alternatives give you more flexibility in how you collect, manage, and display reviews.
