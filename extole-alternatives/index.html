If you run an e-commerce brand, you know that a referral and loyalty program can turn one-time buyers into repeat customers. Many businesses start with Extole because it offers robust campaign management and analytics. But you may find that Extole’s pricing or feature set does not fit your checkout flow perfectly, or you want a platform that integrates more deeply with your existing tech stack. This article explores five strong alternatives that are designed to work with e-commerce checkout processes and help you build customer loyalty.

alternative

ReferenceEdge

. ReferenceEdge focuses on post-purchase referral campaigns that trigger right inside the checkout confirmation page. Its simple setup lets you add a referral widget without custom development, and it tracks conversions back to the original order. This makes it a good fit if you want a lightweight, checkout-integrated referral solution without a full loyalty suite.

alternative

Influitive

. Influitive is built around advocacy and community, not just referrals. It allows you to reward customers for a wide range of actions like reviews, social shares, and referrals, and its API can connect to your checkout flow. For e-commerce brands that want to turn customers into brand champions across multiple channels, Influitive offers more flexibility than a pure referral tool.

alternative

Referral Rock

. Referral Rock provides a referral program that can be embedded directly into the checkout process via a pop-up or a dedicated widget. It includes tiered rewards, auto-generated referral links, and integration with major e-commerce platforms. The tool is especially strong for brands that need a self-service setup with clear analytics and minimal technical overhead.

alternative

Champion

. Champion is a newer platform that combines referrals, loyalty points, and customer reviews into one checkout-integrated experience. It uses a floating widget that appears after purchase to encourage sharing, and it syncs rewards with your order management system. This all-in-one approach helps you avoid juggling multiple vendors.

alternative

Ambassador

. Ambassador offers a referral and affiliate platform that can be customized to appear at order confirmation or in post-purchase emails. It has advanced fraud detection and supports multi-currency and multi-language setups, which is useful for international e-commerce brands. Ambassador’s tiered pricing scales with your program size, making it a flexible option for growing businesses.

How they compare

All five alternatives integrate with e-commerce checkout flows, but they differ in scope. ReferenceEdge and Referral Rock are referral-only tools that are lightweight and easy to set up. Influitive and Champion add loyalty, advocacy, or review capabilities, making them better for brands that want a fuller customer engagement system. Ambassador sits in the middle, with strong referral features plus affiliate management. Pricing varies from low-cost monthly plans (Referral Rock) to custom enterprise quotes (Influitive, Ambassador). Your choice should depend on whether you need only referrals or a broader loyalty program.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives be integrated without a developer?
Most of them offer plug-and-play widgets or integrations with Shopify, Magento, and other major platforms. Referral Rock and ReferenceEdge are particularly known for their simple one-line code snippets. For deeper customization or a custom checkout flow, you may need a developer’s help.
How do these tools handle fraud in referral programs?
Each tool includes basic fraud detection like IP tracking, email verification, and order matching. Ambassador and Influitive have more advanced fraud prevention features, such as flagging suspicious referral patterns. For high-value programs, consider testing the fraud controls during a free trial.
