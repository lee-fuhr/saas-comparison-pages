Advocacy platforms help marketers turn happy customers into brand champions who share referrals, reviews, and testimonials. Influitive is a strong player in this space, but it might not fit every budget, workflow, or feature need. If you are exploring other options, the five alternatives below each offer distinct approaches to building customer advocacy programs. This article covers ReferenceEdge, Referral Rock, Extole, Champion, and CustomerGauge, with plain language descriptions to help you decide.

alternative

ReferenceEdge

focuses on automating reference calls and case study requests so your sales team can quickly connect with happy customers. It includes gamification and reward features to keep advocates engaged, plus detailed analytics to track program performance. This tool works well for B2B companies that rely heavily on customer references in their sales cycle.

alternative

Referral Rock

is built around referral marketing and allows you to create custom referral campaigns with rewards and tracking. It offers a simple setup with options for both customer and partner referrals, and it integrates with common email and CRM tools. Referral Rock is a good fit if your primary goal is driving word-of-mouth leads rather than broader advocacy activities.

alternative

Extole

provides a full suite for referral and advocacy programs, including automation, fraud detection, and detailed performance reporting. It is designed for enterprise brands that need to scale their programs across multiple channels and geographies. Extole also offers a robust API and integrations with major marketing platforms.

alternative

Champion

(by Influitive) is a separate product that focuses specifically on turning top customers into advocates through targeted challenges and rewards. It emphasizes community building and peer-to-peer influence, with features for content sharing and social amplification. Champion works best for companies that want a pure advocacy layer without the complexity of a full referral engine.

alternative

CustomerGauge

specializes in B2B customer experience and advocacy by linking Net Promoter Score feedback to referral and case study generation. It automates the process of identifying promoters and turning them into advocates through targeted campaigns. CustomerGauge is ideal for organizations already using NPS and wanting to close the loop from feedback to advocacy.

How they compare

All five alternatives help marketers generate advocacy, but they differ in focus. ReferenceEdge and CustomerGauge lean toward B2B reference management and NPS integration. Referral Rock and Extole are more referral-centric, with Extole offering larger enterprise scale. Champion is a dedicated advocacy engagement tool, similar to Influitive but more streamlined. Your choice should depend on whether you need referrals, references, reviews, or a mix of activities. Budget, integration needs, and team size also play a role. None of these tools require a long term commitment to start, so you can test a free trial or demo.

Frequently asked questions

What should I look for when choosing an advocacy platform?
First, identify your primary goal: referrals, customer references, reviews, or general advocacy. Then check the tool’s integrations with your CRM, email, and marketing automation. Also consider ease of setup, reward management, and reporting depth. Many vendors offer free trials, so test with a small group of customers before committing.
Can I run multiple advocacy programs at the same time with these tools?
Yes, most of these platforms let you run separate campaigns for referrals, reviews, and case studies. However, some tools are more specialized. For example, Referral Rock is best for referral only programs, while CustomerGauge can handle referrals plus NPS based campaigns. Review each tool’s campaign management features to see if they match your needs.
