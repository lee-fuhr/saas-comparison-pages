BigCommerce is a solid all in one e-commerce platform, but as your business grows you may hit limits with its transaction fees, template costs, or the complexity of its pricing tiers. If you need more flexibility, lower subscription costs, or a platform that scales better with high volume sales, it is worth exploring alternatives. The options below cover hosted tools and self hosted options so you can choose based on your technical comfort and budget.

alternative

Shopify

. Shopify is the most direct competitor, offering a similar hosted experience with a huge app store and 24/7 support. It works well for stores of any size, but transaction fees apply unless you use Shopify Payments, and some advanced features require paid apps.

alternative

WooCommerce

. WooCommerce is a free plugin for WordPress that gives you complete control over design, hosting, and functionality. It is ideal if you want zero monthly fees (you pay only for hosting and extensions), but you will need more technical skill to manage security and performance.

alternative

Wix

. Wix is a drag-and-drop website builder with a built in e-commerce plan. It is very beginner friendly and includes a nice set of marketing tools, but it can become expensive as you add premium apps and its scalability for very large catalogs is limited.

alternative

Volusion

. Volusion is an older hosted platform with a strong focus on built in features like SEO and inventory management. It offers simpler pricing without transaction fees, but its design options and app ecosystem are less advanced than Shopify or BigCommerce. **3dcart (now Shift4Shop)** . 3dcart provides a feature rich hosted solution with no transaction fees on any plan. How they compare

For growing businesses, the main trade offs are between cost, control, and ease of use. Shopify and BigCommerce are the most polished hosted tools, but Shopify’s app costs add up while BigCommerce has revenue based pricing. WooCommerce gives you full ownership and low upfront cost but requires more technical work. Wix is the easiest for beginners but less suitable for high volume stores. Volusion and 3dcart offer no transaction fees and good built in features, but their modern ecosystems are weaker. Choose based on whether you prioritize low monthly cost, customization, or minimal complexity.

Frequently asked questions

Which BigCommerce alternative is cheapest for a growing store?
WooCommerce is typically the cheapest because the core plugin is free and you only pay for web hosting and optional extensions. However, you may need to budget for a developer or premium plugins if you lack technical skills. Shopify’s Basic plan is also low cost but adds transaction fees unless you use Shopify Payments.
Can I migrate from BigCommerce to another platform easily?
Yes, most alternatives offer import tools or third party migration apps. You can export your product data, customer list, and order history from BigCommerce as CSV files. Shopify and WooCommerce have built in migration wizards. Plan for some work on redirects and design, but the data transfer is straightforward.
