Best 1Password alternatives

1Password is a reliable password manager, but its subscription cost can be higher than some users want to pay. Both personal users and small teams often look for 1Password alternatives that offer strong security and useful features at a lower price. This article reviews five popular options: Bitwarden, LastPass, Keeper, Dashlane, and NordPass. Each tool has different strengths for personal or team use, and all are more affordable than 1Password in many cases.

alternative

Bitwarden

offers a free tier that is very capable for personal use, with unlimited devices and core features like autofill and secure sharing. For teams, its paid plans start at a low per-user monthly rate, making it one of the most affordable options available. Its open-source code is regularly audited, which gives transparency that many users value.

alternative

LastPass

has a free tier that covers basic password storage on one device type, but its premium plans are reasonably priced for personal users. Team plans include shared folders and admin controls, though recent changes to the free tier have pushed some users to paid options. It is a mature product with broad platform support.

alternative

Keeper

focuses on high security with zero-knowledge encryption and advanced features like file storage and dark web monitoring. Its personal plans are slightly higher than some competitors, but team plans include granular permissions and reporting. Keeper is a strong choice for organizations that need enterprise-grade security.

alternative

Dashlane

provides a polished user experience with a built-in VPN and dark web monitoring on premium plans. Personal plans are pricier than some, but its team plans offer easy onboarding and sharing. The interface is intuitive, which can save time for non-technical users.

alternative

NordPass

comes from the makers of NordVPN and offers a straightforward password manager. Its free tier includes basic features and one device, while paid plans are competitively priced for personal and team use. NordPass emphasizes simplicity and integrates well with the Nord ecosystem.

How they compare

Bitwarden and LastPass offer the most affordable free tiers, but Bitwarden is the clear winner for teams on a tight budget thanks to its low per-user price and unlimited sharing. Keeper and Dashlane cost more but include extra security features like file storage and dark web monitoring. NordPass is a simple mid-priced option that works well for personal users who already use other Nord services. For teams, Bitwarden and Keeper provide strong admin controls, while Dashlane and LastPass focus on ease of use. Personal users on a tight budget should consider Bitwarden or the free version of LastPass, but be aware that LastPass free now limits you to one device type.

Frequently asked questions

Is it safe to switch from 1Password to a cheaper alternative?
Yes, all the alternatives listed use strong encryption and are reputable companies. Make sure to export your data from 1Password and import it into the new tool using the built-in import feature. Always enable two-factor authentication for extra security after you set up your new password manager.
Which alternative is best for a small team with a low budget?
Bitwarden offers the most affordable team plan with unlimited shared items and an admin console for managing users. LastPass teams also have low per-user pricing, but Bitwarden’s open-source model and transparent pricing often make it the top choice for budget-conscious teams that still need strong security.
