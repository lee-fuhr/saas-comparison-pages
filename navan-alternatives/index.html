Best Navan alternatives

Navan (formerly TripActions) is a popular tool for combining corporate travel booking with expense management. But travel-heavy organizations sometimes find that Navan’s pricing, feature set, or workflow do not perfectly match their needs. This article examines three common alternatives – Expensify, Ramp, and Divvy – to help you decide which might work better for your end-to-end booking and expense requirements.

alternative

Expensify

is best known as an expense reporting tool, but it also offers basic travel booking through its Concierge service. It automates receipt capture and approval workflows, which can reduce manual data entry for travelers. However, its travel booking features are less integrated than Navan’s, so you may need to use a separate booking tool alongside it.

alternative

Ramp

provides a corporate card and expense management platform with strong automation for spend controls and receipt matching. It does not include built-in travel booking, but it integrates with third-party travel agencies and booking tools. For organizations that already use a preferred travel agency, Ramp can simplify expense reconciliation after trips.

alternative

Divvy

combines spend management with a corporate card and limited travel booking capabilities through its partnership with travel providers. It focuses on budget controls and real-time expense tracking, making it easy to enforce policies. However, its travel booking options are not as comprehensive as Navan’s, so heavy travelers may still need a separate booking solution.

How they compare

When compared to Navan, Expensify offers stronger expense automation but weaker travel booking. Ramp excels at spend controls and integration flexibility but lacks native travel booking. Divvy provides solid budget management and some travel features but still falls short of Navan’s end-to-end experience. Travel-heavy organizations should weigh whether they need a single platform for both booking and expenses (Navan’s strength) or prefer to pair a robust expense tool with a separate booking partner.

Frequently asked questions

How does Expensify compare to Navan for travel booking?
Expensify offers basic travel booking through its Concierge service, but it is not as seamless as Navan’s integrated search and booking flow. For frequent travelers needing one click to book flights and hotels, Navan is usually a better fit. Expensify is more focused on expense reporting after the trip.
Can Ramp or Divvy replace Navan’s end-to-end booking?
Not in the same way. Ramp and Divvy are primarily expense management and corporate card platforms. They can integrate with third-party travel tools, but they do not provide the native booking experience that Navan offers. If you need a single tool for both booking and expense tracking, Navan remains the stronger choice.
