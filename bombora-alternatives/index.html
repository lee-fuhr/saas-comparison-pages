Bombora provides B2B intent data that helps marketers identify companies actively researching topics. However, some teams find Bombora’s data too broad, expensive, or lacking integration with their existing tools. For marketers who need precise, actionable intent signals to prioritize outreach, several alternatives offer different strengths in coverage, data sources, and delivery methods.

alternative

G2 Buyer Intent

. G2 Buyer Intent uses activity from G2’s review platform to show which buyers are researching your competitors or related software categories. It provides contact-level intent data based on actual product research, which can be more direct than Bombora’s topical intent. This makes it useful for marketers who want to engage prospects already comparing tools.

alternative

TechTarget Priority Engine

. TechTarget Priority Engine captures intent from a network of B2B technology content sites, focusing on active research around specific IT purchases. It offers account-level and contact-level signals with detailed topic targeting, often including purchase timeframes. This tool is strong for account-based marketing when you need intent tied to specific buying stages.

alternative

Demandbase ABM

. Demandbase ABM combines intent data with account identification and advertising capabilities, using its own data from web traffic and third-party sources. It provides a full account-based platform that scores accounts based on intent, engagement, and fit. This is ideal for teams that want an all-in-one solution rather than a standalone intent feed.

How they compare

Bombora offers broad topical intent based on a large co-op panel, but its data can lack contact-level detail. G2 Buyer Intent excels at direct product comparison intent from a trusted review site. TechTarget Priority Engine focuses on late-stage IT purchase research with strong tech content context. Demandbase ABM provides a complete ABM suite with intent embedded, making it best for teams that need integrated targeting, not just data.

Frequently asked questions

What is the main difference between Bombora and G2 Buyer Intent?
Bombora tracks general topics researched by companies across a co-op network, while G2 Buyer Intent captures explicit product and competitor research from buyers actively reading reviews and comparisons.
Can I use TechTarget Priority Engine without a large account-based marketing platform?
Yes, TechTarget Priority Engine can be used as a standalone intent data source or integrated with your CRM and marketing automation. It does not require a full ABM suite like Demandbase, but it works well alongside one.
