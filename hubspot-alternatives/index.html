Best HubSpot alternatives

If your sales team is outgrowing spreadsheets but not ready for HubSpot’s paid plans, you need a free CRM that can scale with you. HubSpot’s free tier is excellent for basic contact management, but its limitations on automation, pipeline stages, and user seats can frustrate growing teams. The good news is that several platforms offer strong free options or generous trial periods designed for small to mid-sized teams. Below we compare top HubSpot alternatives that keep your costs low while giving you room to add more users, build custom workflows, and close more deals.

alternative

Salesforce

. Salesforce offers a free edition called Salesforce Essentials that includes lead management, email integration, and basic dashboards for up to ten users. While the free tier is limited compared to paid plans, it gives growing teams a direct path to the industry’s most powerful CRM ecosystem with clear upgrade options.

alternative

Pipedrive

. Pipedrive’s free plan supports up to three users with unlimited deals, contact storage, and a visual sales pipeline. It focuses on keeping your sales process organized with drag and drop stages, making it ideal for teams that want a simple, activity driven CRM without complex customizations.

Try Pipedrive →
alternative

Close

. Close provides a free trial but no permanent free plan; however its paid version starts low and includes built in calling, SMS, and email sequences out of the box. For sales teams that rely heavily on outbound communication, Close’s automation features can replace HubSpot’s engagement workflows at a fraction of the cost.

Try Close →
alternative

Copper

. Copper offers a free 14 day trial, not a permanent free plan, but it integrates deeply with Google Workspace to auto fill contact data from Gmail and Google Calendar. Growing teams already using Google apps will find Copper’s lightweight CRM easier to adopt than HubSpot, though you will need a paid subscription after the trial ends.

Try Copper →
alternative

ActiveCampaign CRM

. ActiveCampaign’s free CRM includes contact management, deal tracking, and email marketing for up to three users with limited automation. It is best for teams that prioritize email marketing alongside sales, as the platform’s advanced automations can nurture leads without paying for separate marketing and sales tools.
