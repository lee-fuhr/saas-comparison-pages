Showpad is a popular sales enablement platform that helps teams organize content, train sellers, and track engagement. However, many B2B organizations find that as their needs grow, they require a more unified hub that integrates deeper with CRM, analytics, and coaching tools. If Showpad’s pricing, content management approach, or feature set no longer fits, exploring alternatives can help you find a platform that aligns better with your sales workflow and revenue goals.

alternative

Highspot

is a top-tier alternative that combines content management with AI-powered recommendations and real-time analytics. It emphasizes sales readiness through interactive training modules and integrates tightly with Salesforce and other CRMs to deliver the right content at the right moment.

alternative

Seismic

focuses on delivering a unified content platform with advanced personalization and compliance controls, making it ideal for large enterprises. Its Life Sciences and financial services features allow strict governance while still enabling sellers to quickly find and share approved materials.

alternative

Mindtickle

positions itself as a sales readiness and enablement hub that merges onboarding, coaching, and content management. It uses gamification and microlearning to drive skill development alongside content distribution, which can boost rep confidence and deal velocity.

alternative

Brainshark

(now part of Bigtincan) offers a strong emphasis on coaching and training through video-based assessments and interactive learning paths. Its content management capabilities are more focused on digital sales rooms and “guided selling” experiences rather than a traditional content library.

alternative

Allego

stands out with its AI-powered conversation intelligence and video coaching tools that help teams analyze sales calls and share best practices. It also provides a content hub for organizing sales materials, but its primary strength lies in improving rep performance through voice and video feedback.
