FreshBooks is a solid choice for freelancers and small service businesses, but it may not offer the features or pricing you need as you grow. If you require more advanced accounting, team collaboration, or a lower cost, exploring alternatives like Xero, QuickBooks, and Wave can help you find the right fit. Each of these tools provides invoicing, time tracking, and simple reporting tailored to service-based companies.

alternative

Xero

. Xero offers robust invoicing with customizable templates and automatic payment reminders, and its time tracking integrates directly into projects and invoices. It also provides simple financial reports like profit and loss and balance sheets, making it a strong choice for growing service businesses that need multi-user access.

alternative

QuickBooks

. QuickBooks provides invoicing, time tracking via a built-in timer or mobile app, and a range of reports from expenses to project profitability. It is widely used and integrates with many third-party apps, which can be helpful if you already use tools like payroll or inventory management.

Try QuickBooks →
alternative

Wave

. Wave is a free accounting software that includes invoicing, receipt scanning, and basic reporting, with optional paid add-ons for payments and payroll. Its time tracking is simple and works well for sole proprietors or very small teams on a tight budget, but it lacks advanced features like project management.

Try Wave →

How they compare

FreshBooks is best for freelancers who want an easy-to-use interface and client-friendly invoicing. Xero excels for teams that need strong accounting features and multi-user collaboration. QuickBooks offers a comprehensive solution with many integrations, suitable for businesses that plan to scale. Wave is ideal for cost-conscious solo operators who need basic invoicing and reporting for free.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is easiest to switch to from FreshBooks?
Xero and QuickBooks both offer data import tools from FreshBooks. QuickBooks may be simpler if you have a bookkeeper or accountant familiar with its ecosystem, while Xero provides a similar clean interface.
Can I use Wave for time tracking if I have employees?
Wave’s time tracking is basic and best for a single user or very small team. For employees, QuickBooks or Xero offer more robust tracking and reporting per person.
