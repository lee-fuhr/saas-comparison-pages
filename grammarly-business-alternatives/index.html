Enterprises that rely on Grammarly Business for AI-driven writing assistance across email, docs, and customer relationship management (CRM) platforms may find that other tools better fit their workflow or budget. While Grammarly Business offers comprehensive grammar checks and style suggestions, some organizations need deeper integration with industry-specific terminology or stronger data privacy controls. This article explores four leading alternatives to help you evaluate which solution best supports your team’s writing needs.

alternative

ProWritingAid

offers detailed writing reports that go beyond grammar to analyze readability, sentence variety, and tone across email, documents, and CRM platforms. Its integration with major apps like Google Docs and Outlook makes it easy for enterprises to maintain consistency in professional communications. The tool also includes a plagiarism checker, which is useful for content teams that produce marketing or documentation materials.

alternative

Writer.com

is built specifically for enterprise teams that want to enforce brand voice, style guides, and compliance rules across all written content. It integrates directly with Slack, CRM tools like Salesforce, and document editors, so employees get real-time suggestions without leaving their workflow. Writer also prioritizes data security with SOC 2 compliance and enterprise-grade encryption.

alternative

LanguageTool

combines advanced grammar and spell checking with support for over 30 languages, making it a strong choice for global enterprises. Its open-source core allows customization of rules and dictionaries, and it works with email clients, Google Docs, and major CRMs via browser extensions. LanguageTool also offers an on-premises deployment option for organizations with strict data residency requirements.

alternative

QuillBot

focuses on paraphrasing and rewriting to help users refine tone, clarity, and conciseness in emails and other business communications. Its summarizing features can shorten long CRM notes or document drafts quickly. However, QuillBot’s real-time grammar and style checks are less comprehensive than other tools, so it works best as a supplement for revision rather than a primary writing assistant.

How they compare

Choose ProWritingAid if your team needs deep analytical writing reports and a plagiarism checker alongside standard grammar support. Select Writer.com when brand consistency and strict style governance are critical across your sales, customer support, and marketing teams. LanguageTool is the best fit for multilingual enterprises that require language customization and data sovereignty options. Use QuillBot as a lightweight tool for paraphrasing and summarizing, but not as a full replacement for robust grammar and style checking in enterprise workflows.

Frequently asked questions

Can these alternatives integrate with Salesforce or HubSpot just like Grammarly Business?
Yes, Writer.com and LanguageTool both offer native integrations with popular CRM platforms such as Salesforce and HubSpot. ProWritingAid and QuillBot work through browser extensions that can be used in web-based CRM interfaces, but they do not have dedicated CRM plugins.
Do any of these tools support custom dictionaries or industry-specific vocabulary?
Yes, all four alternatives allow some degree of custom dictionary or rule creation. Writer.com lets you build and enforce company-wide style guides and word lists. LanguageTool supports custom spelling dictionaries and grammar rules, while ProWritingAid and QuillBot offer user-defined word lists to manage jargon or brand terms.
