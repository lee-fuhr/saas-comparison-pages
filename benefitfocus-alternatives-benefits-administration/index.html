As a Benefits Director at a Fortune 500 company, you know that your benefits administration platform must handle scale, complexity, and compliance without compromising the employee experience. Benefitfocus has been a popular choice, but rising costs, integration challenges, or shifting strategic priorities may have you exploring other cloud-based options. This article examines three leading alternatives – Alight, Businessolver, and WEX Health – so you can make an informed decision for your organization.

alternative

Alight

offers a comprehensive benefits administration suite built for large, multi-site employers. Its strength lies in deep HR and payroll integration, plus robust analytics that help you manage total rewards across a global workforce. Alight also provides extensive compliance support and decision-support tools for employees.

Try Alight →
alternative

Businessolver

is known for its high-touch, concierge-style service model and a modern, consumer-grade user interface. The platform emphasizes personalized employee guidance through virtual assistants and live support, which can improve enrollment engagement and reduce call center volume. It works well for companies that want a partner who actively manages the benefits lifecycle.

alternative

WEX Health

focuses on health and wealth benefits, offering a flexible platform that handles CDH accounts (HSAs, FSAs, HRAs), COBRA, and billing alongside core benefits administration. For Fortune 500 firms with significant consumer-directed health plan populations, WEX Health provides seamless account-based integration and real-time claims data that can drive wellness program insights.

How they compare

All three alternatives are capable replacements for Benefitfocus, but each has a different emphasis. Alight excels in large employer complexity and data analytics. Businessolver stands out for employee experience and white-glove service. WEX Health is best when your priority is consumer-directed health account management and financial wellness. Evaluate which strength aligns with your company’s biggest pain points.
