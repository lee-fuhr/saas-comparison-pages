Best Revel Systems alternatives

Revel Systems is a popular cloud based POS for restaurants, but high volume operations sometimes find its pricing, hardware requirements, or offline capabilities limiting. If you run a busy full service or quick service restaurant and need enterprise grade reliability, exploring alternatives can help you find a system that scales with your growth, lowers costs, or integrates more smoothly with your existing tech stack. Below we compare four leading options that compete directly with Revel Systems.

alternative

Toast POS

is built specifically for restaurants and offers a dedicated enterprise tier called Toast Enterprise. It includes advanced reporting, multi location management, and first party hardware that handles high throughput with low latency. Toast also provides integrated online ordering, payroll, and marketing tools, which reduces the need for third party add ons.

alternative

Square Restaurants

combines Square’s flexible payment processing with a robust restaurant POS designed for multi unit operators. Its enterprise plan includes centralized menu management, inventory tracking, and customizable permissions. Square’s transparent flat rate pricing can appeal to high volume restaurants that want predictable costs, though offline mode and advanced customization may be more limited than Revel.

alternative

Lightspeed Restaurant

targets mid market and enterprise food service businesses with a strong back office suite. It offers real time analytics, multi location reporting, and integrations with major payment processors. Lightspeed’s interface is intuitive, and its inventory features (including recipe costing) are particularly useful for high volume kitchens that need tight cost control.

alternative

TouchBistro

is an iPad based POS that has expanded its enterprise capabilities through its TouchBistro Enterprise package. It supports offline mode, which is critical for high volume venues with unreliable internet. TouchBistro also includes table management, menu engineering, and loyalty tools, but its hardware ecosystem is less extensive than Toast’s or Revel’s.

How they compare

All four alternatives provide enterprise grade features for high volume restaurants, but each has distinct strengths. Toast POS offers the most comprehensive restaurant specific ecosystem and first party hardware. Square Restaurants excels at transparent pricing and simplicity. Lightspeed Restaurant provides deep inventory and reporting for operations that prioritize back office efficiency. TouchBistro shines with offline reliability and a lower hardware cost. The best choice depends on your restaurant’s specific needs around payment processing, menu complexity, and integration requirements.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my existing Revel Systems data to one of these alternatives?
Yes, most enterprise POS providers offer data migration services or tools to import menu items, employee records, and sales history from Revel. Check with each vendor about their onboarding process and any fees for data conversion.
Which alternative handles the highest order volumes without slowdowns?
Toast POS is generally considered the most robust for ultra high volume environments due to its purpose built hardware and cloud infrastructure. However, Lightspeed and TouchBistro also support large transaction volumes with local caching to maintain speed during internet outages.
