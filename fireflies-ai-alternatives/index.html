If your sales or leadership team relies on Fireflies.ai to capture meeting notes and action items, you know how valuable AI meeting assistants can be. But maybe you are exploring other options for better accuracy, deeper integrations, or a different pricing model. This article covers four strong alternatives: Otter.ai, Sembly, Grain, and Rev.ai. Each tool has unique strengths for tracking action items and keeping your team aligned.

alternative

Otter.ai

. Otter.ai excels at real-time transcription and automatic generation of action items with speaker labels. Its “Otter Assistant” can join your calendar meetings and deliver searchable notes, making it easy for sales teams to review client conversations. The free tier is generous, but advanced features like custom vocabulary may require a paid plan.

alternative

Sembly

. Sembly focuses on capturing meeting outcomes by creating structured summaries and action item lists tied to each meeting. It works with popular platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams, and its natural language processing highlights decisions and tasks. Sembly’s team dashboard helps leadership track accountability across projects.

alternative

Grain

. Grain is built for sales teams that want to record, transcribe, and share key moments from customer calls. It automatically identifies action items and allows you to clip specific segments for follow-up or training. Grain’s integration with CRMs like Salesforce makes it easy to log next steps directly from a recorded conversation.

alternative

Rev.ai

. Rev.ai provides high-accuracy automated transcription and a human transcription option for critical meetings. While it does not generate action items automatically, you can export transcripts to other tools or use its API to build custom workflows. This option works well for teams that need reliable, speaker-separated text and are willing to layer on their own action-item tracking.
