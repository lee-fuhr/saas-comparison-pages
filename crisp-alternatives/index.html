If you run a small business and need a live chat tool that fits your budget and workflow, you have probably come across Crisp. Crisp is a solid option with a free tier and a clean interface, but it might not be the perfect match for every team. You may need more automation, deeper integrations, or a different pricing structure. This article explores five strong Crisp alternatives Intercom, Tidio, LiveChat, Chaport, and HubSpot Live Chat to help you find the right fit for your customer messaging needs.

Intercom

is a powerful customer messaging platform that goes beyond live chat to include email, in-app messages, and automation. It scales well for growing businesses but can become expensive quickly, so it is best if you plan to invest in a full sales and support solution.

HubSpot Live Chat

How they compare

When choosing among these Crisp alternatives, consider your budget, team size, and required features. Crisp remains a strong contender with its free plan and unified inbox. Intercom is best for larger teams that need advanced automation. Tidio suits ecommerce businesses that want chatbot ease. LiveChat is a dependable standalone support tool. Chaport offers a simple free option for micro teams. HubSpot Live Chat is perfect for businesses already invested in the HubSpot ecosystem. Evaluate each based on your specific needs to find the best fit.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free alternative to Crisp that works well for small teams?
Yes, Tidio and Chaport both offer free plans. Tidio’s free version includes live chat and a basic chatbot, while Chaport gives you unlimited chats and a shared inbox at no cost. HubSpot also has a free live chat tool if you are comfortable with the HubSpot CRM.
Which Crisp alternative is best for a growing ecommerce business?
Tidio is a popular choice for ecommerce because it has easy Shopify and WooCommerce integrations, a visual chatbot builder, and affordable paid plans. Intercom is also powerful but costs more, so it works better if you have a larger budget and need advanced automation.
