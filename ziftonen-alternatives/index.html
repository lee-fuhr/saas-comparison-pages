Best ZiftONE alternatives

If your enterprise relies on channel partners to drive pipeline and joint campaigns, ZiftONE is a well known partner relationship management and channel marketing platform. However, not every organization’s needs agree perfectly with ZiftONE’s feature set, pricing, or deployment approach. This article explores five alternatives to ZiftONE: Impartner, ChannelTivity, Allbound, Kiflo, and PartnerStack. Each solution brings a slightly different angle to integrated ABM and partner orchestration, and we will compare them to help you find the best fit for your team.

alternative

Impartner

. Impartner is a comprehensive PRM and channel marketing automation platform that excels at managing the entire partner lifecycle. It offers strong co-branded campaign tools, a partner portal, and a built-in deal registration system, making it a direct competitor to ZiftONE for enterprises running large, multi-tier channel programs.

alternative

ChannelTivity

. ChannelTivity focuses specifically on partner incentive management and joint campaign execution. Its strength lies in tracking partner-led marketing activities, allocating co-op funds, and measuring campaign ROI, which is ideal for companies that want to tie partner efforts directly to pipeline revenue without a full PRM suite.

alternative

Allbound

. Allbound is a partner experience platform that emphasizes ease of use and content syndication. It provides a streamlined portal for partners to access sales materials, run multi-channel campaigns, and manage leads, with a modern interface that can appeal to enterprises seeking a simpler alternative to ZiftONE’s deeper feature set.

alternative

Kiflo

. Kiflo is a newer PRM designed for B2B SaaS companies that need a cost effective, no-code solution for partner recruitment, onboarding, and co-selling. It includes automated partner tiers, a partner directory, and basic campaign management, making it a viable option for mid-market enterprises scaling their partner programs.

alternative

PartnerStack

. PartnerStack is built for partnership ecosystems, combining PRM, affiliate marketing, and partner referral tracking in one platform. It emphasizes performance based incentives and automated partner payouts, which suits enterprises that want to treat channel partners like an extension of their demand generation team.

How they compare

All five alternatives to ZiftONE offer integrated ABM and partner campaign capabilities, but each prioritizes different aspects. Impartner is the closest feature match for traditional PRM with advanced portal and campaign tools. ChannelTivity stands out for incentive based joint campaign management and ROI tracking. Allbound leads on partner experience and content sharing simplicity. Kiflo is best for SaaS companies wanting a lightweight, affordable PRM with no code flexibility. PartnerStack is optimal for partner programs that rely on performance based referral and affiliate models. Enterprises should evaluate based on their partner program maturity, campaign complexity, and budget.

Frequently asked questions

How do I choose between ZiftONE and these alternatives for joint campaign management?
Start by mapping your partner program’s key workflows: co-branded email campaigns, deal registration, MDF (market development funds) tracking, and partner portal usage. If you need deep campaign automation and multi-tier partner hierarchies, Impartner or ZiftONE are strong. For simpler, incentive driven campaigns, ChannelTivity or PartnerStack may be more cost effective. Trial the platforms that match your most critical processes.
Can these alternatives integrate with my existing CRM and marketing automation tools?
Yes, all five alternatives offer native or API based integrations with major CRMs such as Salesforce and HubSpot, as well as marketing automation tools like Marketo and Eloqua. However, the depth of integration varies. Impartner and Allbound typically have the most mature CRM connectors, while Kiflo and PartnerStack focus more on lightweight data sync. Always verify integration scope during a demo.
