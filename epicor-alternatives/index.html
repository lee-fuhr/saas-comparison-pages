Best Epicor alternatives

Epicor is a popular ERP platform for mid-size manufacturers and distributors, but it may not fit every business perfectly. Some users find it complex to customize, expensive to scale, or lacking in specific industry features. If you are evaluating alternatives, this article covers four strong contenders: Plex, Infor CloudSuite, DELMIAworks, and IQMS. Each offers different strengths for production planning, inventory management, and financial control.

alternative

Plex

is a cloud-native ERP built for discrete and process manufacturers. It excels in real-time shop floor visibility and quality management, which helps mid-size companies reduce waste and improve compliance. Plex also integrates tightly with automation and IoT devices, making it a strong choice for manufacturers aiming for Industry 4.0.

alternative

Infor CloudSuite

is a suite of industry-specific ERPs powered by Amazon Web Services. It offers deep functionality for distribution and manufacturing verticals, with built-in analytics and AI tools to forecast demand. Infor’s flexible deployment (public cloud, private cloud, or on-premise) appeals to distributors who want to move to the cloud at their own pace.

alternative

DELMIAworks

(formerly IQMS) focuses on manufacturing execution and supply chain synchronization. It is particularly popular among plastics, packaging, and automotive suppliers because of its robust production scheduling and quality control modules. DELMIAworks provides a single database for ERP, MES, and supply chain, reducing data silos.

alternative

IQMS

(now part of Dassault Systèmes and renamed DELMIAworks, but still available under the original name for existing users) remains a standalone option for manufacturers that need a straightforward, on-premise ERP. It offers strong inventory tracking, order management, and accounting features without the complexity of larger enterprise systems. IQMS is often chosen by mid-size companies that want a proven solution without a full cloud migration.
