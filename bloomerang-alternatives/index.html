Best Bloomerang alternatives

If your mid-size nonprofit relies on Bloomerang for donor retention analytics, you know how important it is to track engagement, lapses, and giving patterns. But as your organization grows, you might need different features, pricing, or integrations. This article compares three popular alternatives: Salesforce Nonprofit, Blackbaud, and DonorPerfect. Each has strengths for donor retention work, and we will help you see which one fits your team.

alternative

Salesforce Nonprofit

. Salesforce Nonprofit (formerly NPSP) offers deep customization and robust donor retention analytics through its reporting and dashboard tools. It integrates with many third-party apps, so you can build a complete view of supporter behavior. However, it requires more setup time and often a dedicated administrator to maintain complex retention metrics.

alternative

Blackbaud

. Blackbaud (including Raiser’s Edge NXT) provides comprehensive donor analytics with built-in wealth screening, attrition tracking, and campaign performance reports. Its data-driven retention tools help mid-size nonprofits identify at-risk donors and automate stewardship touches. The platform can be expensive and may feel overwhelming for teams that prefer simpler workflows.

alternative

DonorPerfect

. DonorPerfect focuses on ease of use with solid donor retention analytics, including automated lapse alerts, giving summaries, and segment-based reporting. It is more affordable than Salesforce and Blackbaud, making it a strong middle ground for organizations that want reliable retention insights without a steep learning curve. Advanced analytics may require add-on modules or extra training.

How they compare

Choose Bloomerang if you already value its user-friendly interface and want to stick with a dedicated retention analytics tool. Switch to Salesforce Nonprofit if you need deep customization, scalability, and integration with a broader tech stack. Choose Blackbaud if you require enterprise-grade analytics, wealth screening, and strong reporting for larger mid-size nonprofits. Pick DonorPerfect if you want a cost-effective, easy-to-use system that still provides essential retention insights without heavy overhead.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for tracking donor lapses and creating re-engagement campaigns?
All three alternatives support lapse tracking, but DonorPerfect offers automated lapse alerts with simple setup, while Salesforce Nonprofit lets you build custom re-engagement workflows if your team has the technical skills.
Do these alternatives integrate with common email marketing and payment tools?
Yes. DonorPerfect integrates natively with Mailchimp and QuickBooks. Salesforce Nonprofit connects to many tools via its AppExchange. Blackbaud has its own ecosystem but also works with leading email and payment platforms. Check your specific tools before choosing.
