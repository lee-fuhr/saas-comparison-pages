If you run a partner program for a SaaS or e-commerce business, you have likely evaluated Impact as your platform. Impact offers a full suite of partnership automation, but its complexity, cost, and onboarding hurdles often lead teams to search for Impact alternatives. Whether you need a simpler interface, lower fees, or better support for specific program types (affiliate, influencer, channel), the market has several strong options. Below we compare five leading platforms: PartnerStack, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, Refersion, and Tune.

alternative

PartnerStack

. PartnerStack focuses on B2B SaaS partnerships, combining affiliate, referral, and channel management in one platform. It offers tiered commission structures and a built-in marketplace to recruit partners, making it a solid choice for subscription businesses looking to scale without the heavy setup of Impact.

Try PartnerStack →
alternative

ShareASale

. ShareASale is an established affiliate network with a large publisher base, ideal for e-commerce brands that want quick access to thousands of affiliates. The platform offers reliable tracking and a pay-per-sale model without long-term contracts, though it has fewer advanced automation features compared to Impact.

alternative

CJ Affiliate

. CJ Affiliate (formerly Commission Junction) is a top-tier network for enterprise e-commerce programs, known for its robust data insights and premium publisher relationships. It works best for high-volume merchants who prioritize brand safety and performance analytics over flexible partner types.

alternative

Refersion

. Refersion is a user-friendly affiliate management platform designed for e-commerce stores, especially those on Shopify, BigCommerce, and WooCommerce. It offers straightforward tracking, easy onboarding, and solid integrations without the enterprise-level complexity or cost of Impact.

Try Refersion →
alternative

Tune

. Tune (formerly HasOffers) provides a customizable affiliate and partner management backend, often used by performance marketing teams that need deep data control. It excels in attributing multi-touch conversions and offers white-label capabilities, but may require more technical setup than alternatives like Refersion.
