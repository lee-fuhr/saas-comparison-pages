Recurly is a well known subscription billing platform, but it may not be the right fit for every business. As your subscription model grows, you might need different features, pricing, or integrations. This article explores five strong Recurly alternatives: Chargebee, Stripe Billing, Zuora, Paddle, and Maxio. Each offers unique advantages for managing recurring revenue.

alternative

Chargebee

is a flexible billing platform that works well for small to midsize subscription businesses. It supports multiple pricing models, dunning management, and integrates with many payment gateways. Chargebee also offers a self serve portal and automated tax compliance.

alternative

Stripe Billing

is a native part of the Stripe ecosystem, making it a natural choice if you already use Stripe for payments. It provides smart retries, invoicing, and subscription management without additional third party tools. Its pricing is usage based, which can be cost effective for growing businesses.

alternative

Zuora

is an enterprise grade solution designed for large scale subscription operations. It handles complex billing scenarios, revenue recognition, and global compliance. Zuora is best for companies with high transaction volumes or sophisticated pricing needs.

alternative

Paddle

is a merchant of record that handles sales tax, VAT, and compliance for digital products. It simplifies global selling by taking responsibility for tax filing and fraud protection. Paddle is ideal for software companies that want to offload payment logistics.

Try Paddle →
alternative

Maxio

(formerly Chargify) focuses on B2B SaaS businesses with recurring billing and subscription management. It offers advanced features like usage based billing, quote to cash workflows, and detailed analytics. Maxio is a good fit for companies that need strong reporting and invoicing capabilities.

How they compare

All five alternatives cover core subscription billing needs but differ in target audience and complexity. Chargebee and Stripe Billing are best for small to midsize businesses that want ease of use and fast integration. Zuora targets large enterprises with complex billing models. Paddle is unique as a merchant of record, making it ideal for global digital sales without tax headaches. Maxio suits B2B SaaS companies that need robust invoicing and analytics. Compared to Recurly, these alternatives may offer better pricing for specific use cases, stronger native integrations, or simpler compliance handling.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my subscription data from Recurly to another platform?
Yes, most alternatives offer migration tools or professional services to help you move customer data, billing plans, and historical invoices. Plan for some testing and downtime during the transition.
Which alternative is the cheapest for a startup with fewer than 100 subscribers?
Stripe Billing often has the lowest entry cost because it charges a small percentage of transaction volume rather than a fixed monthly fee. Chargebee also offers a free tier for new businesses. Always check current pricing pages for the latest details.
