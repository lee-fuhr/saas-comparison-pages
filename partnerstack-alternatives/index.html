If you run a SaaS affiliate or partner program, you may have considered PartnerStack. It is a solid platform for managing partnerships, but it is not the only option. You might want an alternative for reasons like pricing, ease of use, or specific features that better fit your business model. Below we cover five strong alternatives to help you find the right fit.

alternative

Impact

is an enterprise-grade partnership automation platform. It suits larger SaaS companies with complex partner ecosystems and robust compliance needs. However, its pricing and setup can be heavy for smaller teams.

alternative

ShareASale

is a long-established affiliate network. It offers a huge pool of publishers but is less tailored for SaaS-style recurring commissions and tiered partnerships. It works best if you want broad reach with standard affiliate payouts.

alternative

Rewardful

is a lightweight, developer-friendly platform built for SaaS. It integrates easily with Stripe or Paddle and focuses on simple recurring commission tracking. This makes it a strong choice for bootstrapped or early-stage SaaS companies.

Try Rewardful →
alternative

FirstPromoter

is a feature-rich affiliate management tool designed for digital products. It supports recurring commissions, multi-tier programs, and has a clean dashboard. Its interface can feel dated, but the functionality is solid for mid-growth SaaS teams.

Try FirstPromoter →
alternative

Refersion

is a user-friendly platform popular with ecommerce and SaaS businesses. It offers good tracking for both one-time and recurring sales with easy integration through Shopify or custom APIs. It is often praised for its simple onboarding and support.

How they compare

When choosing between these options, consider your program size and complexity. Impact is the most powerful but also the most expensive and complex. ShareASale excels in scale but lacks SaaS-essential features like flexible recurring billing. Rewardful and FirstPromoter are purpose-built for SaaS, with Rewardful being simpler and FirstPromoter offering more features. Refersion balances simplicity with strong tracking, making it a good mid-range choice for SaaS companies that want a straightforward partner program.

Frequently asked questions

Is PartnerStack still a good choice for some SaaS companies?
Yes, PartnerStack excels at managing complex two-sided partner marketplaces and has strong integrations. But if you do not need those advanced marketplace features, a simpler alternative can save money and time.
Which alternative is best if I have a small budget?
Rewardful and FirstPromoter both offer affordable plans for growing SaaS companies. Rewardful is often the most cost-effective for smaller programs with straightforward needs.
