If your agency currently uses or is considering Applied Systems, you know it is a powerful but complex tool. Many agencies also evaluate Vertafore as a direct competitor, but there are other strong options including HawkSoft, EZLynx, and Jenesi. This article compares these alternatives to help you decide which agency management system best fits your workflows, budget, and growth plans. We focus on real differences in ease of use, integration capabilities, and support without relying on made-up numbers.

alternative

Vertafore

offers a broad suite of products like Sagitta, BenefitPoint, and AMS360 that scale from small to large agencies. It competes directly with Applied Systems in terms of features, but implementation can be lengthy and the total cost may be higher for smaller teams. Vertafore’s cloud options provide strong integration with carriers, though some users report a steeper learning curve compared to simpler platforms.

alternative

HawkSoft

is built around a single-screen, policy-centric workflow that many independent agents find more intuitive than Applied Systems. It focuses on ease of use and requires less training time, making it a good fit for agencies that want to reduce administrative overhead. HawkSoft does not offer as many built-in accounting or document management features, so you may need to pair it with other tools.

alternative

EZLynx

is a cloud-based, comparative rater and agency management system that is especially popular with small to midsize agencies. It combines rating, management, and customer communication in one platform with a clean interface. EZLynx may lack some advanced reporting or accounting functions found in Applied Systems, but it is often more affordable and faster to deploy.

alternative

Jenesi

is a newer, all-in-one cloud system designed for independent agencies that want modern workflows and strong automation. It includes a built-in rater and claims management, and it emphasizes real-time updates and a mobile-first experience. Jenesi’s smaller user base means fewer third-party integrations and community resources compared to Applied Systems or Vertafore, but it can be a cost-effective choice for growing agencies.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is easiest to migrate to from Applied Systems?
Vertafore (especially AMS360) often has the most similar structure to Applied Systems, which can make data migration smoother. HawkSoft and EZLynx also offer dedicated conversion teams, but expect some workflow changes since their interfaces are different.
Do any of these alternatives offer a free trial?
Yes, most vendors provide demo or trial periods. HawkSoft and EZLynx typically offer free trials or sandbox access. Vertafore and Jenesi usually require a sales consultation first, but they can set up a test environment for your agency to evaluate.
