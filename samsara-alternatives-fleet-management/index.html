If you manage a fleet of 1,000 or more vehicles, you know Samsara is a market leader. But no single platform fits every operation. Your team needs GPS tracking and ELD compliance that scales, integrates with existing systems, and stays within budget. This guide covers three strong alternatives – Verizon Connect, Geotab, and Motive – and compares them on the factors that matter most for large fleets: reliability, data access, driver workflow, and total cost.

alternative

Verizon Connect

. Verizon Connect offers a robust platform built for enterprise fleets, with hardware that works across trucks, vans, and trailers. Its ELD solution is FMCSA-certified and integrates tightly with Verizon’s network, providing strong coverage and uptime. For fleets that already use other Verizon services, the unified billing and support can simplify operations.

alternative

Geotab

. Geotab is known for its open platform and massive third-party app marketplace, which lets you customize dashboards and connect with maintenance, routing, and payroll systems. Its GO device is reliable and can be transferred between vehicles easily, making it a good fit for fleets that rotate equipment often. The company also offers a “no lock-in” data model so you own your vehicle information.

alternative

Motive

. Motive (formerly KeepTruckin) combines ELD compliance with an AI-powered dashcam and driver safety scoring system. Its app is driver-friendly and supports team messaging, document capture, and automatic log editing. For large fleets, Motive’s centralized safety management and real-time coaching features can help reduce accident rates and insurance costs.

How they compare

All three alternatives offer FMCSA-compliant ELDs and real-time GPS tracking for large fleets. Verizon Connect gives you the deepest integration with other Verizon products but can be more expensive per vehicle. Geotab provides the most flexibility through its open API and third-party app ecosystem, which is ideal if you need custom reporting or integrations. Motive stands out for its advanced safety features and easy driver experience, though its hardware lock-in may be a concern for some operations. Your choice should depend on your priority: existing vendor relationships (Verizon Connect), data ownership and customization (Geotab), or driver safety and retention (Motive).
