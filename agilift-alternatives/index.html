Best Agiloft alternatives

Agiloft is a leading contract lifecycle management (CLM) platform known for its deep customization. It allows organizations to tailor workflows, fields, and integrations to match their exact processes. However, some teams look for alternatives to Luminance and other tools because they need a different balance of AI features, ease of use, or pricing. This article reviews four common alternatives to help you find a customizable contract management platform that fits your needs.

alternative

Kira Systems

. Kira Systems uses advanced machine learning to extract and analyze contract data with high accuracy. It is best for organizations that prioritize in-depth clause review and due diligence over workflow automation. Kira offers strong customization in its AI models, but its contract management features are more limited than Agiloft’s.

alternative

Luminance

. Luminance focuses on AI powered review and pattern analysis, using a unique approach to identify unusual clauses and risks. It is a strong tool for legal teams that want to quickly assess large contract portfolios. However, its workflow customization and end-to-end contract management capabilities are less mature than Agiloft’s.

alternative

Lexion

. Lexion combines AI automation with simple contract management workflows. It is designed for speed and ease of use, with a clean interface that reduces training time. While Lexion offers some customization, its flexibility is limited compared to Agiloft’s deep configuration options.

alternative

Malbek

. Malbek focuses on simplifying contract creation and approvals with AI assisted drafting and negotiation. It provides a modern, user friendly experience for teams that want to reduce manual work. Malbek’s customization is more structured than Agiloft’s, making it a good fit for organizations that prefer predefined templates over open ended configuration.

How they compare

Agiloft stands out for its unparalleled customization, allowing teams to build any workflow or field they need. Kira Systems excels in AI extraction but lacks full CLM features. Luminance offers unique risk detection but limited workflow control. Lexion is simple and fast, while Malbek is easy to use with guided processes. Your choice depends on whether you need deep customization (Agiloft) or specialized AI analysis (Kira, Luminance) combined with simpler management (Lexion, Malbek).

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my contracts from Luminance to Agiloft easily?
Yes, most contract management platforms support data import through CSV or API. You should plan a dedicated migration project to map metadata and preserve clause tags. Agiloft provides migration services and documentation to help.
Which alternative is best for a mid sized legal team with limited IT support?
Lexion and Malbek are good options because they are easy to set up and use without heavy IT involvement. Luminance also offers a simple onboarding process for contract review, but it focuses on AI analysis rather than full lifecycle management.
