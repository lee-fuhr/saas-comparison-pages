Best Glide alternatives

Glide is a popular no-code platform that lets you turn Google Sheets into mobile-first apps quickly. However, your team may outgrow Glide due to pricing changes, a need for more complex logic, or a desire for native mobile feel. This article covers three Glide alternatives: Bubble, Adalo, and Buildship. Each offers a different balance of power, ease, and cost for teams building apps from Google Sheets.

alternative

Bubble

is a general-purpose no-code platform that works for web and mobile apps. It provides deep flexibility for custom data models and advanced logic, but it has a steeper learning curve than Glide. You can connect Bubble to Google Sheets through APIs or plugins, though it is not designed to treat a spreadsheet as a primary database.

alternative

Adalo

is a no-code tool focused on building native mobile apps with a drag-and-drop interface. It integrates with Google Sheets using built-in connectors or services like Zapier, making it a straightforward choice for teams that want more design control. Adalo offers mobile components that feel polished, but its native functionality adds complexity compared to Glide’s web-based simplicity.

alternative

Buildship

is a low-code platform that lets you build backend workflows and app interfaces directly on top of Google Sheets. It uses a visual builder for logic and data processing, and it can generate mobile-friendly UIs with real-time sync. Buildship is ideal for teams that need custom business rules and automated updates beyond Glide’s limit.

How they compare

Glide is the easiest to start with if you only need a simple spreadsheet-driven app. Bubble offers the most power and customization but requires more learning and development time. Adalo provides a middle ground with native mobile features and reasonable ease of use. Buildship excels when you need complex logic and live data sync without leaving Google Sheets. Your choice depends on your team’s comfort with coding concepts, your need for native mobile behavior, and the complexity of your app’s logic.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my Glide app to one of these alternatives easily?
Direct migration is not possible because each platform has its own structure. You can export your Google Sheets data and then rebuild the app’s screens and logic in the new tool. Plan for manual work to recreate workflows and design elements.
Which alternative is best for a team with no coding experience?
Adalo is the closest to Glide in simplicity, with a drag-and-drop builder and easy spreadsheet integration. Bubble is more powerful but requires time to learn its visual logic. Buildship works well if your team is comfortable with flow charts and data transformations.
