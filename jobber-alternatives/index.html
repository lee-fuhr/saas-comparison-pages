If you run a home-service business like plumbing, HVAC, or cleaning, you need a scheduling tool that keeps your day organized. Jobber is a popular choice for small to medium teams, but it is not the only option. Depending on your budget, team size, and feature needs, other tools may work better for you. Here we look at three strong Jobber alternatives: ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and ServiceFusion.

alternative

ServiceTitan

is built for larger home-service companies with multiple trucks and crews. It offers advanced features like inventory management, marketing automation, and detailed reporting, but it comes with a higher price tag and a steeper learning curve. This tool works best if you have a big team and need enterprise-level control.

alternative

Housecall Pro

is a user-friendly alternative that focuses on small to midsize businesses. It provides easy scheduling, dispatch, and payment processing, plus a mobile app that technicians find simple to use. Many entrepreneurs choose it when they want a clean interface without paying for features they do not need.

alternative

ServiceFusion

is a budget-friendly option that still covers the basics like scheduling, invoicing, and customer management. It lacks some of the bells and whistles of the bigger platforms, but it is straightforward and reliable for sole operators or small crews. If cost is your main concern, this tool is worth a look.

How they compare

All three tools handle scheduling, dispatching, and invoicing, but they differ in scale and price. ServiceTitan is powerful and pricey, best for companies with ten or more employees. Housecall Pro balances ease of use with solid features for growing teams. ServiceFusion keeps things simple and affordable, ideal for very small businesses. Consider your team size, budget, and must-have features when choosing. A free trial is a good way to see which one fits your daily workflow.
