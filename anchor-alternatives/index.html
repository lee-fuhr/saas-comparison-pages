Anchor, now known as Spotify for Podcasters, is a popular free hosting platform that makes it easy to start a podcast. But as your show grows, you may want more advanced analytics, better monetization options, or stronger control over your RSS feed. Many podcasters find that Anchor’s limited analytics and lack of portability can become frustrating. If you are looking for platforms with better analytics or more control, these five alternatives are worth exploring.

alternative

Buzzsprout

offers an intuitive dashboard with clear analytics, including listener locations and device breakdowns. It also provides a free migration tool to move your episodes from Anchor, and its paid plans come with built-in monetization through affiliate links and dynamic ads.

alternative

Podbean

gives you robust analytics such as episode-level listener retention and download trends. It also has a built-in ad marketplace and easy integration with YouTube automation, making it a strong choice for podcasters who want both control and revenue options.

Try Podbean →
alternative

Libsyn

is one of the oldest podcast hosts and provides detailed statistics like unique downloads and geographic data. It offers full RSS feed ownership and advanced publishing controls, which appeal to podcasters who need professional grade hosting and distribution flexibility.

alternative

Transistor

focuses on analytics for businesses and brands, offering per-episode download numbers and listener trends over time. It also supports multiple shows on one account and provides a clean, ad-free listening experience for your audience.

Try Transistor →
alternative

Captivate

is designed for podcasters who want to grow their show with marketing tools, including call to action buttons and email capture. Its analytics include listener demographics and episode performance, and it offers unlimited team members on most plans.

How they compare

All five alternatives provide better analytics and more control than Anchor. Buzzsprout is beginner friendly with a free trial, Podbean excels in monetization, Libsyn is the most established and reliable, Transistor suits business focused podcasts, and Captivate offers marketing features. Each has a paid plan, but none are free like Anchor. Choose based on your priorities: analytics depth, monetization, or marketing tools.

Frequently asked questions

Can I transfer my podcast from Anchor to one of these alternatives?
Yes, all five alternatives offer free migration tools or step by step guides to transfer your episodes, RSS feed, and subscriber base from Anchor. You will need to set up a 301 redirect to avoid losing listeners.
Which alternative has the best analytics for a beginner?
Buzzsprout and Captivate both provide clear, easy to read analytics without overwhelming you. Buzzsprout’s dashboard is especially simple for tracking downloads and listener locations, while Captivate also includes marketing metrics.
