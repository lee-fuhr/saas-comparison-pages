Choosing between Monday.com and Smartsheet often comes down to how your team prefers to work. Monday.com uses visual project boards with columns, cards, and timelines, making it easy to see progress at a glance. Smartsheet looks and feels like a spreadsheet, so it appeals to teams that are comfortable with rows, formulas, and grid-based planning. Both tools can manage projects, but they serve different workflows. This article helps you compare them side by side so you can pick the right fit for your team.

How they compare

Both tools help teams organize work, but they differ in approach and complexity. Monday.com wins on ease of use and visual clarity, making it great for teams that want simple board based planning. Smartsheet is best for data heavy projects where formulas, rollups, and spreadsheet style control matter. Monday.com offers more intuitive automations and templates, while Smartsheet gives you deeper grid customization and advanced reporting. For teams that value speed of setup and a visual interface, Monday.com is the stronger option. For teams that need spreadsheet power with collaboration, Smartsheet fits better.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Monday.com like a spreadsheet with formulas?
Monday.com has limited formula support compared to Smartsheet. You can use simple math and progress calculations in column values, but it does not offer the full formula library, cell references, or cross sheet linking that Smartsheet provides.
Which tool is better for managing multiple projects with dependencies?
Monday.com offers native dependency and timeline views that are easy to set up visually. Smartsheet also supports dependencies, but you need to configure them through columns and formulas. Monday.com is more intuitive for teams new to project management, while Smartsheet gives power users more control over schedule logic.
