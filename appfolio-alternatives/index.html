Best AppFolio alternatives

AppFolio is a popular property management platform used by many landlords and property managers, but it is not the right fit for every portfolio. Costs, feature gaps, or scaling needs may push you to explore alternatives. If you manage multifamily or commercial properties and need an end-to-end management tool, several other software options offer similar or better capabilities. This article covers four strong AppFolio alternatives: Propertyware, Buildium, Rent Manager, and TenantCloud.

alternative

Propertyware

. Propertyware is built for large portfolios and focuses on single-family rentals and small to mid-size multifamily properties. It offers strong accounting, maintenance tracking, and tenant portal features, but it does not include built-in HOA management. This tool scales well for landlords with hundreds of units.

alternative

Buildium

. Buildium is a versatile platform that handles residential, association, and commercial property management in one place. It includes accounting, leasing, maintenance, and online rent collection. Buildium is popular among property managers with mixed portfolios and offers a clear upgrade path as you grow.

alternative

Rent Manager

. Rent Manager is a highly customizable solution that works for multifamily, commercial, and mixed-use properties. It features advanced financial reporting, owner portals, and maintenance management. The software is especially suited for landlords who want deep control over workflows without needing multiple add-ons.

alternative

TenantCloud

. TenantCloud is a budget-friendly option geared toward smaller landlords and independent owners. It covers tenant screening, online payments, maintenance requests, and accounting. While it lacks some advanced enterprise features, it is easy to use and offers a free tier for very small portfolios.

How they compare

All four tools provide core property management functions like lease tracking, rent collection, and maintenance requests. Propertyware and Rent Manager lean toward larger portfolios and offer more customization, while Buildium balances residential and commercial needs with solid association management. TenantCloud is the cheapest and simplest, but it lacks reporting depth and scalability for large operations. Pricing structures vary: Buildium and Propertyware charge per unit, Rent Manager uses a flat fee or per unit, and TenantCloud offers free and paid tiers. Your choice depends on portfolio size, property types, and budget.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use any of these alternatives for commercial real estate management?
Yes. Rent Manager and Buildium both support commercial property features like CAM charges, lease abstraction, and commercial maintenance logs. Propertyware and TenantCloud are more focused on residential but can handle some commercial needs in a limited way.
Which alternative is best for a growing portfolio of 100 to 500 units?
Buildium and Propertyware are both strong choices at this scale. Buildium offers a smooth upgrade path and good support for mixed portfolios. Propertyware provides robust customization and accounting for larger single-family and multifamily holdings.
