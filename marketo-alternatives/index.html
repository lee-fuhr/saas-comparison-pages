Market has long been a powerful platform for enterprise demand generation, but it is not the right fit for every team. High costs, a steep learning curve, and rigid data structures can push B2B marketers to look elsewhere. Whether you need simpler workflows, tighter CRM integration, or more agile pricing, exploring Marketo alternatives can help you find a solution that matches your team’s size, budget, and technical maturity.

alternative

Pardot

(now part of Salesforce Marketing Cloud Account Engagement) is a natural choice for organizations already using Salesforce CRM. It offers strong lead scoring, B2B-specific email marketing, and seamless data sync with Sales Cloud. However, its pricing tiers can limit features like A/B testing and landing pages, and it is best suited for mid-market to enterprise companies that are fully committed to the Salesforce ecosystem.

alternative

HubSpot Marketing Hub

stands out for its ease of use and all-in-one approach. It combines email automation, CRM, landing pages, and analytics in a single platform, making it ideal for teams that want to get up and running quickly. The higher tiers (Enterprise) add advanced functionality like multi-touch attribution and custom reporting, but costs scale quickly as your contact database grows.

alternative

Eloqua

is Oracle’s enterprise-grade marketing automation solution, built for complex, high-volume demand generation. It excels at sophisticated lead scoring, campaign segmentation, and advanced reporting for large B2B organizations. The trade-off is a steeper implementation curve and higher total cost of ownership, making it most appropriate for teams that need deep customization and already have dedicated technical resources.

alternative

ActiveCampaign

offers a more affordable yet feature-rich option with strong email marketing, marketing automation, and CRM capabilities. It is particularly well suited for B2B teams that want advanced automation logic (like conditional content and lead scoring) without enterprise-level pricing. However, its reporting and integrations may not match the depth of Marketo or Eloqua for very large, complex sales cycles.

Frequently asked questions

Which Marketo alternative is best for a small B2B team with a limited budget?
ActiveCampaign offers strong automation and CRM features at a significantly lower price point than Marketo. For very small teams or early-stage companies, HubSpot Marketing Hub’s free or low-tier plans can also work well, though you may outgrow them quickly.
I need a tool that integrates deeply with Salesforce. Should I choose Pardot or Eloqua?
Pardot offers the most native, seamless Salesforce integration because it is built on the same platform. Eloqua also integrates with Salesforce but requires more configuration and is better suited for teams that need advanced orchestration and custom objects. If your primary goal is frictionless sync with Sales Cloud, start with Pardot.
