Best Elfsight alternatives

Elfsight is a popular platform for adding widgets like social feeds, reviews, and counters to websites without coding. But you might be looking for alternatives due to pricing, feature gaps, or specific needs like better social proof or review display. This article covers four no-code options that can replace or complement Elfsight widgets on your site.

alternative

Proof

. Proof is a social proof tool that shows recent customer actions like purchases and sign-ups in real time. It uses no-code pop-up notifications to build trust and urgency on your site. Proof integrates with Shopify, WooCommerce, and other platforms, making it easy to set up without technical skills.

alternative

Trustpilot widget

. The Trustpilot widget displays your existing Trustpilot reviews directly on your website with a simple embed code. It is a no-code solution that shows star ratings, review counts, and recent testimonials. This widget is ideal if you already collect reviews on Trustpilot and want to showcase them seamlessly.

alternative

ProveSource

. ProveSource is a social proof notification tool that shows live visitor activity, such as recent purchases and sign-ups, on your site. It offers no-code integration with popular website builders like WordPress, Squarespace, and Wix. ProveSource also includes A/B testing features to help you optimize for conversions.

alternative

Trustpilot

. Trustpilot as a full platform allows you to collect, manage, and display customer reviews across your website and other channels. Its no-code widgets can show aggregate ratings, review sliders, and trust badges. Unlike the simple widget, the full Trustpilot service includes review invitations and moderation tools.

How they compare

Elfsight offers a wide range of widget types from social media feeds to countdown timers. Proof and ProveSource focus specifically on social proof notifications, while Trustpilot is specialized for review display and collection. If you need a general widget builder, Elfsight is versatile but may be overkill. If your goal is to increase conversions through social proof, Proof or ProveSource are leaner alternatives. For showcasing customer reviews with built-in credibility, the Trustpilot widget or full Trustpilot platform are strong choices. Pricing varies, with Proof and ProveSource offering monthly plans and Trustpilot having free and paid tiers. None of these alternatives require coding skills.

Frequently asked questions

Can I replace all Elfsight widgets with one of these alternatives?
No, because each alternative focuses on a specific type of widget. Proof and ProveSource only handle social proof notifications. Trustpilot only handles reviews. If you need multiple widget types like Instagram feeds, countdown timers, or custom forms, you may need to combine several tools or keep Elfsight for those functions.
Do these alternatives work with my website builder without coding?
Yes, all four tools offer no-code integration methods such as copy-paste embed codes, plugins for WordPress, Shopify, and similar platforms. You can add them to your site without modifying code. Always check each tool’s integration guide for your specific builder.
