Workiva is a popular platform for financial reporting, compliance, and audit, known for its connected data and document management. But compliance and reporting specialists sometimes find that Workiva does not perfectly fit their budget, workflow, or integration needs. This article explores four strong alternatives: Planful, Adaptive Insights, Anaplan, and Vena tools. Each tool offers a different approach to planning, reporting, and compliance, so you can match features to your team’s specific requirements.

alternative

Planful

helps compliance specialists automate financial close processes and manage intercompany reconciliations directly within a single, audit-ready system. Its built-in compliance features support Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) and other regulatory frameworks, reducing manual spreadsheet work. For reporting teams, Planful offers prebuilt dashboards and a centralized data model that keeps versions consistent.

alternative

Adaptive Insights

from Workday provides a cloud-based planning and reporting environment that many compliance teams use for rolling forecasts and driver-based budgeting. Its strong integration with major ERP systems makes data collection for compliance reports easier. However, its reporting capabilities are more focused on financial planning than on detailed document management, which may matter for teams that produce lengthy regulatory filings.

alternative

Anaplan

is a connected planning platform that handles complex, large-scale modeling needed for regulatory capital calculations, risk reporting, and stress testing. Compliance specialists appreciate its ability to link operational data with financial forecasts in real time. The platform requires more upfront configuration than some alternatives, but it offers deep flexibility for organizations with intricate reporting hierarchies.

Frequently asked questions

Which alternative is best for a small compliance team with limited IT support?
Vena tools is often a good fit for small teams because it works inside a familiar Excel interface and requires little custom coding. Its prebuilt compliance templates and approval workflows let you set up controlled reporting quickly without heavy IT involvement.
Will switching from Workiva to another tool require us to redesign our entire reporting process?
Not necessarily. Most alternatives offer data import tools and mapping capabilities to bring in existing reports and structures. Planful and Vena, for example, include import wizards and templates that mimic common Workiva workflows. However, you should plan for a transition period of several weeks to retrain users and adjust any custom checklists or review cycles.
