GoToWebinar is a popular platform for hosting webinars, but marketers sometimes seek alternatives for better pricing, ease of use, or dedicated features like customizable registration pages and detailed analytics. We compare three strong options: Zoom, Webex, and Demio.

alternative

Zoom

offers built-in webinar capabilities with registration pages and attendee reports, making it a flexible choice for marketers who already use Zoom for meetings. Its analytics include poll results and question logs, though advanced features require the higher-priced webinar add-on.

How they compare

Zoom suits marketers who need a multipurpose platform but want webinar add-ons. Webex fits teams that require enterprise security and deep integration with existing Cisco tools. Demio stands out for dedicated marketing features like built-in email sequences and clear analytics without extra costs.

Frequently asked questions

Can I use Zoom for free webinars with registration pages?
Zoom’s free tier does not include webinar functionality. You need a paid plan that includes the webinar add-on to create registration pages and access attendee analytics.
Does Demio integrate with my email marketing software?
Yes, Demio offers native integrations with common email marketing tools such as Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, and ConvertKit, allowing you to sync registrants automatically.
