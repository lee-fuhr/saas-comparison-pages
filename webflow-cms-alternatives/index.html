Best Webflow CMS alternatives

If you are a designer who wants visual control over your site without writing code, Webflow CMS is a strong choice. But it may not fit every project or budget. Here we look at three alternatives that also put visual editing first, each with its own strengths.

alternative

WordPress.org

(the self-hosted version) gives you a familiar block editor and thousands of visual page builders like Elementor or Beaver Builder. You get full design flexibility without code, but you do need to manage hosting and updates yourself.

alternative

Ghost

focuses on clean, fast content publishing with a simple visual editor. It is great for blogs and newsletters, but its design options are more limited than Webflow’s, and you may need a theme or custom code for advanced layouts.

alternative

Contentful

offers a headless CMS with a rich text editor and a visual content modeling interface. It separates content from design, so you can build a custom front end with your own visual tools, but you will need a separate system for layout control.

How they compare

Webflow CMS combines design and content in one visual canvas. WordPress.org gives you more plugins and themes for layout control, but with more maintenance. Ghost is simpler and faster for content-driven sites, while Contentful provides flexibility for developers to build custom visual experiences around your content.
