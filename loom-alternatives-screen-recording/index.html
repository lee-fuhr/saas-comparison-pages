Best Loom alternatives

If your async communication team relies on Loom for screen recording but wants to explore other options, you have several strong alternatives. Each tool offers different strengths for recording your screen, adding personality, or connecting with customers. We will look at Vidyard, Synthesia, and Bonjoro to help you find the fit for your workflow.

alternative

Vidyard

offers a robust screen and webcam recording platform built for sales and marketing teams. It includes detailed analytics, viewer notifications, and easy sharing through links or embedded videos. Vidyard also integrates with popular CRM tools, making it a strong choice if you need to track engagement.

alternative

Synthesia

lets you create screen recordings with AI avatars instead of your own face, which can save time on production. You type your script, pick an avatar, and the tool generates a video with your screen capture overlaid. This works well for training or onboarding where you want a consistent, professional look without filming yourself.

alternative

Bonjoro

focuses on personalized, one-to-one video messages for customer outreach. You record short videos directly on your phone or desktop and send them as personalized links. It shines for building relationships but is less suited for longer screen recordings or team-wide async communication.

How they compare

Loom is great for quick, casual async screen recordings with a free tier and simple sharing. Vidyard offers deeper analytics and CRM integration, perfect for tracking customer video engagement. Synthesia uses AI avatars to produce videos without recording yourself, ideal for scalable training content. Bonjoro prioritizes personal one-on-one messages, best for sales or support follow-ups rather than team updates. Choose based on whether you need analytics, AI avatars, or personal rapport.

Frequently asked questions

Can I migrate my existing Loom videos to another tool?
Most alternatives do not offer a direct import from Loom. You can download your Loom videos as MP4 files and then upload them to another platform like Vidyard or Synthesia.
Which alternative is best for internal team updates?
For internal async communication, Loom itself is still strong, but Vidyard works well if you need viewing analytics. Synthesia is good for standardized training videos. Bonjoro is better for external personal messages, not team updates.
